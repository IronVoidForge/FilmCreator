# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the sudden escalation of conflict and demonstrate the tactical superiority/brutality of the Green Martian warriors.

**Scene Summary:** As the protagonist and Sola attempt to return home, a retreat by the green Martians signals an incoming threat: twenty large, gray-painted aerial vessels. A highly organized skirmish ensues as the green warriors use the city's architecture to target specific vulnerabilities in the fleet, routing the attackers.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- Sola
- Green Martian Warriors (Combatants)

**Participating Environments:**
- Deserted Martian City (Buildings/Rooftops)
- Surrounding Valleys/Skyline

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Calm/Routine $\rightarrow$ Sudden Alarm $\rightarrow$ Intense Observation of Warfare.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the approaching fleet.
- Medium shots of Green Martian warriors engaging targets.
- Close-ups of the protagonist's reaction to the scale of the battle.
- Dynamic tracking shots following the movement of the ships.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Position of the Protagonist and Sola relative to the city structures.
- Directional movement of the gray vessels vs. the green Martian fire.

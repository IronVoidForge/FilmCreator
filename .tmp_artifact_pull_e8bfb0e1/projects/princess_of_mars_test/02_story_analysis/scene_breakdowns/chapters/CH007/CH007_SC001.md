# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the scale of Martian society and introduce the protagonist's integration into their customs through a grand procession.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist joins Sola and a massive cavalcade of two hundred and fifty decorated chariots drawn by enormous beasts. They travel across the Dead Sea Bottom toward a remote incubator site. Upon arrival, the protagonist performs an impressive physical feat by leaping over parked chariots to impress Chieftain Lorquas Ptomel.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Sola, Lorquas Ptomel (Jed), various Martian warriors/drivers.

**Participating Environments:** The Dead Sea Bottom/Valley; The Plaza near the Incubator.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe/Grandeur $\rightarrow$ Tension/Performance (the leap).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Wide establishing shots of the massive chariot procession.
* Medium tracking shots of the protagonist riding with Sola.
* Low-angle hero shot of the protagonist's leap over the chariots.
* Reaction shots from Lorquas Ptomel and the crowd.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Chariot decoration patterns; The physical height/scale of the beasts compared to the protagonist; The specific sequence of the chariot jump.

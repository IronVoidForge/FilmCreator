# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To depict the ritualistic and pitiless nature of Martian biology and social structure.

**Scene Summary:** A ritualized "capture" takes place as three-to-four-foot-tall Martians emerge from the incubator. Women and youths form a gauntlet to collect the newly hatched beings. The scene highlights the communal, non-parental nature of their upbringing.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Sola, Newly Hatched Martians, Martian women/youths.

**Participating Environments:** The Incubator (walled enclosure).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Clinical/Ritualistic Intensity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Close-ups of the eggs cracking and the hatchlings emerging.
* Mid-shots of the gauntlet of women/youths catching the Martians.
* Handheld/unsteady shots to capture the frantic energy of the "capture."

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical appearance/size of the hatchlings; The movement patterns of the gauntlet.

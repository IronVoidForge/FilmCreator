# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To show the logistical reality of Martian survival and the strict management of their dwindling resources.

**Scene Summary:** Following the ceremony, warriors begin the heavy labor of transporting eggs from subterranean vaults to the incubator to prepare for the next five-year incubation cycle. This demonstrates the biological selection process and resource regulation.

**Participating Characters:** Martian Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Incubator; Subterranean Vaults.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Ritualistic high $\rightarrow$ Laborious/Systematic duty.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Wide shots of the warriors moving eggs.
* Close-ups of the eggs and the subterranean environment.
* Montage-style shots of the transport process.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The appearance and handling of the eggs; Lighting transitions between vaults and surface.

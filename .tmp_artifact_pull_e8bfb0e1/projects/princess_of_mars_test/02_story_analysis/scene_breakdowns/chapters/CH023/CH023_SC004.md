# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the aftermath of the crash and the new status quo of being lost.

**Scene Summary:** Carter survives the plunge but finds his navigation equipment (compass and speedometer) destroyed. He flies blindly for hours across the Martian landscape, eventually realizing he has missed Helium and is lost over an unfamiliar, desolate territory.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian Sky / Desolate landscapes (Dead Sea Bottom/Unidentified Cities).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief (survival) $\rightarrow$ Dread/Isolation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups on broken instruments, wide sweeping shots of the desolate landscape to emphasize scale and loneliness, medium shot of Carter's realization.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Time of day (passage of hours), state of the damaged air machine, direction of travel vs. destination.

# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Action climax and demonstration of Carter's superhuman abilities.

**Scene Summary:** While on a solo flight, Carter witnesses three Green Warriors attacking a Zodangan air scout (a relative of the Jeddak). Carter intervenes in the aerial combat, killing the warriors and saving the scout.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Green Warriors, Zodangan Scout (Cousin).

**Participating Environments:** The Martian sky/atmosphere above Zodanga.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Observation $\rightarrow$ High-stakes Action $\rightarrow$ Triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Dynamic aerial combat shots; Fast-paced cutting; Close-ups of the struggle and the kill.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Flight trajectories; Damage to air vessels; Position of the Green Warriors relative to the scout.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Montage/Training sequence to establish Carter's new identity and skill set.

**Scene Summary:** Under the alias "John Carter," facilitated by Kantos Kan, Carter undergoes training as a Zodangan air scout. He spends several days mastering Martian aviation and the mechanics of the eighth-ray propulsion systems.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Kantos Kan (mentor/facilitator).

**Participating Environments:** Zodangan naval training grounds; Air vessels.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Focus $\rightarrow$ Competence/Mastery.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Action montage shots; Close-ups of machinery and controls; POV shots from the air vessels.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Progression of Carter's physical fatigue; Weather/sky conditions across the training days.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the political tension and Dejah Thoris's character through her plea for peace.

**Scene Summary:** Carter observes a trial in the Thark audience chamber. Dejah Thoris defends the Heliumites, arguing for their role in maintaining the atmosphere and pleading for amity. Her words momentarily sway the council, including Tars Tarkas.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tars Tarkas, Lorquas Ptomel, Thark Council members.

**Participating Environments:** The Thark Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From political tension to a moment of profound, fragile empathy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the council; medium close-ups of Dejah Thoris (heroic/graceful); reaction shots from Tars Tarkas and Carter.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The seating arrangement of the Thark leaders; the positioning of Dejah Thoris as a prisoner.

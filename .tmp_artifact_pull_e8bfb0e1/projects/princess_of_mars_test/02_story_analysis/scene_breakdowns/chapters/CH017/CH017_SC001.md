# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To execute the primary rescue mission and establish the stakes of Dejah Thoris's captivity.

**Scene Summary:** The narrator infiltrates the city of Thark, witnessing Tal Hajus's cruelty toward Dejah Thoris. He intervenes by knocking the Jeddak unconscious and facilitating a daring escape with Dejah Thoris and Sola.

**Participating Characters:** The Narrator, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Tal Hajus, Tars Tarkas (observer).

**Participating Environments:** The City of Thark (Jeddak's apartments and circular hall).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension/Terror $\rightarrow$ Action/Relief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Low-angle shots of the Jeddak to emphasize menace.
- Close-ups on Dejah Thoris's expression of dignity under duress.
- Handheld/kinetic movement during the physical confrontation and escape.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The lighting of the Thark apartments; the positioning of the characters relative to Tal Hajus before the blow is struck.

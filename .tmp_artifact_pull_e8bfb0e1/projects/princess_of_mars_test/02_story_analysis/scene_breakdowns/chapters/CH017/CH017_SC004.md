**Scene Summary:** The narrator attempts a desperate defense against an overwhelming force of Thark warriors. He is eventually overwhelmed and beaten into unconsciousness.

- Shaky cam to simulate the violence of the beating.
- Fade to black as the narrator loses consciousness.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The number of warriors appearing; the physical state of the narrator's rifle/equipment.

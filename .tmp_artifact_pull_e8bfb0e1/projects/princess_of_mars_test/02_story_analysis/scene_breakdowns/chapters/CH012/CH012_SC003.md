# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To transition Carter from a prisoner to a chieftain with physical territory.

**Scene Summary:** Tars Tarkas approaches Carter and assists him in selecting quarters within a large building near Sola and the other prisoners. Carter makes a strategic choice, selecting a room on the third floor to maintain proximity to Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas.

**Participating Environments:** The Plaza/Thark City; various multi-story buildings.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension/paranoia to pragmatic planning and tactical positioning.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Two-shots of Carter and Tars Tarkas walking/discussing.
* Establishing shots of the building and the view from different floors.
* Point-of-view (POV) shots of Carter looking out from the third floor toward his goal.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The height/floor level selected; the layout of the buildings relative to the prisoners' locations.

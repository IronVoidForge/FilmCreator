# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To show Carter establishing his new base of operations and managing his resources.

**Scene Summary:** Carter receives his spoils of war—weapons, silks, furs, and food—delivered by the retinues of the chieftains he defeated. He organizes these people into a functional unit to manage his new quarters, effectively setting up his command center.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, various Tharkian retainues/servants.

**Participating Environments:** Carter's New Quarters (Third-floor room overlooking the central court).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From chaos/arrival to organized authority and domestic establishment.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Montage of goods being brought in (silks, weapons, food).
* Wide shots of the room filling up.
* Medium shots of Carter directing his new subordinates.
* Establishing shot of the overgrown central court with fountains/statuary.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The accumulation of items in the room; the specific types of goods being moved; the number of people present in the unit.

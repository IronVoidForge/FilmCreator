# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce external tension and establish the "unseen" horror through others' reactions.

**Scene Summary:** A group of Apache warriors approaches the cave entrance. They are suddenly terrified by a low, unnatural moaning sound emanating from the darkness. In their panicked flight, one warrior falls from the cliffside.

**Participating Characters:**
- Apache Warriors (Group)
- The Protagonist (Unseen/Passive observer)

**Participating Environments:**
- Cave Entrance / Rocky Gorge (Night, starlight)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Bravery/Curiosity to Primal Terror and Chaos.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the gorge/cliff
- Medium shots of warriors reacting to sound
- Handheld/Shaky cam for the panic and fall

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting from starlight; the timing of the moaning sound relative to character movement.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** The supernatural reveal and the "New Reality" establishment.

**Scene Summary:** Having broken free, the protagonist stands up only to realize he is naked. He looks down to see his own physical body—clothed and armed—lying lifeless on the cave floor. He realizes he has undergone a metaphysical metamorphosis.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist (New form/Spirit)
- The Protagonist's Body (Lifeless shell)

**Participating Environments:**
- Interior Cave

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion to Existential Horror and Shock.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Reveal shots (Looking down at the body)
- High-angle shots of the protagonist standing over his corpse
- Disorienting camera movements

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The exact placement of the "dead" body relative to the "new" protagonist; clothing/weapon details on the dead body.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Build tension and transition from peaceful prospecting to imminent danger.

**Scene Summary:** After Powell departs, Carter observes suspicious movements in the valley. He tracks what he believes is an Apache ambush, discovering fresh pony tracks that indicate a large presence nearby.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** The Arizona hills; the trail leading toward the plateau.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From solitude/peace to alertness and growing dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** POV shots of the valley; low-angle tracking shots of Carter on horseback; close-ups of tracks in the dirt.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Horse movement/direction; lighting changes as time passes during the pursuit.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the inciting incident (the gold discovery) and the initial character dynamic/plan.

**Scene Summary:** In the Arizona hills, 1865, John Carter and James Powell discover a massive gold-bearing quartz vein. They agree on a plan: Powell will head back to civilization for machinery while Carter stays to guard the claim.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Captain James K. Powell.

**Participating Environments:** Remote Arizona hills/mountains; the gold-bearing quartz vein site.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From hard labor/prospecting to sudden euphoria and ambitious planning.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the rugged landscape; medium close-ups of the men's faces reacting to the discovery; insert shots of the gold vein.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical appearance of the gold vein; the specific tools/gear being used by the prospectors.

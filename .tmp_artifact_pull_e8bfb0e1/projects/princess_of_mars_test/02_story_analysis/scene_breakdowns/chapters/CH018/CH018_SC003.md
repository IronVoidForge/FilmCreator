# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To show the scale of the threat and transition the protagonist to a new location.

**Scene Summary:** The massive Warhoon horde, numbering ten thousand, begins their retreat toward the city of Warhoon following the duel.

**Participating Characters:** Dak Kova (leading), Warhoon Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Martian plains; approaching the city of Warhoon.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Momentum/Inevitability.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme wide shots (aerial or high angle) to show the scale of the ten thousand warriors; tracking shots following the march.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Direction of travel; lighting changes as they approach the city.

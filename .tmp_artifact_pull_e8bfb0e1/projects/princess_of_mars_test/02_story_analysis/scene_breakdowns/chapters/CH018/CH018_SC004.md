# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To depict the psychological deterioration of the protagonist through sensory deprivation.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist is thrown into a pitch-black subterranean dungeon in Warhoon. He endures weeks or months of isolation, darkness, and terror from unseen things.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist.

**Participating Environments:** Subterranean dungeon (pitch black).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Despair $\rightarrow$ Near-Madness.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Very low-key lighting; extreme close-ups on the protagonist's face to catch minimal light; use of sound design to compensate for visual darkness.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Protagonist's physical appearance (weight loss, hair growth/unkemptness) over the passage of time.

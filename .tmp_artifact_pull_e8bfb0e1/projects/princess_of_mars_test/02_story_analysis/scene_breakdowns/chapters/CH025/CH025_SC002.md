# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Provide the dramatic inciting incident of the chapter via a high-impact entrance.

**Scene Summary:** Inside the audience chamber, as Dejah Thoris is being bound to Sab Than with golden chains, Carter smashes through a window and leaps into the hall, shattering the ceremonial chain.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sab Than, Than Kosis, Zodangan Nobility/Guards.

**Participating Environments:** Throne Room / Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Solemnity $\rightarrow$ Shock/Chaos.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Slow-motion window smash; wide shot of the hall to show scale; reaction close-ups (Dejah's shock, Sab Than's anger); low angle on Carter's landing.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The state of the golden chains (broken), glass shards, Dejah Thoris's ceremonial attire.

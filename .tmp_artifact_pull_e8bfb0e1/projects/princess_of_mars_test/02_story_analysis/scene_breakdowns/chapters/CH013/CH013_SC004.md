# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To deepen the romantic bond between Carter and Dejah Thoris and establish their emotional stakes.

**Scene Summary:** Carter and Dejah walk through the city at night under the two moons. They discuss war and their feelings. Dejah uses the term "my chieftain," hinting at a deeper connection Carter doesn't yet fully grasp. The scene ends with Carter realizing the depth of his love for her.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** Martian city streets/walkways under two moons.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From intellectual discussion to profound romantic realization.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots establishing the dual moons and the night atmosphere.
- Two-shots of the couple walking.
- Extreme close-ups on eyes and lips during the intimate dialogue.
- Soft, low-light lighting setups.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting/moonlight direction; the specific timing of the "chieftain" dialogue; Carter's emotional expression at the conclusion.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce conflict regarding Dejah Thoris's treatment and reveal the social friction within the retinue.

**Scene Summary:** Carter meets Dejah Thoris and Sola in the plaza. He discovers that the older women of the Thark retinue have been sabotaging Dejah, forcing her into hard labor with radium explosives due to jealousy of her status.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Jealous Retinue Women (background/implied).

**Participating Environments:** The city plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From reunion/joy to indignation/protective anger.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of the meeting between Carter and Dejah.
- Close-ups on Dejah's weary expression or hands (implying labor).
- Over-the-shoulder shots during the revelation of the mistreatment.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Dejah Thoris's physical appearance (signs of fatigue/labor); the proximity of Sola to Dejah.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the macabre elements of his arrival and establish the "Earth" setting's grit.

**Scene Summary:** Carter explores the cave further, discovering the mummified remains of an old woman engaged in a ritualistic task with green powder near a charcoal burner. He notices hanging human skeletons, heightening the sense of dread.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter
- The Mummified Woman (Static/Deceased)

**Participating Environments:**
- Dark Arizona Cave (Interior)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Horror/Dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- POV shots of the skeletons and the mummy.
- Medium shots of Carter's reaction.
- Close-ups of the green powder and charcoal burner.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Position of the mummified woman relative to Carter.
- Lighting from the charcoal burner.

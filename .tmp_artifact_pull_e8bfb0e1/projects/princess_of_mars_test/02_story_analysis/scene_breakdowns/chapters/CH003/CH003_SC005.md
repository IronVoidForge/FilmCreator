# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Establish a diplomatic connection and transition the protagonist to a new location.

**Scene Summary:** The Leader dismounts unarmed and offers a metal armlet as a peace gesture. The protagonist accepts, signaling friendship. He is then hoisted onto a Martian mount and taken away toward the distant hills.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist; The Leader; Martian Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The basin; transitioning toward the distant hills.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Relief $\rightarrow$ Acceptance/Wonder.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-ups of the hand-off (the armlet).
- Medium shots of the protagonist being hoisted onto the mount.
- Long shots of the cavalcade moving away toward the horizon.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The presence of the armlet on the protagonist's arm; the direction of travel for the cavalry.

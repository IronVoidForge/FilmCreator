# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the Martian threat and establish their physical scale and weaponry.

**Scene Summary:** A party of twenty Martian warriors on eight-legged mounts approaches. The leader threatens the protagonist with a forty-foot spear, creating an immediate life-or-death tension.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist; Martian Warriors (including the Leader).

**Participating Environments:** The mossy basin near the Incubator.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Dread $\rightarrow$ Terror.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-angle shots to emphasize the 15-foot height of the Martians.
- Wide shots showing the scale of the cavalry and their eight-legged mounts.
- Close-ups of the long spears and radium rifles.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The number of warriors; the positioning of the mounts relative to the protagonist.

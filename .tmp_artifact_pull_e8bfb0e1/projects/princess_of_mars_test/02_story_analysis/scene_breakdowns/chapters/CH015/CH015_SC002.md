# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale and atmosphere of the journey across the mossy sea bottom.

**Scene Summary:** The massive caravan, consisting of two hundred and fifty chariots, moves in eerie silence across the trackless, ochre-colored moss of the sea bottom. The setting emphasizes the isolation and the strange, silent nature of Martian travel.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Sola, (The Caravan/Retinue).

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Sea Bottom.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe/Isolation $\rightarrow$ Introspection.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme wide shots of the massive caravan moving across the vast moss expanse.
- Low-angle tracking shots of chariot wheels on the moss.
- Wide establishing shots showing the lack of spoor or tracks.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The visual texture and color of the ochre moss; the rhythmic movement of the large caravan.

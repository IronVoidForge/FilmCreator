# Scene SC005 - Falling Action

## Purpose
Show Carter's escape and transition to resolution; shift from combat to exhaustion.

## Summary
Carter escapes pursuit by taking wrong trail into a defile. He reaches cave mouth at dawn. The action winds down as he finds shelter.

## Participating Characters
- Carter

## Participating Environments
- Narrow gorge/defile
- Cave mouth (4ft high, 3-4ft wide)
- Arizona hills at dawn

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Combat adrenaline → Physical exhaustion

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Tracking shots through defile
- Wide shots of cave mouth entrance
- Dawn lighting transitions
- Medium shots showing Carter's physical state

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Wrong trail markers for spatial continuity
- Cave mouth dimensions (4ft high, 3-4ft wide)
- Dawn lighting consistency
- Defile path consistency

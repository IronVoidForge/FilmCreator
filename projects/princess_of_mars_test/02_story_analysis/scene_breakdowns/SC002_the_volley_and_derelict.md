# Scene 2: The Volley and Derelict

**Location:** Open Ground Valley / City Buildings Window
**Action:** Martians fire volley disabling sighting apparatus/guns. Ships return fire ineffectually. Fleet retreats, one ship damaged and drifting.
**Visuals:** Warriors loot disabled ship (arms, ammunition, food, water). Burn vessel to prevent use. Carter watches burning warship drift into distance.
**Details:** Warriors have objective points for fire (wireless, sighting, crew). Last warrior throws missile causing flame before leaving.

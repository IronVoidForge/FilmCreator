# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the mission objective and the split-path plan between Carter and Kantos Kan.

**Scene Summary:** Following the news of Dejah Thoris's betrothal to Sab Than, John Carter meets secretly with Kantos Kan. They agree on a dual strategy: Kantos Kan will infiltrate the Zodanga palace via a secret tower entrance to assassinate Sab Than, while Carter attempts to fly to Helium.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Kantos Kan.

**Participating Environments:** A secluded location near Zodanga.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Determined Resolve.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups on faces (intensity/secrecy), medium shots for conspiratorial gestures, wide shot to establish the isolation of their meeting.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels (night/shadows), character positioning relative to each other during the planning.

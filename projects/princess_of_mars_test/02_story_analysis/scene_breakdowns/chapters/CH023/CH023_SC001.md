# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the alliance and the driving motivation (Dejah Thoris's plight).

**Scene Summary:** John Carter meets secretly with Kantos Kan to deliver the news of Dejah Thoris's engagement to Sab Than. Devastated, Kantos Kan vows to assassinate the Zodangan ruler via a secret entrance, leading to a pact where Kan strikes the palace while Carter heads for Helium.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Kantos Kan.

**Participating Environments:** Kantos Kan's private quarters (Zodanga).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension/secrecy to righteous fury and resolve.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Tight medium shots for intimate dialogue.
- Close-ups on facial expressions (grief, determination).
- Low-key lighting to emphasize the clandestine nature of the meeting.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Character positioning relative to the secret pact/vow; props used during the planning.

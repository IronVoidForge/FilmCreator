# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the inciting incident of the chapter (the news of Dejah Thoris's engagement) and set the characters' new objectives.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter meets with Kantos Kan in secret to deliver the devastating news that Dejah Thoris is engaged to Sab Than. Devastated, Kantos Kan vows to assassinate the usurper. The two men coordinate their next moves: Kantos Kan will infiltrate the palace, and Carter will attempt to fly back to Helium.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Kantos Kan.

**Participating Environments:** Guarded quarters in Zodanga.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Shock/Despair $\rightarrow$ Determined Resolve.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups on character reactions (shock/grief), medium shots for the conspiratorial dialogue, low-key lighting to emphasize secrecy.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Character positioning during the revelation; props used in the planning phase.

# Scene 001: Infiltration & Warning

## Scene Purpose
Establish the critical plot development where Dejah Thoris' promise to Sab Than threatens the Helium-Zodanga alliance. Create tension through Carter's stealth entry and Kan's emotional reaction.

## Scene Summary
Carter enters Kantos Kan's private room in Zodanga Palace without detection. He delivers urgent news that Dejah Thoris has promised her hand to Sab Than. Kan reacts with dismay, declaring the alliance impossible for Helium lovers. The scene ends with Kan's pledge to act against Sab Than.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (protagonist, stealth operative)
- Kantos Kan (antagonist/ally, palace leader)

## Participating Environments
- Zodanga Palace Interior (Kantos Kan's private room)
- Private quarters with ornate furnishings

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From tension and secrecy (Carter's undetected entry) to dismay and determination (Kan's reaction). The scene moves from covert information delivery to overt emotional commitment.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Close-ups on character faces during dialogue exchanges
- Medium shots showing room layout and stealth positioning
- Over-the-shoulder shots for intimate conversation framing
- Wide establishing shots of palace interior architecture

- Room furnishings and lighting conditions stay stable throughout
- Character positions during dialogue exchanges match across takes

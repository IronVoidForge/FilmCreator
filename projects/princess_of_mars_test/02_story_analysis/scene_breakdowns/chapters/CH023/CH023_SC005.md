# Scene 005: Pursuit & Crash

## Scene Purpose
Create high-stakes action sequence with visual spectacle. Establish the threat from air scouts and demonstrate consequences of Carter's speed advantage being overcome.

## Scene Summary
Zodanga air scouts pursue them. Carter uses Helium gearing tricks to increase speed but is hit by a shot from a cruiser. The craft suffers concussion damage and plunges downward. The scene ends with the machine in freefall toward ground recovery zone.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (protagonist, pilot)
- Air Scouts (antagonists, pursuers)
- Cruiser Pilot (antagonist, shooter)

## Participating Environments
- Sky Approaching Palace Route
- Pursuit corridor between machines and scouts
- Crash zone near ground recovery area

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From confident speed advantage to shock and danger. The scene moves from tactical superiority to vulnerability and crisis management.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots showing pursuit formation and relative positions
- Dynamic tracking shots following Carter's machine during evasion
- Close-ups on Carter's reaction during hit moment
- Low-angle shots looking up at pursuing machines
- Slow-motion sequences during crash impact
- Sky shots establishing direction of fall

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Machine damage must progress consistently across sequence
- Pursuit formation and relative speeds remain stable
- Hit location and timing match across takes
- Crash trajectory and angle stay consistent
- Any visual markers on pursuing machines remain consistent

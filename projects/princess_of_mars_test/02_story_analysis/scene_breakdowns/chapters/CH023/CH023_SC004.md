# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Establish a sense of isolation and the "ticking clock" of being lost.

**Scene Summary:** After six hours of flight, Carter realizes he is lost in the vastness of Mars. He passes several cities but cannot find Helium because he lacks the visual landmark of its twin scarlet and yellow towers.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** High altitude/Cockpit; passing over various Martian cities.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From exhaustion to growing dread and uncertainty.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme wide shots showing the vast, lonely landscape below.
- Close-ups on Carter's weary face.
- Looking out through the cockpit glass at unrecognizable landmarks.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Time elapsed (6 hours); visual appearance of the passing cities vs. the missing Helium towers.

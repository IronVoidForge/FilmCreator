# SC004

**Scene Summary:** After the crash, Carter is left flying a damaged machine without navigation tools. He spends six hours flying over the desolate dead sea bottom, realizing he is lost as the cities he passes do not match the architecture of Helium.

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the aftermath of the action and transition into a state of isolation and uncertainty.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian Sky (above the Dead Sea Bottom).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Survival Instinct $\rightarrow$ Despair/Isolation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide, lonely shots of the machine against a vast landscape; close-ups on Carter's fatigued face; POV looking down at the desolate sea bottom and distant cities.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Time progression (lighting changes over 6 hours); damage state of the air machine; visual distinction between Heliumite architecture and other Martian cities.

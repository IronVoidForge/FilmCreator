# Scene 004: Machine Retrieval & Takeoff

## Scene Purpose
Transition from stealth infiltration to active departure. Establish the dual-machine setup and create visual spectacle through takeoff sequence.

## Scene Summary
Carter retrieves his machine and Kan's machine from the sheds. They take off together towards the palace route. The scene concludes with both machines airborne and heading toward their respective destinations.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (protagonist, pilot)
- Kantos Kan (antagonist/ally, co-pilot)

## Participating Environments
- Machine Sheds (Zodanga Palace grounds)
- Takeoff zone near palace route
- Sky approaching departure point

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From stealth and tension to action and movement. The scene shifts from covert operations to overt departure and mission execution.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots showing shed layout and machine positions
- Medium shots during retrieval process
- Dynamic tracking shots following takeoff sequence
- Low-angle shots looking up at departing machines
- Sky shots establishing direction of travel

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Machine designs must remain consistent across all shots
- Shed interior details stay stable throughout retrieval sequence
- Takeoff trajectory and speed match across takes
- Any visual markers on machines remain consistent
- Departure timing aligns with subsequent pursuit scene

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Provide high-octane action and escalate the stakes through combat.

**Scene Summary:** Carter and Kantos Kan depart on machines, but are intercepted by a Zodangan patrol. A high-speed aerial chase ensues. During the dogfight, a projectile hits Carter's craft, causing a crash/plunge that destroys his navigation instruments (compass and speedometer).

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Kantos Kan, Zodangan Patrol pilots.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian sky; above the Zodangan landscape.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From adrenaline-fueled combat to sudden shock and disorientation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide aerial shots of the chase.
- Shaky cam/dynamic movement inside the cockpit.
- Close-ups of the damaged instruments (compass/speedometer).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Flight paths; damage patterns on Carter's machine; the timing of the projectile impact.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To provide the chapter's primary action set-piece and escalate the physical stakes.

**Scene Summary:** As Carter and Kantos Kan attempt to depart, they are intercepted by a Zodangan air patrol. A high-speed chase ensues through the Martian sky. Carter uses Heliumite speed-gearing to outrun them, but his machine is struck by a projectile from a cruiser, leading to a crash.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Kantos Kan, Zodangan Air Patrol.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian Sky.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Adrenaline/Excitement $\rightarrow$ Panic/Chaos.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide aerial shots of the chase; cockpit POV (shaky cam); tracking shots of the air machines; VFX-heavy shots of the projectile impact.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Flight paths and relative positions of aircraft; damage to the air machine post-impact.

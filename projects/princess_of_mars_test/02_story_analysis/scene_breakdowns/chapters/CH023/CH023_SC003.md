# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Provide high-octane action and the primary conflict of the chapter.

**Scene Summary:** As Carter and Kantos Kan attempt to depart, Zodangan air patrols intercept them. Kantos Kan dives into darkness to evade capture, leaving Carter to engage in a high-speed aerial chase. Carter uses Heliumite gearing to increase speed but is struck by a projectile from a cruiser, causing his machine to fail.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Kantos Kan, Zodangan Air Patrols.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian Sky above Zodanga.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** High Tension $\rightarrow$ Chaos/Panic.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Dynamic tracking shots (chase), wide aerial vistas, shaky cam/handheld for the impact and near-fatal plunge, close-ups of Carter's frantic piloting.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Flight paths of machines, speed/velocity of the chase, direction of projectile fire.

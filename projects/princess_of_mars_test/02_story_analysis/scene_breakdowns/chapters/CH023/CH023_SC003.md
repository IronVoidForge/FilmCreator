# Scene 003: Roof Access

## Scene Purpose
Demonstrate Carter's physical capabilities and stealth skills. Create visual spectacle through the climbing sequence while maintaining tension about potential fall consequences.

## Scene Summary
Carter climbs the barracks building using ornamental ledges. He swings up to the roof using a leather strap with a hook, risking a fall from a thousand feet. He neutralizes the sentry by throwing him over the edge and binding him there. The scene ends with Carter successfully positioned on the roof.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (protagonist, stealth operative)
- Sentry (antagonist, guard to be neutralized)

## Participating Environments
- Barracks Building Exterior (Zodanga Palace grounds)
- Ornamental ledges and architectural features
- Roof access point with sentry position

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From tension during climbing sequence to relief upon successful positioning. The scene moves from physical risk to tactical success.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots showing building height and climbing approach
- Close-ups on hands gripping ledges and strap mechanics
- Dynamic tracking shots following Carter's upward movement
- High-angle shots from rooftop looking down at sentry position
- Slow-motion sequences during critical climbing moments

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Ornamental ledge positions must match across takes
- Strap hook mechanism shown consistently when referenced
- Sentry's neutralization method remains consistent
- Building height and perspective stay stable throughout sequence
- Any environmental details (lighting, shadows) remain consistent

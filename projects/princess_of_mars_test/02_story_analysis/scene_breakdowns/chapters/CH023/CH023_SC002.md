# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Execute the high-stakes theft of the air machines.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter performs a dangerous climb up the exterior of the massive Zodangan barracks. He uses a grappling hook to reach the roof, subdues a sentry, and hangs him over the edge to buy time. He successfully retrieves two air machines from the machine sheds.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Zodangan Sentry.

**Participating Environments:** The exterior/roof of the massive Zodangan Barracks (1,000 feet high).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Adrenaline-fueled Success.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme wide shots (scale of the building), POV shots (the climb), medium action shots (the struggle with the sentry), close-ups on equipment (grappling hook, air machines).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Height/Scale perception, direction of the wind, position of the incapacitated sentry.

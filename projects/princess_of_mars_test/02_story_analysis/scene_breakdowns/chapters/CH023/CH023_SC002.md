# Scene 002: The Plan

## Scene Purpose
Transition from emotional reaction to concrete action planning. Establish the dual-path strategy where both characters take different routes toward their objectives.

## Scene Summary
Kan pledges his sword to kill Sab Than for Dejah Thoris and Helium's sake. Carter agrees to proceed to Helium while Kan attempts the assassination at the palace. The scene concludes with clear departure instructions and mutual commitment to their respective missions.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (protagonist, heading to Helium)
- Kantos Kan (antagonist/ally, attempting assassination)

## Participating Environments
- Zodanga Palace Interior (planning area)
- Transition space for departure preparation

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From emotional dismay (previous scene) to determined resolve. The scene shifts from reactive emotion to proactive planning and commitment.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Two-shot compositions showing character interaction during planning
- Close-ups on sword as symbolic prop during pledge moment
- Medium shots capturing departure preparation activities
- Overhead or high-angle shots showing spatial relationship between characters

- Character positions during departure instructions remain stable

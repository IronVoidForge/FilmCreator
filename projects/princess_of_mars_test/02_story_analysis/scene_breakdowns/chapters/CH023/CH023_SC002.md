# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Demonstrate Carter's physical prowess and tactical ingenuity.

**Scene Summary:** Carter performs a dangerous climb up the Zodangan barracks, uses a hook and strap to reach the roof, and subdues a sentry. He binds the guard and hangs him over the ledge to buy time before his escape.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Zodangan Sentry.

**Participating Environments:** Exterior walls of the Zodangan barracks; the rooftop.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From high-stakes physical exertion to calculated stealth/triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots for the scale of the climb.
- POV shots from Carter's perspective during the ascent.
- Handheld/dynamic movement during the struggle with the sentry.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The height of the climb; the placement and state of the leather strap and hook; the position of the bound sentry.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To demonstrate Carter's physical prowess and resourcefulness through a high-stakes infiltration.

**Scene Summary:** Carter performs a perilous climb up the thousand-foot Zodanga barracks using a grappling hook. He reaches the roof, neutralizes a sentry, and secures two air machines for his escape.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Zodangan Sentry.

**Participating Environments:** The exterior/roof of the Zodanga Barracks.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Relief/Success.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme wide shots to show scale/height, POV shots of the climb, tight action coverage of the sentry neutralization.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Grappling hook placement; height/altitude perception; lighting changes as he moves from shadow to roof.

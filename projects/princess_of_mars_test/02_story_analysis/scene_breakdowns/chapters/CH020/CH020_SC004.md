# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Provide a high-stakes climax and demonstrate the protagonist's unique abilities.

**Scene Summary:** The Old Man reveals his murderous intent to protect the factory's secrets. The protagonist uses his telepathic powers to manipulate the facility's thought-wave locks, allowing him and Woola to escape the facility.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, The Old Man, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** Interior of the Atmosphere Factory (escape corridors/locks).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Dread $\rightarrow$ Panic $\rightarrow$ Intense Focus/Action $\rightarrow$ Relief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Fast-paced action coverage; Close-ups of the protagonist's intense concentration (telepathy); Handheld shots during the escape.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The timing of the door/lock mechanisms responding to thought-waves.

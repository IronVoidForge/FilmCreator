# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Provide respite and introduce the Red Martian culture/hospitality.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist and Woola encounter a group of hospitable Red Martian government officers at a farm. They are fed and given rest.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Woola, The Ptor Family (Red Martian Officers).

**Participating Environments:** The Ptor Farm (Raised homes on metal shafts).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Safety/Comfort.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Warmly lit interior shots; medium shots of the meal/interaction; wide shots showing the unique architecture of the raised farm.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Food/props on the table; cleanliness levels of the protagonist (transitioning from wild to guest).

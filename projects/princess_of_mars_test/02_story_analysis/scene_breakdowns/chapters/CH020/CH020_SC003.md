# Scene SC003: Arrival at Zodanga

## Scene Purpose
Show the aftermath of escape and Carter's integration into Red Martian society through preparation and acceptance.

## Scene Summary
Carter and Woola reach Zodanga outskirts where Red Martians take them in. Carter is prepared with reddish oil anointing, hair cut to match fashion, and metal/ornaments renewed in Zodangan gentleman style. He receives money and a domestic bull-thoat for saddle purposes.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (now prepared Red Martian)
- Woola (companion)
- Red Martians (accepting newcomers)

## Participating Environments
- Zodanga outskirts (Red Martian territory)
- Preparation area (anointing and grooming space)
- Bull-thoat saddle area

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Escape relief → Acceptance and preparation

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of Zodanga outskirts landscape
- Medium shots of anointing process with reddish oil
- Close-ups on hair cutting to fashion standards
- Over-the-shoulder shots showing metal/ornament renewal
- Tracking shot following bull-thoat presentation

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Carter's Red Man appearance transformation (oil, hair cut)
- Bull-thoat size and gentleness must be clear
- Money presentation details
- Old man's suspicious attitude should remain visible

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** High-stakes escape using Carter's evolving powers.

**Scene Summary:** Realizing the danger, Carter uses his telepathic abilities to intercept the nine thought-waves required to operate the factory doors, allowing him and Woola to flee the facility.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Woola, The Old Man (off-screen/pursuing).

**Participating Environments:** Atmosphere Factory (Doorway/Exit mechanism).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Relief/Exertion.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Tight close-ups on Carter's eyes/face during telepathic effort; Dynamic movement shots of the escape; Wide shots of the massive doors opening.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The timing of the door operation relative to Carter's mental state.

# Scene SC001: Meeting at Atmosphere Factory

## Scene Purpose
Establish Carter's arrival at the factory and introduce the old man's role in atmosphere production while building tension about his intentions.

## Scene Summary
Carter meets Woola at the massive Atmosphere Factory after escaping Warhoons and surviving wild beast attacks. The old man explains the factory produces atmosphere via the ninth ray stored in reservoirs, warns Carter against Helium due to ongoing war, and reveals his suspicious nature.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (Red Man protagonist)
- Woola (loyal companion, half-starved)
- Old Man (factory operator, gold collar with diamond-studded ornament)

## Participating Environments
- Atmosphere Factory interior (walls 150 feet thick, radium pumps, telepathic door locks)
- Zodanga outskirts (visible from factory)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Suspicion and warning → Realization of danger

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide establishing shots of factory exterior
- Medium two-shots of Carter and Woola
- Close-ups on old man's gold collar and diamond-studded ornament
- Over-the-shoulder shots during explanation dialogue

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Woola's half-starved state must remain consistent
- Old man's gold collar and diamond-studded ornament placement
- Factory interior details (radium pumps, telepathic door locks)
- Carter's Red Man appearance before anointing

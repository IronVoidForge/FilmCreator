# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's physical struggle, isolation, and the danger of the Martian environment.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist undergoes a grueling two-week trek northwest through the wilderness. He survives on meager vegetable milk and faces several beast attacks, including one where Woola must intervene to save him.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian Wilderness (desolate, dangerous terrain).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation/Exhaustion $\rightarrow$ Terror (during beast attacks) $\rightarrow$ Relief/Survival.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the vast landscape; Close-ups of the protagonist's exhaustion and hunger; Action coverage for the beast attack/Woola's intervention.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Protagonist's level of physical deterioration (dirt, weight loss); Woola's condition.

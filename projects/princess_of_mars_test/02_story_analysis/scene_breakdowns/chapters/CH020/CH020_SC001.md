# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale of the Atmosphere Factory and the protagonist's arrival.

**Scene Summary:** After weeks of wilderness survival, the protagonist and Woola arrive at a massive, four-square-mile concrete structure. He is scanned by an invisible host using advanced technology before being admitted into the facility.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Woola, Invisible Host (Voice/Tech).

**Participating Environments:** Barsoomian Wilderness, Atmosphere Factory Entrance.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion $\rightarrow$ Awe/Intrigue.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the massive factory; close-ups of the protagonist's weary face and Woola; POV shots of the scanning technology.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Protagonist's level of physical grime/exhaustion; Woola's positioning relative to the protagonist.

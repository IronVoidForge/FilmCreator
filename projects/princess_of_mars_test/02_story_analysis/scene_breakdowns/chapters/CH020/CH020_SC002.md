# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the scale of Martian technology and transition from survival to mystery.

**Scene Summary:** After fifteen days of travel, the protagonist discovers a massive, four-square-mile atmosphere factory. He successfully navigates the automated entry systems to enter the facility.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist.

**Participating Environments:** Exterior of the Atmosphere Factory; Automated Entryway.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Fatigue $\rightarrow$ Awe/Wonder.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme wide shots (establishing scale); Low-angle shots of the factory walls; Medium shots of the automated doors operating.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Scale of the machinery relative to the character.

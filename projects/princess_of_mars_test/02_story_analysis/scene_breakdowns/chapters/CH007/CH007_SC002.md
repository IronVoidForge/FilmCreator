# Scene SC002: Incubator Hatching Ceremony

## Scene Purpose
Reveal the culmination of the five-year incubation period and showcase young Martian hatchlings, establishing their physical characteristics and community significance.

## Scene Summary
After the five-year period elapses, Incubator eggs begin hatching. Young Martians emerge at 3-4 feet tall. Tars Tarkas (chieftain) explains the hatching process to Lorquas Ptomel Jed (principal chieftain). Narrator performs an entertaining leap over parked chariots to demonstrate his growing comfort with Martian customs and physical capabilities.

## Participating Characters
- Sola (narrator's companion)
- Narrator (protagonist)
- Tars Tarkas (chieftain)
- Lorquas Ptomel Jed (principal chieftain)
- Warriors (200+ members of procession)
- Women/Children (forming gauntlet walls)

## Participating Environments
- Incubator enclosure (primary location)
- Plaza (surrounding area with chariots)
- Hills (nearby terrain)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Awe and wonder → Understanding of cultural significance → Growing comfort with Martian environment

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Close-up shots of hatching eggs cracking open
- Medium shots of young Martians emerging from incubator
- Two-shot composition of Tars Tarkas and Lorquas Ptomel explaining hatching
- Wide shot of narrator's leap over chariots
- Tracking shots following hatchlings moving through enclosure

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities

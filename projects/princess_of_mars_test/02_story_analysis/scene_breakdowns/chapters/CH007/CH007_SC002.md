# SC002 - The Gauntlet Release

## Scene Purpose
Reveal the harsh Martian social system through the gauntlet ceremony and demonstrate the community's approach to offspring selection and communal upbringing.

## Scene Summary
The gauntlet ceremony releases the newly hatched Martians into the community, with women and children capturing them for communal upbringing. This scene exposes the lack of parental bonds and survival-focused social structure.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (observer)
- Sola (facilitator)
- Community women
- Community children

## Participating Environments
- Gauntlet ceremony area
- Community gathering space
- Outdoor capture zones

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Awe → Realization of harsh social reality → Understanding of survival priorities

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of gauntlet release sequence
- Medium shots of women and children capturing young Martians
- Close-ups of narrator's reactions to the social system
- Group shots showing community dynamics

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce key Martian leaders and demonstrate the protagonist's physical capability within their culture.

**Scene Summary:** Upon arriving at the incubator on the dead sea bottom, the protagonist meets Chieftain Lorquas Ptomel and Tars Tarkas. To prove his worth/impress the leaders, the protagonist performs a feat of athleticism by leaping over parked chariots. The hatching ceremony begins as the young Martians emerge.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Sola, Lorquas Ptomel, Tars Tarkas, Martian Youths/Women.

**Participating Environments:** The Dead Sea Bottom, The Incubator.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension to impressive display.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of the protagonist's leap; medium shots of the Chieftain and Tars Tarkas observing; wide shots of the incubator structure.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Height/scale of the chariots relative to the protagonist; timing of the leap; placement of the leaders.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Showcase Martian leadership and the protagonist's attempt to integrate through physical prowess.

**Scene Summary:** Upon arrival at the incubator, leaders Lorquas Ptomel and Tars Tarkas oversee the hatching process. The protagonist leaps over parked chariots to demonstrate strength/agility to the leaders. As the young Martians emerge, they are met by a ritualistic gauntlet of women and youths.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- Sola
- Tars Tarkas
- Lorquas Ptomel
- Young Martians (newly hatched)

**Participating Environments:**
- The Incubator (Dead Sea Bottom)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension and spectacle; the protagonist seeks respect through bravado.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-angle shots of the protagonist's leap to emphasize height/scale.
- Medium shots of the Martian leaders observing.
- Group shots of the ritualistic gauntlet.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The position of the chariots; the timing of the hatching relative to the protagonist's feat.

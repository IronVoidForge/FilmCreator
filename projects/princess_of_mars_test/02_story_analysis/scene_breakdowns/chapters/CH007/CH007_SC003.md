# Scene SC003: Child-Raising Gauntlet Ritual

## Scene Purpose
Demonstrate the communal child-rearing practices and establish the physical training methods used for young Martians, showing both the ritualistic and practical aspects of Martian parenting.

## Scene Summary
Women and children form two solid walls leading out through the chariots, creating a gauntlet path. Young Martians run the length of the gauntlet, being captured one at a time by women or older children positioned along the walls. The last young Martian in line captures the first to reach the end, demonstrating both physical agility and social hierarchy within the training system.

## Participating Characters
- Sola (narrator's companion)
- Narrator (protagonist)
- Women (forming gauntlet walls)
- Children (forming gauntlet walls)
- Warriors (200+ members of procession)
- Young Martians (hatchlings participating in ritual)

## Participating Environments
- Plaza (gauntlet formation area)
- Incubator enclosure (surrounding location)
- Hills (nearby terrain)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Observation of ritual → Understanding training methodology → Recognition of communal parenting structure

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shot establishing gauntlet wall formation through chariots
- Medium shots of young Martians running gauntlet length
- Over-the-shoulder shots from women/children perspective capturing young Martians
- Tracking shots following individual young Martian progression
- Low angle shots emphasizing physical agility and group dynamics

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities

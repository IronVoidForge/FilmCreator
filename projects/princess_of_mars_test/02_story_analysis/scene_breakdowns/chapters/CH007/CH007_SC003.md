# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Reveal the core cultural philosophy and the harshness of Martian society.

**Scene Summary:** The narrative details the communal, emotionless upbringing of the Martians. Following the ceremony, warriors move eggs from subterranean vaults to the incubator, which is then sealed for five years.

**Participating Characters:**
- Warriors
- Women/Youths (as part of the social fabric)

**Participating Environments:**
- The Incubator
- Subterranean Vaults

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Coldness and detachment; a sense of systemic brutality.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Montage or observational shots of the egg transport.
- Close-ups of the eggs/vaults to emphasize the biological/mechanical nature of their life cycle.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical handling of the eggs; lighting transitions from subterranean vaults to the sunlit incubator.

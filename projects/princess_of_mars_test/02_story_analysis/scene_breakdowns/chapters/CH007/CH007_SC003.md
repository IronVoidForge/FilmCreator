# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Reveal the cold, utilitarian, and Darwinian nature of Martian society.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist observes the ritualistic gauntlet where newly hatched Martians are captured by women and youths. He witnesses the communal nature of their upbringing and the chilling efficiency with which defective specimens are eliminated to ensure only warriors survive.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Sola, Newly Hatched Martians, Martian Women/Youths.

**Participating Environments:** The Dead Sea Bottom (near the incubator).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity to horror/disillusionment.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups of the hatching process; medium shots of the gauntlet; tight close-ups on the protagonist's reacting face; clinical/detached shots of the "culling" of defectives.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The movement pattern of the gauntlet; the physical appearance of the newly hatched Martians.

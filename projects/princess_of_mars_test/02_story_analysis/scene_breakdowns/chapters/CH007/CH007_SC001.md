# SC001 - The Incubator Ceremony Setup

## Scene Purpose
Establish the ceremonial atmosphere of Mars' child-raising ritual and introduce the narrator's first encounter with the hatching process.

## Scene Summary
The narrator is escorted by Sola to an incubator where young Martians are being hatched after five years of incubation. The scene captures the anticipation and solemnity of this community event.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (protagonist)
- Sola (escort/guide)

## Participating Environments
- Incubator facility on Mars
- Community ceremonial space

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Curiosity and reverence → Awe at the hatching process

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of incubator facility
- Medium shots of narrator following Sola
- Close-ups of hatching young Martians
- Over-the-shoulder perspectives during escort sequence

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Incubator interior design consistency
- Young Martian appearance and movement patterns
- Narrator's emotional progression throughout ceremony

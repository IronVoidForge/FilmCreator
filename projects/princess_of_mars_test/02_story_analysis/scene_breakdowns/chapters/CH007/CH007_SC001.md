# Scene SC001 - Opening Sequence & Procession Setup

## Scene Purpose
Establish narrator's telepathic development, show breakfast replica ritual, introduce plaza procession beginning

## Scene Summary
Narrator and Sola arrive at plaza with green men; breakfast shows exact replica of preceding day's meal; entire community watches harnessing of mastodonian animals to three-wheeled chariots

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (protagonist)
- Sola (mentor figure, telepathic tutor)
- Green Men (community members)
- Community Members (spectators)

## Participating Environments
- Plaza (gathering space)
- Incubator Facility Entrance (transition point)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Curiosity → Awe at scale of community ritual

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide establishing shots of plaza and procession setup
- Medium shots of breakfast replica showing exact meal duplication
- Over-the-shoulder perspectives from narrator's telepathic viewpoint
- Low angle shots emphasizing animal harnessing process

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Exact meal replica must match preceding day's meal precisely
- Green men positioning and community member reactions need consistent blocking
- Animal harnessing sequence requires clear visual progression
- Narrator's telepathic sensing ability should be visually indicated but not overused

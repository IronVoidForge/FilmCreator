# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale of Martian military might and transport the protagonist to the ritual site.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist travels with Sola and a massive military cavalcade consisting of two hundred decorated chariots drawn by enormous beasts across the landscape toward the dead sea bottom.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- Sola

**Participating Environments:**
- The Plaza/City (Departure)
- The Dead Sea Bottom (Approach)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe and intimidation at the sheer scale of the Martian military force.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide establishing shots of the massive chariot procession.
- Tracking shots alongside the protagonist and Sola.
- Close-ups of the enormous animals pulling the chariots.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Movement direction of the cavalcade; lighting consistency across the vast expanse of the dead sea bottom.

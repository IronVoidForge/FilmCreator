# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale of Martian military culture and the protagonist's integration into their society.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist travels with Sola and a massive military cavalcade consisting of two hundred decorated chariots drawn by enormous beasts across the landscape toward the dead sea bottom.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** The Plaza (departure), Martian Wilderness/Plains.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe to anticipation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide establishing shots of the massive procession; medium tracking shots of chariots and beasts; close-ups of the protagonist observing the scale.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Movement direction of the cavalcade; lighting consistency across the vast landscape; chariot/beast positioning.

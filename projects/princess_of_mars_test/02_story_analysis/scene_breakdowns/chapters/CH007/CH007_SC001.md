# Scene SC001: Procession Setup and Movement

## Scene Purpose
Establish the community's organized procession system and introduce narrator to Martian cultural practices around animal husbandry and public ceremony.

## Scene Summary
Sola escorts narrator from their quarters to the central plaza where the community is preparing for the incubator hatching ceremony. Warriors and women harness decorated three-wheeled chariots with Mastodonian draft animals (no bit/bridle). The procession moves in single file through the plaza, with warriors positioned at head and rear. Narrator is placed in an empty chariot and dragged along to the incubator location.

## Participating Characters
- Sola (narrator's companion)
- Narrator (protagonist)
- Warriors (200+ members of procession)
- Women/Children (forming gauntlet walls)
- Community members (harnessing animals)

## Participating Environments
- Plaza (central gathering space)
- Incubator enclosure (destination location)
- Hills (surrounding terrain)
- Dead Sea Bottom (nearby area)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Curiosity and observation → Integration into community ritual → Anticipation of ceremony

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide establishing shots of plaza and procession movement
- Medium shots of chariot harnessing details
- Tracking shots following single-file procession
- Over-the-shoulder shots from narrator's perspective
- Low angle shots emphasizing chariot decoration and animal size

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities

- Animal positioning and harness details

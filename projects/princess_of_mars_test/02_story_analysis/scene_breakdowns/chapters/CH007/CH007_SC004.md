# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Transition to the protagonist's personal development and establish his new social dynamic.

**Scene Summary:** The community returns home. The protagonist settles into life in the Dead City, where Sola provides intensive instruction in the Martian language and telepathy. He also begins a competitive relationship with the newly hatched male Martian who is being raised alongside him.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Sola, The Young Martian.

**Participating Environments:** The Dead City.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Routine to intellectual/telepathic focus.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of teaching sessions; close-ups on eyes (for telepathy); over-the-shoulder shots during language lessons; shots showing the interaction between the protagonist and the young Martian.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Progression of the protagonist's linguistic/telepathic ability; physical growth/interaction with the Young Martian.

# Scene SC004: Societal Explanation of Martian Parenting

## Scene Purpose
Provide crucial exposition about Martian child-rearing philosophy and practices, establishing the stark contrast between Martian and Earth parenting approaches while revealing cultural values around survival and community.

## Scene Summary
Narrator receives detailed explanation from Tars Tarkas about Martian child-rearing practices: weapons and language are taught from infancy, no parental love exists as children are communal property rather than individual possessions. Deformed or defective young are shot to maintain population quality. Birth rate is regulated to offset death on the dying planet. This scene establishes the harsh but logical survival philosophy underlying Martian society.

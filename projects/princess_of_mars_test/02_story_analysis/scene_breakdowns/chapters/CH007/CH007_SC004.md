# Scene SC004 - Capture Process & Child-Raising Education

## Scene Purpose
Detail capture system and explain child-raising philosophy from first year of life

## Scene Summary
Women/younger Martians form walls leading through chariots; little Martians scamper wild as deer, captured one at a time; education focus on talking and weapons from very first year

## Participating Characters
- Women Martians (capture team)
- Younger Martians (assistants in capture)
- Older Children (participate in capture)
- Council of Chieftains (examines yearly supply)

## Participating Environments
- Plain (release area)
- Incubator Interior (selection process)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Fascination → Understanding of harsh selection process

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots showing women and younger Martians forming two solid walls
- Tracking shots following little Martians scampering wild as deer
- Close-ups on capture process (one at a time by women/older children)
- Medium shots of council examining yearly supply
- Educational montages showing early weapons training

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Two solid wall formation must be clearly visible and maintained
- Little Martian movement patterns need to show deer-like scampering
- Capture sequence timing (one at a time) requires precise blocking
- Council examination process needs clear visual hierarchy
- Early weapons training scenes must show age-appropriate progression

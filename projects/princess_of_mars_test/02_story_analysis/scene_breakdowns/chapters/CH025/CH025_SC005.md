# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Provide emotional payoff and romantic resolution.

**Scene Summary:** Amidst the distant sounds of war in the streets, Carter and Dejah Thoris find a moment of quiet to profess their love and formally promise themselves to one another in marriage.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** Palace (Interior/Quiet corner).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Chaos $\rightarrow$ Intimacy/Peace.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Tight close-ups; soft lighting; shallow depth of field to isolate the couple from the background war sounds.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Costume damage (battle wear), dirt/blood on faces, emotional tone consistency.

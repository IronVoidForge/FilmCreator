# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To provide emotional payoff and establish the new romantic status quo amidst the larger war.

**Scene Summary:** Amidst the distant sounds of looting and warfare echoing through Zodanga, John Carter and Dejah Thoris share an intimate moment in the throne room, officially pledging themselves to marriage.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room (post-battle).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From the grimness of war to romantic intimacy and profound connection.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium close-ups of the couple.
- Soft focus/shallow depth of field to isolate them from the background.
- Wide shots showing their isolation against the backdrop of a city in turmoil.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Costume damage (blood/dust) on Carter and Dejah; the lighting level of the room compared to SC002.

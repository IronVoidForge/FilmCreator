# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To provide a secondary objective/rescue beat and expand the scope of the conflict.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter descends into the labyrinthine dungeons beneath the palace. He navigates the darkness to find Kantos Kan in a remote recess, eventually freeing him using keys scavenged from a fallen jailer.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Kantos Kan, Fallen Jailer (corpse).

**Participating Environments:** The dark, labyrinthine Zodangan Dungeons.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From the adrenaline of battle to suspenseful tension and relief upon rescue.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-light/chiaroscuro lighting shots.
- POV shots of Carter navigating the darkness.
- Close-ups of the keys and lock mechanisms.
- Tight close-ups on Kantos Kan's face in the dark.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting sources (torches/lanterns); the specific set of keys used; dirt/grime levels on Carter after the battle.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the primary political conflict through combat.

**Scene Summary:** A chaotic skirmish breaks out between Carter, the Tharks, and the Zodangan guards/nobility. In the heat of the battle, Sab Than and Than Kosis are killed. Tars Tarkas eventually secures the room.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Sab Than, Than Kosis, Zodangan Guards.

**Participating Environments:** Zodanga Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Chaos/Violence $\rightarrow$ Exhaustion/Victory.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Handheld/shaky cam for combat; medium shots of the melee; close-ups of the deaths of Sab Than and Than Kosis.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Blood splatter, weapon damage, character positions relative to the throne.

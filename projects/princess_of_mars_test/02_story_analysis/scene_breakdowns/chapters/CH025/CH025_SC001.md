# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the high-stakes infiltration and provide a dramatic disruption of the antagonist's ritual.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter infiltrates the Zodangan palace by shattering a window and leaping into the middle of the marriage ceremony between Sab Than and Dejah Thoris. He successfully destroys the golden ceremonial chain, preventing the forced union.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sab Than, Than Kosis, Zodangan Nobility/Guards.

**Participating Environments:** The brilliantly illuminated Audience Chamber of the Palace of Zodanga.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From ceremonial tension and dread to shock, chaos, and sudden heroic intervention.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots of the ceremony setup.
- Low-angle hero shots of Carter's entrance/leap.
- Close-ups of Dejah Thoris's reaction.
- Macro shots of the golden chain shattering.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The state of the broken window; the physical position of the ceremonial chain before and after destruction; debris from the glass.

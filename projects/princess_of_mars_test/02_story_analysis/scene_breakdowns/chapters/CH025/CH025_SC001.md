# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale of the assault and the infiltration phase.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter and fifty Thark warriors, led by Tars Tarkas, breach the gates of Zodanga and move through the palace gardens toward the main audience chamber.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Thark Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Palace Gates, Zodanga Palace Gardens.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension/Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Aggression.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the breach; tracking shots following the Tharks through gardens; close-ups of Carter's determination.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Weapon positions, Thark troop numbers, lighting transitions from exterior gates to garden shadows.

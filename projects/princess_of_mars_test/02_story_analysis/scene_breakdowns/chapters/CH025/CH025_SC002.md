# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Provide the dramatic climax of the infiltration and disrupt the antagonist's plan.

**Scene Summary:** Inside the illuminated audience chamber, Than Kosis attempts to bind Dejah Thoris to Sab Than with golden chains. Carter shatters the window and leaps into the hall, physically breaking the ceremonial chain.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Than Kosis, Sab Than.

**Participating Environments:** Zodanga Audience Chamber (Brilliantly illuminated).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Ceremonial Solemnity $\rightarrow$ Shock/Chaos.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Slow-motion of the window shattering; low-angle hero shot of Carter's leap; reaction shots of Dejah Thoris and the nobility.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The state of the golden chains (intact vs. broken), glass shards, lighting levels in the chamber.

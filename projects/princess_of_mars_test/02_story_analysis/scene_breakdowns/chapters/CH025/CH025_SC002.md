# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To execute the primary action climax and resolve the political threat of the Zodangan leadership.

**Scene Summary:** A chaotic skirmish breaks out in the throne room between Carter, the Zodangan guards, and the arriving Tharks led by Tars Tarkas. In the heat of the battle, both Sab Than and Than Kosis are killed. The Tharks eventually secure the hall, leaving only the protagonists and Dejah Thoris standing amidst the carnage.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Dejah Thoris, Sab Than (deceased), Than Kosis (deceased), Zodangan Guards, Thark Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Audience Chamber (now a battlefield).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From chaotic violence and combat adrenaline to the grim stillness of victory/aftermath.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Handheld/shaky cam for the skirmish.
- Medium shots of Thark warriors entering the hall.
- Close-ups of the deaths of Sab Than and Than Kosis.
- Wide shots showing the scale of the carnage in the room.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Blood splatter patterns; positions of fallen bodies; weapon states (broken/used); lighting changes if smoke or dust fills the room.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To demonstrate John Carter's unique mastery over Martian beasts and his rising status among the Tharks.

**Scene Summary:** Following recent battles, John Carter spends his time training thoats. Unlike the traditional Thark method of brute force, Carter uses a blend of authority and kindness. His success is so great that he teaches these methods to Tars Tarkas and other warriors. As a reward for improving the mobility of the Thark forces, Lorquas Ptomel grants him a gold anklet.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Lorquas Ptomel, various Thark warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The city of Lorquas Ptomel; training grounds/plazas.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From struggle/tension (taming the beasts) to triumph and communal respect.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the Martian cityscape and large thoats.
- Medium shots of Carter interacting with the animals (focus on hands/eyes).
- Close-ups of the gold anklet being presented.
- Group shots of Thark warriors observing the new technique.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The appearance and placement of the gold anklet; the physical behavior/posture of the thoats before and after Carter's intervention.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To demonstrate John Carter's unique approach to Martian life and establish his rising status among the Tharks.

**Scene Summary:** Following the airship battle, John Carter spends several days training the fierce Martian thoats. Unlike the Tharks who use force, Carter uses Earth-based animal husbandry techniques—kindness combined with authority. His success earns him respect from the community and a gold anklet reward from Lorquas Ptomel.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Lorquas Ptomel, various Tharks.

**Participating Environments:** The city of the Tharks (plazas/stables area).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From struggle/tension to wonder and social validation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots of the Martian cityscape/training grounds.
- Medium shots of Carter interacting with the thoats.
- Close-ups of the animals' reactions and the gold anklet being placed.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The appearance/placement of the gold anklet on Carter; the physical state of the thoats during training.

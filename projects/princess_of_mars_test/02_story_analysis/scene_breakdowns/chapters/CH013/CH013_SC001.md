# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To demonstrate John Carter's unique ability to master Martian beasts through empathy rather than force, establishing his specialized skill set.

**Scene Summary:** Following the airship battle, Carter spends several days training with the "thoats." While Tharks use brute strength, Carter uses a mix of authority and kindness. His success is so profound that he teaches Tars Tarkas and other warriors his methods, transforming the beasts into docile mounts.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Green Martian Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The training grounds/stables within the Green Martian city.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From struggle/aggression to mastery/harmony.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots of the beasts and riders.
- Close-ups of Carter's hands and facial expressions (the "kindness" aspect).
- Medium shots of Tars Tarkas observing and learning.
- Low-angle shots of the thoats to emphasize their size/viciousness.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical behavior of the thoats (from wild to docile); Carter's attire during training; the specific movements used in the taming process.

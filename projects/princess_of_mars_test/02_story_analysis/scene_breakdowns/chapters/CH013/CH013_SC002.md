# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To provide exposition on Martian technology and introduce the social conflict/jealousy facing Dejah Thoris.

**Scene Summary:** Dejah Thoris explains the volatile nature of radium projectiles to Carter, noting their danger in sunlight. She then reveals the hostility she faces from the older Thark women, who are forcing her into menial labor (mixing radium powder) due to jealousy of her status.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** A working area/plaza within the Thark city.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From intellectual curiosity to indignation and empathy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Two-shots of Carter and Dejah discussing technology.
- Cutaways to the radium powder being mixed.
- Observational shots of the older Thark women performing their tasks/watching her.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The presence of radium powder; the physical labor/exhaustion visible on Dejah's person.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To transition the narrative from the training period to the next phase of the journey.

**Scene Summary:** After seven days of preparation and training, the community begins the logistical process of packing and preparing for the march toward Thark.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, various Thark citizens/warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Lorquas Ptomel; city streets and encampments.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From routine training to focused anticipation/readiness.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Montage of movement: packing supplies, mounting thoats, moving crowds.
- Wide shots of the community preparing for departure.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Equipment and supply levels; time of day (transitioning toward night).

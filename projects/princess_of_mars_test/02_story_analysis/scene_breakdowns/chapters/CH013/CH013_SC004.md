# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To provide the emotional climax of the chapter and solidify the romantic bond.

**Scene Summary:** As they walk together under the two Martian moons, Dejah Thoris calls Carter "my chieftain," a title of profound weight. In the quiet intimacy of their proximity, Carter experiences a moment of deep realization regarding the intensity of his love for her.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** The outskirts/plazas of Lorquas Ptomel under the Martian night sky.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From quiet conversation to profound romantic epiphany and intimacy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-light/moonlit cinematography.
- Extreme close-ups on faces to capture subtle emotional shifts.
- Wide shots of the two figures walking against the backdrop of the two moons.
- Tight shots focusing on their physical proximity (hands, shoulders).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting consistency (the dual moons); the specific positioning of the characters as they walk.

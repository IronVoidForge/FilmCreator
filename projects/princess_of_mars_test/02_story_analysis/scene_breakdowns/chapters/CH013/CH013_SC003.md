# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To advance the romantic relationship and highlight cultural misunderstandings.

**Scene Summary:** Carter and Dejah Thoris walk together under the two moons of Barsoom. Dejah refers to him as "my chieftain," a term with deep cultural weight that Carter doesn't fully understand. The scene concludes with an intimate realization of Carter's profound love for her.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** An avenue or walkway in the Thark city under the night sky/two moons.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From quiet companionship to deep romantic epiphany.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-light/Moonlit wide shots of the couple walking.
- Tight close-ups on faces to capture subtle emotional shifts and dialogue.
- Extreme close-ups for the "chieftain" moment.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting consistency (the two moons); the physical proximity/distance between the characters as they walk.

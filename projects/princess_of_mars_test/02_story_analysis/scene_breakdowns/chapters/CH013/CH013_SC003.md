# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To advance the romantic subplot and provide exposition regarding Martian technology/conflict.

**Scene Summary:** Carter orchestrates a private moment with Dejah Thoris by sending Sola away. Dejah reveals how the Thark women have been attempting to isolate them by forcing her into labor, specifically mixing radium-based explosives. She explains the nature of these projectiles—how they detonate upon contact with sunlight.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola (briefly).

**Participating Environments:** A private area within Lorquas Ptomel.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tactical/frustrated (discussing the women's interference) to intellectual/informative (explaining the explosives).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Two-shots of Carter and Dejah in conversation.
- Close-ups on Dejah as she explains the mechanics of the radium explosives.
- Reaction shots from Carter regarding the Thark women's tactics.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The presence/absence of Sola; the physical state of Dejah (signs of recent labor).

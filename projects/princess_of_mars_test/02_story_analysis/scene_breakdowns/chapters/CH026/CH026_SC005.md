# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To conclude the arc with a formal political and emotional resolution.

**Scene Summary:** A grand triumphal procession enters Helium. Carter, Tars Tarkas, and Thark leaders are received by Jeddak Tardos Mors. The alliance is formalized, and Carter is embraced by the royal family as a savior.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Tardos Mors (Jeddak of Helium), Dejah Thoris, Thark Leaders, Heliumite Citizens.

**Participating Environments:** The streets and palace of Helium.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Solemnity $\rightarrow$ Grandeur $\rightarrow$ Deep Emotional Connection/Warmth.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Slow tracking shots of the procession; Medium shots of the formal greeting; Close-ups of the royal family's embrace; Wide establishing shots of the city in celebration.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Costume/Armor cleanliness (post-battle); The specific order of the procession.

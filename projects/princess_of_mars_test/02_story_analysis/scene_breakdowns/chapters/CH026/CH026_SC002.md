# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To provide emotional payoff for the rescue and transition from sea to land combat.

**Scene Summary:** Following the naval victory, John Carter transfers Princess Dejah Thoris to the Heliumite flagship. She is celebrated as a hero by her people. Amidst the celebration, Carter remains focused on the mission, deciding to stay with the Tharks to complete the land campaign rather than resting.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tars Tarkas, Heliumite Sailors.

**Participating Environments:** The Heliumite Flagship (Deck).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief/Joy $\rightarrow$ Determination.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of the reunion; Close-ups of Dejah's emotional reaction; Low angle hero shots of Carter and Tars Tarkas.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Dejah Thoris's physical condition (exhaustion vs. relief); The presence of the Thark fleet in the background.

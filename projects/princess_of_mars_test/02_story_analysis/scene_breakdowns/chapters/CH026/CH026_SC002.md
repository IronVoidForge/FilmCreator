# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To depict the grim, ritualistic end of the Zodangan aerial threat.

**Scene Summary:** The battle concludes as the Zodangan fleet is broken. In a display of ritualistic honor/despair, the Zodangan commanders leap from their vessels to their deaths rather than face capture or defeat.

**Participating Characters:** Zodangan Commanders, Thark Warriors, Heliumite Sailors.

**Participating Environments:** The wreckage-strewn skies near Helium.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Victory $\rightarrow$ Somber/Horror.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** High-angle shots of falling figures; Reaction close-ups of the victors; Wide shots of the drifting, empty Zodangan ships.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Timing of the leaps relative to the cessation of fire.

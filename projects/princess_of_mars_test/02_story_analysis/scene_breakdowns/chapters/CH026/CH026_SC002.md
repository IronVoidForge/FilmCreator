# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To facilitate the emotional reunion and public recognition of the hero.

**Scene Summary:** Upon reaching Helium, John Carter assists in transferring Princess Dejah Thoris to the Heliumite flagship. In a moment of high ceremony, Dejah Thoris publicly credits Carter for her rescue and the salvation of the city before being reunited with her father, Mors Kajak.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Mors Kajak, Tars Tarkas, Heliumite Officers.

**Participating Environments:** The Heliumite Flagship / Helium Harbor/Docking area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief $\rightarrow$ Public Reverence $\rightarrow$ Intense Familial Emotion.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of the transfer; Close-ups of Dejah Thoris's speech; Reaction shots of the crowd and Mors Kajak; Wide shots of the flagship against the Helium skyline.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical state/clothing of Dejah Thoris (post-rescue); Positioning of Carter relative to the Princess during the transfer.

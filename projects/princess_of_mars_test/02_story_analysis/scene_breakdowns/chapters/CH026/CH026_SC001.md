# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the military dominance of the Thark-Heliumite alliance and resolve the naval threat.

**Scene Summary:** The Thark fleet, led by Tars Tarkas and Kantos Kan, intercepts a Zodangan fleet attempting to besiege Helium. A massive aerial battle ensues, utilizing Heliumite naval tactics combined with Thark small-arms fire. The Zodangans are decisively defeated, ending in the ritualistic suicide of their commanders.

**Participating Characters:** Tars Tarkas, Kantos Kan, John Carter (implied/observing), Zodangan Commanders.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian Skies / Aerial Naval Theater.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Chaotic Violence $\rightarrow$ Grim Triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide aerial shots of fleet movements; Medium tracking shots of Thark warriors firing; Close-ups of Zodangan commanders' ritualistic deaths; POV shots from the cockpits/vessels.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Ship positions relative to one another; Direction of flight paths; Timing of small-arms fire vs. naval maneuvers.

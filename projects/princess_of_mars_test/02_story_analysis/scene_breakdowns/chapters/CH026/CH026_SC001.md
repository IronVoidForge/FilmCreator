# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To depict the large-scale military conflict and the decisive naval victory.

**Scene Summary:** Tars Tarkas and Kantos Kan lead a fleet of Thark vessels toward Helium, only to intercept a Zodangan siege force. A massive aerial battle ensues between the fleets. The tide turns with the arrival of the Heliumite navy and superior Thark marksmanship. The conflict ends when the Zodangan commanders commit ritualistic suicide by plunging from their vessels, leading to a total surrender.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Kantos Kan, Zodangan Commanders.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian Skies (Aerial combat zone).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension/Chaos $\rightarrow$ Intense Combat $\rightarrow$ Somber Victory.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide aerial shots of fleets; Medium tracking shots of Thark warriors firing; Close-ups of Zodangan commanders' ritualistic descent; POV shots from vessels.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Vessel positions in the sky; Number of remaining ships; Direction of flight paths during the melee.

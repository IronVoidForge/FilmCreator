# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the scale of the conflict and demonstrate the military synergy between Tharks and Heliumites.

**Scene Summary:** Tars Tarkas and Kantos Kan lead a fleet toward Helium, only to intercept a Zodangan siege force. A massive aerial battle erupts, characterized by Thark marksmanship and the arrival of the Heliumite navy, turning the tide against the Zodangans.

**Participating Characters:** Tars Tarkas, Kantos Kan, Zodangan Commanders, Heliumite Navy Officers.

**Participating Environments:** The skies above the approach to Helium; various Thark and Zodangan aerial vessels.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Chaos/Action $\rightarrow$ Triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of fleet movements; Medium tracking shots of aerial combat; Close-ups of warriors firing weapons; POV shots from vessels.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Vessel positions in the sky; Number of ships remaining; Weaponry/ammunition usage during the dogfight.

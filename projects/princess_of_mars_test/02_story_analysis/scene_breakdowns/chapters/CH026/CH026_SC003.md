# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To resolve the remaining ground-based threat and demonstrate Thark martial prowess.

**Scene Summary:** Carter and Tars Tarkas lead a land assault against the remaining Zodangan ground forces on the plains near Helium. Despite being outnumbered ten to one, the combined force of Thark warriors and Heliumite reinforcements crushes the Zodangan camp.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Thark Warriors, Heliumite Reinforcements, Zodangan Ground Forces.

**Participating Environments:** The Plains near Helium.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ High-Stakes Combat $\rightarrow$ Decisive Victory.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide sweeping shots of the charge; Low-angle hero shots of Carter and Tars Tarkas; Handheld/shaky cam for ground combat; Close-ups of Thark thoats and weaponry.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The number of troops visible in wide shots; The direction of the charge; Weaponry usage (small arms vs melee).

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To provide emotional payoff for Dejah Thoris and establish her status as a hero.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter transfers Princess Dejah Thoris to the Heliumite flagship. She is greeted with immense celebration, where she publicly credits Carter for her rescue, cementing his reputation.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Princess Dejah Thoris, Heliumite Officers/Citizens.

**Participating Environments:** The deck of the Heliumite flagship.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief $\rightarrow$ Jubilation/Gratitude.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium two-shots (Carter/Dejah); Close-ups of Dejah's expression; Wide shots of the cheering crowd on deck.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's physical state (battle-worn) vs. Dejah's appearance.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To conclude the chapter with a formal recognition of the hero and the new alliance.

**Scene Summary:** A grand triumphal procession enters the city of Helium. John Carter is formally honored by Tardos Mors, Jeddak of Helium, and the royal family for his bravery in saving Dejah Thoris and securing the victory. The chapter ends on a note of high honor and political stability.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tardos Mors, Mors Kajak, Tars Tarkas.

**Participating Environments:** Helium (Greater City/Palace Grounds).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion $\rightarrow$ Grandeur/Celebration.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Slow-motion procession shots; Low angle "hero" shots of Carter being honored; Wide establishing shots of the red towers of Helium.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The attire/regalia of the Heliumite royalty; The physical state (dirt/blood) of the soldiers in the procession.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To formalize the political alliance and provide emotional resolution.

**Scene Summary:** A grand triumphal procession enters Helium. John Carter and Tars Tarkas are honored by Jeddak Tardos Mors, marking the historic unification of the Red and Green races.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Tardos Mors, Dejah Thoris, Heliumite Citizens.

**Participating Environments:** The streets/palace of Helium (Greater City).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion $\rightarrow$ Grandeur $\rightarrow$ Solemn Unity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Slow-motion tracking shots of the procession; Wide shots of the architecture and crowds; Close-ups of the formal exchange between Tardos Mors, Carter, and Tars Tarkas.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The level of "battle grime" on the characters (should be present but transitioning to ceremonial); The scale of the crowd/procession length.

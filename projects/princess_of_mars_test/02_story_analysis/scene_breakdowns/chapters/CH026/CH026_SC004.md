# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To resolve the ground war and show the combined might of the coalition.

**Scene Summary:** Carter stays with Tars Tarkas to lead the land campaign. After unloading Thark mounts, the combined Thark and Heliumite forces launch a decisive assault on the remaining Zodangan land camps, crushing the resistance despite being outnumbered.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Thark Warriors, Heliumite Soldiers, Zodangan Remnants.

**Participating Environments:** The plains/terrain surrounding Helium; Zodangan land camps.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Intense Combat $\rightarrow$ Decisive Victory.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide sweeping shots of the charge; Low-angle "hero" shots of Carter and Tars; Handheld/shaky cam for ground combat; Close-ups of Thark mounts (thoats) in action.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Coordination between land and air forces; The scale of the Zodangan camps being destroyed.

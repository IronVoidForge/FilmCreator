# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Execute the narrative pivot where Carter's status changes via Martian custom.

**Scene Summary:** Following the combat, Carter is recognized as having won the "spoils" of the deceased warrior. By acquiring his regalia and weapons, Carter is inadvertently elevated to the status of a Thark chieftain.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Lorquas Ptomel, Tars Tarkas, Thark Council.

**Participating Environments:** The Thark Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From shock/violence to formal recognition and awe.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of the regalia being presented/taken; reaction shots of the council members recognizing the change in status.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific items (weapons/regalia) Carter is wearing or holding.

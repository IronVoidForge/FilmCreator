# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Climax of the chapter; action beat and status shift.

**Scene Summary:** A young Thark warrior strikes Dejah Thoris in a moment of cruel laughter. Carter intervenes with lethal force, killing the aggressor. Following custom, the dead warrior's possessions are transferred to Carter, granting him chieftain status.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Young Thark Warrior, Thark Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Council Chamber/Plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Shock/Outrage to violent action to sudden social elevation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Handheld/shaky cam for the combat; close-ups of the strike and Carter's reaction; medium shots of the ritualistic transfer of possessions.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The movement of weapons/possessions from the dead warrior to Carter; blood/injury on Dejah Thoris.

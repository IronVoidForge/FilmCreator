# SC004 - Action Climax

## Scene Purpose
John Carter's intervention and killing of the young warrior, establishing his new status as Tharkian chieftain.

## Scene Summary
A young warrior strikes Dejah Thoris across the face; John Carter intervenes in his rage, killing the brute. He gains status as a Tharkian chieftain through this act, receiving the dead warrior's trappings. This is the action climax of the chapter.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter** - Intervenes and kills
- **Woola** - Present during action
- **Young warrior** - Deceased antagonist

## Participating Environments
- Audience chamber/interrogation room (same as SC003)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Rage → Consequence → Status elevation

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots capturing the strike and intervention
- Close-ups on Carter's facial expression during rage
- Action sequences showing the killing blow
- Reaction shots from other Thark chieftains
- Medium shots showing trappings being received

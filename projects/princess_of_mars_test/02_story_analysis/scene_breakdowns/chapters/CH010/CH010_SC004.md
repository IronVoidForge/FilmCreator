# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Establish Carter's new social standing and his moral stance.

**Scene Summary:** In the aftermath of the fight, Carter is recognized as a chieftain by virtue of the spoils of war. Tars Tarkas confronts him, questioning his language skills and his "unprecedented temerity." Carter asserts his moral code, declaring his intent to protect Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Lorquas Ptomel, Thark Council.

**Participating Environments:** The interior audience chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From the adrenaline of combat to heavy, political/social tension and resolve.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Two-shots between Carter and Tars Tarkas.
- Close-ups on Carter's face during his declaration.
- Reaction shots from the surrounding Tharks.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's physical state (sweat, blood, disheveled clothing) following the fight.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the emotional bond and mutual loyalty between John Carter and Woola.

**Scene Summary:** Early in the morning, on the outskirts of the Thark city, John Carter tests his connection with Woola. Instead of using commands, he uses affection to solidify their bond, resulting in a deep, unspoken understanding between man and beast.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** Outskirts of the Thark city (low hills/ravines).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From testing/uncertainty to profound mutual connection/loyalty.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-ups on Carter's face and hands.
- Extreme close-ups on Woola's eyes and expressions.
- Wide shots of the Martian landscape to establish isolation.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Woola's physical positioning relative to Carter; lighting/time of day (early morning).

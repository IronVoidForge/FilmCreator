# Scene SC001 - Intervention Setup

## Scene Purpose
Transition from verbal debate to physical combat, establishing the immediate conflict that drives the chapter's action sequence.

## Scene Summary
John Carter returns to the audience chamber with Woola after exploring city limits. Lorquas Ptomel questions Dejah Thoris regarding her expedition from Helium. A young Thark warrior insults and strikes Dejah Thoris during proceedings. John Carter intervenes, killing the warrior in a fierce combat sequence.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter** - Protagonist, intervenes to protect Dejah Thoris
- **Woola** - Companion, follows closely throughout scene
- **Lorquas Ptomel** - Host of audience chamber, questions Dejah Thoris
- **Dejah Thoris** - Captive, target of warrior's insult
- **Young Thark Warrior** - Antagonist, initiates physical conflict

## Participating Environments
- Audience Chamber of Lorquas Ptomel (steps of rostrum, benches at side)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Tension escalates from verbal debate to physical violence; protective instinct overrides diplomatic protocol.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shots during dialogue exchanges
- Close-ups on facial expressions (insult, pain, determination)
- Wide shots for combat sequence
- Tracking shots following John Carter's intervention

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Woola's heavy neck/body for affection display
- Dejah Thoris injuries (nosebleed)
- Warrior bleeding and lifeless state
- Location markers within audience chamber

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the bond between Carter and Woola, demonstrating Carter's ability to command through affection rather than just force.

**Scene Summary:** Early in the morning, John Carter tests his relationship with the Martian hound Woola. By using affection to bypass the creature's instinctual duty to confine him, Carter establishes himself as Woola's undisputed master.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** Carter's confinement area/living quarters.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension/confinement to mutual trust and dominance.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups of Carter's expressions; medium shots of the physical interaction between man and hound; low-angle shots of Woola to emphasize size.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical positioning of Woola relative to Carter; the specific way Carter uses affection (touch/voice).

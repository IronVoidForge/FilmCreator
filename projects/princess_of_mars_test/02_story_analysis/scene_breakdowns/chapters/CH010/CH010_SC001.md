# Scene 001: City Limits Bonding

## Scene Purpose
Establish John Carter's connection with Woola before the council confrontation, showing their shared understanding and affection.

## Scene Summary
John Carter explores the city limits with Woola. They bond over their mutual affection and understanding. The scene ends as they approach the audience chamber where the council meeting is taking place.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter
- Woola

## Participating Environments
- City streets/limits
- Approach to audience chamber

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From cautious exploration to confident bonding; Woola transitions from anxious about leaving city to playful and affectionate after bonding with John Carter.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shots of John and Woola walking together
- Close-ups of Woola's expressions (anxious → playful)
- Wide shots establishing city scale
- Over-the-shoulder shots showing their interaction

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Woola's behavioral transition from anxious to playful must be clear
- Location continuity between city limits and chamber approach
- Any physical props or costume changes during the bonding sequence

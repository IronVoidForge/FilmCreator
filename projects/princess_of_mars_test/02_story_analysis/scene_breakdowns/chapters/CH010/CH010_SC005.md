# SC005 - Aftermath and Resolution

## Scene Purpose
Status elevation, character revelation, and departure establishing the bond between John Carter and Dejah Thoris.

## Scene Summary
John Carter explains himself to Dejah Thoris, revealing his name and origin (Virginia, Earth). Tars Tarkas informs him that he is now accorded respect as a conqueror but warns him of responsibilities attached to his new status, including potential danger if Tal Hajus deems him unworthy. Chapter concludes with John Carter and Dejah Thoris leaving the audience chamber together accompanied by Woola, having established a bond of protection and friendship despite their captor status.

- **Woola** - Following throughout departure
- **Dejah Thoris** - Bonding with John Carter
- **Tars Tarkas** - Informing of responsibilities

# SC003 - Interrogation Scene

## Scene Purpose
Main confrontation with Thark leadership and Sarkoja's cruelty, establishing the political stakes.

## Scene Summary
Lorquas Ptomel and Thark chieftains interrogate Dejah Thoris; she explains her peaceful scientific mission from Helium and pleads for amity between the red and green men. Sarkoja is present and displays cruelty toward the prisoner. This scene builds tension before the action climax.

- **Thark chieftains** - Supporting interrogators

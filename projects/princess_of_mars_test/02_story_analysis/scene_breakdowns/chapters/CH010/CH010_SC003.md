# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Provide the central action climax and demonstrate Carter's combat prowess.

**Scene Summary:** The tension is broken when a young Thark warrior strikes Dejah Thoris. Infuriated, John Carter intervenes, engaging the massive twelve-foot warrior in a violent struggle and killing him.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Young Thark Warrior, Sarkoja (guarding).

**Participating Environments:** The Thark Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From fragile peace to sudden violence/outrage.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Handheld/dynamic shots for the combat; close-ups of the strike against Dejah; wide shots to capture the scale of the warrior vs. Carter.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The direction of the blow to Dejah's face; the choreography of the kill; the physical location of the bodies post-combat.

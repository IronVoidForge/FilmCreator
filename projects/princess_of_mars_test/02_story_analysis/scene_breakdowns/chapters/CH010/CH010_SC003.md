# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Provide the chapter's action climax and demonstrate Carter's combat prowess.

**Scene Summary:** A young Thark warrior strikes Dejah Thoris in a moment of mirthless laughter. Enraged, Carter intervenes, engaging the warrior in single combat. Despite the physical disparity, Carter uses superior technique to kill the warrior.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Young Thark Warrior, Sarkoja, Thark Council.

**Participating Environments:** The interior audience chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension/outrage to violent action and sudden shock.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Handheld/shaky cam for the combat sequence.
- Medium shots of the strike on Dejah Thoris.
- Wide shots to capture the movement within the chamber.
- Close-ups of the killing blow.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Weapon positions; blood splatter; character blocking during the fight.

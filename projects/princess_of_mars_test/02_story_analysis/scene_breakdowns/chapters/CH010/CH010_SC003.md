# Scene SC003 - Silent Departure

## Scene Purpose
Establish new dynamic as chieftain and prisoner, showing the transformed relationship through action.

## Scene Summary
John Carter leaves chamber with Dejah Thoris and Woola. Passing through audience hall in silence. Ignoring guards/harpies. Establishing new dynamic as chieftain and prisoner.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter** - Protagonist, now chieftain, leads departure
- **Woola** - Companion, follows closely throughout scene
- **Dejah Thoris** - Captive, walks with John Carter in silence

## Participating Environments
- Audience Chamber → Plaza/Exit
- Audience Hall

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Quiet determination; new relationship established through silent action rather than words.

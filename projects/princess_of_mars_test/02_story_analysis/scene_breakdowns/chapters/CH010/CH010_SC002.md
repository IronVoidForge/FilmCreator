# SC002 - Return to Plaza and Observation

## Scene Purpose
Transition from exploration back to the main plaza setting and build anticipation for the interrogation.

## Scene Summary
Carter returns to the plaza area where he observes Dejah Thoris before the interrogation begins. This scene serves as a bridge between the personal moment with Woola and the larger political confrontation.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter** - Returning from exploration
- **Woola** - Following Carter back
- **Dejah Thoris** - Observed from distance (not yet interacting)

## Participating Environments
- Plaza entrance
- Observation point near audience chamber

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Anticipation → Tension → Readiness for confrontation

## Likely Visual Coverage Families

- Long shots showing Dejah Thoris in the distance
- Over-the-shoulder shots from Carter's perspective
- Establishing shots of the plaza architecture

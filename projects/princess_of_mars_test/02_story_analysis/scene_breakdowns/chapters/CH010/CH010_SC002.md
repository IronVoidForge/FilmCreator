# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the tension, cultural brutality, and Dejah Thoris's character.

**Scene Summary:** Carter observes from a distance/periphery as Dejah Thoris is brought before the Thark council. He witnesses the cruelty of Sarkoja and hears Dejah Thoris deliver a sophisticated plea for peace and scientific cooperation to the uncivilized Green Martians.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sarkoja, Lorquas Ptomel, Thark Council members.

**Participating Environments:** The interior audience chamber of Lorquas Ptomel.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From observation/curiosity to growing indignation and tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of the council members.
- Low-angle shots of the Thark leaders to emphasize power.
- Close-ups on Dejah Thoris to capture her dignity amidst captivity.
- Reaction shots from Carter.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Placement of prisoners vs. guards; the scale of the audience chamber.

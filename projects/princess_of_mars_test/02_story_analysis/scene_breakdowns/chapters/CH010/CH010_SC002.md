# Scene 002: Council Confrontation

## Scene Purpose
Present the council meeting confrontation where Dejah Thoris is questioned and physically attacked, escalating to John Carter's intervention.

## Scene Summary
In the audience chamber, Lorquas Ptomel questions Dejah Thoris about her expedition from Helium. A young Thark warrior insults Dejah Thoris and strikes her down with a blow that causes a nosebleed. The scene builds tension as guards stand below and prisoners are held before the entrance.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter
- Dejah Thoris
- Lorquas Ptomel (questioning)
- Young Thark Warrior (attacker)
- Council members
- Guards
- Prisoners

## Participating Environments
- Audience chamber
- Steps of rostrum where council squats
- Entrance area with guards and prisoners

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From formal questioning to violent confrontation; Dejah Thoris's demeanor shifts from haughty glance to wide, staring eyes while injured; John Carter moves from observer to protector.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of audience chamber layout
- Medium shots of council members on rostrum steps
- Close-ups of Dejah Thoris's injury (nosebleed)
- Over-the-shoulder shots during questioning
- Action coverage for the strike and John Carter's intervention

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Dejah Thoris's injury progression must be clear
- Warrior's body position after being struck
- Regalia/arms visibility before they are brought to John Carter
- Guard positioning and prisoner placement consistency

# Scene SC002 - Aftermath Conversation

## Scene Purpose
Establish new dynamic through dialogue and customs explanation, revealing character motivations and moral positions.

## Scene Summary
John Carter assumes dead warrior's chieftain status and regalia. Tends to Dejah Thoris's injuries. Significant conversation where John Carter reveals his name and origin (Virginia, Earth). Tars Tarkas explains Martian customs regarding prisoners and chieftains. John Carter asserts moral stance against harming Dejah Thoris.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter** - Protagonist, assumes chieftain status, reveals identity
- **Woola** - Companion, follows closely throughout scene
- **Lorquas Ptomel** - Host of audience chamber, observes proceedings
- **Dejah Thoris** - Captive, recipient of care and revelation
- **Tars Tarkas** - Cultural guide, explains Martian customs

## Participating Environments
- Audience Chamber of Lorquas Ptomel (steps of rostrum, benches at side)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Shift from violence to reflective dialogue; moral clarity emerges through cultural exchange and personal revelation.

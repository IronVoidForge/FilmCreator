# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Setup tension and introduce the political stakes.

**Scene Summary:** Returning to the plaza, Carter observes Dejah Thoris held captive under guard by Sarkoja. He uses his unique ability to understand the Martian tongue to eavesdrop on the council meeting led by Lorquas Ptomel.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sarkoja, Lorquas Ptomel, Thark Council members.

**Participating Environments:** The City Plaza / Council area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity/Observation to heightened political tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Over-the-shoulder shots (Carter's POV); medium shots of the guards; wide shots of the council assembly.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's hiding spot/positioning relative to the Council members.

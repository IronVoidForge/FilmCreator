**Scene Summary:** A temporal jump reveals Carter twenty years after his arrival on Mars/Earth. He is now a wealthy man due to an untouched mine, yet he remains emotionally tethered to his memories of Mars and Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter (Older)

**Participating Environments:**
- Wealthy Estate / Study (Interior)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Melancholy/Longing

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**

- Medium close-ups of Carter's aging face
- Detail shots of objects representing his wealth vs. his memories

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**

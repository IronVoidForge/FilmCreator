# SC003 - Exit from Cave and Earth Realization

## Scene Purpose
Major location change triggers critical realization. Narrator exits cave to discover he is back on Earth, not Mars as expected. Dramatic revelation scene.

## Scene Summary
Narrator moves toward cave exit and steps outside into Arizona landscape. Realizes with shock that he has returned to Earth after ten years on Mars. The location shift confirms his situation has changed fundamentally.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (primary)

## Participating Environments
- Cave interior (exit threshold)
- Arizona cave exterior/outside
- Desert landscape beyond cave entrance

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Shock → Realization → Disbelief as Earth location is confirmed

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shot at cave exit threshold
- Wide shot of narrator stepping outside
- Establishing shot of Arizona desert landscape
- Close-up on narrator's shocked expression
- Over-the-shoulder view showing landscape context

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Cave exterior must match interior lighting and atmosphere
- Arizona landscape continuity across shots
- Narrator's position at exit needs to be consistent
- Transition from cave interior to exterior should feel natural but dramatic

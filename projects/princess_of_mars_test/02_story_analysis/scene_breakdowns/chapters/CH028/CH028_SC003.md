# SC003

**Scene Purpose**
Time jump to establish the protagonist's current state and introduce the supernatural/prophetic element.

**Scene Summary**
Twenty years have passed. An older version of the protagonist sits in his study overlooking the Hudson River. He experiences a profound vision involving Dejah Thoris, a young boy, and a creature with a heart of gold, sensing an imminent reunion.

**Participating Characters**
- Protagonist (Older, contemplative)
- Visionary figures: Dejah Thoris, Young Boy, Creature

**Participating Environments**
- Study overlooking the Hudson River (Sophisticated, quiet, reflective)

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
Stasis $\rightarrow$ Spiritual/Prophetic Awakening

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
- Medium shots (the older man in his study)
- Dream/Vision sequences (soft focus, ethereal lighting, surreal compositions)
- Establishing shots (Hudson River view)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
- Aging makeup/prosthetics for the protagonist.
- Visual style distinction between "reality" and "vision."

# SC001 - Awakening and Initial Discovery

## Scene Purpose
Establish narrator's disorientation after ten years on Mars, introduce key artifacts that hint at his situation, create immediate mystery through garment disintegration.

## Scene Summary
Narrator awakens in Arizona cave interior. Strange garments he finds begin to disintegrate as he rises. Discovers matches and green powder among artifacts scattered around the cave.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (primary)

## Participating Environments
- Arizona cave interior (discovery space)
- Cave floor and walls (artifact locations)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Confusion → Curiosity → Growing unease as garments disintegrate

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Close-up on narrator's face (awakening)
- Medium shot of cave interior (establishing space)
- Extreme close-up on artifacts (matches, green powder)
- Detail shots of garment disintegration process

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Garment disintegration must be shown clearly for visual storytelling
- Artifact placement needs consistency across shots
- Cave lighting and atmosphere should remain consistent throughout scene

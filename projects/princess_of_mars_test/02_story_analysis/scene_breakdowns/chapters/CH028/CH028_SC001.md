# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish protagonist's disorientation and return to Earth.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter awakens in a dark, claustrophobic cave in Arizona. He is wearing strange, decaying garments that crumble as he moves, signaling a transition from his Martian life back to terrestrial reality.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter

**Participating Environments:**
- Dark Arizona Cave (Interior)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Disorientation/Physical discomfort.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme close-ups of eyes opening/breathing.
- Macro shots of crumbling fabric.
- Low-light, high-contrast handheld shots to emphasize disorientation.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The state of the clothing (how much has crumbled).
- Lighting levels as he moves through the cave.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose**
Establish the protagonist's disorientation and the immediate physical threat/horror of his new environment.

**Scene Summary**
The protagonist awakens in darkness, feeling the constriction of strange garments. Using a match for light, he explores a dark cave, discovering the mummified remains of an old woman near a charcoal burner and a macabre collection of human skeletons suspended from the ceiling by rawhide.

**Participating Characters**
- Protagonist (disoriented, vulnerable)

**Participating Environments**
- Dark Cave (stifling, claustrophobic, filled with skeletal remains)

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
Confusion $\rightarrow$ Terror/Revulsion

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
- Extreme Close-ups (eyes opening, striking a match, textures of stiff garments)
- Low-key lighting shots (flickering matchlight, deep shadows)
- Wide/Medium shots (revealing the scale of the hanging skeletons)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
- Match strike timing and light intensity.
- Position of the protagonist relative to the mummified remains.

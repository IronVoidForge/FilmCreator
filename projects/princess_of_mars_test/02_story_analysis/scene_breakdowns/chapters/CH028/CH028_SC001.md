# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's disorientation and the eerie, primal setting of his arrival on Earth.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter awakens in a dark Arizona cave, confused by his stiff clothing. He discovers the macabre sight of a mummified woman near a charcoal burner with green powder, surrounded by hanging human skeletons.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter

**Participating Environments:**
- Dark Arizona Cave (Interior)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Horror/Dread

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme close-ups of eyes/breathing (disorientation)
- Low-angle medium shots of the mummified figure and skeletons (horror)
- Macro shots of the green powder/charcoal burner

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The specific state of Carter's "stiff garments"
- Lighting levels within the cave vs. the glow of the charcoal

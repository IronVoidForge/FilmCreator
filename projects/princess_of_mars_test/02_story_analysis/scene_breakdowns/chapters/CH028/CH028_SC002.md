# SC002 - Mummified Woman and Skeletons Revealed

## Scene Purpose
Deepen the mystery of narrator's situation by revealing more disturbing artifacts. Escalate tension through discovery of mummified woman and hanging skeletons.

## Scene Summary
Narrator explores deeper into cave interior, discovering a mummified woman preserved in the space. Hanging skeletons are revealed, suggesting this is not a natural cave but some kind of storage or ritual site.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (primary)

## Participating Environments
- Cave interior (deeper sections)
- Mummification chamber area
- Skeleton hanging display area

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Curiosity → Shock → Dread as discoveries become more disturbing

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shot of cave interior (establishing full space)
- Medium shot approaching mummified woman
- Close-up on mummified figure details
- High angle or side view of hanging skeletons
- Reaction shots of narrator's face

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Mummified woman positioning must be consistent
- Skeleton hanging arrangement needs to remain stable across shots
- Cave atmosphere and lighting should escalate appropriately
- Narrator's emotional progression from curiosity to dread must be clear

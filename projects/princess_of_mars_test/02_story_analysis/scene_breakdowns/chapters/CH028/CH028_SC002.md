# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Transition from the claustrophobic horror of the cave to the vastness of Earth.

**Scene Summary:** Carter exits the cave and experiences the sensory shock of realizing he is on Earth, looking out over the Arizona landscape.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter

**Participating Environments:**
- Cave Entrance / Arizona Landscape (Exterior)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Disorientation/Awe

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the desert landscape
- POV shots from Carter's perspective as he emerges into light
- Medium shot of Carter reacting to the sun/environment

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Lighting transition (Cave darkness to bright sunlight)
- Carter's physical condition/dishevelment upon exiting

# SC002

**Scene Purpose**
Provide a massive narrative pivot and character backstory through internal reflection.

**Scene Summary**
The protagonist emerges from the cave onto a ledge, discovering he is on Earth in Arizona rather than Mars. He experiences an emotional crisis, reflecting on his ten-year absence from Mars and his profound, lingering love for Dejah Thoris amidst the uncertainty of her survival during the atmospheric crisis.

**Participating Characters**
- Protagonist (shocked, melancholic)

**Participating Environments**
- Rocky Ledge (Arizona landscape, bright/harsh daylight compared to cave)

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
Shock $\rightarrow$ Melancholy/Longing

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
- Wide shots (establishing the Arizona landscape/ledge)
- Close-ups (protagonist's facial reaction to the realization)
- POV shots (looking out over the desert)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
- Lighting transition from cave darkness to outdoor brightness.
- Direction of gaze toward the horizon.

# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Provide the emotional climax and the supernatural/visionary connection to Mars.

**Scene Summary:** In his study overlooking the Hudson River, Carter gazes at Mars through his window. He experiences a profound vision or sensation of Dejah Thoris and a child, sensing their presence across the void.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter
- Dejah Thoris (Vision/Sensation)
- The Child (Vision/Sensation)

**Participating Environments:**
- Study overlooking the Hudson River (Interior)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Loneliness $\rightarrow$ Spiritual Connection/Yearning.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Slow zooms on Carter's eyes.
- Soft-focus or ethereal overlays for the vision.
- Wide shots of the river and the night sky/Mars.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The visual appearance of Mars in the sky.
- Lighting within the study (moonlight vs. lamp light).

# SC005 - Vision of Reunion with Dejah Thoris

## Scene Purpose
Dramatic payoff and emotional climax. Chapter concludes with vision of Dejah Thoris waiting in garden on Mars, suggesting reunion. Visionary sequence with emotional resolution.

## Scene Summary
Narrator experiences vision of Dejah Thoris standing in garden on Mars, appearing to wait for him. Vision suggests reunion is possible or has occurred. Emotional climax and chapter conclusion with hopeful yet mysterious tone.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (primary)
- Dejah Thoris (visionary appearance)

## Participating Environments
- Arizona cave exterior (narrator's location)
- Mars garden (visionary space)
- Transition between Earth reality and Martian vision

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Longing → Hope → Acceptance → Mystical wonder

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shot of narrator in vision state
- Soft focus or dream-like visual treatment for Dejah Thoris
- Wide shot of Mars garden setting (visionary)
- Close-up on narrator's emotional reaction to vision
- Dissolve or fade transitions between Earth and Martian spaces

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Visionary elements should be clearly distinguished from reality
- Dejah Thoris appearance needs consistency within vision sequence
- Transition from Arizona landscape to Mars garden must feel dream-like
- Emotional progression from longing to hope needs clear visual markers
- Vision ending should resolve back to narrator's emotional state

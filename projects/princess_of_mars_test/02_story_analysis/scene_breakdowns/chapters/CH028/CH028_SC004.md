# SC004 - Reflection on Wealth and Longing

## Scene Purpose
Emotional mode shifts from discovery to contemplation. Narrator reflects on material wealth but expresses deep longing for Dejah Thoris and fate of Helium. Character-driven reflection scene.

## Scene Summary
Narrator stands outside cave, reflecting on his accumulated wealth from old mine. Despite material success, he expresses profound longing for Dejah Thoris and concern about Helium's fate. Emotional vulnerability revealed.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (primary)

## Participating Environments
- Arizona cave exterior (reflection space)
- Desert landscape surrounding cave

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Disbelief → Contemplation → Longing → Melancholy

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shot of narrator standing alone
- Close-up on narrator's face during reflection
- Wide shot showing narrator in vast desert landscape
- Over-the-shoulder shots emphasizing isolation
- Soft focus or shallow depth of field for emotional emphasis

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Narrator's position and posture should remain consistent across reflection shots
- Desert landscape continuity must be maintained
- Emotional progression from disbelief to longing needs clear visual cues
- Lighting should support contemplative mood throughout scene

# SC003

**scene purpose**: To introduce the Martian social dynamic and establish the narrator's moral character through an act of defiance against Martian brutality.

**scene summary**: Tars Tarkas, Sola, and a group of Martian warriors arrive to witness the aftermath of the fight. While they admire the narrator's strength, tension rises when a warrior attempts to execute the wounded Watch-thing with a pistol. The narrator physically intervenes, stopping the shot and asserting his bond with the creature.

**participating characters**: The Narrator, The Watch-thing (wounded), Tars Tarkas, Sola, Martian Warriors.

**participating environments**: The Martian chamber/plaza edge.

**dominant emotional shift**: Awe $\rightarrow$ Confrontational Tension $\rightarrow$ Solemn Resolution.

**likely visual coverage families**:
* Wide shots of the arriving group of Martians to establish scale and presence.
* Close-ups on Sola's face (compassion) and Tars Tarkas (observation).
* Tight, high-tension close-up of the narrator's hand grabbing the warrior's arm.
* Reaction shots from the onlookers.

**likely continuity sensitivities**: The presence of the pistol; the physical contact between the narrator and the warrior; the wounded state of the Watch-thing.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To transition from physical combat to social/political consequence, establishing the protagonist's new status among Martians.

**Scene Summary:** Tars Tarkas, Sola, and a group of warriors arrive. They witness the aftermath and applaud the protagonist's bravery, interpreting his fight as a display of high status. While Sola shows compassion, the other Martians react with laughter at the spectacle.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- Tars Tarkas
- Sola
- Martian Warriors

**Participating Environments:** Interior chamber/area overlooking a plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Adrenaline $\rightarrow$ Confusion $\rightarrow$ Social Validation/Alienation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the group arrival to establish scale and presence.
- Reaction shots: Sola (concern) vs. Warriors (amusement).
- Medium shots of Tars Tarkas observing with respect.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The physical condition/exhaustion level of the protagonist.
- The positioning of the dead apes relative to the arriving Martians.

# Scene SC003: Emotional Payoff & Aftermath

## Scene Purpose
Resolve the chapter by showing the narrator's bond with Martians through defense of beast and receiving care, establishing emotional payoff.

## Scene Summary
Upon seeing his beast threatened by a warrior with pistol, narrator defends it, shooting the window to save it. Sola examines him for wounds. The narrator gains further respect and friendship from the Martians. This scene provides the emotional resolution and shows how the Martians' worship of physical prowess translates into care for the narrator.

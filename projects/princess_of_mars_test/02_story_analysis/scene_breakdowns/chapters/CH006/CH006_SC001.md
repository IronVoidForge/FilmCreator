# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish immediate life-or-death stakes and demonstrate the loyalty of the Watch-thing.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist is pinned by a massive Bull Ape. As its mate approaches with a stone cudgel to finish him, the Watch-thing leaps into the fray, engaging the mate in a brutal struggle to buy the protagonist time.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Watch-thing
- The Bull Ape
- The Mate (Bull Ape)

**Participating Environments:** Interior chamber, Martian city building.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Desperation $\rightarrow$ Fierce Loyalty.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tight close-ups on the protagonist's struggle/pinned position.
- Wide shots of the Watch-thing's intervention.
- Handheld, kinetic shots of the creature combat.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The positioning of the Bull Ape's limbs; the location of the fallen cudgel; the physical state (injuries) of the Watch-thing during the fight.

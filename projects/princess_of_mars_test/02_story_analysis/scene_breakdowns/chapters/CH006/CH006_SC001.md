# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish immediate life-threatening peril and introduce the Watch-thing's loyalty.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist is pinned by a massive bull ape. As its mate approaches with a stone cudgel to deliver a killing blow, the Watch-thing leaps into the fray to defend its master, engaging the first ape in a desperate struggle.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, The Watch-thing, Bull Ape 1, Bull Ape 2.

**Participating Environments:** Interior chamber overlooking a Martian plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Desperation/Hope.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme close-ups of the protagonist's struggle; wide shots of the massive apes' scale; low-angle shots of the Watch-thing's intervention.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The position of the protagonist under the first ape; the location/availability of the fallen cudgel.

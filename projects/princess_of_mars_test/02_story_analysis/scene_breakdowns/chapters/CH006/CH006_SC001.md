# Scene SC001: Capture & First Battle

## Scene Purpose
Establish the initial confrontation between narrator and bull ape warrior, introducing the threat and escalating tension through beast intervention.

## Scene Summary
The narrator is captured by a large bull ape warrior who intends to kill him with a cudgel. His beast attacks the executioner, leading to a fierce battle between the beast and the ape's mate. This scene sets up the dramatic stakes and introduces key visual elements that must remain consistent throughout the chapter.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (captured protagonist)
- Bull Ape Warrior (antagonist, ten or fifteen feet tall, four great arms, laterally located ears, snouts like African gorilla)
- Beast (protector, four great arms, paws transcending Martian men's muscles, eyes bulging from sockets during fight)

## Participating Environments
- Interior of building
- Doorway

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Tension → Action escalation

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide establishing shots for Bull Ape visual continuity
- Medium action sequences for cudgel impact
- Close-up on beast's eyes and arm positioning during battle
- Over-the-shoulder shots for narrator's perspective of threat

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Bull Ape height and arm count must remain consistent across all shots
- Beast arm positioning and paw details need continuity
- Cudgel material (stone) should be identifiable in close-ups
- Interior building lighting and doorway framing must match throughout scene

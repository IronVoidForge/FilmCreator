# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish immediate life-threatening peril and introduce the combat dynamic between the protagonist, the apes, and his guardian.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist is pinned by a massive ape-creature while its mate prepares to deliver a killing blow with a stone cudgel. The Watch-thing intervenes, engaging the second ape in a brutal struggle, though it begins to lose against the creature's superior strength.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Watch-thing
- Ape-creature 1 (The Pinner)
- Ape-creature 2 (The Attacker)

**Participating Environments:** Interior chamber/area overlooking a plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Desperation $\rightarrow$ Violent Escalation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tight close-ups on the protagonist's face (terror).
- Low-angle shots of the massive apes to emphasize scale.
- Handheld/shaky cam for the Watch-thing's combat struggle.
- Wide shots showing the physical disparity between the creatures.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Position of the protagonist under the first ape.
- The location and availability of the stone cudgel.
- The specific movements/limbs of the Watch-thing during the fight.

# SC001

**scene purpose**: To establish the immediate life-threatening stakes and demonstrate the brutal, sacrificial nature of the Watch-thing's loyalty.

**scene summary**: The narrator is pinned to the ground by a massive ape-creature. As its mate approaches to deliver a killing blow with a stone cudgel, the narrator's pet, the Watch-thing, intervenes. A violent struggle ensues between the two beasts, resulting in the Watch-thing being severely wounded and nearly suffocated.

**participating characters**: The Narrator, The Watch-thing, Ape-creature 1, Ape-creature 2.

**participating environments**: A chamber within a Martian building, overlooking a plaza.

**dominant emotional shift**: Terror $\rightarrow$ Desperation.

**likely visual coverage families**:
* Extreme close-ups of the narrator's face/eyes (terror).
* Low-angle shots of the massive ape looming over the narrator.
* Handheld, shaky footage during the beast-on-beast struggle to emphasize chaos.
* Close-ups of the Watch-thing being crushed/choked.

**likely continuity sensitivities**: The position of the narrator under the first ape; the level of injury/blood on the Watch-thing; the presence of the stone cudgel in the second ape's hand.

# SC001 - Capture & Execution Setup

## Scene Purpose
Establish narrator's vulnerable position and the immediate threat from Bull Ape. Introduce second ape with execution intent.

## Scene Summary
Narrator is captured by Bull Ape on Mars. Second ape arrives carrying stone cudgel to execute him. This scene sets up the dramatic tension of imminent death and introduces the intervention that will change everything.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (captured, vulnerable)
- Bull Ape (first - captor)
- Watch-thing (observer/intervener)

## Participating Environments
- Martian surface (Bull Ape territory)
- Combat zone (implied threat)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Fear → Desperation → Anticipation of intervention

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots establishing narrator's isolation
- Close-ups on narrator's fearful expression
- Medium shots showing Bull Ape's dominance
- Over-the-shoulder shots from Watch-thing's perspective

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Narrator's position relative to Bull Ape
- Second ape's entrance timing and positioning
- Stone cudgel visibility and placement
- Martian landscape consistency

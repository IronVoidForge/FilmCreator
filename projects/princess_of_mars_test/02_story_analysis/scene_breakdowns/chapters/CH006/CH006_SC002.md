# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To showcase the protagonist's combat prowess using Earthly tactics and resolve.

**Scene Summary:** Seeing his guardian losing, the protagonist kills the first ape with a cudgel. The mate returns in a rage; rather than fleeing, the protagonist uses precise strikes to the chin and stomach to incapacitate and ultimately kill the second ape.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Watch-thing (wounded/dying)
- The Mate (Bull Ape)

**Participating Environments:** Interior chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Panic $\rightarrow$ Resolve $\rightarrow$ Adrenaline-fueled triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of the protagonist's tactical movements.
- Impact shots (the strike to the chin/stomach).
- Close-ups on the protagonist's face showing determination over fear.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The direction of the cudgel swings; blood splatter patterns; the physical exhaustion levels of the protagonist.

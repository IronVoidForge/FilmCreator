# Scene SC002: Intervention & Victory

## Scene Purpose
Show the narrator's decisive action in turning the tide of battle and achieving victory over the bull ape warrior.

## Scene Summary
The narrator intervenes with a cudgel, killing the mate. The bull ape returns enraged, but Tars Tarkas and warriors arrive. The narrator fights and defeats the bull ape alone. This scene represents the climax of the physical confrontation and establishes the narrator's capability and courage in the eyes of the Martians.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (protagonist, now active defender)
- Bull Ape Warrior (antagonist, enraged after mate's death)
- Tars Tarkas (Martian warrior leader)
- Martians Warriors (supporting characters, worship physical prowess and bravery)

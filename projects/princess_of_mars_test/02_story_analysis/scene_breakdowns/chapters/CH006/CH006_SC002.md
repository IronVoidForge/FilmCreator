# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To showcase the protagonist's lethal combat capability and resolve the first threat.

**Scene Summary:** The Watch-thing begins to lose its struggle against the ape's superior strength. Seizing a moment of opportunity, the protagonist grabs a fallen stone cudgel and delivers a crushing blow to the ape's skull, killing it.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, The Watch-thing, Bull Ape 1.

**Participating Environments:** Interior chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Violent Triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Handheld/shaky cam for the struggle; slow-motion or high-impact close-up on the killing blow; reaction shots of the Watch-thing.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The blood/gore from the skull crush; the physical state (wounds) of the Watch-thing.

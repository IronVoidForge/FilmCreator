# SC002

**scene purpose**: To showcase the narrator's unique combat style (Earthly boxing and primitive weaponry) as a way to overcome superior physical strength.

**scene summary**: Driven by rage for his wounded pet, the narrator kills the first ape with a fallen cudgel. The second ape, enraged by its mate's death, attacks. The narrator utilizes Earthly boxing techniques—specifically targeting the chin and stomach—to destabilize the creature before finishing it with the cudgel.

**participating characters**: The Narrator, The Watch-thing (wounded), Ape-creature 1 (deceased), Ape-creature 2.

**participating environments**: The Martian chamber.

**dominant emotional_shift**: Rage $\rightarrow$ Triumphant Exhaustion.

**likely visual coverage families**:
* Medium shots capturing the "boxing" footwork and punches.
* Impact shots: Close-ups of the cudgel striking the skull/stomach.
* Wide shots to show the scale difference between the man and the 15ft ape.

**likely continuity_sensitivities**: The location of the fallen cudgel; the state of the narrator's clothing/physical condition after the fight; the position of the dead first ape.

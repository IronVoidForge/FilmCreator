# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To showcase the protagonist's combat prowess and resolve through a climax of action.

**Scene Summary:** Seeing his guardian failing, the protagonist seizes a fallen cudgel and kills the first ape by crushing its skull. The second ape, enraged, charges. The protagonist stays to defend the dying Watch-thing, using Earthly combat tactics (striking chin/stomach) to incapacitate and eventually kill the second beast.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Watch-thing (wounded/dying)
- Ape-creature 2

**Participating Environments:** Interior chamber/area overlooking a plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Ferocity $\rightarrow$ Exhausted Triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of the protagonist's tactical strikes.
- Close-ups on the impact of the cudgel (the kill).
- High-intensity close-ups of the protagonist's face during the second fight.
- Over-the-shoulder shots from the dying Watch-thing's perspective.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Blood splatter/gore levels on the protagonist and environment.
- The physical state/wounds of the Watch-thing.
- The trajectory and handling of the cudgel.

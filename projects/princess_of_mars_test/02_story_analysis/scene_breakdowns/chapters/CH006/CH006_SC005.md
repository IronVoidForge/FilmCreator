# SC005 - Emotional Payoff & Friendship Gained

## Scene Purpose
Final scene where narrator saves dog from bullet and gains friends on Mars.

## Scene Summary
Narrator saves Watch-thing from bullet fired by warrior. Demonstrates gratitude and compassion that Martians struggle to understand. By end, narrator has gained two friends on Mars: Sola and his loyal beast. This scene delivers the chapter's emotional resolution and thematic payoff.

- Narrator (protagonist)
- Tars Tarkas (friend)
- Sola (friend)
- Martian warriors (understanding allies)
- Watch-thing (saved ally)

- Combat zone aftermath area

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Conflict → Understanding → Friendship

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To transition from combat to social consequence and introduce Martian cultural values.

**Scene Summary:** Tars Tarkas, Sola, and a group of warriors arrive, applauding the protagonist's prowess. However, tension rises when the Martians attempt to execute the wounded Watch-thing. The protagonist physically intervenes to protect his beast from a warrior's weapon.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Tars Tarkas, Sola, Martian Warriors, The Watch-thing.

**Participating Environments:** Interior chamber/Plaza edge.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Adrenaline $\rightarrow$ Social Tension $\rightarrow$ Defiance.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Group shots of the Martians; medium two-shots between the protagonist and Tars Tarkas; close-ups of the confrontation over the beast.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The presence/position of weapons; the visible wounds on the Watch-thing.

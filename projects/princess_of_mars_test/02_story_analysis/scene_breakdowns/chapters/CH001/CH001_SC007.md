# Scene SC007 - Morning Examination & Cave Reconnaissance

## Scene Purpose
Aftermath and investigation. Morning examination reveals Powell confirmed dead despite resuscitation efforts. Carter explores cave interior, establishing the location that will become his undiscovered resting place.

Morning arrives; painstaking examination reveals no spark of life despite water forced between lips and continuous resuscitation efforts. Carter enters cave to examine - finds large chamber possibly hundred feet diameter, thirty or forty feet height; smooth well-worn floor with evidence of remote habitation. The scene provides emotional closure to Powell's death while establishing the cave as significant location.

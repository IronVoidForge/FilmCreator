# Scene SC006 - Wrong Trail to Cave Mouth

## Scene Purpose
Transition to new location. Carter enters defile leading to range summit instead of pass to valley - mistake that saves his life. Discovers cave mouth, setting up final scene.

The scene marks the transition from action sequence to aftermath investigation.

# SC003

## Purpose
Resolve the immediate conflict, transition Carter to a new location, and establish the beginning of his long-term stay.

## Summary
Carter charges into enemy lines, shooting Apaches until they flee. He retrieves Powell's body, escapes through a defile into a large cave. He lays Powell down, fails to resuscitate him, enters the cave, and falls asleep.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter
- Captain James K. Powell (Body)

## Participating Environments
- Defile
- Large Cave

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Rage/Action to Grief/Fatigue/Surrender.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Action: Charge and Escape
- Static Interior: Cave exploration

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- State of Powell's body (dead/unresuscitated).
- Carter's physical exhaustion level upon falling asleep.

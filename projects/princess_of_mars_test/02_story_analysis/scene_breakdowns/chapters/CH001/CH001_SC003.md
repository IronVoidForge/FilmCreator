# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the primary conflict and physical threat.

**Scene Summary:** Carter notices suspicious movement in the valley. Suspecting an ambush, he pursues the trail and discovers a large Apache camp on a plateau, leading to a chaotic skirmish.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (Younger), Apache Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Arizona hills/plateau.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Suspicion $\rightarrow$ Alertness $\rightarrow$ Chaos/Adrenaline.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Handheld/shaky cam for the skirmish, POV shots of the movement in the valley, wide shots of the Apache camp, medium shots of Carter engaging in combat.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Number of warriors, direction of pursuit, weapon handling (rifles/sidearms).

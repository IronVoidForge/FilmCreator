# Scene SC003 - Deserted Campsite Discovery

## Scene Purpose
Build tension through the discovery of an empty campsite. Establish that Powell's party has been ambushed and escape is imminent, raising the stakes dramatically.

## Scene Summary
Midnight arrival at expected campsite reveals entirely deserted location with no recent occupation signs. Carter hears faint report of two shots far ahead and urges horse to topmost speed up mountain trail. The scene creates urgency as Carter realizes Powell's party has been attacked and he must follow immediately.

## Participating Characters
- Captain John Carter (primary)
- Horse (supporting character)
- Apaches (implied through shots)

## Participating Environments
- Deserted campsite location
- Mountain trail at midnight
- Arizona moonlight conditions

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Shock → Urgency → Determination to follow

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of deserted campsite
- Low-angle shots showing empty water hole
- Tracking shots of horse movement
- Close-ups on Carter's reaction to shots

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Midnight timing markers
- Empty campsite details (no occupation signs)
- Shot sound consistency
- Horse speed and direction
- Trail leading up mountain

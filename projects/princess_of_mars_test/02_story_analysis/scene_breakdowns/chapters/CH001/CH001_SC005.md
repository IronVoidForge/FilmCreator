# Scene SC005 - The Charge and Escape

## Scene Purpose
Action climax. Carter single-handedly charges the warrior army, creating chaos and escape opportunity. Demonstrates character heroism and sets up the escape sequence.

## Scene Summary
Carter single-handedly charges entire warrior army shooting rapidly and whooping. Apaches flee in terror. Carter pulls Powell's body across saddle while pursued by Indians but reaches shadows of surrounding peaks. The scene combines action with emotional payoff as Carter avenges his friend.

## Participating Characters
- Captain John Carter (primary)
- Horse (supporting character)
- Apaches (fleeing warriors)
- Powell (body on saddle)

## Participating Environments
- Apache camp area
- Mountain trail to peak shadows
- Surrounding peaks

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Action → Relief → Pursuit tension

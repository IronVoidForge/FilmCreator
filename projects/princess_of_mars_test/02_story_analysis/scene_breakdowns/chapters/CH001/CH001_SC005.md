# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the supernatural element and transition to the next narrative phase.

**Scene Summary:** Carter's horse leads him to a large cave at the end of a cliffside trail. After checking Powell, Carter enters the cave to scout it, only to be overcome by an unnatural drowsiness that causes him to collapse.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Captain James K. Powell (body).

**Participating Environments:** Cliffside trail; Large mysterious cave.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion/Fear $\rightarrow$ Confusion $\rightarrow$ Unnatural Lethargy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the cliffside path; Tight close-ups on Carter's eyes/face as he loses consciousness; Slow, drifting camera movements to mimic dizziness.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting transition (outdoor light to cave darkness); Carter's physical state (sweat/dirt).

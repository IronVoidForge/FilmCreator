# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Transition the protagonist from the terrestrial world to the inciting supernatural event.

**Scene Summary:** Carter's horse leads him to a narrow cave entrance on a high trail. After checking Powell, Carter enters the large, ancient chamber. Inside, he is overcome by an unnatural drowsiness and collapses into unconsciousness.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (Younger).

**Participating Environments:** High cliffside trail, Ancient Cave interior.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion $\rightarrow$ Confusion $\rightarrow$ Lethargy/Loss of consciousness.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the cave entrance on the cliff, low-angle shots inside the chamber to emphasize scale, close-ups of Carter's eyes drooping, slow fade to black.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting transition (from bright exterior to dark/dim cave), the "unnatural" quality of the drowsiness (visual cues like heavy eyelids or blurred vision).

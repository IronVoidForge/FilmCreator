# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the immediate conflict and transition into the "disappearance" status quo.

**Scene Summary:** Carter's horse leads him away from the pursuit toward a cliff, where he finds an ancient cave. After confirming Powell is dead, Carter enters the cave, but exhaustion and a strange drowsiness overcome him, causing him to collapse.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** A narrow trail between a cliff and a ravine; a large, ancient cave.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From frantic survival to profound exhaustion and mysterious lethargy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the desolate landscape/cliff; tight close-ups on Carter's eyes as he succumbs to sleep; slow fade to black.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The lighting inside the cave (dimming); Carter's physical state (dirt, blood, exhaustion).

# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Transition the protagonist to a new state of being and introduce the supernatural element.

**Scene Summary:** While fleeing pursuit, Carter finds refuge in an ancient mountain cave, only to be overcome by an inexplicable, heavy sleep.

**Participating Characters:**
* John Carter

**Participating Environments:**
* High cliff trail / The Ancient Cave

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From desperate flight/exhaustion to eerie, unnatural lethargy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Deep shadow cinematography within the cave.
* Slow zooms onto Carter's face as he loses consciousness.
* Wide shots showing the isolation of the cave entrance.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
* Lighting transition (from daylight/twilight to cave darkness).
* Carter's level of physical exhaustion/injury.

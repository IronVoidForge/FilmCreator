# SC001

## Purpose
Establish Carter's internal state regarding mortality and set the historical context of the 1865 gold expedition.

## Summary
John Carter reflects on his fear of death despite surviving two previous deaths. He decides to document his life, focusing on the events surrounding a gold discovery with Captain James K. Powell.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (Narrator)
- Captain James K. Powell (Mentioned/Flashback context)

## Participating Environments
- Mental Landscape (Reflection)
- Gold Claim Site (Arizona Hills)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Philosophical contemplation transitioning to anticipation of action.

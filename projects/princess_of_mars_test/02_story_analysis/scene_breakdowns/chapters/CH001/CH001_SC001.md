# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the setting, the protagonists' bond, and the high stakes of their discovery.

**Scene Summary:** In the rugged Arizona mountains, Captain John Carter and Captain James K. Powell celebrate the discovery of a massive gold vein, marking a turning point in their post-Civil War lives.

**Participating Characters:**
* John Carter
* James K. Powell

**Participating Environments:**
* Arizona Mountains (Mining site)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From exhaustion/toil to triumph and excitement.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Wide establishing shots of the desolate mountain landscape.
* Close-ups of the gold vein and the characters' weathered faces.
* Medium shots of Carter and Powell interacting over the find.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
* Condition of mining tools/picks.
* Lighting (time of day during the discovery).

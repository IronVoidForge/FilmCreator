# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the setting, the stakes (the gold vein), and the inciting incident (Powell's departure).

**Scene Summary:** In the Arizona mountains, 1865, John Carter and James Powell discover a massive gold-bearing quartz vein. Powell decides to leave to secure machinery, leaving Carter alone to guard the claim.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Captain James K. Powell.

**Participating Environments:** Remote gold-bearing quartz vein, Arizona mountains.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Excitement/Triumph $\rightarrow$ Solitude/Anticipation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide establishing shots of the landscape; Close-ups on the gold vein; Medium two-shots of Carter and Powell discussing plans.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Gold vein appearance; Equipment/supplies left with Carter.

# Scene SC001 - Powell's Departure

## Scene Purpose
Establish Captain John Carter's isolation and claim to the Arizona territory after his friend Powell leaves. Set up the dramatic tension of a lone man holding ground against potential threats.

## Scene Summary
March 3, 1866. Captain John Carter stands alone in the Arizona hills following Powell's departure with burros and provisions toward the valley. Carter holds his claim against prospectors who might otherwise take over the territory. The scene establishes Carter's resolve and the geographical stakes of this location.

## Participating Characters
- Captain John Carter (primary)
- Powell (mentioned/absent - departed)

## Participating Environments
- Arizona hills landscape
- March 1866 setting
- Territory boundary area

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Solitude → Determination → Growing unease (foreshadowing)

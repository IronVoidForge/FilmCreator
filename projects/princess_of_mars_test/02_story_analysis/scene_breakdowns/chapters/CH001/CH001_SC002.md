# SC002

## Purpose
Depict the escalation from guarding duty to violent conflict, driving the plot toward the discovery of Powell's death.

## Summary
Carter guards the claim while Powell leaves for machinery. Apaches pursue the party. Carter chases them down the trail and discovers Powell dead at a campsite surrounded by warriors.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter
- Apache Warriors (Antagonists)
- Captain James K. Powell (Victim)

## Participating Environments
- Trail
- Campsite

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Duty to Shock and Horror.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Tracking Shot: Chase sequence
- Handheld: Combat at campsite

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Position of Powell's body relative to the camp perimeter.
- Number of Apache warriors visible vs. implied threat.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Build tension and transition the protagonist from a prospector to a tracker.

**Scene Summary:** Carter notices suspicious movement in the valley. Believing it to be an Apache raiding party, he tracks the signs through the night, moving deeper into hostile territory.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** Arizona hills/valleys; Nighttime wilderness.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Vigilance $\rightarrow$ Growing Tension/Urgency.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** POV shots of tracks/signs; Low-light handheld tracking shots of Carter moving through brush; Extreme wide shots to emphasize isolation.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting (moonlight vs. darkness); Direction of travel; Condition of Carter's gear.

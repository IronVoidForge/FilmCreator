# Scene SC002 - The Mountain Chase Begins

## Scene Purpose
Initiate the chase sequence through the mountainside. Introduce the discovery of Apache tracks joining Powell's trail, escalating the threat level from prospectors to hostile warriors.

## Scene Summary
Armed with two Colt revolvers, carbine, and cartridge belts, Carter follows Powell's morning trail down the mountainside. At dusk, he discovers unshod pony tracks joining Powell's trail - evidence that ponies had been galloping. The scene transitions from concern about prospectors to realization of Apache involvement.

- Powell (absent - dead)
- Apaches (implied through tracks)

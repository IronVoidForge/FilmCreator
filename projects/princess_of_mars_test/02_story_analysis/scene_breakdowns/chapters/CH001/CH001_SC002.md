# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the inciting incident and the stakes (the gold claim).

**Scene Summary:** In 1865, Carter and Powell discover a massive gold-bearing quartz vein in the Arizona hills. They agree on a plan: Powell will head to civilization for machinery while Carter stays to guard the site.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (Younger), Captain James K. Powell.

**Participating Environments:** Remote Arizona quartz vein/hills.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Excitement / Triumph $\rightarrow$ Determination.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the landscape, medium shots of the men inspecting the vein, close-ups of the gold/quartz, two-shots for the planning dialogue.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Appearance of the gold vein, gear/clothing of 1865 prospectors, lighting (bright desert sun).

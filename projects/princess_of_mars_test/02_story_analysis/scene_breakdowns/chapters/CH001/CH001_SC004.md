# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Raise the emotional stakes and transition to the escape.

**Scene Summary:** Amidst the skirmish, Carter discovers Powell's body, killed by arrows. Devastated, Carter retrieves the body and attempts to flee from the pursuing warriors.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (Younger), Captain James K. Powell (Deceased), Apache Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The trail/valley near the ambush site.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Horror / Grief $\rightarrow$ Desperation / Urgency.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups of Carter's reaction to Powell, medium shots of the struggle to move the body, tracking shots of the flight/pursuit.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Condition of Powell's body (arrow placement), level of injury on Carter, proximity of pursuers.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Deliver the emotional tragedy and consequence of the action.

**Scene Summary:** In the aftermath of the skirmish, Carter discovers that his friend Powell has been killed by Apache arrows.

**Participating Characters:**
* John Carter
* James K. Powell (Deceased)

**Participating Environments:**
* The periphery of the Apache camp/Trail area

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From the high of battle to profound grief and horror.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Slow, somber tracking shots following Carter's movement.
* Extreme close-ups of Powell's wounds and Carter's reaction.
* Static, heavy compositions to emphasize the weight of death.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
* Blood/arrow wound placement on Powell.
* Carter's physical state (sweat, dirt, dishevelment from battle).

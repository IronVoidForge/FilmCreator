# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Deliver the emotional gut-punch and shift the tone from action to tragedy.

**Scene Summary:** Amidst the chaos of the skirmish, Carter finds Powell's body, killed by arrows. He retrieves the body and begins a desperate flight from the pursuing warriors.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Captain James K. Powell (deceased).

**Participating Environments:** The plateau/skirmish site.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Adrenaline $\rightarrow$ Shock/Grief/Desperation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups on Carter's reaction; Medium shots of the body discovery; Tracking shots of the flight/escape.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Condition of Powell's clothing/body; Presence of arrows; Direction of pursuit.

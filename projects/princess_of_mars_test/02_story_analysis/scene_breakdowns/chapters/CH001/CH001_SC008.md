# Scene SC008 - Collapse and Chapter Resolution

## Scene Purpose
Chapter resolution. Carter's physical state at chapter end - exhausted from long strenuous ride and fight/pursuit excitement. Sets up his unconscious state in cave for ten years.

## Scene Summary
Carter feels pleasant drowsiness from fatigue and excitement; resists collapsing on cave floor due to Apache threat; reels drunkenly against side wall then slips prone upon cave floor. Chapter ends with Carter physically exhausted, mentally resolved to write chronicle of interesting periods of life and death, currently unconscious or semi-conscious on cave floor unaware this location will become his undiscovered resting place for ten years.

## Participating Characters
- Captain John Carter (primary - collapsing)
- Horse (supporting character)
- Apaches (threat outside cave)

## Participating Environments
- Cave floor interior
- Side wall of cave chamber
- Dense shadow areas

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Exhaustion → Resistance → Unconscious surrender

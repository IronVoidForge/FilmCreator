# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the political tension through a climax of leadership and combat.

**Scene Summary:** In the palace, Tal Hajus orders Carter's torture. Carter appeals to the council, accusing Tal Hajus of cowardice and championing Tars Tarkas. A duel ensues where Tars Tarkas kills Tal Hajus.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tal Hajus, Tars Tarkas, Thark Council members.

**Participating Environments:** The Thark Palace/Council Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Oppression to Defiance to Violent Resolution.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the council assembly; medium shots for the verbal debate; intense close-ups during the duel; reaction shots from the Council.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Positioning of characters in the council circle; weapon types used in the duel.

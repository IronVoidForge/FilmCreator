# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Climax of the political arc; transition of power.

**Scene Summary:** In a formal council, John Carter challenges Tal Hajus's leadership based on his cowardice. This leads to a sanctioned duel between Tars Tarkas and Tal Hajus. Tars Tarkas emerges victorious, becoming Jeddak and elevating Carter to chieftain status.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Tal Hajus, Lorquas Ptomel, Thark Council members.

**Participating Environments:** Thark (Council Chamber).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Defiance $\rightarrow$ High Tension $\rightarrow$ Victory/Ascension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the council assembly; Dynamic combat coverage for the duel; Reaction shots from the various chieftains.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical state of the duelists; The shifting hierarchy/seating in the chamber.

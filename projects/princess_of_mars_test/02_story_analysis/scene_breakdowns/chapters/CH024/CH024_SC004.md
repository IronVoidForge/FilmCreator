# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Transition from political victory to military mobilization.

**Scene Summary:** Leveraging his new status, John Carter proposes a massive raid on Zodanga to rescue Dejah Thoris and seize wealth. The Thark hordes enthusiastically agree, and a force of 150,000 Green Martians begins their march.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Thark Hordes.

**Participating Environments:** Thark (City/Plains).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Ambition $\rightarrow$ Collective Excitement/War Fever.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of Carter delivering the proposal.
- Wide sweeping shots of the massive army marching out of Thark.
- Montage elements showing the mobilization.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Army size/composition, time of day (transitioning to night for the next scene).

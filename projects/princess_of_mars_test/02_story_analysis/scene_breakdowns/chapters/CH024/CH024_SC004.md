# SC004 - New Jeddak and Alliance Proposal

## Scene Purpose
Tars Tarkas becomes new Jeddak of Thark. He is made a chieftain and proposes an alliance with Helium to rescue Dejah Thoris from Zodanga. The Tharks agree, gathering a massive force of 150,000 warriors.

## Scene Summary
Following the combat victory, Tars Tarkas is formally recognized as the new Jeddak of Thark and elevated to chieftain status. In this position of power, he proposes an alliance with Helium to rescue Dejah Thoris from Zodanga. The Thark council agrees to the proposal. They begin gathering a massive force of 150,000 warriors for the upcoming campaign.

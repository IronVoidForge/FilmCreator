**Scene Purpose:** Depict the logistical scale of the Thark horde's mobilization and the journey toward Zodanga.

**Scene Summary:** A force of 150,000 warriors marches toward Zodanga over ten days. They travel by night and camp in deserted cities during the day. John Carter rides beside Tars Tarkas; Woola trots at the heels of his mount.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Woola (dog), Thark Horde (mass).

**Participating Environments:** Marching route (night travel), Deserted cities (day camps), Open plains.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation/Scale -> Endurance/Process.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme wide shots of the horde at night, Tracking shots along the march path, Medium shots of Carter and Tarkas riding together, Establishing shots of deserted cities.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Night/day cycle transitions, Camp locations relative to route, Consistency of horde size in wide shots.

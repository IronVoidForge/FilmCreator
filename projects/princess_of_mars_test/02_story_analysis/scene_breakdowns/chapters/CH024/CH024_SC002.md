# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce political conflict and immediate tension following the victory.

**Scene Summary:** Carter returns to Thark and reunites with Woola, but the celebration is cut short when Tal Hajus orders him to be brought before the council for punishment. Carter has a brief, tense encounter with Sarkoja before being marched toward the palace.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Woola, Tal Hajus, Sarkoja.

**Participating Environments:** Thark settlement, paths leading to the palace.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief/Joy (Woola) to Dread/Tension (Tal Hajus).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of reunion; low-angle intimidating shots of Tal Hajus; tight close-ups on Sarkoja's enigmatic expressions.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's physical condition (injuries from SC001); Woola's proximity to Carter.

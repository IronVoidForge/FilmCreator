# SC006

**Scene Purpose:** Execute the tactical infiltration and set up the final assault.

**Scene Summary:** After a ten-day march, the Thark army reaches Zodanga. John Carter leads a stealthy infiltration, scaling the carborundum walls using human ladders and leather straps to enter the palace grounds and prepare the gates for the main attack.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Small group of Thark warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Walls/Palace Grounds of Zodanga.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Stealthy Tension $\rightarrow$ Tactical Focus.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Vertical tracking shots of the wall scaling; Tight close-ups on the climbing equipment (straps/ladders); POV shots from Carter's perspective looking into the city.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting (night/stealth conditions); The physical movement of the "human ladder."

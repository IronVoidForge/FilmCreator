# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Show the scale of the mobilization and transition to the next setting.

**Scene Summary:** Tars Tarkas assembles 150,000 Tharks. The massive expeditionary force marches under the cover of night toward Zodanga.

**Participating Characters:** Tars Tarkas, John Carter, Thark Army.

**Participating Environments:** Martian plains/wilderness at night.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination and mounting scale.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme wide shots (EWS) of the massive army; silhouette shots against the night sky; tracking shots of marching feet/gear.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting (moonlight vs. darkness); direction of march.

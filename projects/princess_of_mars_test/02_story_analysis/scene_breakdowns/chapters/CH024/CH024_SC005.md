# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Execute a high-stakes infiltration mission.

**Scene Summary:** Under cover of night, John Carter leads a small group to infiltrate Zodanga. They scale the massive carborundum walls using a human ladder. Carter successfully enters the palace grounds and begins coordinating the silent takeover of the city gates.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Small Group of Tharks.

**Participating Environments:** Zodanga (The Walls/Palace Grounds).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Stealth/Tension $\rightarrow$ High Stakes Action $\rightarrow$ Calculated Control.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Night photography with high contrast (shadows vs. moonlight).
- Vertical tracking shots of the wall scaling.
- Close-ups on Carter's face to convey focus and stealth.
- POV shots from the top of the walls looking down into the city.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting/Moonlight direction, height of the walls, placement of the "human ladder" elements.

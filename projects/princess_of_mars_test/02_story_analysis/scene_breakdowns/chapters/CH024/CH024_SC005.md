# SC005 - March on Zodanga

## Scene Purpose
Tharks agree, gathering a massive force of 150,000 warriors. They march on Zodanga and enter the city through a gate constructed by John Carter using his men as steps.

## Scene Summary
The Thark army begins their march toward Zodanga with 150,000 warriors assembled. The journey shows the massive scale of the force moving across the Martian landscape. Upon reaching Zodanga, they enter the city through a special gate constructed by John Carter using his men as steps. This demonstrates John Carter's continued contribution to the campaign and provides a dramatic entrance into the enemy city.

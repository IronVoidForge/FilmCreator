# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the new mission and scale of the upcoming conflict.

**Scene Summary:** Now a chieftain, John Carter proposes an ambitious raid on Zodanga to rescue Dejah Thoris. The Thark hordes enthusiastically commit to the cause, amassing a massive army of 150,000 warriors.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Thark Warriors/Hordes.

**Participating Environments:** Thark (City).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Collective Zeal.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the gathering masses; Medium shots of Carter delivering his proposal; Close-ups of the warriors' fervor.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Scale of the army depicted in wide shots.

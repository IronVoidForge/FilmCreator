# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish John Carter's combat prowess and create a pivotal bond with Tars Tarkas.

**Scene Summary:** During a chaotic battle between Tharks and Warhoons, John Carter's craft is downed. He intervenes in a life-or-death struggle to save Tars Tarkas from three Warhoon warriors, proving his worth through sheer strength.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Warhoon Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Battlefield (Plains near an ancient dead city).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Chaos/Peril $\rightarrow$ Heroic Intervention $\rightarrow$ Mutual Respect.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the battlefield chaos; Medium tracking shots of Carter's descent; Close-ups of combat and eye contact between Carter and Tars Tarkas.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Damage to Carter's craft; Blood/injury status of Tars Tarkas; Weaponry used in the melee.

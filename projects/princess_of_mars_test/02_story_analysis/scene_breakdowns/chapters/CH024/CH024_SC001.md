# SC001 - Crash Landing and Rescue of Tars Tarkas

## Scene Purpose
John Carter crashes his craft into a fierce battle between Tharks and Warhoons on ancient Mars. He saves Tars Tarkas from death by Warhoons, earning the green warrior's respect and friendship. Woola, the dog, also returns to Tharks.

## Scene Summary
The scene opens with John Carter's crash landing amidst chaotic battlefield action. He encounters Tars Tarkas being attacked by Warhoon warriors. Through combat prowess, John saves Tars Tarkas from certain death. The rescue establishes a bond between the two characters and introduces Woola rejoining his Thark companions.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter** - Protagonist, crash survivor
- **Tars Tarkas** - Green warrior, being attacked
- **Woola** - Dog companion, returning to Tharks
- **Thark warriors** - Allies in the battle
- **Warhoon warriors** - Antagonists attacking Tars Tarkas

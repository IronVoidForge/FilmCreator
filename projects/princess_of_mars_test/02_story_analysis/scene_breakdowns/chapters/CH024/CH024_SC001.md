# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish high-stakes action and demonstrate Carter's combat prowess/heroism.

**Scene Summary:** While flying over a dead city, Carter's craft is shot down amidst a massive battle between green warriors. He crashes into the fray, saves Tars Tarkas from three Warhoons, and fights alongside him until the enemy retreats.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Warhoon Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Ancient dead city (aerial/battlefield), combat zone.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From isolation/flight to chaotic survival to triumphant partnership.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the massive battle; handheld/shaky cam for the crash; close-up combat choreography; sweeping aerials of the dead city.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Damage state of Carter's craft; blood/dirt levels on characters after the fight; weapon positions.

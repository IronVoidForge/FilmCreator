# SC007

**Scene Purpose:** Set up the final battle climax through strategic coordination.

**Scene Summary:** Having secured knowledge to open the gates, Carter coordinates a multi-pronged plan: one group to seize the great gates and another detachment to strike at the palace heart.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Infiltration Team, Detachment Leaders.

**Participating Environments:** Zodanga (atop walls/near gates).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Strategic intensity and readiness for war.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of Carter directing troops; wide shots showing the different moving parts of the plan; close-ups on maps or tactical signals.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Position of the various detachments relative to the gates and palace.

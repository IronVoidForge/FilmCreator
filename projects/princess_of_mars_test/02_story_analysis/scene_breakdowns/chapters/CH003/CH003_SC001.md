# Scene 001 - The Narrator Wakes and Discovers Low Gravity

## Scene Purpose
Establish the narrator's disorientation upon waking on Mars and reveal the low gravity environment through physical action.

## Scene Summary
The narrator awakens in a circular glass incubation basin on Mars. Upon standing, they immediately jump three yards due to significantly lower gravity. They attempt to walk but hop and sprawl from unfamiliar muscle dynamics, demonstrating their complete lack of adaptation to this alien environment.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (first-person perspective)

## Participating Environments
- Circular glass incubation basin on Mars surface
- Yellowish mosslike vegetation surrounding the basin
- Quartz-bearing rock formations visible in background
- Low hills in distance
- Solid glass roof (4-5 inches thick) overhead

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Confusion and disorientation → Physical realization of alien environment through action

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide establishing shots showing narrator's jump from standing position
- Medium action shots capturing hopping and sprawling movements
- Close-up reaction shots of narrator's facial expressions during physical reactions
- Overhead or high-angle shots emphasizing the circular basin geometry

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Gravity effects must be consistent throughout scene (three-yard jumps)
- Narrator's physical reactions to low gravity must match established patterns
- Glass incubation basin appearance and transparency must remain consistent
- Environmental details (vegetation, rock formations) must not shift between shots
- Camera angles showing narrator's height relative to environment must be maintained

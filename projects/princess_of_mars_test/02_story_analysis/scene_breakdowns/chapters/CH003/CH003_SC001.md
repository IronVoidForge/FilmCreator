# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's disorientation and the physical reality of the Martian environment (low gravity).

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist wakes up on Mars, naked and confused. He discovers that the low gravity makes even minor movements result in massive, uncontrolled leaps across the yellowish mossy basin.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist

**Participating Environments:**
- A circular basin on Mars covered in yellowish, moss-like vegetation.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Disorientation/Physical Shock.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the vast Martian landscape to establish scale.
- Medium/Close-up tracking shots following the protagonist's erratic, leaping movements.
- POV shots showing the ground rushing up and away.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The height and distance of the leaps must remain consistent with the "ludicrous" physics described.
- Protagonist's physical state (naked/unarmed).

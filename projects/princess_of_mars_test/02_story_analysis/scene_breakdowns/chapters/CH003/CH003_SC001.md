# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's new physical reality and the alien environment.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist wakes up on Mars, naked and disoriented. He discovers that the low gravity makes normal movement impossible, causing him to leap uncontrollably with every step.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist.

**Participating Environments:** A circular basin on Mars covered in yellowish, moss-like vegetation.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Physical Bewilderment/Surprise.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots of the Martian landscape.
- Medium shots of the protagonist's clumsy, leaping movements.
- Close-ups of his facial expressions (disorientation).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The height and distance of the leaps; the protagonist's physical state (nakedness/exposure).

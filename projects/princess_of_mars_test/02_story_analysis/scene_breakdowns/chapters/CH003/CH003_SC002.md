# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the biological horror/wonder of Martian life.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist explores a nearby glass-roofed enclosure (an incubator) and finds large white eggs alongside grotesque, multi-limbed young Martians that have already hatched.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist.

**Participating Environments:** The glass-roofed incubator enclosure.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Horror/Awe.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Macro shots of the eggs and the multi-limbed hatchlings.
- POV shots from the protagonist looking through the glass.
- Medium shots of his reaction to the grotesque creatures.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The movement patterns of the hatchlings; lighting through the glass roof.

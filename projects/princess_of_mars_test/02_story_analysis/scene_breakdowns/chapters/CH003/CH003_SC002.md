# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the alien biology and the presence of other Martian life forms.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist explores a nearby low-walled enclosure with a thick glass roof. Inside, he discovers large white eggs and several grotesque, six-limbed young Martians that have already hatched.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist

**Participating Environments:**
- The Incubator (glass-roofed enclosure).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Horror/Awe.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Macro shots of the eggs and the hatchlings.
- Medium shots of the protagonist peering through the glass.
- Low-angle shots to emphasize the strange anatomy of the young Martians.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The appearance and movement patterns of the six-limbed hatchlings.
- Lighting/reflections on the thick glass roof.

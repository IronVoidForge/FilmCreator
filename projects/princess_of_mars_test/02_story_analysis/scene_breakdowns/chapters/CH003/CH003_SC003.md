# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the primary antagonist force and create immediate life-threatening tension.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist hears the rattle of equipment and realizes a party of twenty mounted Martian warriors is approaching. He narrowly avoids being killed by the leader's spear as they pass near his hiding spot.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Martian Warriors (Cavalcade).

**Participating Environments:** The basin/vegetation area near the incubator.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Terror/Panic.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-angle shots of the approaching mounts to emphasize scale.
- Close-ups of the spear tip narrowly missing the protagonist.
- Rapid cutting between the warriors and the protagonist's hiding position.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The speed/rhythm of the mounted warriors; the proximity of the spear to the protagonist.

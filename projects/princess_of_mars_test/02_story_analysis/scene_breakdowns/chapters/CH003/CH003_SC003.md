# Scene 003 - Adult Martians Approach and Nearly Strike Narrator

## Scene Purpose
Create conflict and tension through the arrival of adult Martians and their threatening approach.

## Scene Summary
A group of adult Martians approaches the narrator near the incubation chamber. The leader's spear nearly strikes the narrator, but the sound alerts the leader. This establishes immediate threat and raises tension before the narrator's escape action.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (first-person perspective)
- Adult Martians (group)
- Martian Leader

## Participating Environments
- Mars basin surface near incubation chamber
- Yellowish mosslike vegetation surrounding area
- Quartz-bearing rock formations visible
- Low hills in background

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Tension and threat awareness → Immediate danger recognition

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Low angle shots showing approaching Martians (15 feet tall)
- Medium shots of leader's spear movement (40-foot metal-tipped)
- Close-up reaction shots of narrator's fear response
- Wide shots establishing group formation and positioning

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Martian physical traits must match established specifications:
  - 15 feet tall
  - Dark slate skin
  - Yellow feet
  - Small cup-shaped antennae ears
  - Blood red iris
  - White teeth with upward-curving tusks

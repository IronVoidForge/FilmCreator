# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Create high-stakes tension and demonstrate the protagonist's unique advantage in this environment.

**Scene Summary:** A party of twenty Martian warriors on multi-legged mounts charges toward the protagonist. The leader nearly kills him with a forty-foot spear, but the protagonist uses the low gravity to perform a massive thirty-foot leap over the incubator to escape death.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- Martian Warriors (The Cavalry)
- The Leader

**Participating Environments:**
- The Basin / The Incubator area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Adrenaline/Survival Instinct.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Rapid pans and tracking shots of the charging mounts.
- Extreme long shots to show the scale of the twenty-man cavalry.
- Slow-motion or heightened frame rate for the protagonist's massive leap.
- Close-ups of the spear narrowly missing.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The speed and rhythm of the eight-legged mounts.
- The trajectory and height of the protagonist's jump relative to the incubator.

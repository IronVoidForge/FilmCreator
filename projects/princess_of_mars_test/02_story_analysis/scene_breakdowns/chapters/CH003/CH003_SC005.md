# Scene 005 - Peace Token Offered and Cavalcade Departs

## Scene Purpose
Resolution showing Martian peace gesture and departure establishing new relationship between narrator and Martians.

## Scene Summary
The leader offers a metal armlet as a peace token to the narrator. The narrator accepts it, and they walk together before the leader mounts his animal (ten feet high at shoulder, four legs, broad flat tail, no hair, smooth glossy). The entire cavalcade departs towards the range of hills, establishing ongoing relationship.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (first-person perspective)
- Martian Leader
- Martians on mounts

## Participating Environments
- Mars basin surface near departure point
- Yellowish mosslike vegetation surrounding area
- Quartz-bearing rock formations visible
- Low hills in background (destination)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Acceptance → Departure with new relationship established

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shots of armlet exchange and acceptance gesture
- Wide shots showing leader mounting animal
- Overhead or high-angle shots of cavalcade formation
- Long shots following departure towards hills

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Mount appearance must match established specifications:
  - Ten feet high at shoulder
  - Four legs
  - Broad flat tail
  - No hair
  - Smooth glossy surface
  - No bridle/reins

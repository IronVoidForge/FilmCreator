# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Establish first contact and a tentative peace.

**Scene Summary:** The leader dismounts unarmed and offers the protagonist a metal armlet. The protagonist accepts, signaling peace. He is then lifted onto a Martian mount and taken away toward the distant hills.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, The Leader, Martian Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The basin/open landscape.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Relief/Cautious Acceptance.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-ups of the hand-off (the armlet).
- Medium shots of the non-verbal communication/eye contact.
- Wide shot of the cavalcade moving toward the distant hills.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The armlet on the protagonist's arm; the height difference when he is lifted onto the mount.

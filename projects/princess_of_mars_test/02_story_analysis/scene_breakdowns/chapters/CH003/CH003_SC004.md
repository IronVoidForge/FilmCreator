# Scene 004 - Narrator Leaps Away and Martians React with Surprise

## Scene Purpose
Show action consequence demonstrating narrator's agility and Martian reaction to unexpected capability.

## Scene Summary
The narrator leaps thirty feet into the air to escape, landing safely on the opposite side of the basin. The Martians are surprised by this unexpected agility. The leader dismounts unarmed, showing willingness to engage peacefully after witnessing narrator's capabilities.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (first-person perspective)
- Adult Martians (group)
- Martian Leader

## Participating Environments
- Mars basin surface
- Yellowish mosslike vegetation surrounding area
- Quartz-bearing rock formations visible
- Low hills in background

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Escape success → Martian surprise at narrator's capabilities

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide action shots capturing thirty-foot leap trajectory
- Medium reaction shots of Martians' surprised expressions
- Close-up shots of leader dismounting unarmed
- Overhead or high-angle shots showing landing position

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Leap distance and trajectory must be consistent (thirty feet)
- Landing position must match established location in basin
- Martians' reactions to narrator's agility must be shown consistently
- Leader's dismounting action must remain consistent across shots
- Group positioning before and after leap must be maintained

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the conflict through non-verbal communication and transition the protagonist into the next phase of the story.

**Scene Summary:** The Martians, impressed by the protagonist's agility, shift from aggression to curiosity. The leader approaches unarmed and offers a metal armlet as a peace gesture. The protagonist accepts, is hoisted onto a mount, and joins the cavalcade heading toward the hills.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Leader
- Martian Warriors

**Participating Environments:**
- The Basin / Moving towards distant hills.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Relief/Wonder/Acceptance.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-ups of the hand-off (the armlet).
- Medium shots capturing the non-verbal exchange of looks between the Leader and Protagonist.
- Wide sweeping shots of the cavalcade moving across the landscape toward the hills.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The metal armlet as a physical prop.
- The protagonist's position on the Martian mount during the departure.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Demonstrate the protagonist's unique advantage and change the power dynamic.

**Scene Summary:** To escape, the protagonist performs a massive thirty-foot leap, landing on the far side of the enclosure. The Martians witness this feat, shifting their view of him from prey to a "wonderful discovery."

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Martian Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The basin/enclosure area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Triumph $\rightarrow$ Observation (Martian side).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Slow-motion or wide tracking shots of the massive leap.
- Reaction shots from the Martian warriors (shock/interest).
- Wide shot showing the distance covered by the jump.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The trajectory and landing point of the leap; the visual scale of the Martians vs. the protagonist.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Demonstrate the protagonist's unique advantage (agility) and resolve the immediate conflict through action.

**Scene Summary:** Facing certain death, the protagonist utilizes the low gravity to perform a massive thirty-foot leap over the incubator, escaping the spear's reach. The Martians observe this with curiosity rather than hostility.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist; Martian Warriors (The Leader).

**Participating Environments:** The Incubator; the mossy basin.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Panic $\rightarrow$ Adrenaline/Exhilaration $\rightarrow$ Bewilderment.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Slow-motion or high-frame-rate shots of the leap.
- Extreme wide shots to capture the arc of the jump over the structure.
- Reaction shots from the Martians watching the feat.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The trajectory and landing point of the protagonist's leap; the proximity of the spear tip.

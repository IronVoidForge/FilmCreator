# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Deliver the central narrative reveal regarding Sola's lineage and Tars Tarkas.

**Scene Summary:** During the night, Sola shares her secret history with Carter. The scene culminates in the revelation that Sola's father is Tars Tarkas, who remains unaware of her existence.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** The Caravan (Night/Camp setting).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Intimacy $\rightarrow$ Tragedy $\rightarrow$ Shock/Revelation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tight two-shot for the intimate conversation.
- Close-ups on Sola's face to capture the emotional weight of her confession.
- Subtle lighting (firelight or moonlight) to enhance the mood of secrecy.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting consistency during the night dialogue; character positioning relative to the campfire/environment.

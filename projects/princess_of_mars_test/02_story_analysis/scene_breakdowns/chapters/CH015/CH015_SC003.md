# SC003

## Scene Purpose
Deliver the major revelation about Sola's parentage and Tars Tarkas's motivations, establishing a secret that will drive future plot developments.

## Scene Summary
While traveling with Tars Tarkas in the massive silent caravan of chariots and animals across the mossy sea bottom, Carter meets Sola again at night. She requests he listen to her story about her parents. Sola recounts how her mother (a small compassionate green Martian woman) fell in love with a warrior named Tars Tarkas while hiding an egg in an ancient tower. They kept their love secret for years until Sarkoja discovered them. To save the child, the mother lied about the father's identity to Sarkoja before escaping with Sola into the returning caravan of young Tharks. The mother was subsequently imprisoned and tortured by Tal Hajus, dying without revealing the father's name. Sola reveals that her father is indeed Tars Tarkas, who has been waiting for his chance to avenge his wife and kill Tal Hajus. She entrusts Carter with this secret, trusting his discretion despite his reputation for truthfulness.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (recipient of secret)
- Sola (storyteller and primary focus)
- Tars Tarkas (mentioned/connected to story)

## Participating Environments
- Interior caravan space during night journey

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From casual travel conversation to deep emotional revelation. The scene moves from curiosity to trust, culminating in a significant secret being entrusted with serious consequences.

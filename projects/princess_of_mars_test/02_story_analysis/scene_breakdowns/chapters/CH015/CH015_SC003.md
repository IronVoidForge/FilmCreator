# Scene SC003 - The March - Caravan Spectacle

## Scene Purpose
Establish the visual spectacle and scale of the Thark caravan, creating atmospheric immersion in the Martian landscape and establishing movement through moss-covered sea.

## Scene Summary
250 chariots advance with guard riding five abreast, flankers, 50 zitidars, loose thoats. Gleaming metal/jewels/silks create barbaric splendor across trackless moss waste. No sound from moss-covered sea bottom; silence broken only by goaded zitidars or fighting thoats. Green Martians converse in low rumbling monosyllables.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (observer)
- Sola (implied presence in column)
- Dejah Thoris (implied presence in column)
- Thark warriors (collective)
- Zitidars and thoats (animals)

## Participating Environments
- Trackless moss waste
- Caravan column
- Moss-covered sea bottom
- Open landscape with barbaric splendor

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Awe → Immersion in spectacle → Environmental integration

## Likely Visual Coverage Families

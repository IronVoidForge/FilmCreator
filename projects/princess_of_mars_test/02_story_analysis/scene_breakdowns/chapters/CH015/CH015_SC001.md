# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the aftermath of the duel and the emotional stakes regarding Dejah Thoris.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter recovers from a non-fatal chest wound inflicted by Zad. Upon reuniting with his retinue, he observes Dejah Thoris in a state of profound mourning, believing him to be dead. Sola, still recovering from her own injuries sustained from Sarkoja, explains the depth of Dejah's grief.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** The immediate vicinity of the duel site/recovery area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief (from survival) to Melancholy (observing Dejah's grief).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-ups on Carter's wound and physical recovery.
- Medium shots of the reunion with the retinue.
- Emotional close-ups of Dejah Thoris in mourning.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific placement/appearance of Carter's chest wound; Sola's visible injuries from Sarkoja.

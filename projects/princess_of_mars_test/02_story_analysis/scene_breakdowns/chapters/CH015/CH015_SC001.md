# SC001

**Scene Purpose**
Establish the immediate aftermath of the combat and the emotional stakes regarding Dejah Thoris's grief.

**Scene Summary**
John Carter recovers from his near-fatal wound under the care of the Martian women. As he regains consciousness, he observes the somber atmosphere of the caravan and realizes that Dejah Thoris is in deep mourning, believing him to be lost.

**Participating Characters**
- John Carter
- Retinue of Martian Women
- Dejah Thoris (observed/mentioned)

**Participating Environments**
- The moving caravan (traveling across a moss-covered sea bottom)

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
Physical pain/vulnerability $\rightarrow$ Melancholy/Realization of loss.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
- Close-ups: Carter's eyes opening, medical treatments being applied.
- Mid-shots: The movement of the caravan against the mossy sea floor.
- Reaction shots: Dejah Thoris's grief-stricken expression.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
- Wound placement and bandage state on Carter.
- Lighting/Atmosphere consistency of the mossy sea bottom environment.

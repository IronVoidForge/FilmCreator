# SC001

## Scene Purpose
Establish John Carter's physical recovery and immediate aftermath of the stabbing incident, showing his vulnerability and the care he receives from Dejah Thoris's women.

## Scene Summary
John Carter regains consciousness after being stabbed by Zad (Sarkoja's weapon). He carefully removes the blade from his body and tends to his wounds using Martian healing agents provided by Dejah Thoris's women who are present to help him recover.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (primary focus)
- Dejah Thoris's women (supporting characters providing care)

## Participating Environments
- Recovery area/bedside location where Carter is being treated
- Interior space with healing agents and medical supplies

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From physical pain and vulnerability to acceptance of care and gradual stabilization. The scene moves from shock/recovery to gratitude for the support received.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Close-up shots of wound removal and healing process
- Medium shots showing Carter's facial expressions during recovery
- Wide shots establishing the care environment and presence of women attending to him

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Wound location and progression must remain consistent
- Healing agent usage and placement needs clear visual tracking
- Character positioning around Carter for care scenes

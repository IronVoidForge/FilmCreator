# Scene SC001 - Opening Scene & Physical Recovery

## Scene Purpose
Establish Carter's physical recovery from the green breast encounter and his arrival at Dejah Thoris's chariot, introducing the emotional stakes of her grief.

## Scene Summary
Carter regains consciousness with sword buried in green breast of Zad (stone dead). Wound is not dangerous due to Martian anatomy (blade passed beneath muscles). Removes blade, moves toward chariots bleeding and weak. Arrives at Dejah's chariot where Sola is bandaged from Sarkoja encounter and Dejah Thoris lies prone on silks/furs wracked with sobs.

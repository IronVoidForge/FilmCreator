# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the aftermath of the combat and the emotional stakes regarding Dejah Thoris.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter recovers from a sword wound to his chest. Upon reuniting with his retinue, he observes the profound grief of Dejah Thoris, who believes him dead. Sola is also present, recovering from her own injuries sustained during the encounter with Sarkoja.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** The Caravan Camp (near the southern hills).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief (survival) $\rightarrow$ Melancholy/Pathos (Dejah's grief).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-ups of Carter's wound and physical recovery.
- Medium shots of Dejah Thoris expressing silent, heavy mourning.
- Reaction shots from Sola.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific placement/severity of the chest wound; the state of Sola's injuries.

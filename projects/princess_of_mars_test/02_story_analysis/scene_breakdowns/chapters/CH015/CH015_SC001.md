# Scene 1: Recovery & Arrival at Chariot

## Scene Purpose
Establish John Carter's physical recovery and transition from battlefield to chariot camp, introducing the wounded women and Martian atmosphere.

## Scene Summary
John regains consciousness after being struck by Zad's blade, removes sword from body, moves toward chariots where bleeding women are weak. Murmur of Martian applause heard as he reaches women who dress wounds with healing agents.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (primary)
- Wounded women (secondary)
- Sola (present in background)

## Participating Environments
- Ochre moss battlefield
- Green breast of dead Zad
- Chariot interior
- Left breast bleeding wound

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From physical pain and disorientation to curiosity about surroundings and concern for wounded women.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Close-up on sword hilt buried in flesh
- Wide shot showing chariot camp layout
- Medium shots of women applying healing agents
- Over-the-shoulder from John's perspective approaching chariot

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Wound status (healed except weakness)
- Martian applause murmur presence
- Healing agent application details
- Character positioning relative to chariot

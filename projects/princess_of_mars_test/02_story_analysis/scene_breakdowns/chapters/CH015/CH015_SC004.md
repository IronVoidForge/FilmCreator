# Scene SC004 - Camp Setup & Storytelling Invitation

## Scene Purpose
Transition from caravan movement to intimate camp setting, establishing Carter's search for Sola and her emotional vulnerability about love and isolation.

## Scene Summary
Camped at foot of hills marking southern boundary of sea. Animals two days without drink but survive on moss moisture. Evening: Carter eats cheese-like food/vegetable milk, seeks out Sola working by torchlight on Tars Tarkas' trappings. Sola expresses isolation among her own people; wishes to be true green Martian woman without love/hope but is lost due to knowing love.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (seeker, eater)
- Sola (worker, lonely)
- Tars Tarkas (implied presence with trappings)

## Participating Environments

- Torchlight area
- Tars Tarkas' trappings
- Hill boundary markers

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Loneliness → Vulnerability → Intimate connection invitation

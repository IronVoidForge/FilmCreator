# SC002

**Scene Purpose**
Provide backstory for Sola and reveal the connection between her parentage and Tars Tarkas.

**Scene Summary**
During the transit toward the hills, Carter meets with Sola. She recounts the tragic story of her mother—a Thark woman who lived in secret to protect Sola from Thark society—and her mother's death following Sarkoja's discovery of them. The scene culminates in the revelation that Sola's father is Tars Tarkas.

**Participating Characters**
- John Carter
- Sola

**Participating Environments**
- Moving caravan / Sea bottom landscape

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
Quiet companionship $\rightarrow$ Tragic exposition $\rightarrow$ Shocking revelation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
- Two-shot: Carter and Sola conversing.
- Close-ups: Sola's face during the emotional recounting of her mother's death.
- Extreme close-up: Carter's reaction to the final reveal.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
- Character positioning within the moving caravan.
- Emotional tone consistency (transitioning from sadness to the weight of the revelation).

# Scene SC002 - Dialogue & Emotional Beats

## Scene Purpose
Develop the emotional stakes through Sola's explanation of Dejah's grief and her offer to share her story, establishing Carter's promise to protect the secret.

## Scene Summary
Sola explains Dejah thinks Carter is dead; her pride prevents admitting his existence while mourning him as if he died. Sola invites Carter to hear her strange story tonight at the chariot before resuming march. Carter promises to visit and instructs not to let Dejah know he saw her tears.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (listener, promise-maker)
- Sola (explainer, storyteller)
- Dejah Thoris (grieving presence)

## Participating Environments
- Chariot interior with silks/furs
- Torchlight area near chariot
- Interior space for intimate conversation

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Grief → Curiosity/Intimacy → Protective commitment

# Scene 2: Dejah's Mourning & Sola's Explanation

## Scene Purpose
Reveal the emotional core of the chapter - Dejah's grief and Sola's cultural explanation of Barsoomian mourning practices.

## Scene Summary
John finds Dejah Thoris lying prone on silks and furs, wracked with sobs believing John is dead. Sola explains that Barsoomians weep from pride: two people wept - mother from sorrow, Sarkoja from baffled rage.

## Dominant Emotional Shift

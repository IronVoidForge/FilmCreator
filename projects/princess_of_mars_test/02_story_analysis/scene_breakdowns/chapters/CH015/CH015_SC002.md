# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale and atmosphere of the Martian journey.

**Scene Summary:** The massive caravan, consisting of hundreds of chariots, warriors, and beasts, moves silently across the trackless, mossy sea bottom. The environment is vast and absorbing, leaving no trace of their passage. They eventually settle into a camp at the foot of southern hills.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (observing), various caravan members/warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Sea Bottom; the Southern Hills campsite.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe/Solemnity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme wide shots of the massive caravan moving across the moss.
- Low-angle shots of chariots and warriors to emphasize scale.
- Establishing shots of the camp at the foot of the hills.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The movement patterns of the large group; lighting transitions from day travel to night camping.

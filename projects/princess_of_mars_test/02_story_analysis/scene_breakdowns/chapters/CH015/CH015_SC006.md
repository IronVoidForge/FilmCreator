# Scene SC006 - The Origin Story - Incubation & Tragedy

## Scene Purpose
Complete the tragic narrative arc of Sola's origin, showing the mother's sacrifice and death, establishing emotional weight for the revelation.

## Scene Summary
Lover gained distinction as warrior; planned to wrest metal from Tal Hajus to claim mother and protect child. Ordered away to ice-clad south for four years. Egg hatched while he was gone. Mother kept Sola in tower, visited nightly. Sarkoja appears at tower with loathing; demands name of partner. Mother lies and says she knows nothing to save Sola. Escape & Death: Mother wraps Sola in silks/furs, runs to outskirts towards father. Meets returning caravan (not father). Mother hides on tailboard, mixes Sola with other children. Imprisoned by Tal Hajus; tortured for name of father; dies steadfastly. Claims she killed Sola and threw body to white apes. Sarkoja suspects truth but fears exposure.

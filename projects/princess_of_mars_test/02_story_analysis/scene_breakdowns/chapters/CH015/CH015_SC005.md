# Scene SC005 - The Origin Story - Mother's Background & Affair

## Scene Purpose
Begin Sola's origin story, establishing her mother's background and the secret love affair that led to her birth, creating emotional investment in the backstory.

## Scene Summary
Sola recounts mother's background: small, less cruel than most Thark women; roamed deserted avenues of Thark alone. Met young warrior guarding zitidars/thoats. Secret love affair for six years; mother belonged to retinue of Tal Hajus, lover was simple warrior. Defection would mean penalty in arena. Egg hidden beneath glass vessel on highest tower of ancient Thark. Mother visited annually for five years during incubation.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Show the shifting power dynamics and Tars Tarkas's pragmatic compassion.

**Scene Summary:** During the march, John observes that Sarkoja has heavily manacled Dejah Thoris. In a moment of unexpected dignity, Tars Tarkas intervenes and unchains her, signaling a shift in how the Princess is being treated by the Thark leadership.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tars Tarkas, Sarkoja.

**Participating Environments:** The marching column/trail.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Relief/Surprise.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of the interaction between Tars Tarkas and Dejah; reaction shots from John.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Position of the manacles; physical proximity of the characters in the march.

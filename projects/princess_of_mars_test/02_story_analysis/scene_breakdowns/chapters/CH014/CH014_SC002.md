# SC002 - The Duel with Zad

## Scene Purpose
Main action sequence during the march, escalating conflict and introducing Dejah's combat intervention.

## Scene Summary
During the march, John Carter engages in a duel with Zad, a warrior who struck his animal. The fight is interrupted when Dejah Thoris throws a mirror that blinds Zad, allowing him to strike John in the shoulder. Sarkoja attempts to stab Dejah Thoris, but Sola intercepts.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (protagonist, duelist)
- Zad (antagonist, warrior who struck animal)
- Dejah Thoris (love interest, combat intervener)
- Sarkoja (antagonist, attacker)
- Sola (ally, interceptor)

## Participating Environments
- March route between Korad and Thark
- Open terrain suitable for combat

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Action escalation → Combat intervention → Escalation of threat

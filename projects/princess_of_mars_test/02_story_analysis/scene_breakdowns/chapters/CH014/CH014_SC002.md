# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the logistical tension and the power dynamics within the group.

**Scene Summary:** The party prepares to depart for Thark. Carter protests the heavy chains on Dejah's chariot, which Tars Tarkas defends as necessary security. Later, during a campout, Tars Tarkas shows a moment of compassion by unfastening her fetters. Carter observes the intense hatred from Sarkoja and Zad.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tars Tarkas, Sarkoja, Zad.

**Participating Environments:** Camp/Chariot staging area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Protest/Conflict $\rightarrow$ Quiet Compassion $\rightarrow$ Growing Dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots (group movement), Medium shots (the chariot and chains), Close-ups (Sarkoja and Zad's glares).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The state of Dejah's fetters/chains; the positioning of the chariot.

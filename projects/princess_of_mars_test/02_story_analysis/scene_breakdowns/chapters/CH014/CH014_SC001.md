# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the emotional distance and internal conflict between Carter and Dejah Thoris.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter grapples with his unexpressed love for Dejah Thoris, fearing the weight of his feelings. A misunderstanding occurs, resulting in Dejah withdrawing from him with silent, dignified contempt.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** Camp/Living quarters.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Intimacy/Longing $\rightarrow$ Tension/Alienation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups (internal monologue/reaction), Medium shots (the distance between them).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Eye contact patterns, physical proximity/distance during the misunderstanding.

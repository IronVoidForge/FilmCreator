# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the emotional stakes and the cultural rift between John Carter and Dejah Thoris.

**Scene Summary:** As the expeditionary force begins its march toward Thark, John Carter reflects on his deep love for Dejah Thoris and the agonizing misunderstanding caused by Barsoomian social customs that have led to her coldness toward him.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (internal monologue/reflection), Dejah Thoris (distant).

**Participating Environments:** The outskirts of the Thark expeditionary camp; Martian wilderness.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Romantic longing $\rightarrow$ Melancholy/Frustration.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups on John's face; wide shots of the marching column to establish scale and movement.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Direction of march; pace of the walking expedition.

# SC001 - Setup & Tars Tarkas Intervention

## Scene Purpose
Establish the journey preparation, emotional stakes between John Carter and Dejah Thoris, and introduce Tars Tarkas's protective role.

## Scene Summary
John Carter reflects on his love for Dejah as they prepare to travel from Korad to Thark. Dejah is chained by Sarkoja's order to ensure security during the journey. Tars Tarkas intervenes, taking custody of the key and promising to protect her from Sarkoja's harassment.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (protagonist, romantic interest)
- Dejah Thoris (love interest, captive)
- Sarkoja (antagonist, captor)
- Tars Tarkas (ally, protector)

## Participating Environments
- Korad outskirts
- Preparation area for journey from Korad to Thark

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Romantic reflection → Protective intervention

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shots for character interactions and emotional beats
- Close-ups on Dejah's chained hands and John Carter's reflective expressions
- Wide shots establishing the preparation environment and Sarkoja's threatening presence

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Dejah's chains must remain consistent throughout scene
- Key custody transfer needs clear visual confirmation
- Character positioning relative to each other during intervention sequence

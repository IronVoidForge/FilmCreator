# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Heighten the drama through a sacrificial act and character confrontation.

**Scene Summary:** As John is blinded, Dejah Thoris confronts Sarkoja. In the ensuing chaos, Sola leaps in front of Sarkoja's dagger to protect the Princess, taking a mortal wound.

**Participating Characters:** Dejah Thoris, Sarkoja, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** The duel site.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anger $\rightarrow$ Horror/Shock.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of the confrontation; slow-motion or high-impact close-ups of the stabbing; reaction shots from Dejah.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Position of Sola relative to Dejah and Sarkoja.

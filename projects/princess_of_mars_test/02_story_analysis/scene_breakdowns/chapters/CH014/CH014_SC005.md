# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Execute the action climax and the tragic turning point.

**Scene Summary:** As Carter and Zad duel, Sarkoja uses a mirror to blind Carter with sunlight. In the chaos, Sola leaps to protect Dejah from Sarkoja's dagger, but this distraction allows Zad to deliver a fatal thrust to Carter's chest, leaving him fading into unconsciousness.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Zad, Sarkoja, Sola, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** Duel site/Camp area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Combat/Intensity $\rightarrow$ Chaos $\rightarrow$ Tragedy/Loss.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Handheld/Dynamic shots (the duel), Close-ups (the mirror flash, the dagger strike), Slow motion (Sola's leap).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The angle of the sun/mirror reflection; blood/wound location on Carter; Sola's position relative to Dejah.

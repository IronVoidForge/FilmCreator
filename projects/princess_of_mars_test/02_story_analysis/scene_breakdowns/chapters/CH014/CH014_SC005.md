# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the action with a tragic consequence and a cliffhanger.

**Scene Summary:** Amidst the duel, chaos erupts as Dejah Thoris confronts Sarkoja. Sola attempts to protect the princess and is stabbed by Sarkoja. In the ensuing distraction, Zad delivers a fatal thrust to Carter's chest, leaving him dying on the ground.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Sarkoja, Zad.

**Participating Environments:** Open Martian plains.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Chaos/Panic $\rightarrow$ Tragedy/Despair.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Handheld/unstable shots for chaos; close-ups on Sola's injury and Carter's wound; wide shot of the aftermath.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Blood splatter/wound locations; character positions during the melee; lighting shifts if the sun moves significantly.

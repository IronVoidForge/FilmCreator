# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Escalate the physical conflict and introduce the primary antagonist's interference.

**Scene Summary:** Zad challenges John Carter to a formal long-sword duel. The fight is intense until Sarkoja uses a mirror to catch the sunlight, blinding John Carter and stripping him of his combat advantage.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Zad, Sarkoja (observer/intervener).

**Participating Environments:** Open terrain near the march path.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Competitive/Formal $\rightarrow$ Disorientation/Panic.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the duel; extreme close-ups on eyes and the mirror flash; POV shots from John's blinded perspective.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Sword positions; lighting angle for the mirror reflection.

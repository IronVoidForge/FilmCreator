# SC003 - Climax & Cliffhanger

## Scene Purpose
Final confrontation leading to chapter's cliffhanger ending.

## Scene Summary
The chapter culminates in a fierce sword fight where John Carter is stabbed in the chest and collapses, ending on a cliffhanger of his potential death or unconsciousness.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (protagonist, critically injured)
- Dejah Thoris (love interest, combatant)
- Sarkoja (antagonist, attacker)
- Sola (ally, combatant)

## Participating Environments
- March route between Korad and Thark
- Combat zone during journey

## Dominant Emotional Shift
High-stakes combat → Critical injury → Cliffhanger tension

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Intense action sequences with rapid camera movement
- Close-up on chest wound for dramatic impact
- Collapse sequence needs clear spatial relationship to environment
- Final shot must establish cliffhanger without resolution

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- John Carter's chest wound must be clearly visible and consistent
- Character positions during final combat need to track across shots
- Environmental elements (terrain, obstacles) must remain consistent
- Final collapse needs clear visual confirmation of character state

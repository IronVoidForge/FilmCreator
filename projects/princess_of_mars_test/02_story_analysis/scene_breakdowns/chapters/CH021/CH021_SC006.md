**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the massive ceremony; Low-angle "hero" shots of Carter and Than Kosis; Crowd reaction shots.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's new uniform or insignia; The presence of the rescued scout.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Transition Carter from the wilderness into the urban setting of Zodanga.

**Scene Summary:** Carter reaches the walled city of Zodanga. To avoid detection, he separates from Woola and enters the metropolis, observing its defensive architecture designed to prevent assassinations.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Woola (briefly).

**Participating Environments:** The walls/gates of Zodanga, urban streets.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Caution/Stealth.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of city walls; POV shots of the architecture; tracking shots of Carter moving through crowds.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Separation from Woola; Carter's level of disguise or inconspicuousness.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Transition Carter into the urban setting and create a moment of emotional sacrifice.

**Scene Summary:** Carter reaches the massive walls of Zodanga. To maintain his undercover status and avoid drawing attention to his unusual companion, he makes the difficult decision to leave Woola behind.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** The gates/approach to the walled city of Zodanga.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Melancholy/Reluctance.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of the massive Zodangan walls; Close-ups of the bond between Carter and Woola during the parting.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Woola's position relative to Carter; Gear/clothing state after travel.

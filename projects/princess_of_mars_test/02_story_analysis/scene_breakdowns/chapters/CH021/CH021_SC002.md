# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Transition Carter from the wilderness to the urban environment and establish a moment of emotional sacrifice.

**Scene Summary:** Carter reaches the massive gates of Zodanga. Recognizing the danger of his upcoming infiltration, he makes the difficult decision to leave Woola behind to ensure the dog's safety.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** Gates of Zodanga (massive, walled city).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Melancholy/Heartbreak.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of the massive gates; Close-ups on the emotional bond and parting between Carter and Woola.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Woola's position relative to the city gates; Carter's level of preparedness for infiltration.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Reintroduce an ally and establish the mechanism for Carter's infiltration.

**Scene Summary:** In the central plaza of Zodanga, Carter reunites with Kantos Kan. Kan reveals his mission to find Dejah Thoris (who is rumored to be held by Prince Sab Than) and proposes a plan for Carter to join the air-scout squadron.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Kantos Kan.

**Participating Environments:** Zodanga Central Plaza (high-raised residences).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief $\rightarrow$ Strategic Focus.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of the reunion; Over-the-shoulder shots during the planning/intelligence exchange.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The scale and height of the surrounding Zodangan architecture.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the primary contact and the mission objective.

**Scene Summary:** In the central plaza, Carter meets Kantos Kan. Kan reveals his plan to infiltrate the Zodangan navy to reach Prince Sab Than. Using a ruse, Kan manages to enroll Carter into an air-scout squadron.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Kantos Kan.

**Participating Environments:** Central Plaza of Zodanga.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Conspiracy/Determination.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Over-the-shoulder shots during the secret meeting; medium shots of the enrollment process.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific ruse used for enrollment; Carter's new status as a recruit.

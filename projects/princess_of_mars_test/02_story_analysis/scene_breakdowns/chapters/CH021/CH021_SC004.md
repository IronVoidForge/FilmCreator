# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Demonstrate the mechanics of Martian flight and Carter's aptitude.

**Scene Summary:** Carter undergoes training to pilot aircraft, learning to manipulate the "eighth Barsoomian ray"—a repulsive force of gravity derived from light.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Air-scout instructors (background).

**Participating Environments:** Flight training grounds/Airspace above Zodanga.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Concentration/Learning.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Aerial shots; close-ups of flight controls/light manipulation; wide shots of aircraft movement.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The visual representation of the "eighth ray."

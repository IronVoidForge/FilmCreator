# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Montage/Training sequence to establish Carter's new role and the technology of the world.

**Scene Summary:** Using a ruse, Carter joins the Zodangan air-scout squadron. He spends several days undergoing training in Martian aviation and mastering the mechanics of eighth-ray propulsion.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Zodangan Air-Scouts.

**Participating Environments:** Zodangan air-fields/training grounds.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Competence.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Montage shots; Wide shots of airships using eighth-ray propulsion; Close-ups on mechanical controls and flight maneuvers.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The visual behavior of the eighth-ray propulsion (gravity repulsion effects).

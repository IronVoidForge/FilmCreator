# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale of Barsoomian technology and the political tension surrounding Zodanga.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter travels toward Zodanga, observing the massive irrigation systems pumping water from polar ice caps. He encounters Zodangan nobility who discuss the unjust war with Helium and the disappearance of Princess Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Zodangan Nobility (unnamed).

**Participating Environments:** The Barsoomian plains/deserts, near irrigation conduits.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Wonder (at technology) $\rightarrow$ Tension (political discourse).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide establishing shots of irrigation systems; medium close-ups of nobility in conversation.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's travel gear and position relative to the landscape.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the setting, provide exposition regarding the war's unpopularity, and introduce the tragic rumor of Dejah Thoris's death.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter travels through the agricultural tracts toward Zodanga, observing Martian technology. He stops at a farmhouse where a Zodangan noble informs him that the war is unpopular and shares the grim rumor that Dejah Thoris may have perished during an encounter with Tharks and Warhoons.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Zodangan Noble.

**Participating Environments:** Martian agricultural tracts (underground water conduits/refined flora), Zodangan farmhouse.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Somberness/Dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide landscape shots of Martian agriculture; Medium close-ups of the conversation between Carter and the Noble; Close-ups on Carter's reaction to the news.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's travel gear/appearance; The specific visual look of the "refined" Martian flora.

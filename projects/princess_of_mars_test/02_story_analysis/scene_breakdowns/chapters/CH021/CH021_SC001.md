# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the Martian landscape, world-building lore, and the grim news regarding Dejah Thoris.

**Scene Summary:** Carter travels through highly advanced agricultural tracts, observing the sophisticated irrigation systems of Mars. During his journey, he receives word from a noble that Dejah Thoris is presumed dead following recent conflicts with Tharks and Warhoons.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Martian Noble (off-screen/dialogue).

**Participating Environments:** Cultivated agricultural tracts between the polar ice caps and Zodanga.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Wonder/Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Somberness/Grief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide establishing shots of Martian infrastructure; Close-ups of Carter's reactions to the landscape and news.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Travel progression/distance; Lighting consistency across the vast plains.

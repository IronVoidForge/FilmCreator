# SC005

**Scene Summary:** During a solo flight, Carter encounters a Zodangan scout (a cousin of the Jeddak) being hunted by three Green Martian warriors. Carter intervenes in mid-air, kills the warriors, and saves the scout.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Zodangan Scout, Three Green Martian Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian sky/aerial combat zone.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ High Action $\rightarrow$ Relief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Dynamic aerial shots; Fast-paced action coverage (handheld or tracking); Close-ups of the combat and the kill.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Flight trajectories; The physical state/damage to the airships post-combat.

# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Action climax and demonstration of Carter's combat prowess.

**Scene Summary:** During a solo flight, Carter encounters a skirmish between a Red Martian scout and three Green warriors. Carter rams one warrior with his craft and kills the remaining two on foot. The scout is revealed to be a relative of Than Kosis.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Red Martian Scout, Green Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Mid-air/Sky above Barsoom.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Peril $\rightarrow$ Action/Violence $\rightarrow$ Relief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** High-speed aerial action shots; handheld/shaky cam for the ground combat; close-ups of the kill.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Damage to Carter's aircraft; weapon usage; the identity reveal of the scout.

# Scene SC006 - Discovery/Capture Threat

## Scene Purpose
Reveal that the escape plan has been discovered and establish the capture threat, raising stakes to crisis level.

## Scene Summary
The escape plan is discovered by warriors who intend to capture John Carter upon his return alone. John hides in a courtyard with his animals, then climbs back to Dejah Thoris's room. He overhears warriors planning to chain him in vaults beneath the Jeddak's quarters if he returns without her.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (primary, hiding/overhearing)
- Warriors (off-screen/threat source)
- Dejah Thoris (affected by capture threat)

## Participating Environments
- Courtyard with animals
- Second-story windows
- Jeddak's quarters vaults area
- Room where warriors are planning

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Urgency → Vulnerability → Crisis realization

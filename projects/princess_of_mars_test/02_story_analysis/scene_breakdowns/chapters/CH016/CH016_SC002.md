# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the new setting and the physical separation of the protagonists.

**Scene Summary:** The group arrives at the massive city of Thark. Due to their status, they are separated; John Carter is assigned quarters in a large building near the central plaza, while Dejah Thoris remains under Thark supervision.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Thark guards/officials.

**Participating Environments:** The grand, massive architecture of the city of Thark; Central Plaza; John's new quarters.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe (at the city) $\rightarrow$ Isolation/Dread (due to separation).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Grand establishing shots of Thark architecture; medium shots of the group being processed/separated; POV shots of the scale of the city.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Scale of the environment relative to characters; movement through different city sectors.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose**
Develop the escape logistics and introduce the conflict regarding Sola's participation.

**Scene Summary**
John, Dejah, and Sola attempt to finalize their escape. They discuss a northern route using a diamond to map waterways on a marble floor. Sola expresses doubt about the safety of the journey despite promises of sanctuary in Helium.

**Participating Characters**
- John Carter
- Dejah Thoris
- Sola

**Participating Environments**
- Tharkian Quarters (Marble Floor Area)

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
Hopeful Planning $\rightarrow$ Skepticism/Anxiety

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
- Top-down shots of the marble floor and diamond mapping.
- Group medium shots for the discussion.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
- The position and markings on the marble floor.
- The diamond used as a tool.

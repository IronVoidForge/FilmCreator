# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Escalation and the realization of failure.

**Scene Summary:** Carter sneaks out of the city with his thoats to reach the rendezvous point. Upon arrival, he realizes the women are not there and that the plan has been compromised.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** The dark streets/canals near the city perimeter; the rendezvous point.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confidence $\rightarrow$ Panic/Alarm.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Handheld or kinetic shots of Carter moving through shadows; wide shots showing his isolation at the empty rendezvous point; POV shots searching the darkness.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Presence/absence of thoats; light sources (moonlight vs. city glow).

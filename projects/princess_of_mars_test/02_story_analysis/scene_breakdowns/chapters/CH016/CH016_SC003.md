# SC003

**Scene Purpose**
Escalate tension through the realization that the protagonists are being hunted.

**Scene Summary**
As night falls, John Carter prepares his thoats for the escape. While moving toward the rendezvous point, he overhears mounted Thark warriors discussing their intentions, realizing the plan has been compromised.

**Participating Characters**
- John Carter
- Thark Warriors (Off-screen/Background)

**Participating Environments**
- Thark City Streets / Stables area

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
Readiness $\rightarrow$ Alarm/Urgency

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
- Low-light/Night cinematography.
- POV shots of Carter observing the warriors.
- Handheld movement to reflect rising tension.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
- Lighting (Moonlight/Torches).
- Equipment/Thoat gear preparation.

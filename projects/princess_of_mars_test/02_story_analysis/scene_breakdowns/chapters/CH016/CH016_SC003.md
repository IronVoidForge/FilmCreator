# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the romantic stakes and the central motivation for the escape.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter visits Dejah Thoris in her quarters. He formally declares his devotion to her and commands her to work with him to facilitate their mutual escape.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** Dejah Thoris's quarters.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Intense Romance/Determination.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Tight close-ups on faces; medium two-shots to capture intimacy and intensity of the declaration.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Character positioning within the room; emotional intensity levels.

# SC004

**Scene Summary:** Sola arrives with urgent news: Sarkoja has advised Tal Hajus to sacrifice Dejah Thoris to the wild dogs during the upcoming yearly games.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Reaction shots (close-ups); medium shots of the three characters interacting; handheld camera to increase urgency.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Sola's entrance/urgency; character reactions to the news.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose**
Deliver the climax of the chapter's tension and reveal the dire stakes.

**Scene Summary**
Carter maneuvers through the city to find the women. He climbs into Dejah Thoris's quarters and overhears a Thark chieftain ordering an ambush to capture him for Tal Hajus. He also discovers that Sola has already been captured.

**Participating Characters**
- John Carter (Eavesdropping)
- Thark Chieftain (Voice/Shadow)
- Thark Warriors (Mentioned/Implied)

**Participating Environments**
- Exterior of Dejah Thoris's Quarters / Interior (Overheard)

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
Urgency $\rightarrow$ Despair/Shock

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
- Tight close-ups on Carter's reaction.
- Shadowy, high-contrast lighting to emphasize the eavesdropping.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**

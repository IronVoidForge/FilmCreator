# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Climax and the reveal of the trap.

**Scene Summary:** Carter maneuvers through the inner courts and uses his agility to climb into Dejah Thoris's quarters. While hiding, he overhears a Thark chieftain ordering an ambush on him and revealing that Dejah Thoris is already in Tal Hajus's custody.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Thark Chieftain, Thark Warriors (off-screen/background).

**Participating Environments:** Inner courts of Thark; Dejah Thoris's quarters (marble floors, winding stairs).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Stealth/Tension $\rightarrow$ Horror/Despair.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme close-ups of Carter's face as he listens; high-angle shots showing his climbing/infiltration; shadows and silhouettes for the Chieftain.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Sound design (overhearing dialogue); Carter's physical position relative to the door/window.

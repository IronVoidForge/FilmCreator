# Scene SC001 - Arrival in Thark

## Scene Purpose
Establish the new setting after 30 days on Barsoom and introduce Thark's grand architecture while showing John Carter and Dejah Thoris settling into their assigned quarters.

## Scene Summary
John Carter and Dejah Thoris enter the ancient city of Thark after their journey. They are shown being guided through the city streets to their new quarters near the central plaza. The scale of Thark's public buildings is emphasized, showing this as a significant destination after their long absence from Barsoomian territory.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (primary)
- Dejah Thoris (primary)
- Community guides/assigners (supporting)

## Participating Environments
- Ancient city of Thark streets
- Central plaza area
- Assigned quarters building exterior
- Grand public buildings visible in background

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Anticipation → Relief → Awe at the scale of Thark's architecture

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide establishing shots of Thark entrance
- Medium shots of characters walking through city streets
- Over-the-shoulder shots showing assigned quarters location
- Detail shots of architectural features (marble, stone carvings)

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Character positioning relative to plaza landmarks
- Quarter building exterior consistency
- Lighting transition from journey to city arrival
- Map/direction indicators if shown in scene

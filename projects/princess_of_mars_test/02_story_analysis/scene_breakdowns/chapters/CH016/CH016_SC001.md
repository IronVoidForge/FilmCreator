# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish arrival at the destination and reunite the protagonists.

**Scene Summary:** After a twenty-day journey, John Carter and his companions arrive at the massive city of Thark. Carter is assigned quarters in the central plaza, where he finally reunites with Dejah Thoris and Sola.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** The outskirts/gates of Thark; the Great Plaza of Thark.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion/Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Relief/Intimacy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide establishing shots of Thark's scale; medium close-ups of the reunion; tracking shots of Carter moving through the plaza.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Travel-worn clothing/appearance for Carter and companions; scale of the architecture relative to characters.

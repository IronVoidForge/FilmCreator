# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the passage of time, the difficulty of the journey, and the growing tension of being hunted.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, and Sola travel stealthily for twenty days across sea bottoms and canals, moving under the cover of darkness to evade Martian patrols.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** Martian sea bottoms, canal networks, desolate landscapes.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion/Tension $\rightarrow$ Relief (at reaching safety) $\rightarrow$ Apprehension (approaching the city).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide landscape shots of the Martian terrain; medium tracking shots of the trio moving through shadows; close-ups of weary faces.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels (night/darkness); character fatigue/disheveled appearance; equipment/clothing wear over 20 days.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose**
Establish the setting of Thark and transition the emotional tone from travel to intimate planning.

**Scene Summary**
After a long journey, John Carter and Dejah Thoris arrive at the ancient city of Thark. Once settled in their quarters, John confesses his absolute devotion to Dejah and proposes an escape plan.

**Participating Characters**
- John Carter
- Dejah Thoris

**Participating Environments**
- The City of Thark (Interior Quarters)

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
Exhaustion/Tension $\rightarrow$ Intimacy/Determination

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
- Close-ups on faces during the confession.
- Medium shots for character interaction in the quarters.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
- Clothing/armor worn after the twenty-day journey.
- Lighting levels within the Tharkian quarters.

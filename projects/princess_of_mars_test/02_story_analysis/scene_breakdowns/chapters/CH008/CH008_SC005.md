# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce a new character and create a moment of missed connection/dramatic irony.

**Scene Summary:** Returning to the plaza, the protagonist sees a beautiful, copper-skinned female prisoner from the airship. She makes a silent plea for help, but due to his lack of understanding of Martian customs, he fails to respond, leading her to view him with contempt as she is led away.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, The Prisoner, Green Martian Warriors (escorting).

**Participating Environments:** City Plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Hope $\rightarrow$ Dejection/Contempt.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Eye-level medium shots of the prisoner; Extreme close-ups of her eyes and gestures; Reaction shots of the protagonist's confusion.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific hand gesture used by the woman; The physical distance between the protagonist and the prisoner during the exchange.

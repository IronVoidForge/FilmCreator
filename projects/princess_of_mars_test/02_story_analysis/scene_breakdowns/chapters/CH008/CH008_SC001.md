# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the sudden escalation from peace to high-stakes aerial combat.

**Scene Summary:** As the protagonist and Sola attempt to return home, a retreat is ordered as twenty gray aerial craft approach the city. The green Martian warriors engage in a highly disciplined tactical defense, using precision ground fire to dismantle the ships' steering, weaponry, and crews.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- Sola
- Green Martian Warriors (Tactical combatants)

**Participating Environments:**
- Deserted Martian city outskirts/hills
- City rooftops/defensive positions

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Peace $\rightarrow$ Sudden Alarm $\rightarrow$ Intense Combat Tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the approaching fleet (Scale).
- Medium shots of Martian warriors firing from buildings (Tactical precision).
- POV/Close-ups of ship components being struck (Impact/Destruction).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Timing of the aerial approach relative to the ground fire; trajectory of the projectiles.

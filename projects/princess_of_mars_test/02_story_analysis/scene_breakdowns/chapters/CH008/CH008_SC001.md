# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish immediate tension and shift from departure to combat readiness.

**Scene Summary:** As the protagonist and Sola prepare to leave the deserted city, the green Martians suddenly retreat into cover as twenty large, gray-painted airships descend upon the valley.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Sola, Green Martian Warriors (background/distant).

**Participating Environments:** Deserted Martian City / Valley overlook.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Sudden Alarm/Tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the approaching fleet; Medium shots of protagonist and Sola reacting to the sudden movement of the green Martians.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Position of the protagonist relative to the city exit; timing of the airships' appearance in the sky.

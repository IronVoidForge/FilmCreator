# Scene Purpose
Establish the immediate threat posed by enemy airships and the Green Martians' tactical response of hiding within the city infrastructure.

# Scene Summary
On the third day after the incubator ceremony, the procession is ordered to return home but immediately recalled due to the arrival of enemy airships. Twenty gray-painted vessels swing over the hills toward the city. The green Martians, trained for this evolution, melt into nearby buildings within three minutes.

# Participating Characters
- **Narrator:** Implied observer of the event from a distance or general awareness.
- **Green Martians:** Active participants hiding in doorways and buildings.

# Participating Environments
- **City Streets:** Open ground with spacious doorways.
- **Building Doorways/Interiors:** Where Martians melt into for cover.

# Dominant Emotional Shift
**Tension & Alertness:** The arrival of the enemy creates a sudden spike in threat level, countered by the disciplined reaction of the Martians to avoid detection.

# Likely Visual Coverage Families
- **Wide Shots:** Establishing the gray airships swinging over the hills and approaching the city.
- **Medium/Close Shots:** Green Martians entering building doorways rapidly (time-lapse or fast cuts to show 3-minute duration).
- **POV Shots:** From a distance looking up at the ships against the sky.

# Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- **Ship Count:** Ensure exactly twenty gray-painted vessels are visible or referenced.
- **Hiding Speed:** Visual pacing must convey the rapidity of Martians entering buildings (within 3 minutes).
- **Martian Appearance:** Consistent green skin tone and tactical gear during hiding phase.

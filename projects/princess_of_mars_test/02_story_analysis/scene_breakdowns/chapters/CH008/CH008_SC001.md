# SC001 - Enemy Fleet Arrival & Battle Setup

## Scene Purpose
Establish the escalation of conflict as the enemy air fleet approaches the Green Martian city, creating immediate tension and action.

## Scene Summary
Following the incubator ceremony, the procession returns to the Green Martian city. Upon entering the open ground, orders are given for an immediate retreat as a massive enemy air fleet (twenty gray ships) approaches from the hills. The Green Martians fire a volley from their buildings, initiating a battle. The enemy fleet responds with gunfire, but the initial volley catches them off guard.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (observer from window)
- Woola (implied presence following close at heel)
- Green Martian warriors (building defenders)
- Enemy fleet personnel (officers, crew on ships)

## Participating Environments
- Green Martian City buildings (upper floors, windows)
- Open ground/plaza entrance
- Valley/hills approach route
- Martian Plains battle zone

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Tension building → Anticipation of conflict → Initial combat engagement

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide establishing shots of fleet approaching from hills
- Medium shots of Narrator observing from window
- Quick cuts between city defenders and enemy ships
- Dynamic action sequences with volley fire

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Fleet count (twenty gray ships) must remain consistent
- Building locations for Narrator's observation point
- Timing of retreat orders vs. opening fire
- Enemy ship positioning relative to hills

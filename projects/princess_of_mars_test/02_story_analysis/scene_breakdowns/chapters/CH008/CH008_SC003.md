# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Show the aftermath of the battle and the ritualistic treatment of the defeated.

**Scene Summary:** An unmanned, damaged ship drifts toward the city. The green warriors use grappling hooks to pull it down, loot it for supplies and weapons, and then set it on fire, creating a drifting funeral pyre.

**Participating Characters:** Green Martian Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** City outskirts / Ground level near the impact site.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Chaos $\rightarrow$ Methodical/Ruthless Efficiency.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the grappling process; Medium shots of looting; Low-angle shots of the burning ship drifting away.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The direction and speed of the drifting, burning vessel.

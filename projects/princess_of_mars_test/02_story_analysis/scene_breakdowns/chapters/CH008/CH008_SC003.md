# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce a new character type and create a moment of missed connection/dramatic irony.

**Scene Summary:** Upon returning to the plaza, the protagonist sees a prisoner taken from the drifting craft: a beautiful, reddish-copper skinned woman who looks remarkably human. She makes a silent gesture toward him, pleading for help, but due to his lack of understanding of Martian customs, he fails to react. She is dragged away in dejection and contempt.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Captive Woman (Reddish-copper skinned)
- Green Martian Warriors (Guards/Captors)
- Sola

**Participating Environments:**
- The City Plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Intense Connection $\rightarrow$ Guilt/Regret (due to inaction).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme close-ups of the woman's eyes and her silent gesture.
- Eye-line matches between the Protagonist and the Woman.
- Medium shots of the guards dragging her away.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The specific visual distinction of the woman's skin tone compared to the Green Martians.
- The exact nature/motion of her silent gesture.

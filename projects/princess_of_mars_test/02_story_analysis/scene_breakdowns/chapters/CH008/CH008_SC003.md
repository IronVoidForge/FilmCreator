# SC003 - Ship Capture & Looting Aftermath

## Scene Purpose
Show the immediate aftermath of battle and introduce the captured vessel as a narrative element.

## Scene Summary
The fleet retreats, damaged and limping. One ship is disabled and captured by the Green Martians. The warriors loot the vessel of arms, food, and valuables, then set it ablaze before towing it away.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (observer from window)
- Woola (implied presence following close at heel)
- Green Martian warriors (looting crew)
- Enemy ship personnel (disabled/disposed)

## Participating Environments
- Green Martian City plaza
- Captured vessel interior/exterior
- Building windows for Narrator's observation
- Towing path through city

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Victory satisfaction → Resource acquisition → Transition to quieter aftermath

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of disabled ship being towed into city
- Medium shots of warriors looting vessel contents
- Close-ups of arms, food, valuables being collected
- Atmospheric shots of vessel being set ablaze

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Ship must remain identifiable as the same captured vessel
- Looting sequence needs clear visual confirmation of items
- Fire progression on vessel must be consistent
- Narrator's observation point remains stable throughout

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Show the aftermath of the battle and the ritualistic destruction of the enemy vessel.

**Scene Summary:** The fleet is routed, leaving one unmanned, drifting craft. Martian warriors use grappling hooks to pull the ship down, systematically looting it of weapons, silks, jewels, and supplies before setting it on fire and watching it drift away as a funeral pyre.

**Participating Characters:**
- Green Martian Warriors (Looters)

**Participating Environments:**
- Ground near the city/valleys
- The derelict aerial craft

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Chaos $\rightarrow$ Methodical Efficiency $\rightarrow$ Somber Spectacle.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low angle shots of grappling hooks catching the ship.
- Close-ups of looted items (silks, jewels).
- Wide shot of the burning vessel drifting away (The "Funeral Pyre").

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The movement/drift of the burning ship; the amount of loot being carried away.

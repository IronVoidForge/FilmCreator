# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To show the aftermath of the battle and the ritualistic/ruthless treatment of the defeated.

**Scene Summary:** One unmanned, damaged gray vessel drifts erratically toward the city. The green Martians capture it, systematically strip it of all valuables (arms, silks, food, water), and set it ablaze, sending it away as a drifting funeral pyre. The protagonist watches with growing melancholy and empathy for the lost crew.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- Green Martian Warriors (Looters)
- Woola (Observing)

**Participating Environments:**
- City outskirts/Landing area near the city walls.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Somber Melancholy/Empathy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the drifting, burning ship.
- Medium shots of the systematic looting process.
- Close-ups of the protagonist's face reflecting empathy.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The intensity and color of the fire on the vessel.
- The amount of debris/loot being moved from the craft.

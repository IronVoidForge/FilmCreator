# SC002 - Battle Sequence & Enemy Retreat

## Scene Purpose
Depict the intense combat and demonstrate Green Martian tactical superiority through deadly accuracy.

## Scene Summary
The enemy fleet responds with gunfire, but the initial volley catches them off guard. The Green Martians demonstrate deadly accuracy, targeting specific points on the enemy vessels (guns, officers, crew). After twenty minutes, the fleet retreats, damaged and limping.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (observer from window)
- Woola (implied presence following close at heel)
- Green Martian warriors (building defenders)
- Enemy fleet personnel (officers, crew on ships)

## Participating Environments
- Martian Plains battle zone
- Green Martian City buildings (upper floors, windows)
- Hills/valley approach route
- Damaged enemy vessels

## Dominant Emotional Shift
High action intensity → Tactical satisfaction → Enemy defeat

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Dynamic action sequences with volley fire
- Close-ups of targeting accuracy on enemy ships
- Wide shots showing fleet damage and retreat
- Quick cuts between city defenders and enemy responses

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Twenty-minute battle duration must be clear
- Specific target points (guns, officers, crew) need visual confirmation
- Fleet damage progression from initial volley to final retreat
- Enemy ship positioning relative to hills during retreat

# Scene Purpose
Depict the systematic looting and destruction of the disabled vessel, emphasizing methodical requisitioning and the tragic funeral pyre ending.

# Scene Summary
The warriors systematically rifle the vessel for several hours, requisitioning chariots to transport loot: arms, ammunition, silks, furs, jewels, stone vessels, and food/water (including casks of water). After removing the last load, they tow the craft out into the valley. They empty carboys over the dead sailors and decks. A final missile is thrown onto the vessel, igniting it. The ship soars majestically into the air as a floating funeral pyre, drifting southeast until lost in the distance.

# Participating Characters
- **Warriors:** Rifling vessel and transporting loot.
- **Green Martians:** Assisting in looting and towing operations.

# Participating Environments
- **Valley/Plain:** Where the ship is towed out.
- **Disabled Ship Interior/Exterior:** Being rifled for items.
- **Open Sky:** Where the burning ship drifts away.

# Dominant Emotional Shift
**Methodical & Tragic:** The scene balances the systematic nature of looting with the solemnity of the ship becoming a funeral pyre drifting away.

# Likely Visual Coverage Families

- **Wide/Tracking Shots:** Chariots transporting loot, ship being towed out.
- **Cinematic Wide Shot:** Ship igniting and soaring into the air as a floating funeral pyre drifting southeast.

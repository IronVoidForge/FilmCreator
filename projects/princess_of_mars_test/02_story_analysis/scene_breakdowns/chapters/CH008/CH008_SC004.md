# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce a new character and create a moment of missed connection/dramatic irony.

**Scene Summary:** Upon returning to the plaza, the protagonist sees a beautiful, copper-skinned female prisoner from the craft. She makes a silent gesture for help, but the protagonist—unaware of Martian customs—fails to respond. The woman reacts with dejection and contempt before being led away.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Prisoner (Copper-skinned, black hair)
- Green Martian Warriors (Guards)

**Participating Environments:**
- City Plaza

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Tension/Connection $\rightarrow$ Regret/Dejection.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Eye-contact close-ups (The connection).
- Medium shot of the prisoner's gesture.
- Reaction shots of the protagonist's confusion vs. her contempt.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific hand gesture used by the prisoner; the direction of movement as she is taken away.

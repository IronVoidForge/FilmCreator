# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the protagonist's vulnerability and the supernatural threat within the cave.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist is trapped in a state of conscious paralysis caused by a mysterious, pungent vapor inside the Arizona mountain cave. He is unable to move despite being fully aware.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist

**Participating Environments:**
- Interior Cave (Dark, claustrophobic, filled with tenuous vapor)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From awareness/confusion to helpless terror and sensory isolation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme Close-ups (Eyes, dilated pupils, sweat, gas particles)
- POV shots (Blurred or restricted vision)
- Macro shots of the environment (The texture of the cave walls)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Eye movement and pupil dilation.
- The specific visual quality/density of the vapor.

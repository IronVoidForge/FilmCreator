# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's vulnerability and the supernatural threat within the cave.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist wakes in a cave, paralyzed by a pungent vapor. Apache warriors enter the cave but are driven into a panic by an unseen, moaning presence in the darkness, leading to one warrior's death during their flight.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist (paralyzed)
- Apache Warriors

**Participating Environments:**
- Interior Cave (dark, filled with vapor)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Vulnerability $\rightarrow$ Terror/Panic

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme Close-ups (Protagonist's eyes/face to convey paralysis)
- Low-angle shots (Apache warriors entering)
- Handheld/Shaky cam (The chaotic retreat of the warriors)
- Deep shadows/Chiaroscuro lighting

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The density and movement of the vapor.
- The timing of the moaning sound relative to the warrior's movements.

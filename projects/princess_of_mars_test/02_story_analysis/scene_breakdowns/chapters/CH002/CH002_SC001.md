# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the narrator's extreme vulnerability and the physical sensation of being trapped by the mysterious vapor.

**Scene Summary:** The narrator, paralyzed by an unknown gas within a cave, is forced to watch helplessly as a group of Apache warriors approaches the ledge of his hiding place.

**Participating Characters:**
* The Narrator (internal/POV)
* Apache Warriors

**Participating Environments:**
* A cave interior/ledge in the Arizona mountains.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From silent observation to intense, helpless tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Extreme close-ups of the narrator's eyes and labored breathing.
* POV shots looking out from the darkness toward the approaching warriors.
* Wide shots establishing the distance between the narrator and the scouts.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
* The density and visibility of the "mysterious vapor" in the cave.
* Lighting transition (dawn light entering the cave).

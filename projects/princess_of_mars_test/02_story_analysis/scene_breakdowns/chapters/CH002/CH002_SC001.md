# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's vulnerability and the supernatural threat.

**Scene Summary:** Inside a cave in the Arizona mountains, the protagonist is incapacitated by a mysterious, pungent vapor. He remains mentally conscious but is physically paralyzed, unable to move or defend himself.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist (Paralyzed)

**Participating Environments:**
- Interior Cave (Dark, claustrophobic, filled with tenuous vapor)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From alertness/combat readiness to helpless terror and sensory isolation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme Close-ups (Eyes, sweating skin, gas swirling)
- POV shots (Blurred, limited movement)
- Macro shots of the vapor

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The exact position of the protagonist's limbs; the visual density/movement of the gas.

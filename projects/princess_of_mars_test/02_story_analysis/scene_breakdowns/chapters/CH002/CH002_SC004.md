# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To transition the protagonist from the supernatural interior to the natural exterior.

**Scene Summary:** Driven by fear of whatever is in the dark cave, the protagonist flees the cavern and emerges into the Arizona night.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist

**Participating Environments:**
- Cave Interior (Transitioning)
- Arizona Mountain Landscape (Night/Moonlight)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From shock to frantic urgency/flight.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tracking shots following the protagonist's movement
- High-contrast lighting (Dark cave vs. Moonlight)
- Wide shots of the character emerging into the vast landscape

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The protagonist's nudity and physical state as he moves through different light levels.

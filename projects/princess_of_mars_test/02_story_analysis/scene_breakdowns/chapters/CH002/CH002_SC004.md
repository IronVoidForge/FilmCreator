# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To transition the narrative from a terrestrial horror setting to a cosmic science-fiction setting.

**Scene Summary:** Fearing the presence in the cave, the narrator escapes into the Arizona night. Upon seeing the red star of Mars, he feels an irresistible magnetic pull that launches him through the vacuum of space.

**Participating Characters:**
* The Narrator (spirit)

**Participating Environments:**
* Arizona mountains/night sky.
* Deep space/The void.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From terrestrial fear to cosmic awe and overwhelming momentum.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Wide shots of the narrator under a vast, star-filled night sky.
* VFX-heavy sequence: The "pull" effect, stars streaking, and the transition from Earth's atmosphere into the darkness of space.
* Extreme wide shots of the planet Mars.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
* The visual appearance of Mars (the red star).
* The seamlessness of the transition from the mountain landscape to the cosmic void.

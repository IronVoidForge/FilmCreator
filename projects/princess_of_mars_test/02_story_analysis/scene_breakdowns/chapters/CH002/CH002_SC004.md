# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Trigger the inciting incident of the cosmic journey.

**Scene Summary:** While observing the landscape, the protagonist becomes mesmerized by Mars. A sudden surge of will causes an instantaneous, violent transport through cold and darkness toward the planet.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist

**Participating Environments:**
- Arizona Mountain Overlook (Starlight night)
- Deep Space/Void (Transitionary)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Wonder $\rightarrow$ Transcendence/Disorientation

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme Wide shots (The landscape and the red star)
- Close-ups (Protagonist's mesmerized expression)
- Abstract/Surreal VFX sequences (The transition through space)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The visual appearance of Mars in the sky.
- The suddenness of the "cut" or transition from Earth to the void.

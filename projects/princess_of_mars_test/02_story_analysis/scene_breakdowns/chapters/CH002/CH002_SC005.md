# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To trigger the protagonist's cosmic displacement and end the chapter on a high-concept cliffhanger.

**Scene Summary:** While observing the landscape, the protagonist becomes mesmerized by Mars (the red star). He feels an irresistible pull toward it. As he closes his eyes, he is violently transported through space into darkness and cold.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist

**Participating Environments:**
- Arizona Mountain Landscape (Night)
- Deep Space / Void

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From mesmerized trance to sudden, overwhelming cosmic displacement.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme Wide shots of the landscape and the red star
- Close-ups of the protagonist's eyes reflecting the star
- Abstract/VFX heavy sequences for the space transport (Motion blur, light streaks)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The visual appearance and brightness of Mars in the sky.
- The transition from the grounded Arizona environment to the abstract space sequence.

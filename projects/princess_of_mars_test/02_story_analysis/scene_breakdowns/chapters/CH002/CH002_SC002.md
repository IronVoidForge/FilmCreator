# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce the supernatural threat and strip away the narrator's last connection to humanity (the warriors).

**Scene Summary:** A terrifying, low moan erupts from the depths of the cave, causing the Apache warriors to flee in absolute panic, leaving the narrator completely isolated.

**Participating Characters:**
* The Narrator (internal/POV)
* Apache Warriors

**Participating Environments:**
* Cave entrance/ledge.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension to pure, unadulterated horror and isolation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Reaction shots of the warriors' faces as they hear the sound.
* Low-angle shots of the cave mouth to emphasize the scale of the darkness.
* Rapid movement/handheld shots of the retreating warriors.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
* Sound design synchronization (the timing of the moan relative to character reaction).
* The direction and speed of the warriors' retreat.

# Scene SC002: Breaking Free & Flight

## Scene Purpose
Show the narrator's superhuman effort to break free from paralysis, reveal the supernatural truth of his body state (naked vs clothed corpse), and execute flight into the Arizona night. Transition from cave interior to open landscape.

## Scene Summary
The narrator remains paralyzed until late afternoon when his horse wanders off. Silence persists until midnight, then a weird moan returns. With a superhuman effort of will, the narrator breaks free from paralysis. He discovers his original body lies lifeless and clothed on the cave floor while he stands naked. Unable to defend himself (revolvers/carbine inaccessible), he flees into the Arizona night.

## Participating Characters
- **Narrator:** Breaks from paralysis, discovers body state, flees

## Participating Environments
- **Cave interior:** Dim light, opening to daylight, dead friend's body on ledge
- **Arizona night landscape:** Rocky gorge, cacti-studded flat, mountains

## Dominant Emotional Shift
**Paralysis -> Superhuman effort -> Desperation**  
The narrator moves from complete immobility through willpower to desperate flight. The revelation of his naked state vs clothed corpse creates shock and vulnerability.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- **Extreme close-ups:** Narrator's eyes during superhuman effort, facial tension breaking paralysis
- **Wide shots:** Cave interior showing original body lifeless/clothed on floor, narrator standing naked
- **Tracking shots:** Narrator fleeing into Arizona night landscape
- **Low-angle shots:** Emphasizing vulnerability and desperation during flight

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- **Body state revelation:** Original body must appear clothed/lifeless while narrator is naked/standing
- **Weapon accessibility:** Revolvers/carbine must be shown inaccessible (strapped to lifeless body, in boot on saddle)
- **Horse wandering off:** Must occur before midnight silence period
- **Lighting transition:** Moonlight flooding cave during breaking free sequence

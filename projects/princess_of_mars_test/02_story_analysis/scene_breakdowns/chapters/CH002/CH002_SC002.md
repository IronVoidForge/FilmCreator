# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Execute the supernatural reveal of the protagonist's dual existence.

**Scene Summary:** After hours of mental struggle, the protagonist breaks free from the paralysis only to realize he has undergone a metamorphosis; his new, naked self stands above his original, clothed body which remains lifeless on the cave floor.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist (New/Astral form)
- The Protagonist's Physical Body (Dead/Clothed)

**Participating Environments:**
- Interior Cave

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Struggle $\rightarrow$ Shock/Existential Horror

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Macro shots (The physical body's features)
- POV shots (Protagonist looking down at himself)
- Wide shot (Establishing the spatial relationship between the two bodies)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The exact positioning of the dead body vs. the standing protagonist.
- Lighting shifts as he regains consciousness/movement.

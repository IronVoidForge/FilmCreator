# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce external conflict and demonstrate the supernatural power of the cave's contents.

**Scene Summary:** A group of Apache warriors approaches the cave entrance. They find the protagonist but are interrupted by a terrifying, low moaning sound emanating from the depths. The warriors flee in panic; one warrior falls to his death during the retreat.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist (Immobile observer)
- Apache Warriors

**Participating Environments:**
- Cave Entrance / Rocky Gorge

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From curiosity/investigation to primal terror and chaos.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the gorge/entrance
- Handheld/Shaky cam (To emphasize the panic of the warriors)
- Low angle shots of the warriors' approach

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Positioning of the warriors relative to the cave mouth.
- The timing and direction of the "moaning" sound in relation to character reactions.

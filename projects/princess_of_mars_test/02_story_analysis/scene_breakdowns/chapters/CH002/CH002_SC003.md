# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To execute the central supernatural reveal and the narrator's transition from physical to spiritual existence.

**Scene Summary:** Through sheer mental willpower, the narrator breaks the paralysis, only to realize that his consciousness has detached from his body; he discovers his own lifeless, naked corpse lying on the cave floor.

**Participating Characters:**
* The Narrates (as a separate entity/spirit)
* The Narrator's physical body (corpse)

**Participating Environments:**
* Deep within the cave.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From struggle/exertion to profound shock and existential horror.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Close-ups of the narrator's "spirit" perspective looking down.
* High-angle shots looking down at the lifeless body on the floor.
* Dissolving or ethereal visual effects to represent the separation of consciousness.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
* The physical state/position of the corpse must match the narrator's previous paralyzed position.
* Lighting changes as the "spirit" moves through the cave.

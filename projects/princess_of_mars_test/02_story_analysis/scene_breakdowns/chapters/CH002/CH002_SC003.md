# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Transition the protagonist from the claustrophobic cave to the vastness of the landscape.

**Scene Summary:** Driven by fear of the presence in the cave, the protagonist flees into the Arizona night.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist (Naked)

**Participating Environments:**
- Cave Exit / Rocky Gorge (Night)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Fear $\rightarrow$ Desperation/Exhaustion

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tracking shots (Following the protagonist's flight)
- Wide landscape shots (Emphasizing his exposure and isolation)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The level of physical exertion/sweat on the protagonist.
- Moonlight direction and intensity.

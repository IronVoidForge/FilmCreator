# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To execute the central supernatural twist and reveal the protagonist's transformation.

**Scene Summary:** After hours of isolation, the protagonist uses intense mental willpower to break the paralysis. Upon standing, he realizes he has undergone a metamorphosis: his new, naked self stands upright while his original, clothed body remains lying lifeless on the cave floor.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist (The "New" Self)
- The Protagonist (The "Old" Body)

**Participating Environments:**
- Interior Cave (Deep shadows)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From intense mental struggle to profound shock and existential horror.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of the physical struggle/effort
- Reveal shot: A wide or medium shot showing both the standing naked figure and the lying clothed body in one frame.
- Close-ups of the protagonist's face as he realizes his state.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The exact placement of the "old" body relative to where the "new" self stands.
- Lighting consistency between the two versions of the character.

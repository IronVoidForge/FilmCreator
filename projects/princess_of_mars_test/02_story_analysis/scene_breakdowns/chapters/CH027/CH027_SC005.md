# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** The climax and resolution of the immediate crisis.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter arrives at the Atmosphere Plant, finding a graveyard of workers. He uses his mental thought waves to unlock the three great doors and commands a survivor to restart the pumps before he collapses from oxygen deprivation.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Surviving Worker.

**Participating Environments:** The Atmosphere Plant (interior/exterior).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** High Tension $\rightarrow$ Exhausted Relief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups of the "thought wave" mental effort; wide shots of the massive doors opening; shaky, handheld footage as John collapses.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The mechanical state of the plant (doors opening/pumps starting); John's physical condition (exhaustion/asphyxiation).

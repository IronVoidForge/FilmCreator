# Scene SC002 - Escalation: Carter Discovers Key and Flies to Plant

## Purpose
Show John Carter discovering the thought waves as the key to unlock the atmosphere plant doors, then executing his high-speed flight to reach the plant before time runs out.

## Summary
John Carter discovers "nine long forgotten sounds" (thought waves) which serve as the key to unlock the three great doors of the atmosphere plant engine. He flies to the plant at high speed, discarding all weight except the engine and compass. The flight path crosses an old sea bottom to maintain sufficient air for breathing. Carter lies on the deck and discards ornaments during the journey.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter

## Participating Environments
- Airship interior (flight preparation)
- Flight path across old sea bottom
- Atmosphere Plant exterior (great walls looming, small door with crew laboring to pierce wall)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Determination → Urgency

Carter's discovery of thought waves creates determination, but the rapidly decreasing air pressure and time constraints create urgency. His weight reduction actions show desperation while maintaining mission focus.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Low angle shots of airship interior during preparation
- Tracking shots along flight path across old sea bottom
- Establishing shots of Atmosphere Plant exterior with great walls
- Close-ups of Carter discarding ornaments and lying on deck

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Weight reduction visual cues (discarded ornaments, engine and compass only)
- Flight path continuity (old sea bottom crossing)
- Air pressure maintenance during flight (breathing difficulty)
- Plant exterior crew laboring to pierce wall positioning

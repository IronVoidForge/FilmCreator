# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the inciting incident and the sudden shift in planetary stakes.

**Scene Summary:** News reaches the palace that the keeper of the atmosphere plant has been murdered and the engine has stopped. The physical sensation of the air thinning begins to affect the population.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tardos Mors, various messengers/officials.

**Participating Environments:** The Palace of Helium; streets of Helium.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Peace $\rightarrow$ Panic/Urgency.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Rapid cuts; handheld camera to increase tension; shots of people gasping or looking at the sky; news-bearing messengers in motion.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Atmospheric visual cues (haze, light refraction changes).

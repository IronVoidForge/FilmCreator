# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Escalate the stakes to a life-or-death crisis and establish the emotional toll.

**Scene Summary:** As air pressure drops, Helium faces mass asphyxiation. In the sunken garden, the royal family prepares for death. Dejah Thoris falls into a coma near the incubator, leaving John Carter alone in his desperation.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tardos Mors, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** The Sunken Garden (Helium).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Despair/Grief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-light, suffocating atmosphere shots.
- Close-ups of labored breathing and gasping.
- Static, heavy compositions as the characters succumb to lethargy.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The level of oxygen deprivation (physical symptoms like blue lips or lethargy); Dejah Thoris's position relative to the incubator.

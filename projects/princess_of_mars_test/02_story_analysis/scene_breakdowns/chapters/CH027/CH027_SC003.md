# Scene SC003 - Climax: Carter Arrives and Opens Doors Sequentially

## Purpose
Execute the climax sequence where Carter arrives at the atmosphere plant and opens the three great doors sequentially while his companion starts the pumps to save Barsoom.

## Summary
Carter arrives at the atmosphere plant. Three great doors open sequentially; Carter opens the second and third while collapsing unconscious. The companion starts the pumps to save Barsoom from extinction. This scene represents the critical moment where Barsoom's survival hangs in the balance.

## Participating Characters

- Companion (unnamed)

The scene begins with intense focus as Carter opens the doors, then shifts to exhaustion as he collapses unconscious. The companion's pump activation provides relief but doesn't prevent Carter's collapse.

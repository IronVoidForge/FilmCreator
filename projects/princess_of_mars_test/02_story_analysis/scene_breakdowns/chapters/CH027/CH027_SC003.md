# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Provide the protagonist's "Aha!" moment and shift the mode from passive dying to active rescue.

**Scene Summary:** As Dejah Thoris slips into a coma, John Carter experiences a sudden, intense mental breakthrough. He recalls nine specific "thought waves" that act as the keys to the atmosphere plant's locks.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris (unconscious).

**Participating Environments:** Sunken Garden, Helium Palace.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Despair $\rightarrow$ Intense Mental Clarity/Resolve.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Abstract/Surreal visual effects to represent the "thought waves."
- Extreme close-ups on John Carter's eyes and face.
- High-contrast lighting to differentiate the mental breakthrough from the physical lethargy.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The timing of the coma onset relative to the mental epiphany.

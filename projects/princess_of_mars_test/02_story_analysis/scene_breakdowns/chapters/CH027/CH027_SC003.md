# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Transition from despair to action; provide the "hero's realization."

**Scene Summary:** John Carter recalls the nine "thought waves" required to unlock the plant. He frantically modifies a high-speed air-scout machine, stripping it of all non-essential weight to race through the thinning atmosphere toward the plant.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** Palace/Hangar area; The sky above Barsoom.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Despair $\rightarrow$ Focused Determination/Adrenaline.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Fast-paced, rhythmic editing during the machine modification.
- Kinetic, high-speed tracking shots of the air-scout machine flying through a thinning, hazy sky.
- Extreme close-ups of John's eyes as he recalls the thought waves.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The state of the stripped-down air-scout; the visual density/clarity of the atmosphere (getting thinner as he flies).

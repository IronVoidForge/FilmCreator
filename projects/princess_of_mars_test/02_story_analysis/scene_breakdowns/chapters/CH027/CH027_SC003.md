# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Heighten the stakes through personal tragedy and physical desperation.

**Scene Summary:** As oxygen levels plummet, the royal family retreats to a sunken garden. Dejah Thoris falls into a coma due to asphyxiation, leaving John Carter in a state of desperate grief and isolation.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tardos Mors.

**Participating Environments:** A sunken garden in Helium.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Panic $\rightarrow$ Despair/Suffocation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of the dying environment; tight close-ups on Dejah's face; heavy use of shallow depth of field to simulate disorientation.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical state of Dejah Thoris (unconsciousness); lighting shifts as the atmosphere thins.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the status quo of peace and introduce the inciting incident (the murder and engine failure).

**Scene Summary:** A montage/sequence showing nine years of prosperity for John Carter and Dejah Thoris, centered around their golden egg incubator. This peace is shattered by a dispatch bearer announcing the murder of the Atmosphere Plant keeper and the subsequent drop in planetary air pressure.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tardos Mors, Dispatch Bearer.

**Participating Environments:** The Palace in Helium (Rooftop Shrine/Sunken Garden).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Contentment/Peace $\rightarrow$ Panic/Urgency.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Warm, golden-hued close-ups of the incubator and the couple.
- Wide shots of a peaceful Helium.
- High-angle/chaotic handheld for the arrival of the messenger.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The appearance/age of the characters over the nine-year jump; the physical state of the golden incubator.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the baseline of peace and introduce the inciting catastrophe.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter and Dejah Thoris enjoy a period of prosperity and domestic bliss in Helium, awaiting their child's hatching. This peace is shattered by news that the Keeper of the Atmosphere Plant has been murdered, causing the planetary air pressure to plummet.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tardos Mors.

**Participating Environments:** Palace Gardens, Helium.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Bliss $\rightarrow$ Terror/Urgency.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Warm, soft lighting for the garden bliss.
- Rapid, handheld or jarring cuts when news of the murder arrives.
- Wide shots of the peaceful city transitioning to panicked close-ups.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The transition from the relaxed posture of the lovers to the sudden tension of the royal council.

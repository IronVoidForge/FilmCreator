# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** The Climax; resolve the immediate threat through mental and physical effort.

**Scene Summary:** John arrives at the Atmosphere Plant to find a graveyard of workers. He uses his mental "thought waves" to unlock the three great doors, finds a surviving technician, and commands him to restart the pumps before John collapses from exhaustion and hypoxia.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Surviving Technician.

**Participating Environments:** The Atmosphere Plant (Interior/Exterior).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Intense Concentration $\rightarrow$ Relief $\rightarrow$ Physical Collapse.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the massive, imposing doors.
- Macro shots/abstract visuals representing the "thought waves" interacting with the locks.
- Handheld, shaky camera to mirror John's oxygen deprivation and eventual collapse.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The mechanical state of the pumps; the visual representation of the thought waves (if any); the physical exhaustion level of John Carter.

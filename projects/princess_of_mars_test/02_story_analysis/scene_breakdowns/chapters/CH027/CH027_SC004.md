# Scene SC004 - Aftermath: Barsoom Saved, Carter Collapses

## Purpose
Show the resolution of the crisis with Barsoom saved from extinction and John Carter collapsing from exhaustion after saving everyone.

## Summary
The atmosphere plant engine has been restored through Carter's actions. The entire Barsoom population is saved from extinction by asphyxiation. John Carter collapses from exhaustion after saving everyone, having opened all three great doors and enabled the pumps to start. This scene provides emotional payoff for the audience while showing the physical cost of Carter's heroism.

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Relief → Collapse

The scene begins with relief as Barsoom is saved, then shifts to collapse as Carter succumbs to exhaustion. The emotional payoff for the audience contrasts with Carter's physical deterioration.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Transition from despair to action; provide the "hero's realization."

**Scene Summary:** In a moment of clarity amidst the thinning air, John Carter remembers the nine "thought waves" needed to operate the plant. He strips an air-scout machine of all weight and launches into a high-speed, desperate flight through the dying atmosphere.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** The Palace/Hangar; the Martian sky (thinning).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Despair $\rightarrow$ Determination/Adrenaline.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** POV shots from the cockpit; extreme wide shots of the scout machine against a vast, empty sky; fast-paced tracking shots.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The speed and trajectory of the air-scout machine; the visual appearance of the thinning atmosphere (sky color/clarity).

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the protagonist's psychological isolation and sensory deprivation.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist is cast into a pitch-black dungeon in Warhoon and chained to the floor, enduring long periods of terrifying darkness.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist.

**Participating Environments:** Dark, claustrophobic dungeon.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Psychological Exhaustion/Madness.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-key lighting; heavy use of shadows (Chiaroscuro).
- Extreme close-ups on the protagonist's eyes and breathing.
- Sound-driven coverage (focusing on ambient dungeon noises).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Chain length/position; level of darkness/visibility.

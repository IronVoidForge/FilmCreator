# SC004 - Power Transfer and Imprisonment

## Scene Purpose
Bar Comas killed by Dak Kova, expedition abandoned, narrator imprisoned in dark dungeon. This scene processes the aftermath of the confrontation and establishes Narrator's new captivity situation.

## Scene Summary
Following the brutal combat, Bar Comas wins but is killed by Dak Kova, who then assumes title of Jeddak of Warhoon. The expedition is abandoned until after great games; ten thousand warriors turn back toward Warhoon. Narrator is imprisoned in dark dungeon, chained to floor and walls for indeterminate period. This scene establishes the horror of darkness and unseen threats while showing Dak Kova's consolidation of power.

## Participating Characters
- **Narrator**: Now fully captive in dark dungeon
- **Dak Kova**: Now Jeddak of Warhoon after killing Bar Comas
- **Bar Comas**: Dead, killed by Dak Kova in brutal fight
- **Ten thousand warriors**: Turn back toward Warhoon (implied presence)

## Participating Environments
- Warhoon camp transitioning to dark dungeon
- Dark dungeon in city of Warhoon, filled with creeping things and fiery eyes
- Transition from outdoor camp to underground imprisonment

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Shock → Horror → Powerlessness → Darkness → Hopelessness

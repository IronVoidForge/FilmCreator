# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To show the protagonist's transition from victim to survivor through a violent act.

**Scene Summary:** Driven by desperation, the protagonist ambushes his food deliverer using a heavy chain. He kills the man and successfully retrieves the keys to his cell.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Jailer

**Participating Environments:**
- The Dungeon (Cell)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Primal Survival/Adrenaline.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tight, claustrophobic framing.
- Sudden, kinetic movement shots during the ambush.
- Close-ups of the keys and the chain.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The weight and length of the chain used.
- Blood/gore on the protagonist's hands post-kill.

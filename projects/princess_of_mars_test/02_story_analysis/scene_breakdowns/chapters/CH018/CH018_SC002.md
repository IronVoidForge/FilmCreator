# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To execute a major narrative pivot through character conflict and the death of a leader.

**Scene Summary:** Dak Kova openly defies Jeddak Bar Comas. The confrontation escalates into a primal, brutal duel. Bar Comas is killed, and Dak Kova seizes power through violence.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist (observer), Dak Kova, Bar Comas, Warhoon Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Open Martian plains (military camp/march area).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Chaos/Violence $\rightarrow$ Shock/Awe.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Mid-shots of the confrontation between Kova and Comas.
- Handheld, kinetic shots during the duel to emphasize brutality.
- Reaction shots from the protagonist and surrounding warriors.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Blood splatter patterns; weapon positions; Dak Kova's physical state before/after the kill.

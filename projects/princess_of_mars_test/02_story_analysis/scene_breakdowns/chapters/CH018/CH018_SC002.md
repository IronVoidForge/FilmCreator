# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To depict the violent political transition and the brutality of Warhoon culture.

**Scene Summary:** Upon arrival at the main camp, a power struggle erupts between the young Jeddak Bar Comas and his lieutenant Dak Kova. They engage in a primal duel that ends with the death of Bar Comas and Dak Kova's ascension to leadership.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist (Observer)
- Bar Comas
- Dak Kova
- Warhoon Horde

**Participating Environments:**
- Warhoon Main Camp

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Visceral Violence $\rightarrow$ Shock/Awe.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-angle shots of the combatants to emphasize ferocity.
- Handheld/shaky cam during the duel to increase intensity.
- Reaction shots from the protagonist and the surrounding horde.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Blood splatter patterns on characters and ground.
- Weaponry used in the duel.

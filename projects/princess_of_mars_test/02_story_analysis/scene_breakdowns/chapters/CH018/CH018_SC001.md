# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's vulnerability and his new status as a captive of the Warhoon.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist regains consciousness in a medical setting, treated by an ancient Warhoon healer. He is subsequently strapped to a wild thoat and forced into the middle of a massive military column.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Female Healer, Warhoon Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Small medical room; Martian plains (during the march).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion/Pain $\rightarrow$ Dread/Disorientation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Extreme close-ups of eyes opening/pain.
- Wide shots of the vast, intimidating Warhoon column.
- POV shots from the protagonist's perspective on the thoat.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Wound placement/bandaging; tightness of the restraints on the thoat.

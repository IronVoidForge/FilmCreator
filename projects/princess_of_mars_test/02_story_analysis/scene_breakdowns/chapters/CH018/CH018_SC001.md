# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's vulnerability and the alien, grotesque nature of his captors.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist regains consciousness in a Warhoon camp. He is treated by an ancient, ugly female healer before being strapped to a wild thoat for transport to the main column.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Female Healer, Warhoon Warriors (background).

**Participating Environments:** Small room within a Warhoon camp; Martian plains (exterior).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion/Pain $\rightarrow$ Dread/Helplessness.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme close-ups of the protagonist's eyes and wounds; medium shots of the healer's grotesque features; wide shots of the vast, harsh Martian landscape during transport.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Wound placement and severity; state of the protagonist's bindings/straps to the thoat.

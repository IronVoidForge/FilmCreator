# Scene 001: Awakening and Report

## Purpose
Establish protagonist's vulnerable state after capture and introduce power dynamics between captors through Dak Kova's report to Bar Comas.

## Summary
Protagonist regains consciousness in a small room filled with sleeping silks and furs. Dak Kova attends him and reports to Bar Comas, the young jeddak of Warhoon, noting the tension between them. The scene establishes the political context and the protagonist's helpless position.

## Participating Characters
- Protagonist (conscious but vulnerable)
- Dak Kova (attendant/captor)
- Bar Comas (young jeddak, mentioned in report)

## Participating Environments
- Small room with sleeping silks and furs
- Political setting of Warhoon court

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Neutral curiosity → Underlying tension and political stakes

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shots establishing room atmosphere
- Close-ups on facial expressions during report
- Over-the-shoulder shots for dialogue dynamics
- Establishing shots showing silk/fur textures

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Protagonist's physical state (conscious but restrained)
- Dak Kova's positioning relative to protagonist
- Bar Comas' presence/absence clarity in scene
- Room lighting consistency with silk/fur elements

# SC001 - Awakening and Revelation

## Scene Purpose
Narrator regains consciousness after capture, discovering he is alive among Warhoon warriors. Dak Kova reveals plans to use narrator as sport for the great games.

## Scene Summary
The chapter opens with Narrator waking in a small room decorated with sleeping silks and furs. An Ancient Female Warrior attends to his wounds, revealing therapeutic skills. Dak Kova appears and explains his intentions - the narrator will be used as entertainment for the great games at Warhoon. The scene establishes the captivity situation and the brutal culture of Warhoon.

## Participating Characters
- **Narrator**: Captured outsider experiencing confusion and horror upon awakening
- **Dak Kova**: The jed who captured him, now displaying his plans with cold calculation
- **Ancient Female Warrior**: Attends to narrator's wounds, reveals therapeutic skills and knowledge of Warhoon culture

## Participating Environments
- Small room with sleeping silks and furs (initial capture location)
- Warhoon camp visible in background during conversation

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Confusion → Horror → Realization of captivity → Understanding the brutal culture

- Medium shots showing Dak Kova's authoritative presence
- Over-the-shoulder shots during revelation dialogue

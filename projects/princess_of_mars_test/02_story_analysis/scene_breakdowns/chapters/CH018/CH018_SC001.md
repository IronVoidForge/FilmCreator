# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's vulnerability and his status as a captive prize.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist regains consciousness after being rescued by Warhoon warriors. He is treated by an ancient female healer before being strapped to a wild thoat to be transported to the main Warhoon column for the "great games."

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Healer
- Warhoon Warriors

**Participating Environments:**
- Warhoon Camp (Outskirts/Medical area)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion/Pain $\rightarrow$ Helplessness/Dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme close-ups of eyes opening/healing hands.
- Medium shots of the protagonist being bound to the thoat.
- Wide shots showing the scale of the Warhoon movement.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Wound placement and bandage state.
- The physical restraints used on the thoat.

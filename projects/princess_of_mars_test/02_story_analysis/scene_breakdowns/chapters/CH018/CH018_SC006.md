# Scene 006: Mysterious Eyes

## Purpose
Create mystery and establish ongoing threat beyond the immediate escape.

## Summary
Six pairs of gleaming eyes approach in the darkness before retreating. This scene introduces an unknown element that suggests larger forces at play and maintains tension after the escape attempt.

## Participating Characters
- Unknown entities (represented by eyes)
- Protagonist (witness)

## Participating Environments
- Dark dungeon cell
- Shadowed confinement space

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Uncertainty → Dread and mystery

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Extreme close-ups on gleaming eyes
- Shadow play for atmosphere
- Pull-back to reveal protagonist's reaction
- Minimal lighting for mystery effect

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Eye count accuracy (six pairs)
- Eye appearance consistency
- Protagonist's reaction timing
- Darkness level maintenance

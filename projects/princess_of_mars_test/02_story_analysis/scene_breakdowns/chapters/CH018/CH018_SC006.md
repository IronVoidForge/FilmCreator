# SC006

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce a new, supernatural or predatory threat and end the chapter on a cliffhanger.

**Scene Summary:** As the protagonist prepares to use the keys, he realizes he is not alone; multiple pairs of eyes emerge from the darkness, watching him before retreating.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Unseen Entities (Eyes in the dark).

**Participating Environments:** Dark dungeon.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Hope $\rightarrow$ Pure Terror/Paranoia.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Shallow depth of field focusing on the gleaming eyes.
- Slow pans through the darkness to reveal nothing but shadows.
- Tight close-up on the protagonist's frozen expression.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Eye placement in the frame; lighting levels.

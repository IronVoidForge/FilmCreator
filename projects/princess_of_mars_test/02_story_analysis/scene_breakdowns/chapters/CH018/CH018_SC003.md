# Scene 003: Aftermath and Return

## Purpose
Show the consequences of the battle and establish the scale of forces returning to Warhoon city.

## Summary
The expedition is abandoned due to injuries, and the ten thousand warriors return to Warhoon city. This scene emphasizes the magnitude of the force and the protagonist's continued captivity context.

## Participating Characters
- Warriors (collective, ten thousand)
- Protagonist (unseen during procession)

## Participating Environments
- Return journey from battle site
- Outskirts/entry to Warhoon city

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Shock → Resignation and movement toward established power center

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Tracking shots for procession scale
- Establishing shots of returning force
- Wide aerial or high-angle shots for magnitude
- Slow zooms on key figures in procession

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Warrior count visual consistency
- Protagonist's position relative to returning force
- Warhoon city establishing clarity
- Injury indicators on warriors if shown

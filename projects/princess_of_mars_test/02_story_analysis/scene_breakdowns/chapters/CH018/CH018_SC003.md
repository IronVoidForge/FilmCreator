# SC003 - Confrontation at Camp

Narrator taken before Bar Comas, Dak Kova displays insubordination leading to brutal combat. This is the central action sequence establishing power dynamics within Warhoon society.

Dak Kova displays open insubordination toward Bar Comas, leading to brutal hand-to-hand combat between them.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To convey the psychological toll of sensory deprivation and isolation.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist is imprisoned in a pitch-black dungeon. He endures prolonged isolation, accompanied only by the sound and sensation of unknown crawling creatures in the dark.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist

**Participating Environments:**
- The Dungeon (Pitch-black cell)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Despair $\rightarrow$ Psychological Terror.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- High-contrast lighting (Chiaroscuro).
- Macro shots of skin/eyes reacting to darkness.
- Sound-driven sequences where visuals are minimal or obscured.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Protagonist's physical appearance (dirt, hair length, beard growth) to indicate passage of time.

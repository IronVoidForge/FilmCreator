# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To illustrate the cultural brutality of the Warhoon and build atmospheric dread.

**Scene Summary:** The army turns back toward the city of Warhoon. The protagonist observes the constant, casual violence and dueling that defines the Warhoon march.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, various Warhoon Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Martian plains (moving towards Warhoon).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Observation $\rightarrow$ Growing Horror/Despair.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tracking shots following the movement of the army.
- Montage-style coverage of smaller skirmishes/duels within the ranks.
- Close-ups of the protagonist's face reflecting the horror.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Movement direction of the column; lighting (time of day transition).

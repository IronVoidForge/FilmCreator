# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To provide a climax to the protagonist's isolation and end on a high-tension cliffhanger.

**Scene Summary:** Driven by madness, the protagonist ambushes his food deliverer with a heavy chain, killing him. He finds the jailer's keys but is immediately confronted by glowing eyes in the dark.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Jailer (deceased), Unseen Entities (eyes only).

**Participating Environments:** Subterranean dungeon.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Violent Release $\rightarrow$ Primal Terror.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** High-contrast lighting; rapid cutting during the ambush; sudden stillness/freeze frame on the glowing eyes in the dark.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The heavy chain used as a weapon; the positioning of the keys; the timing of the "eyes" appearing in relation to the protagonist's movement.

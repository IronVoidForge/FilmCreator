# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce a new supernatural or unknown threat in the aftermath of the protagonist's escape attempt.

**Scene Summary:** As the protagonist regains his composure after the killing, he is confronted by several pairs of glowing eyes watching him from the darkness before they retreat.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- Mysterious Entities (Eyes only)

**Participating Environments:**
- The Dungeon

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief/Sanity $\rightarrow$ Paranoia/Dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme close-ups of the glowing eyes in the blackness.
- Shallow depth of field to isolate the protagonist's face.
- Slow pans into the darkness.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The position and number of the glowing eyes.

# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To provide a moment of violent agency and a potential path to escape.

**Scene Summary:** Driven by near-madness, the protagonist ambushes his food deliverer using his heavy chains, killing him. He discovers the jailer's keys on the corpse.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Jailer/Food Deliverer.

**Participating Environments:** Dark dungeon.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Primal Violence $\rightarrow$ Sudden Clarity/Hope.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- High-tension close-ups of the protagonist's movement in the dark.
- Impact shots of the chains hitting the target.
- Close-up on the keys being recovered.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Position of the corpse; state of the chains; lighting (minimal light source from the door).

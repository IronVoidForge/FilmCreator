# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the high-stakes rescue and the hero's physical dominance over the antagonist.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter infiltrates the city of Thark, witnesses Tal Hajus's plan to torture Dejah Thoris, and intervenes by knocking the Jeddak unconscious. He secures Dejah Thoris and Sola for their escape.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Tal Hajus.

**Participating Environments:** The City of Thark (circular halls/high platforms).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror/Dread $\rightarrow$ Sudden Action/Triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Low-angle hero shots of Carter.
- Close-ups of Tal Hajus's cruelty.
- Wide shots of the Thark architecture to establish scale.
- Handheld/kinetic movement during the strike.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's physical position relative to the Jeddak; Dejah Thoris's state of dress/disarray after captivity.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the high-stakes rescue and the protagonist's decisive intervention against the antagonist.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter infiltrates the city of Thark to find Dejah Thoris being threatened by the Jeddak, Tal Hajus. Rather than engaging in a prolonged duel, Carter uses his superior strength to knock Tal Hajus unconscious, facilitating a swift escape for Dejah and Sola.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Tal Hajus, Tars Tarkas (observer).

**Participating Environments:** The City of Thark (circular halls/raised platforms).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror/Despair $\rightarrow$ Sudden Action $\rightarrow$ Relief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-ups on Tal Hajus's cruelty and Dejah's terror.
- Medium shots of Carter's infiltration.
- Dynamic, fast-paced movement during the knockout blow.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The positioning of the characters on the raised platforms; the specific moment Tal Hajus is incapacitated.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the high stakes of the rescue and execute the successful extraction from Thark.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist infiltrates the city to find Dejah Thoris under threat by Tal Hajus. He knocks out the Jeddak, rescues Dejah and Sola, and escapes through a window into the night.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Tal Hajus (unconscious).

**Participating Environments:** The interior of the Jeddak's palace/chambers in Thark; city rooftops/windows.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension/Dread $\rightarrow$ Action/Adrenaline $\rightarrow$ Relief/Urgency.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Tight close-ups on Dejah's terror, medium shots of the physical struggle with Tal Hajus, wide tracking shots of the escape through the window.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels in the palace vs. the exterior; wounds or dishevelment of characters after the scuffle.

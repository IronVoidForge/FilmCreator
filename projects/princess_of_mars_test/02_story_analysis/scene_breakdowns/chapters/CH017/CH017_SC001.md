# Scene SC001: The Rescue

## Scene Purpose
Establish the immediate threat to Dejah Thoris and execute the rescue from Thark City. Transition characters from interior confinement to exterior escape.

## Scene Summary
John Carter overhears Tal Hajus's plan to torture Dejah Thoris for ten days. Carter kills Tal Hajus with a punch while protecting her from the jeddak's grasp. Using straps and leather trappings, Carter rescues Dejah Thoris and Sola from the unlighted third floor of the building, lowering them to the ground below. They escape through the courtyard on thoats, heading south towards Helium.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter:** Armed with rifle/revolvers; determined protector.
- **Dejah Thoris:** Riding thoat; threatened but rescued.
- **Sola:** Riding thoat; loyal companion.
- **Tal Hajus:** Dead jeddak (corpse).

## Participating Environments
- **Thark City Courtyard:** Escape route.
- **Third Floor Windows:** Rescue origin point.
- **Inner Chamber with Raised Platform:** Where the plan is overheard.

## Dominant Emotional Shift
**Urgency -> Action.** Carter moves from listening to a threat to immediate physical intervention and escape execution.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- **Close-ups:** Faces of Carter, Dejah Thoris during the punch/rescue.
- **Wide Shots:** Escape through courtyard on thoats.
- **Medium Shots:** Use of straps/trappings for lowering characters.

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- **Location Change:** Interior (3rd floor) to Exterior (Courtyard).
- **Character State:** Tal Hajus transitions from active threat to corpse.
- **Object Count:** Two thoats initially present; escape vehicle confirmed.

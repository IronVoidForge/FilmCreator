# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce the primary antagonist force and trigger the climax.

**Scene Summary:** A massive party of Thark warriors emerges from a mountain pass. The protagonist shoots the chieftain to create a distraction, then commands Sola to flee with Dejah on the last thoat.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Thark Warriors/Chieftain.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste near a mountain pass.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Dread $\rightarrow$ Decisiveness $\rightarrow$ Heartbreak (during the farewell).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme wide shots of the approaching Thark army; close-ups of the protagonist's rifle fire; intimate medium shots of the emotional embrace between the protagonist and Dejah.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Direction of the Thark charge; ammunition count (rifle shots); position of the fleeing thoat.

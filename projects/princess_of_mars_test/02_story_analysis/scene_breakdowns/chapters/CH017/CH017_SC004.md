# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To conclude the chapter with a moment of defeat and vulnerability for the hero.

**Scene Summary:** Having exhausted his ammunition, Carter attempts to lure the warriors away through movement but is eventually overwhelmed by the sheer numbers. He is beaten into unconsciousness as the pursuit continues.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Thark Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Rugged terrain/ravines near the Low Mountains.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Defiance $\rightarrow$ Overwhelming Odds $\rightarrow$ Total Defeat/Blackout.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Handheld, shaky camera to simulate the chaos of the melee.
- Close-ups on Carter's struggle and eventual loss of consciousness.
- Low-angle shots of the overwhelming Thark force.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical damage/bruising on Carter; the transition from movement to stillness as he falls unconscious.

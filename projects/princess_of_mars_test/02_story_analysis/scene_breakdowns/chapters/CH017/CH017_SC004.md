# Scene SC004: The Capture

## Scene Purpose
Depict the final defeat and capture of John Carter. Show the consequence of the pursuit and the loss of resistance.

## Scene Summary
Carter fights the pursuing warriors alone, firing until his rifle is empty. He is eventually struck by a projecting piece of quartz and falls. Despite drawing his long-sword, he is overwhelmed by the torrent of blows from the green warriors and goes down to oblivion, captured by them.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter:** Armed with rifle (empty), long-sword; defeated fighter.
- **Thark Warriors:** Green warriors; overwhelming force.

## Participating Environments
- **Mossy Waste/Ground:** Site of final confrontation and capture.

## Dominant Emotional Shift
**Resistance -> Oblivion/Capture.** Carter moves from fighting alone to being struck down and captured.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- **Action Shots:** Carter firing rifle until empty.
- **Slow Motion/Close-ups:** Struck by projecting piece of quartz; falling.
- **Wide Action Shots:** Torrent of blows from green warriors overwhelming Carter.

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- **Weapon State:** Rifle transitions to empty; long-sword drawn but ineffective.
- **Character State:** Carter transitions from fighter to captured prisoner.
- **Location:** Final ground level engagement in the Waste.

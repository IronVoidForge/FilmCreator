# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To conclude the chapter with a moment of defeat and vulnerability for the hero.

**Scene Summary:** Having exhausted his ammunition, Carter attempts to use his superhuman leaping ability to escape, but he is eventually overtaken by the Thark warriors and beaten into unconsciousness.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Thark Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Defiance $\rightarrow$ Despair/Loss of Control.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Slow-motion or stylized shots of the superhuman leaps.
- Chaotic, close-quarters combat shots (shaky cam).
- High-angle shot looking down at the defeated Carter to emphasize vulnerability.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical state of Carter (injuries/blood); the number of warriors surrounding him.

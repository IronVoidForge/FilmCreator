# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To conclude the chapter with a dramatic defeat and a cliffhanger.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist holds off nearly a thousand warriors alone. After running out of ammunition, he attempts to leap away but trips on quartz and is beaten into unconsciousness by the Tharks.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Thark Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Heroic Defiance $\rightarrow$ Panic/Desperation $\rightarrow$ Total Defeat.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** High-action handheld shots of the rifle fire; wide shots showing the scale of the warriors vs. one man; POV or close-up of the trip and the subsequent swarm of attackers.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The moment the rifle clicks empty; the specific terrain (the quartz piece) that causes the fall.

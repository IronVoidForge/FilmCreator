# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To transition the characters from the immediate danger of the city to the long-term survival struggle.

**Scene Summary:** The group escapes on thoats, heading northeast across a two-hundred-mile mossy waste toward Helium, suffering from extreme exhaustion and lack of supplies.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste (vast, trackless, desolate).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief $\rightarrow$ Desperation/Exhaustion.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide sweeping shots of the landscape to emphasize isolation; medium shots of the characters' weary faces and labored breathing.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Thoa movement/speed; level of dehydration/sweat on characters.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To build tension through environmental hardship and the sense of being pursued.

**Scene Summary:** The group flees on thoats across the Mossy Waste. They face starvation, dehydration, and exhaustion. Woola joins the party, providing a sense of companionship amidst the struggle. One thoat eventually collapses.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste (trackless, mossy, desolate).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief (escape) $\rightarrow$ Desperation/Exhaustion.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide sweeping vistas of the waste to show isolation.
- Medium shots of the characters' physical fatigue.
- Tracking shots of the thoats moving through the terrain.
- Close-ups of Woola tracking.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Water/food levels; the condition of the thoats; the positioning of the group relative to the horizon.

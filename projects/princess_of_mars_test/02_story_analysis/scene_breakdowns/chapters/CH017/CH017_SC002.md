# Scene SC002: The Waste Journey

## Scene Purpose
Depict the travel through the Mossy Waste, highlighting exhaustion and resource loss. Introduce Woola to the party.

## Scene Summary
The party travels through the mossy waste but gets lost due to lack of waterways. Woola joins them after sleeping near Carter. They suffer from exhaustion and hunger. One of the two thoats collapses and is left behind; they continue with one beast, walking while Dejah Thoris rides.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter:** Exhausted; leading party.
- **Dejah Thoris:** Riding remaining thoat.
- **Sola:** Following commands.
- **Woola:** Faithful beast; joins party.
- **Thoats:** Two initially; one left behind (collapsed).

## Participating Environments
- **Mossy Waste:** Yellow moonlit moss, hills, ridges, passes.
- **Flat Terrain:** Sea bottom/flat terrain mentioned for travel.

## Dominant Emotional Shift
**Hope -> Exhaustion.** The party moves from escape to survival mode, marked by physical collapse and disorientation.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- **Wide Landscape Shots:** Yellow moonlit moss, hills, ridges.
- **Medium Shots:** Woola sleeping near Carter; joining the group.
- **Action Shots:** Thoat collapsing, being left behind.

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities

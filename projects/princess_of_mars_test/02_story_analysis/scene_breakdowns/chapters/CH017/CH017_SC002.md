# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To escalate the physical stakes and demonstrate the grueling environmental hardship of Mars.

**Scene Summary:** The group attempts a two-hundred-mile trek across the Mossy Waste toward Helium. Exhaustion, hunger, and thirst take their toll; one thoat collapses, forcing the party to struggle on foot through the desolate landscape.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste (desolate, trackless expanse).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief $\rightarrow$ Physical Exhaustion/Desperation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the vast, empty landscape to emphasize isolation.
- Close-ups on sweating, fatigued faces and parched lips.
- Tracking shots of the struggling thoat and walking feet.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The state of the supplies/water skins; the physical condition (dirt/sweat levels) of the characters.

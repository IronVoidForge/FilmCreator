# Scene SC003: The Ambush

## Scene Purpose
Introduce the massive threat of Thark Warriors. Execute the initial shot and the emotional sacrifice of Dejah Thoris to save Carter.

## Scene Summary
While traveling towards the hills, they spot a party of mounted Thark warriors heading southwesterly. Carter shoots a chieftain using his rifle, but is immediately pursued by the remaining warriors (some thousand men). Dejah Thoris sacrifices herself to save Carter, staying behind to die with him while he fights off the green warriors alone.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter:** Armed with rifle; determined fighter.
- **Dejah Thoris:** Sacrificing herself; riding thoat.
- **Sola:** Following commands.
- **Woola:** Present in party.
- **Thark Warriors:** Mounted party (thousands); some thousand men pursuing.

## Participating Environments

- **Pursuit Path:** Route of the green warriors.

## Dominant Emotional Shift
**Tension -> High Stakes/Sacrifice.** The threat escalates from a single chieftain to an army, culminating in Dejah's sacrifice.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To create a climactic action set-piece and showcase Carter's self-sacrifice.

**Scene Summary:** A Thark chieftain spots the group with a fieldglass. Carter kills the chieftain with his rifle to protect the women, then stays behind to hold off a massive force of nearly a thousand warriors, using his ammunition and acrobatic skills to buy them time.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Thark Chieftain, Thark Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste / Low Mountains.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Panic $\rightarrow$ Heroic Resolve $\rightarrow$ Intense Combat Tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Long shots of the approaching Thark horde.
- POV through the fieldglass.
- High-action coverage of rifle fire and Carter's acrobatic maneuvers.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The count/depletion of rifle ammunition; the distance between Carter and the fleeing women.

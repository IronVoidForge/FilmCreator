# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To execute a high-action climax where the hero sacrifices himself for the protagonists.

**Scene Summary:** A Thark scouting party spots the group. As a chieftain charges, Carter kills him with his rifle and stays behind to provide cover fire against an incoming wave of warriors, allowing Dejah Thoris and Sola to escape.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Thark Warriors/Chieftain.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Panic $\rightarrow$ Heroic Resolve $\rightarrow$ Intense Combat Tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Long lens shots of the approaching Thark force.
- Rapid-fire editing for the rifle shots.
- POV shots from Carter's perspective as he aims.
- Wide shots showing the distance between Carter and the fleeing women.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Rifle ammunition count; direction of fire; the speed/trajectory of the retreating thoats.

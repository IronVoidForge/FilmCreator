# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To escalate the survival stakes and introduce a moment of unexpected companionship.

**Scene Summary:** The party becomes lost in the waste. One thoat collapses from exhaustion, leaving them with only one beast. Amidst the struggle, the protagonist discovers Woola has followed them.

**Participating Characters:** Protagonist, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Woola (the hound).

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Despair $\rightarrow$ Surprise/Warmth $\rightarrow$ Hardship.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of the collapsing thoat; close-ups of the protagonist's reaction to Woola; medium shots of Dejah being hoisted onto the remaining thoat.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The number of functioning thoats; the presence/position of Woola.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To escalate the conflict from survival to active combat and force a heroic sacrifice.

**Scene Summary:** After one thoat collapses, a Thark chieftain spots the group. The narrator kills the chieftain with his rifle but realizes they are being hunted. He orders Dejah Thoris and Sola to flee toward the hills while he stays behind to provide cover fire.

**Participating Characters:** The Narrator, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Thark Chieftain.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste (near the low mountains).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Panic $\rightarrow$ Heroic Resolve.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Long shots of the chieftain scanning with a fieldglass.
- Sharp, rhythmic cuts during the rifle fire.
- High-angle shots looking down on the women fleeing toward the hills.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The location of the fallen thoat; the direction of the narrator's cover fire relative to the retreating party.

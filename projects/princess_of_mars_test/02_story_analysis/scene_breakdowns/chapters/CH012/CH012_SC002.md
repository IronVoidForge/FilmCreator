# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To reveal the internal betrayal (Sarkoja) and solidify Carter's motivation for escape.

**Scene Summary:** Following the audience, Carter realizes that Sarkoja has been spying on him and reporting his intentions to the Thark leadership. This realization of being watched and undermined by a high-ranking female Thark intensifies his resolve to execute an escape before Dejah Thoris is handed over to Tal Hajus.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (Internal monologue/Realization).

**Participating Environments:** Transitioning from the Audience Chamber to the city streets/plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension to focused, desperate determination.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Tight close-ups on Carter's eyes/face to convey internal realization.
* Wide shots of the Thark city to emphasize his isolation and being watched.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's movement through the city; timing of his realization relative to his physical location.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose**
To transition Carter from a prisoner to a settled chieftain and establish his new base of operations.

**Scene Summary**
Tars Tarkas approaches Carter, offering him quarters within the Tharkian complex. Carter chooses a room on the third floor of an adjoining building to maintain proximity to Dejah Thoris. He receives his spoils of war—weapons, silks, furs, and food—from the retinues of the defeated chieftains. Carter also takes charge of the women and youths from the fallen leaders, assigning them roles within his new quarters.

**Participating Characters**
* John Carter
* Tars Tarkas
* Retinues (unnamed Tharkian warriors)
* Women and youths (belonging to the defeated chieftains)

**Participating Environments**
* The Tharkian Plaza/Buildings (winding corridors, multi-story apartments, overgrown courtyards)
* Carter's new quarters (Third floor)

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
From political tension to a sense of rising agency and domestic organization.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
* Montage-style coverage of the spoils being brought in (silks, weapons, food).
* Tracking shots through the winding corridors/stairs.
* Medium shots showing Carter organizing his new household/retinue.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
* The volume and variety of "spoils" (ensuring consistent props across shots).
* Movement between different levels/floors of the Tharkian buildings.

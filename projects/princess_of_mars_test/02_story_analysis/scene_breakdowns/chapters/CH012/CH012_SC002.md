# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To reveal the internal threat (Sarkoja) and solidify Carter's primary motivation for escape.

**Scene Summary:** Following the audience, Carter realizes that Sarkoja has been spying on him and reporting his words to Ptomel. The realization of her betrayal, combined with the fear of what Tal Hajus will do to Dejah Thoris, hardens Carter's resolve to escape at any cost.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (Internal Monologue/Reaction).

**Participating Environments:** Transitioning from the Audience Chamber / Thark City streets.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From suspicion to clarity and intense determination.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Tight close-ups on Carter's eyes/face to convey internal realization.
* Wide shots of the Tharkian environment to emphasize his isolation/surveillance.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's movement through the city; time of day.

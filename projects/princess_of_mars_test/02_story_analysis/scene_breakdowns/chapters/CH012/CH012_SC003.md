# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To show Carter's integration into Thark society through spoils and to establish his new base of operations.

**Scene Summary:** Tars Tarkas assists Carter in setting up his new quarters. As a victor, Carter receives the spoils of the two defeated chieftains (weapons, silks, furs). He moves into a third-floor apartment, strategically choosing a location close to Dejah Thoris's quarters. He organizes the retinues of the fallen chieftains and reflects on the cold, communal nature of Thark culture while settling into his new environment.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Retinues (women/youths).

**Participating Environments:** The Plaza/Thark City; Carter’s New Quarters (Third-floor apartment overlooking a central court).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From the tension of the meeting to a pragmatic, observant settling-in.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Establishing shots of the massive Thark architecture and the central court.
* Montage/Sequence of Carter moving items and organizing the retinues.
* Medium shots of Carter and Tars Tarkas interacting during the logistics.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The arrangement of the "spoils" (silks, weapons) in the room; the number and placement of the retinue members.

# Scene SC003: Room Selection

## Scene Purpose
Carter selects his room on the third floor near Dejah Thoris's apartment. This establishes his intention to maintain communication and creates narrative connection between characters.

## Scene Summary
In the back rooms receiving loads of weapons, silks, furs, and cooking utensils, Carter makes his selection. He chooses a room on the third floor specifically because it is near Dejah Thoris's apartment, establishing his desire to maintain communication with her despite the trial situation.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose**
To establish the political tension and the direct threat posed to John Carter's freedom, while revealing the presence of internal spies.

**Scene Summary**
John Carter is summoned to an audience with Lorquas Ptomel. Ptomel confronts Carter about his growing influence and accuses him of plotting an escape with Dejah Thoris. He issues a stern ultimatum: any attempt at escape or self-defense against a Thark will result in death. During this confrontation, Carter realizes that Sarkoja has been monitoring his every move to undermine him.

**Participating Characters**
* John Carter
* Lorquas Ptomel
* (Mentioned) Sarkoja
* (Mentioned) Dejah Thoris

**Participating Environments**
* The Audience Chamber (Tharkian architecture, martial and austere)

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
From wary respect to acute political peril and paranoia.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
* Low-angle shots of Ptomel to emphasize dominance/threat.
* Tight close-ups on Carter to capture his realization regarding Sarkoja.
* Wide shots of the audience chamber to establish scale and isolation.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
* Carter's physical posture (from prisoner to chieftain status).
* Lighting shifts if moving from shadow to light during the confrontation.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the political stakes, Carter's precarious status as a prisoner-warrior, and the immediate threat to Dejah Thoris.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter is summoned to an audience with Lorquas Ptomel. Ptomel issues a stern ultimatum: Carter has earned respect through combat but remains an alien under watch. He is accused of plotting escape with Dejah Thoris and is warned that any attempt to flee with her will result in his death. Ptomel emphasizes the mandate to deliver Dejah Thoris safely to the brutal leader Tal Hajus.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Lorquas Ptomel.

**Participating Environments:** The Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From wary respect to intense political pressure and threat.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Medium shots of the two leaders facing off.
* Close-ups on Ptomel to emphasize his stern authority.
* Reaction shots of Carter processing the threat.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's physical posture/expression during the verbal threats; placement of characters within the chamber.

# Scene SC001: The Accusation

## Scene Purpose
Establish Carter's immediate predicament - he has been summoned by Lorquas Ptomel and accused of plotting an escape with Dejah Thoris. This creates dramatic tension around his trial on return to Thark.

## Scene Summary
Carter stands before Lorquas Ptomel in the audience chamber interior. He is formally accused of conspiring with Dejah Thoris ("red girl") to escape. The accusation carries weight as he faces a trial upon his return to Thark. The scene establishes the stakes and Carter's vulnerable position.

## Participating Characters
- **Carter** - Protagonist, accused, facing trial
- **Lorquas Ptomel** - Authority figure making the accusation

## Participating Environments
- Audience Chamber Interior (formal setting for accusations)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From anticipation to confrontation and vulnerability. Carter moves from being summoned to being formally accused, creating dramatic tension around his trial.

- Wide shots showing the formality of the setting

# Scene SC001: The Prisoners' Exchange

## Scene Purpose
Establish the immediate situation of captivity, introduce the ally Kantos Kan, and raise the narrative stakes by revealing the fate of Dejah Thoris and Sola.

## Scene Summary
John Carter regains composure after a failed escape attempt and two days of hunger, realizing Dejah Thoris and Sola were taken to be devoured. Kantos Kan is brought in as a fellow prisoner. They exchange greetings ("kaor") and backstories; Kantos reveals the expedition's defeat and the futile search for Dejah Thoris by Helium fleets.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter
- Kantos Kan

## Participating Environments
- Cages at each end of the amphitheater
- Dim torch light dungeon area

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Despair -> Composure/Resilience

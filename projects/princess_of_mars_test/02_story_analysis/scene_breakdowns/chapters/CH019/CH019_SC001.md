# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the dire situation and the loss of agency.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter is left in darkness within his cell, only to realize that the jailer's keys have been stolen by creatures from a nearby lair, leaving him without immediate means of escape.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter

**Participating Environments:**
- Subterranean prison cell (dark/ruined)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Realization of helplessness.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme close-ups (eyes, hands in dark)
- Low-light handheld/POV shots

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels in the cell; Carter's physical state/injuries from previous capture.

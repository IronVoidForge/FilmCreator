# SC001 - Key Discovery & Reveal

## Scene Purpose
Establish Carter's desperate situation and reveal the stakes of Dejah Thoris and Sola being taken by Tharks.

## Scene Summary
John Carter attempts to retrieve keys from his dead jailer but discovers they are gone, realizing Dejah Thoris and Sola have been taken by the Tharks to be devoured.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (protagonist)
- Dead Jailer (mentioned/absent)
- Dejah Thoris (mentioned)
- Sola (mentioned)

## Participating Environments
- Prison cell interior
- Thark territory (implied)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Hope → Desperation → Shock at revelation of capture

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Close-up on Carter's face showing realization
- Medium shots of empty key location
- Flashbacks or visual cues to Dejah and Sola being taken

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Key placement consistency
- Dead jailer state (undisturbed)
- Thark territory visual language

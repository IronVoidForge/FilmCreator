# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish tension and the loss of agency through the theft of the keys.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter discovers that his jailer's keys have been stolen by mysterious creatures lurking in the darkness of the prison, heightening the sense of peril and confinement.

**Participating Characters:**
* John Carter

**Participating Environments:**
* Subterranean prison cells/dark corridors

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Security $\rightarrow$ Vulnerability/Panic

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Close-ups on Carter's face (reaction)
* POV shots of the darkness/shadowy movement
* Macro shots of the missing keys/empty belt

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels in the cell; position of the stolen keys.

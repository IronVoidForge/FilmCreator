# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish immediate tension and loss of agency.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter is in his cell when he realizes the jailer's keys have been stolen by mysterious creatures lurking in the darkness, leaving him trapped.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter

**Participating Environments:**
- Subterranean prison cells (Warhoon city)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Realization of entrapment/Danger.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-ups on Carter's face and hands.
- Low-light, high-contrast shots of the shadows/creatures.
- POV shots of the empty key ring/missing keys.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels in the cell; position of the stolen keys.

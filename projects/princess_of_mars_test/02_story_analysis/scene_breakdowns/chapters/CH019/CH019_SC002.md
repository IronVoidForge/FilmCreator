# Scene SC002: The Arena Games Begin

## Scene Purpose
Establish the arena setting, the scale of the audience, and demonstrate John Carter's physical dominance which provokes a reaction from the crowd.

## Scene Summary
The amphitheater fills with 20,000 Warhoons seated on a raised platform. Initial contests involve green females versus calots (wild dogs), followed by beast battles. John Carter fights men and beasts, winning easily due to strength and agility; the crowd demands he join the hordes.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter
- Green Warriors
- Calots (Wild Dogs)
- Crowd (Warhoons)

## Participating Environments
- Underground amphitheater excavated below ground surface
- Raised platform for audience
- Stone piled around arena to prevent escape

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Tension -> Excitement/Admiration

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of the amphitheater and audience
- Action sequences (combat)
- Crowd reaction shots (cheering/demanding)

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Audience size (20,000 Warhoons)
- Arena layout (cages at ends)
- Lighting changes during combat

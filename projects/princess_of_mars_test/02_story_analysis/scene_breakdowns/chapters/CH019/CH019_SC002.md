# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Provide exposition and establish a secondary protagonist/ally.

**Scene Summary:** Carter meets Kantos Kan, a padwar from Helium. Kan provides critical intelligence regarding the search for Dejah Thoris and explains his own capture during scouting missions.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter
- Kantos Kan

**Participating Environments:**
- Subterranean prison cells

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Isolation $\rightarrow$ Hope/Information gathering.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Two-shot medium shots for dialogue.
- Over-the-shoulder shots to establish intimacy/secrecy.
- Close-ups on Kan during exposition.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Character positioning in the cramped cell; dirt/blood levels on characters.

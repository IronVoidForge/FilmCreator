# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Climax and resolution of the immediate conflict (the games).

**Scene Summary:** The final duel between Carter, Kantos Kan, and a massive northern warrior. Kan kills the giant using specialized technique. To avoid fighting each other for freedom, Carter and Kan stage Carter's death; Kan "kills" him, wins his freedom, and leaves Carter for dead.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter
- Kantos Kan
- Massive Northern Warrior
- Dak Kova/Warhoon Horde

**Participating Environments:**
- The amphitheater floor

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** High Tension $\rightarrow$ Deception $\rightarrow$ Relief (for the characters).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tight, fast-paced combat coverage.
- Slow-motion for the "fatal" blow.
- Close-ups on the eyes of Carter and Kan to signal the plan.
- Wide shots of the crowd's reaction to the "death."

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific angle/placement of the blade during the fake kill; blood splatter (real vs. fake).

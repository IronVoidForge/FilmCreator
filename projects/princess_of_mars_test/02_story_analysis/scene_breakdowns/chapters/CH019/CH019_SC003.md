# Scene SC003: The Final Duel & Escape

## Scene Purpose
Depict the climax of the arena games, the resolution of the combat between Kantos and John, and John's successful escape to the hills.

## Scene Summary
The final three combatants are a giant green warrior, Kantos Kan, and John Carter. The green warrior is killed when Kantos throws his sword at the giant's heart using Martian swordsmanship. Kantos then fights John Carter. To avoid death, they stage a duel until dark. John pretends to be dead by having Kantos thrust his sword into his chest; he falls to the ground with the weapon protruding. Kantos leaves to claim his freedom, instructing John to meet him in the hills east of the city. John Carter creeps to the top of the amphitheater and escapes into the untenanted portion of the dead city, reaching the hills beyond.

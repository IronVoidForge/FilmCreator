# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale and brutality of the setting.

**Scene Summary:** The prisoners are moved to a massive, excavated amphitheater for the start of a ten-day gladiatorial tournament involving humans, warriors, and beasts.

**Participating Characters:**
- John Carter
- Kantos Kan
- Warhoon Horde (crowd)
- Dak Kova (observing)

**Participating Environments:**
- Subterranean amphitheater (immense/ruined)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Dread $\rightarrow$ Overwhelming scale.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme wide shots (establishing the arena size)
- High-angle crowd shots
- Tracking shots of prisoners being led out

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Crowd density and movement; lighting transition from cell to arena.

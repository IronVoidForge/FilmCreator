# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale of the setting and the protagonist's status as a captive within an alien civilization.

**Scene Summary:** The Narrator and Tars Tarkas arrive at a massive, ancient city built by a forgotten race. They enter a central plaza filled with hundreds of Martians, highlighting the narrator's smallness compared to the environment and its inhabitants.

**Participating Characters:** The Narrator, Tars Tarkas, Various Martians (crowd).

**Participating Environments:** Central Plaza of the Ancient City (Marble/Gold architecture, massive scale).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe/Trepidation $\rightarrow$ Alienation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Extreme Wide Shots (EWS) to establish the scale of the city vs. characters.
* Low Angle shots looking up at Martians.
* POV shots from the Narrator's perspective showing the overwhelming crowd.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Scale of props/architecture relative to the Narrator; movement patterns of the Martian crowd.

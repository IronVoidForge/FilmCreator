# SC001 - Arrival at City

## Scene Purpose
Establish the new setting and introduce Carter's captivity situation through visual observation of Martian civilization.

## Scene Summary
Carter and his escort Tars Tarkas travel approximately ten miles to reach the foot of mountains. They traverse a narrow gorge and enter an open valley containing low table land with enormous ruined city. Buildings show signs of extreme antiquity, possibly belonging to long-extinct race. Martians camped in plaza and buildings are observed as naked except for ornaments, with large tusks and varying heights (adult females 10-12 feet).

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter:** Human protagonist, currently prisoner attempting to gain favor through physical demonstration
- **Tars Tarkas:** Vice-chieftain of community, escort, statesman, warrior explaining situation regarding Carter's capture
- **Martians (Green Men):** Approximately nine hundred creatures camped in city

## Participating Environments
- Mountain foot/valley entrance
- Narrow gorge
- Open valley with low table land
- Ruined city plaza and buildings
- Deserted ancient architecture

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Initial tension → Curiosity → Awe at ancient civilization

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide establishing shots of mountain range and valley
- Medium shots of Carter and Tarkas walking through gorge
- Over-the-shoulder shots observing Martians from distance
- Detail shots of ruined architecture showing antiquity
- Low angle shots emphasizing Martian height and presence

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Scale relationships between human characters and giant Martians
- Consistent lighting across valley/city transition
- Building decay level consistency (not greatly decayed)
- Martian ornamentation patterns

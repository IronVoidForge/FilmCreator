# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale of the setting and the alien, brutal nature of Martian culture.

**Scene Summary:** The narrator and Tars Tarkas arrive at a grand, ancient city built by an extinct race. As they move through the streets, the narrator observes the biological differences of the Martians and their callous attitude toward death and violence.

**Participating Characters:**
* The Narrator
* Tars Tarkas

**Participating Environments:**
* Ancient Martian City (White marble, gold inlay, grand scale)
* Central Plaza/Streets

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe $\rightarrow$ Disquiet/Dread

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Wide establishing shots of the massive architecture.
* Medium tracking shots of the duo walking through crowds.
* Close-ups on Martian biological details (tusks, skin texture).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
* Scale comparison between the narrator and the oversized architecture/Martians.
* Lighting consistency in the open plaza.

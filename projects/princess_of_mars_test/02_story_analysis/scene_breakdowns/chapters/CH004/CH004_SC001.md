# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale, atmosphere, and alien nature of the Green Martian civilization.

**Scene Summary:** The narrator and Tars Tarkas arrive at a massive, ancient city situated on a table land near a dried sea. They observe the sheer number and intimidating physical presence of the Green Martian population.

**Participating Characters:** Narrator, Tars Tarkas, Green Martian citizens (background).

**Participating Environments:** The Table Land/Dried Sea periphery; the outskirts of the massive ancient city.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe and intimidation transitioning into observational tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme Wide Shots (EWS) to establish the scale of the city and the dried sea.
- Medium Shots (MS) of the Narrator and Tars Tarkas reacting to the sight.
- Close-ups (CU) of Martian features (tusks, large size) to emphasize their alien nature.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The level of light/sunlight over the dried sea; the physical condition/exhaustion of the protagonists upon arrival.

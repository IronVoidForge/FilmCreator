# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale of the Martian environment and the grandeur of the ancient, abandoned city.

**Scene Summary:** The Narrator and Tars Tarkas travel across a rising landscape toward the edge of a dead sea, eventually entering an enormous, white marble and gold city that dwarfs its current inhabitants.

**Participating Characters:**
* The Narrator
* Tars Tarkas

**Participating Environments:**
* Martian landscape (rising terrain/dead sea edge)
* The Grand City (exterior/streets)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe/Wonder $\rightarrow$ Sense of insignificance.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Wide establishing shots of the city architecture and scale.
* Tracking shots following the duo through the streets.
* Close-ups on the Narrator's face reacting to the environment.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical height difference between the human and the Martian escort; the scale of the architecture relative to the characters.

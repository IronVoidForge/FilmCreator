# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Transition from public political tension to private discovery and introduce a new element of biological mystery.

**Scene Summary:** Seeking respite, the narrator is led by Sola to a decorated sleeping chamber. Upon entering, he encounters a strange, ten-legged creature inhabiting the space.

**Participating Characters:** Narrator, Sola, Ten-legged creature.

**Participating Environments:** A decorated Martian sleeping chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Fatigue/Relief transitioning to curiosity and uncanny unease.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium Shots (MS) of the interaction between Sola and the Narrator.
- Close-ups (CU) on the intricate decorations of the room.
- Macro or tight shots of the ten-legged creature to emphasize its strangeness.
- Low-key lighting to differentiate this private space from the bright plaza.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The level of light/shadow in the chamber; the presence of any items brought from the previous scene (e.g., weapons or gear).

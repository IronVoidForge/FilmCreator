# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the Narrator's agency and his ability to command respect through violence/physicality.

**Scene Summary:** After being mocked, the Narrator punches a Martian warrior. This act of defiance earns him unexpected respect. He is then commanded to perform "sak" (jumping), which he does with impressive distance, further astounding the assembly.

**Participating Characters:** The Narrator, Tars Tarkas, The Chieftain, Martian Warrior, Martian Crowd.

**Participating Environments:** Grand Audience Chamber / Plaza area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Humiliation $\rightarrow$ Defiance $\rightarrow$ Triumph/Respect.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Tight Close-ups (CU) on the punch and the impact.
* Wide shots to capture the scale of the "sak" jump.
* Reaction shots from the crowd (shifting from mockery to applause).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physics/trajectory of the jump; the physical contact during the fight sequence.

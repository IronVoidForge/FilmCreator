# SC003 - Physical Struggles & Sak Demonstration

## Scene Purpose
Showcase Carter's human physiology as both liability and asset through physical challenges and successful adaptation to Martian expectations.

## Scene Summary
Carter attempts to walk but fails, skipping like grasshopper and bruising himself. Forced to stand, he fights a Martian guard after being commanded, winning applause for his ability to fight back. Martians demand Carter perform the jump ("sak"). He succeeds in clearing 150 feet and landing squarely.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter:** Human protagonist demonstrating physical capabilities
- **Martian Guard:** Opponent in physical confrontation
- **Martians (Green Men):** Audience applauding Carter's demonstrations

## Participating Environments
- Ground level where Carter attempts walking
- Open space for jump demonstration
- Assembly area where applause occurs

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Physical struggle → Humiliation → Triumph → Approval from Martians

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Low angle shots of Carter attempting to walk (emphasizing difficulty)
- Medium action shots of fight with Martian guard
- Slow-motion or dynamic tracking shot for jump sequence
- Wide shots showing 150-foot distance cleared
- Reaction shots of Martians applauding and approving

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Jump height consistency relative to character sizes
- Fight choreography maintaining power dynamics
- Audience reaction timing and intensity
- Landing position accuracy for jump sequence

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Demonstrate the Narrator's physical prowess and ability to command respect through action.

**Scene Summary:** To prove his worth, the Narrator performs a massive "sakk" (leap) of 150 feet, successfully impressing the gathered Martian warriors.

**Participating Characters:**
* The Narrator
* Tars Tarkas
* The Martian Warriors (audience)

**Participating Environments:**
* The Grand Audience Chamber / Plaza area

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Triumph/Awe.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Wide shots to capture the full arc of the 150-foot leap.
* Slow-motion or dynamic tracking of the jump.
* Reaction shots from the warriors.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The height and distance of the jump; the Narrator's physical state (exhaustion vs. energy).

# SC004 - Sustenance & Quarters

## Scene Purpose
Provide emotional payoff after physical demonstrations, transitioning Carter from public ceremony to private accommodation.

## Scene Summary
Hungry and thirsty, Carter ignores further commands to jump and instead signals for food/water. Tars Tarkas arranges for him to be taken by young female Sola (about eight feet tall, light olive-green, belonging to Tars Tarkas' retinue). Sola leads Carter to spacious chamber decorated with murals and mosaics. She signals for ten-legged creature to appear, which waddles in like obedient puppy.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter:** Human protagonist receiving accommodation
- **Sola:** Young female Martian about eight feet tall, light olive-green, belonging to Tars Tarkas' retinue
- **Ten-legged Creature:** Waddles in as obedient puppy

## Participating Environments
- Transit path from assembly area to quarters
- Spacious chamber with murals and mosaics
- Interior space where creature appears

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Physical exhaustion → Relief (food/water) → Comfort (quarters) → Curiosity (creature)

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shots of Carter signaling for food/water
- Over-the-shoulder shots following Sola leading Carter
- Wide interior shots showing chamber decoration and murals
- Close-ups on ten-legged creature's movement
- Detail shots of mosaic work and mural artistry

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities

- Mural/mosaic detail accuracy throughout sequence

# SC004

**Scene Summary:** The Narrator refuses further feats until fed. Tars Tarkas assigns Sola to care for him. Sola leads him to a decorated sleeping chamber, where he encounters a strange, ten-legged Martian pet.

**Participating Characters:**
* The Narrator
* Tars Tarkas
* Sola
* Small Martian creature (pet)

**Participating Environments:**
* The Audience Chamber (briefly)
* Decorated Sleeping Chamber

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion/Hunger $\rightarrow$ Relief/Curiosity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Medium shots of the non-verbal communication between Narrator and Tars Tarkas.
* Close-ups on Sola's face and the small Martian creature.
* Establishing shot of the sleeping chamber.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The level of hunger/fatigue expressed by the Narrator; the appearance of the ten-legged pet.

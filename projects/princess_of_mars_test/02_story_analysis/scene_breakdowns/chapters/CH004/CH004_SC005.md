# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Provide a moment of respite and introduce local fauna, grounding the "alien" nature of the world.

**Scene Summary:** Sola leads the Narrator to a decorated sleeping chamber. Inside, the Narrator encounters a strange, ten-legged Martian creature with a frog-like head and tusks.

**Participating Characters:** The Narrator, Sola, Small Martian Creature.

**Participating Environments:** Decorated Sleeping Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Fatigue $\rightarrow$ Curiosity/Uncanniness.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Medium shots of the chamber's decor.
* Close-ups on the strange creature to emphasize its alien features.
* Low-key lighting to suggest a nighttime/rest setting.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The appearance and movement of the small creature; the level of light in the room.

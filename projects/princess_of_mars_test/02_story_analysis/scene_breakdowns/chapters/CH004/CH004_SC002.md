# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the political hierarchy and the physical absurdity of the Martian culture.

**Scene Summary:** The Narrator is presented to the Chieftain in a grand audience chamber. He observes the mismatch between the massive, ancient furniture and the Martians' biology, emphasizing the antiquity of the ruins.

**Participating Characters:** The Narrator, Tars Tarkas, The Chieftain, Martian Court.

**Participating Environments:** Grand Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Intimidation $\rightarrow$ Observational Curiosity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Medium shots of the Chieftain in regalia.
* Close-ups on the Narrator's face reacting to the environment.
* Detail shots of the oversized, ancient furniture.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific placement of the Narrator within the massive chamber; the visual details of the Chieftain's regalia.

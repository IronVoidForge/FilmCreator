# SC002 - Audience Chamber & Martian Culture

## Scene Purpose
Deliver exposition on Martian civilization while advancing Carter's integration into their society through formal ceremony.

## Scene Summary
Text details Martian life expectancy (average 300 years after maturity, up to 1000 without violence), violent death causes (duels, war, hunting), and voluntary pilgrimage down river Iss at age 1000. Carter is lowered to ground and brought before Principal Chieftain seated on rostrum during assembly. Greeting ceremony occurs where names exchanged between Tars Tarkas and chieftain.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter:** Human protagonist, prisoner being integrated into Martian society
- **Tars Tarkas:** Vice-chieftain acting as intermediary and statesman
- **Principal Chieftain:** Ruler of community, seated on rostrum during assembly
- **Martians (Green Men):** Assembly participants observing ceremony

## Participating Environments
- Audience chamber/assembly hall
- Rostrum where chieftain is seated
- Ground level where Carter is lowered

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Formal ceremony → Cultural exposition → Acceptance into society

## Likely Visual Coverage Families

- Medium shots of name exchange ceremony between Tars Tarkas and chieftain
- Close-ups on Carter's reactions during lowering to ground
- Wide shots showing scale of assembly hall
- Detail shots of ancient architecture in chamber

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Demonstrate the protagonist's physical prowess and establish his standing within the Martian social hierarchy.

**Scene Summary:** Inside a magnificent marble hall, the narrator meets the local Chieftain. To earn respect among the warriors, he performs "sak" (jumping) and physically asserts himself by punching a Martian warrior who treats him roughly.

**Participating Characters:** Narrator, Tars Tarkas, The Chieftain, Green Martian Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The Central Plaza; The Grand Marble Hall (ancient/advanced architecture).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension and hostility transitioning to hard-won respect/triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low Angle Shots to emphasize the imposing nature of the Chieftain and the hall's architecture.
- Action coverage for the "sak" demonstration (tracking shots or dynamic movement).
- Close-ups on the impact of the punch and the reactions of the surrounding warriors.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical state of the narrator's hands/knuckles after the punch; the positioning of the Chieftain relative to the throne/seat.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the Chieftain and establish the Narrator's social standing (and physical struggle) within Martian culture.

**Scene Summary:** The party enters a massive audience chamber to meet the Chieftain. Amidst the oversized furniture and mocking Martians, the Narrator accidentally strikes a Martian while struggling with his movement, resulting in unexpected laughter and respect rather than punishment.

**Participating Characters:**
* The Narrator
* Tars Tarkas
* The Chieftain
* Various Green Martian warriors/spectators

**Participating Environments:**
* The Grand Audience Chamber (oversized furniture, marble/gold)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension/Humiliation $\rightarrow$ Surprise/Respect.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Low-angle shots to emphasize the massive scale of the chamber and Chieftain.
* Medium shots of the physical struggle/clumsiness.
* Reaction shots of the crowd (laughter/applause).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific movement/stumble that leads to the strike; the placement of the Narrator relative to the oversized furniture.

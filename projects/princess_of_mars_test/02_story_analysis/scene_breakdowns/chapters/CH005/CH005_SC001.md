# SC001

**scene purpose**: Establish the protagonist's isolation, the atmospheric setting of Mars, and the immediate threat of the Watchdog.

**scene summary**: Alone in his chamber after Sola's departure, the protagonist observes detailed Martian murals and learns about the planet's biology and climate. He remains under the watchful eye of a ferocious Martian watchdog guarding the door.

**participating characters**: The Protagonist, The Watchdog.

**participating environments**: The Prisoner's Chamber.

**dominant emotional shift**: From lonely contemplation to wary observation.

**likely visual coverage families**: 
- Macro/Extreme Close-ups: Details of the murals and Martian flora.
- Medium Shots: Protagonist interacting with his surroundings.
- Low-angle/Static Shots: The Watchdog positioned at the threshold to emphasize threat.

**likely continuity sensitivities**: Lighting levels (transition from dusk to moonlight); visibility of breath if temperature drops significantly.

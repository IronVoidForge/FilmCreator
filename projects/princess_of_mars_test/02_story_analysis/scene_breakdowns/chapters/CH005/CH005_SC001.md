# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's status as a captive, introduce the Martian environment through sensory details, and establish Sola's role.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist observes his surroundings, noting the murals of landscapes devoid of life. He experiences the care provided by Sola, learning about Martian biology (plant-milk) and the environmental extremes (nocturnal cold and moon cycles).

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Sola, The Watch Dog (presence/observation).

**Participating Environments:** Captive's chamber (decorated with murals).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Isolation/Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Comfort/Observation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-ups of the protagonist's eyes and facial expressions.
- Macro shots of the murals and Martian food.
- Medium shots of Sola providing care.
- Wide shots establishing the chamber's scale.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting changes based on moon cycles; amount/type of food provided by Sola; position of the Watch Dog near the door.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's confinement, the Martian environment through murals, and the immediate threat of the Watch Dog.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist is held in a decorated chamber. He observes murals that explain Martian biology (milk-producing plants) and the harsh climate (dual moons/thin atmosphere). A ferocious Watch Dog stands guard at the exit.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Watch Dog (non-verbal threat)

**Participating Environments:**
- Captive's Chamber (decorated with murals)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity/Observation $\rightarrow$ Apprehension/Confinement.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Macro shots of murals (world-building)
- Close-ups of Protagonist's eyes/reactions
- Low-angle shots of the Watch Dog to emphasize threat

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Mural details must remain consistent; lighting changes as Martian moons move.

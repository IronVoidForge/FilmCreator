# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's confinement, the presence of the Watch Dog, and the world-building through environmental observation.

**Scene Summary:** Sola leaves the protagonist in his chamber, leaving him under the watchful eye of a ferocious Martian beast. The protagonist spends time studying the murals on the walls, which provide insight into Martian biology and celestial cycles.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Sola (briefly), The Watch Dog.

**Participating Environments:** Captive's chamber (decorated with Martian landscape murals).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Isolation $\rightarrow$ Curiosity/Observation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-ups of the protagonist's eyes and expressions.
- Macro shots of the murals.
- Wide shots showing the scale of the room and the beast's position.
- Low-angle shots of the Watch Dog to emphasize threat.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Mural details, lighting changes as time passes (implied by narration), position of the Watch Dog relative to the door.

# SC002

**scene purpose**: To escalate tension through a high-stakes physical confrontation and chase sequence.

**scene summary**: The protagonist attempts to test the limits of his captivity by leaving the room. This triggers a pursuit through the deserted, moonlit Martian streets, culminating in a desperate, high-speed escape attempt as the Watchdog charges to block his exit.

**participating characters**: The Protagonist, The Watchdog.

**participating environments**: Martian City Streets.

**dominant emotional_shift**: From cautious experimentation to frantic terror.

**likely visual coverage families**: 
- Dynamic Tracking Shots: Following the protagonist's movement through streets.
- Handheld/Shaky Cam: Capturing the chaos of the chase and the sudden charge.
- Wide Shots: Establishing the scale of the deserted city and the distance between characters.

**likely continuity sensitivities**: Direction of travel (ensuring the path leads toward the building); lighting consistency from oil lamps and moons during movement.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To initiate the action and demonstrate the physical threat of the Martian guard beast.

**Scene Summary:** Seeking to test his limits and the capabilities of his captors, the protagonist attempts to leave his room. He quickly realizes that the short-legged Watch Dog is incredibly fast and intelligent, turning a test into a survival situation.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, The Watch Dog.

**Participating Environments:** Captive's chamber, immediate hallway/threshold.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Alarm/Panic.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-angle shots of the Watch Dog to emphasize speed.
- Handheld/shaky cam for the protagonist's movement.
- Tracking shots following the dog's rapid approach.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical distance between the protagonist and the dog; the direction of the chase.

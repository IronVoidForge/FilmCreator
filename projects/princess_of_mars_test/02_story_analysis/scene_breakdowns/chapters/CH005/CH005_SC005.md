# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Sudden reversal and cliffhanger reveal.

**Scene Summary:** Having successfully entered the building through the window, the protagonist's relief is short-lived. He is immediately seized by a massive, white, hairless, ape-like creature and thrown to the floor.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Colossal Creature

**Participating Environments:**
- Interior of an Overlooking Building

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief $\rightarrow$ Terror/Shock.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Sudden, violent movement (shaky cam)
- Close-up on the creature's grip/hands
- Wide shot showing the scale difference between man and beast
- Rapid descent to floor level

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The protagonist's physical state (breathless from jump) upon entry.

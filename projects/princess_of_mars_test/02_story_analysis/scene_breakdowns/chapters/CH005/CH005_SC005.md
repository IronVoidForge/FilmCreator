# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** A sudden reversal and cliffhanger reveal.

**Scene Summary:** Just as the protagonist manages to pull himself onto the windowsill, he is seized from behind by a massive, ape-like creature and dragged into a new room.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, The Colossal Creature.

**Participating Environments:** Windowsill/Building interior.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief $\rightarrow$ Sudden Terror/Shock.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-up of the protagonist's face showing relief.
- Sudden, jarring movement (shaky cam) as he is grabbed.
- Wide shot of the creature dragging him away.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The moment of contact, the physical grip of the Colossal Creature, the transition from the window to the interior room.

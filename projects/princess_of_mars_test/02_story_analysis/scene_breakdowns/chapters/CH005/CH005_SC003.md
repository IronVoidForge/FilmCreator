# SC003

**scene purpose**: To introduce a new, much larger threat and subvert the protagonist's moment of perceived safety.

**scene summary**: Believing he has successfully evaded the Watchdog by leaping into a high building window, the protagonist is suddenly ambushed and violently dragged away by a massive, white, ape-like creature lurking in the shadows.

**participating characters**: The Protagonist, The Ape-like Creature.

**participating environments**: Building Ledge/Window.

**dominant emotional shift**: From relief/triumph to sudden horror and helplessness.

**likely visual coverage families**: 
- POV Shots: The protagonist's view of the ledge/window.
- Sudden Impact/Close-up: The moment of the grab and the creature's features.
- Obscured/Shadowy Framing: Using darkness to hide the creature until the reveal.

**likely continuity sensitivities**: Physical position of the protagonist relative to the window sill; the suddenness of the movement (timing of the drag).

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Initiate the escalation and test the protagonist's resolve to escape.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist decides to test the limits of his guard by attempting to leave the room, observing how the Watch Dog reacts to his movement.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, The Watch Dog.

**Participating Environments:** Captive's chamber, transition to deserted city street.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Restlessness $\rightarrow$ Calculated Risk.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Tracking shots following the protagonist.
- Point-of-view (POV) shots of the Watch Dog watching him.
- Handheld/unsteady shots to increase tension.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Distance between the protagonist and the beast, lighting transition from indoors to outdoors.

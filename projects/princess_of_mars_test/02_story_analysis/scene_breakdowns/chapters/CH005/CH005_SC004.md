# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Action climax and demonstration of protagonist's unique skills.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist reaches the edge of the city. The Watch Dog shifts from following to attacking. A high-stakes chase ensues where the protagonist uses his "jumper" abilities to evade the beast, culminating in a massive 30-foot vertical leap into a building window.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- The Watch Dog

**Participating Environments:**
- Deserted Martian City Street
- Building Exterior (Wall/Window)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Adrenaline/Panic $\rightarrow$ Exhilaration/Escape.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the chase through the city
- Tracking shots following the protagonist's movement
- Slow-motion or high-frame-rate for the 30-foot jump
- Low-angle "hero" shots during the leap

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Speed and distance of the Watch Dog; height of the window relative to the ground.

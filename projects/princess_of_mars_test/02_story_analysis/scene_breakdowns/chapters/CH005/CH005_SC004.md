# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** The action climax of the chapter; a high-stakes chase sequence.

**Scene Summary:** The Watch Dog breaks its distance and charges with extreme speed. The protagonist uses his superior jumping ability to evade the beast, leaping thirty feet up into a window.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, The Watch Dog.

**Participating Environments:** Deserted city street, building exterior.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Calm $\rightarrow$ Terror $\rightarrow$ Adrenaline/Exhilaration.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Fast-paced tracking shots (dolly or gimbal).
- Wide shots to show the scale of the jump.
- Extreme close-ups of the beast's charge and the protagonist's exertion.
- Slow-motion for the leap.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Speed of the beast, height of the window, direction of movement.

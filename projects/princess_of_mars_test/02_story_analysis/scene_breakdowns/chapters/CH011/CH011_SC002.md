# SC002

## Scene Purpose
Explore the central mystery of John Carter's origins through intimate dialogue and character logic.

## Scene Summary
Dejah Thoris questions John Carter's origins due to his lack of ornaments and clothing. She initially doubts he is from Earth but eventually believes him after hearing his story about Virginia and Earth, driven by her "earthly, feminine logic."

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (protagonist)
- Dejah Thoris (love interest)

## Participating Environments
- Private quarters or intimate conversation space in Korad

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From skepticism and doubt to understanding and belief through logical reasoning.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Close-up single shots during questioning
- Over-the-shoulder shots during story telling
- Reaction shots showing emotional transitions

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Character expressions during doubt-to-belief transition
- Consistency of intimate conversation setting
- Timing and pacing of revelation moments

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To bridge the gap between Carter's mystery and Dejah Thoris's understanding through a major character reveal.

**Scene Summary:** In the privacy of their new quarters, Dejah Thoris presses Carter about his origins. Carter makes the startling claim that he is from Earth (which he identifies as Mars). Though the concept is alien to her, Dejah Thoris relies on her intuition and Carter's sincerity to accept his truth.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** The luxurious ancient quarters.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Disbelief $\rightarrow$ Deepened Trust.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tight close-ups on faces to capture the intimacy and gravity of the confession.
- Two-shots to establish the emotional connection/bond forming between them.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Eye contact patterns; the physical distance between characters as trust is established.

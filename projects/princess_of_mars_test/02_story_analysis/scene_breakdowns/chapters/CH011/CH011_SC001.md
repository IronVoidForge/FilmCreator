# Scene SC001: Arrival & Hierarchy

## Scene Purpose
Establish the new quarters location and John Carter's protective status over Dejah Thoris while introducing Sarkoja's threat and John's rank explanation to Sola.

## Scene Summary
John Carter arrives with Dejah Thoris and Sola in their new quarters on the second floor overlooking the plaza. Sarkoja attempts to assume custody of Dejah Thoris, but John waves her away and threatens her demise for harming Dejah (threat fails on Mars as men do not kill women). John then explains his rank as 11th chieftain to Sola and discusses the requirement to kill Lorquas Ptomel for first place.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter**: Protective, assertive, authoritative
- **Dejah Thoris**: Vulnerable but dignified
- **Sola**: Guide/protector role
- **Sarkoja**: Antagonist attempting custody

## Participating Environments
- New Quarters (second floor overlooking plaza)
- Building near audience chamber
- Ancient beds of wrought metal swinging from gold chains
- Marble ceilings with elaborate wall decoration portraying fair-haired Martians in robes

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Protective → Assertive → Informative

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide establishing shots of quarters interior and architecture
- Medium two-shots during conversation exchanges
- Close-ups on facial expressions during threat exchange
- Over-the-shoulder shots for dialogue flow

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Room architecture continuity across shots
- Character positioning relative to camera
- Weapon accouterments visibility (metal weapon carried by John)
- Bedding and food utensils placement if shown

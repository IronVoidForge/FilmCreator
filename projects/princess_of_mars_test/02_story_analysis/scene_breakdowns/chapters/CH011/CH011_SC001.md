# SC001

## Scene Purpose
Establish John Carter's integration into Thark society through practical arrangements and social recognition.

## Scene Summary
John Carter arranges new quarters for Dejah Thoris and Sola in Korad, a former center of commerce and culture. He establishes his rank among the Tharks with Sola, who acknowledges his prowess as eleventh in the community.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (protagonist)
- Sola (Thark warrior)
- Dejah Thoris (mentioned in quarters arrangement)

## Participating Environments
- Korad (ancient city of commerce and culture)
- Living quarters area

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From practical matter-of-fact to social recognition and acceptance within Thark hierarchy.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide establishing shots of Korad
- Medium two-shots during conversation with Sola
- Close-ups on facial expressions during rank acknowledgment

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities

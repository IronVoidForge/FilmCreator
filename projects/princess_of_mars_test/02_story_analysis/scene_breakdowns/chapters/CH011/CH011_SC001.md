# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the new status quo for Carter and Dejah Thoris, providing physical security and social context.

**Scene Summary:** Following the confrontation with Sarkoja, Sola escorts Dejah Thoris to luxurious, ancient quarters in Korad. Sola informs John Carter that his combat prowess has elevated his standing among the Tharks to the rank of eleventh chieftain, surpassed only by Lorquas Ptomel.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** Ancient building near the audience chamber (Korad); luxurious quarters featuring ancient frescoes.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension/Conflict $\rightarrow$ Security/Status Recognition.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots of the elaborate architecture and frescoes.
- Medium tracking shots of Sola escorting Dejah Thoris.
- Close-ups on Carter and Sola during the status revelation.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The transition from the rugged Thark environment to the refined, ancient architecture; the physical state of the characters after the previous conflict.

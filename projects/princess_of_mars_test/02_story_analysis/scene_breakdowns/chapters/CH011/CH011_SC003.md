# SC003

## Scene Purpose
Expand world-building through historical discussion between main characters.

## Scene Summary
John Carter and Dejah Thoris discuss Martian history, including the extinct fair-haired race and the alliance of red, dark, and yellow Martians against the green men.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (protagonist)
- Dejah Thoris (love interest)

## Participating Environments
- Conversation space in Korad (possibly same as SC002 or adjacent area)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
From personal revelation to broader intellectual curiosity about Martian history and politics.

- Cutaways to symbolic representations of different Martian races
- Establishing shots connecting conversation to larger world context

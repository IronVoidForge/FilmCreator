# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To provide world-building exposition regarding Martian technology and the history of the planet.

**Scene Summary:** Dejah Thoris shares knowledge about the Martians' ability to observe other planets through advanced telescopic technology. She explains the history of the ancient, fair-skinned race and how environmental shifts shaped the current red-skinned Martian races.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** The luxurious ancient quarters (amidst frescoes of the ancient race).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Intimacy $\rightarrow$ Intellectual Wonder/Awe.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of Dejah Thoris as she lectures/explains.
- Cutaways to the frescoes on the walls to visually represent the history being discussed.
- Reaction shots from Carter as he absorbs the scale of Martian history.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Hand gestures used during explanation; positioning relative to the wall frescoes.

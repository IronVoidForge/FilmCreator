# SC001 - Language Acquisition

## Scene Purpose
Establish protagonist's intellectual adaptation to Martian society through language mastery

## Scene Summary
Protagonist undergoes intensive instruction in the Martian language, demonstrating rapid acquisition capabilities that impress his instructors

## Participating Characters
- Protagonist (learner)
- Local women (instructors)

## Participating Environments
- Training facility/educational space
- Martian settlement interior

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Curiosity → Confidence → Respect for instructors' knowledge

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shots of protagonist listening intently
- Close-ups on facial expressions during comprehension
- Over-the-shoulder shots showing instructor demonstrations

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Language learning progression markers
- Instructor positioning consistency
- Protagonist's engagement level changes

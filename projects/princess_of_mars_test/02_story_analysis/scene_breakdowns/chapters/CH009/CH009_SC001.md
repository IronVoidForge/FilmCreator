# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's new status as a warrior and his integration into Martian material culture.

**Scene Summary:** A respectful but menacing Martian presents the protagonist with warrior's arms and ornaments. Sola and the other women work together to remodel these large pieces of equipment to fit the protagonist's smaller, Earthly frame.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Sola, Other Female Martians, unnamed Martian warrior.

**Participating Environments:** Communal living area/workshop.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From intimidation (the gift) to communal cooperation and adaptation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-ups of the ornate weaponry/ornaments.
- Medium shots of the women working on the gear.
- Reaction shots of the protagonist observing his new status.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific appearance and fit of the remodeled armor/weapons; the state of the tools used for remodeling.

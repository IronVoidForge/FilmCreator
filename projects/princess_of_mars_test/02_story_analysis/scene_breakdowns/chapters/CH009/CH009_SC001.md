# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's integration into Martian warrior culture through a ritualistic gift.

**Scene Summary:** A respectful but menacing Martian warrior presents the protagonist with specialized combat arms and ornaments, marking his transition from outsider to trainee.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Martian Warrior.

**Participating Environments:** Exterior/Plaza area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Acceptance/Duty.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups on the ornate weaponry; medium shots of the warrior's intimidating presence; reaction shots of the protagonist.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific appearance and fit of the new Martian arms and ornaments.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's rapid adaptation to Martian culture and his physical training.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist undergoes intensive training in Martian combat and weaponry under the guidance of Sola and other female warriors. He learns the gendered social structure of Green Martian society, where women serve as defenders and manufacturers while men handle law and strategy.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist
- Sola
- Martian Female Warriors/Youth

**Participating Environments:**
- The Plaza / Training Grounds

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From disorientation to disciplined determination.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of training drills and weapon handling.
- Medium close-ups of the protagonist's physical exertion.
- Close-ups of Martian weaponry and attire details.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Weapon types used, sweat/dirt levels on the protagonist, progression of martial stances.

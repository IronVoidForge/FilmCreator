**Scene Summary:** Carter sees the prisoner briefly in the audience chamber, overhearing guards speaking to her. Driven by this, he nearly drives Sola distracted with importunities to hasten his education. Within days, he masters the tongue enough for passable conversation. Carter realizes Sola is friendly and hates cruelty/barbarity. He feels fortunate to be in her hands rather than others.

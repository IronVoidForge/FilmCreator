# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To deliver critical plot information via eavesdropping and raise the stakes for the Red prisoner.

**Scene Summary:** Late at night in the communal sleeping quarters, the protagonist listens to a conversation among the women. He learns that the beautiful Red prisoner is being held to be taken to Thark for public execution in games.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist (Listening)
- The Women (Group)

**Participating Environments:**
- The Sleeping Quarters (Communal/Dimly lit)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension and growing dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-light shots of the sleeping quarters.
- Close-ups of the protagonist's face in shadow, reacting to what he hears.
- Over-the-shoulder shots of the women talking in the distance/mid-ground.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels (nighttime), positioning of characters in the communal space.

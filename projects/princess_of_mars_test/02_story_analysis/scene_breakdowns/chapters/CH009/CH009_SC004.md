# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Deliver a major plot revelation regarding the fate of the Red prisoner.

**Scene Summary:** Through eavesdropping, the protagonist learns from Sarkoja that a beautiful Red female prisoner is being transported to Thark to be executed during the great games before Tal Hajus.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist (listening), Sarkoja, Other Martians (voices).

**Participating Environments:** Communal Sleeping Quarters.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Shock and growing urgency/resolve.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme close-ups of the protagonist's reaction.
- Over-the-shoulder shots of the adults talking (if visible) or focus on the shadows/silhouettes.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific information revealed must be clear to the audience via sound/subtitles.

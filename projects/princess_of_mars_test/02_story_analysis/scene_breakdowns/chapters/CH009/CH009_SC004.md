# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Reveal a major plot point regarding the Red Martian prisoner's fate.

**Scene Summary:** During a nighttime conversation, the protagonist overhears Sarkoja revealing that the beautiful Red Martian prisoner is being taken to Thark for execution at the great games rather than being held for ransom.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist (eavesdropping), Sarkoja, Sola (nearby).

**Participating Environments:** The Sleeping Quarters (Night).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Shock/Dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Tight close-ups on the protagonist's face to capture his reaction; medium shots of the adults talking in the shadows.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels (nighttime/dimly lit).

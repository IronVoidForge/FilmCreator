# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the protagonist's motivation and his intellectual drive to understand his surroundings.

**Scene Summary:** Driven by the memory of a beautiful Red female prisoner being treated brutally, the protagonist focuses his efforts on mastering the Martian language to better understand the world around him.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist

**Participating Environments:**
- Various training/living areas (Montage style)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity and growing empathy/indignation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-ups of the protagonist studying or listening intently.
- Flashback/Memory inserts of the Red prisoner (if visually represented).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Protagonist's level of linguistic fluency/effort shown through facial expressions.

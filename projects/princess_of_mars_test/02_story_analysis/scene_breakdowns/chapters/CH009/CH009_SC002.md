# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Demonstrate the protagonist's physical training and provide world-building regarding Martian social roles.

**Scene Summary:** Sola and the other women remodel the gear for the protagonist. He begins combat training in the plaza with the Martian youth, observing that while men handle law/strategy, women are the primary educators and manufacturers.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Sola, Martian Youth (ensemble).

**Participating Environments:** The Plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Focused exertion $\rightarrow$ Observational curiosity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the plaza training; montage-style coverage of gear modification; medium tracking shots of combat drills.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Condition/fit of the modified armor during training sequences.

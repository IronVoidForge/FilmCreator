# SC002 - Weapons Training

## Scene Purpose
Showcase physical combat training and reveal gendered division of labor in Martian society

## Scene Summary
Protagonist participates in weapons training under tutelage of local women who manufacture all goods and educate youth, highlighting that men handle strategy while women manage production and combat

## Participating Characters
- Protagonist (trainee)
- Local women (combat instructors)

## Participating Environments
- Training grounds/weaponry area
- Manufacturing facility adjacent space

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Physical challenge → Realization of societal structure → Adaptation to new role

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots showing training ground layout
- Action sequences with weapon handling
- Split-screen or cross-cutting between strategy discussions and physical training

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Weapon types and positions
- Training equipment consistency
- Gender role visual cues (who instructs whom)

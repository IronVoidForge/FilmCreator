# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To resolve the chapter with a display of Carter's unique abilities and transition him into the next phase of his journey.

**Scene Summary:** Realizing he is a wanted man, Carter waits for nightfall. He utilizes his superhuman strength and agility to leap from a palace balcony, escaping into the city of Zodanga.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** Palace balcony; overlooking Zodangan avenue/cityscape at night.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Decisive Action $\rightarrow$ Freedom/Escape.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots (the scale of the leap)
- Tracking shots (following his descent/movement)
- Extreme Long Shots (Carter disappearing into the city)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Time of day (nightfall); lighting from the city below vs. palace lights.

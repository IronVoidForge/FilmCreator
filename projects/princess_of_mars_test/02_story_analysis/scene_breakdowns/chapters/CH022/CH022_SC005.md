# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To provide a high-action escape and transition the character out of the palace setting.

**Scene Summary:** Under the cover of darkness, Carter makes his break for freedom. He leaps from a balcony, strikes a glass wall to redirect his momentum, and descends into the streets of Zodanga.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** Palace Balcony; Exterior Zodanga (streets/walls).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Suspense $\rightarrow$ Adrenaline/Release.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide tracking shots of the leap.
- POV or "follow" shots of the descent.
- Extreme wide shot showing the scale of the city at night.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting (moonlight/shadows) and the physics of the glass wall impact.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Stealthy tension $\rightarrow$ Growing dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** POV shots from Carter's hiding spots; Deep focus to show guards in the distance; Low-key lighting.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Sound design (footsteps, muffled voices); Lighting/Shadow placement for Carter's concealment.

# SC005

## Scene Purpose
Escape sequence showing John Carter's tactical decision-making and departure.

## Scene Summary
Afterward, realizing the danger posed by the dead guardsmen, John Carter escapes the palace through a glass wall into the night. This scene demonstrates his survival instincts and creates transition to next chapter.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (escapee)

## Participating Environments
- Palace exterior/wall area
- Glass wall/exit point
- Night environment outside palace

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Resignation → Survival mode/determination

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of escape through glass wall
- Tracking shots following John Carter's departure
- Establishing shots of night environment

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Glass wall location and accessibility

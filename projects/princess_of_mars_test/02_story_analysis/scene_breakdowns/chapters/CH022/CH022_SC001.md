# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the emotional inciting incident and the betrayal of Carter's expectations.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter, hidden behind a tapestry while on guard duty, witnesses Dejah Thoris enter Jeddak Than Kosis's private chambers. She publicly declares her intention to marry Sab Than to secure peace, effectively severing her bond with Carter.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (observer), Dejah Thoris, Than Kosis, Sab Than.

**Participating Environments:** Private apartments of Jeddak Than Kosis; Zodangan palace interior.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Devotion/Hope $\rightarrow$ Shock/Heartbreak.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Tight Close-ups (Carter's eyes/reaction)
- Medium shots (Dejah Thoris's formal declaration)
- Over-the-shoulder (from behind the tapestry)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's position relative to the tapestry; Dejah Thoris's regal attire and posture.

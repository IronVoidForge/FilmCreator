# SC001

## Scene Purpose
Establish John Carter's position and introduce the dramatic arrival of Dejah Thoris at the palace setting.

## Scene Summary
John Carter is positioned to guard Jeddak Than Kosis during wartime. He witnesses Dejah Thoris's arrival at the palace, creating tension between his duty and personal emotions.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (narrator)
- Jeddak Than Kosis (mentioned/observed)
- Dejah Thoris (arrival witness)

## Participating Environments
- Palace grounds/war zone
- Guard positions
- Entrance areas of palace

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Neutral duty → Anticipation/curiosity about Dejah Thoris's presence

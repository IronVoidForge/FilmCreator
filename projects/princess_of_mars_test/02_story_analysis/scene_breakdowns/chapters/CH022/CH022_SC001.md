## Scene Purpose
Dejah Thoris publicly announces her engagement to Sab Than, resolving the conflict with Than Kosis. John Carter witnesses this, realizing his romantic dream is destroyed as she chooses the enemy's son.

## Scene Summary
Dejah enters Than Kosis's apartment with four soldiers and courtiers. She stands before Than Kosis and Sab Than, radiant. She announces her engagement to Sab Than, explaining Barsoom custom regarding promises. She begs Than Kosis to forget her rash words from two days ago. Than Kosis accepts and orders a proclamation issued. Dejah leaves with guards. Carter realizes she gave herself to the enemy's son.

## Participating Characters
John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Than Kosis, Sab Than, Four Soldiers, Courtiers.

## Participating Environments
Than Kosis's Apartment (Jeddak's passage behind tapestries).

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Hopeful -> Disappointed/Heartbroken.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
Medium shots of group interaction, Close-ups on Dejah and Carter's reaction, Wide shots of the proclamation being issued.

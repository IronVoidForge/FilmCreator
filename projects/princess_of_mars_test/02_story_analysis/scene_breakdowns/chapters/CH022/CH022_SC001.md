# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the emotional inciting incident and the betrayal of Carter's expectations.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter, hidden behind a tapestry while guarding Jeddak Than Kosis, witnesses Dejah Thoris enter the private chamber. Instead of seeking him, she publicly pledges herself to Sab Than to maintain peace between their warring cities.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (observer), Dejah Thoris, Than Kosis, Sab Than.

**Participating Environments:** Private apartments of Jeddak Than Kosis; interior palace.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Hope/Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Devastation/Betrayal.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- POV shots from behind the tapestry (Carter's perspective).
- Medium shots of Dejah Thoris making her declaration.
- Close-ups on Carter's reaction to the news.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's position relative to the tapestry and his visibility to the royal party.

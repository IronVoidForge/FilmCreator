# Scene SC001 - The Promise Made

## Scene Purpose
Establish the inciting incident where Carter witnesses Dejah Thoris making her promise to Sab Than, creating the emotional stakes for his confrontation.

## Scene Summary
Carter observes from a distance as Dejah Thoris publicly promises herself to Sab Than before Than Kosis in her apartments. The scene captures the moment that triggers Carter's decision to abandon his post and confront her directly.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (observer)
- Dejah Thoris (promise-maker)
- Sab Than (recipient of promise)
- Than Kosis (witness)

## Participating Environments
- Dejah Thoris Apartments (main chamber)
- Observation point within apartments

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Shock → Abandonment → Determination

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots establishing apartment interior and tapestry-hung walls
- Medium shots of Dejah Thoris during promise ceremony
- Over-the-shoulder shots from Carter's perspective
- Close-ups on facial expressions during vow exchange

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Tapestry positions hiding windows/doors must remain consistent
- Apartment layout (passage behind tapestries) needs clear mapping
- Character positioning in main chamber for wide shot coverage

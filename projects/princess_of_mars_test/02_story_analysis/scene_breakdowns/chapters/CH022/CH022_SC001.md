# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the inciting incident and emotional devastation for the protagonist.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter, hidden in a secret passage, watches as Dejah Thoris enters Jeddak Than Kosis's apartment. He overhears her formally retract her preference for Tal Hajus and pledge herself to Sab Than of Zodanga to ensure Helium's safety.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (observer), Dejah Thoris, Jeddak Than Kosis.

**Participating Environments:** Hidden passage/observation niche; Tapestried apartment of Jeddak Than Kosis.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Observational tension $\rightarrow$ Devastation/Heartbreak.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** POV shots from the hidden passage; Close-ups on Carter's reaction; Medium shots of Dejah Thoris performing the formal pledge.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels in the dark passage vs. the brightly lit apartment; Carter's physical position relative to the viewing slit.

# Scene SC004 - The Escape

## Scene Purpose
Execute the chapter's resolution as Carter escapes the palace, maintaining dramatic tension while providing narrative closure.

## Scene Summary
Carter realizes the promise is final by custom and chooses to escape the palace via a balcony and glass wall to avoid detection of his unauthorized presence within the building.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (escapee)

## Participating Environments
- Balcony overlooking broad avenue of Zodanga
- Glass wall (20 feet high, polished glass) separating building from avenue
- Apartment exit points

## Dominant Emotional Shift

- Over-the-shoulder shots showing height perspective

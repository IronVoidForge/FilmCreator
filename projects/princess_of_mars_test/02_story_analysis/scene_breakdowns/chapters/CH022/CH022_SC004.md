# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To raise the stakes and establish the scale of the pursuit.

**Scene Summary:** As the palace erupts in chaos over the dead guards, Carter hides inside a large hanging ornament in the Great Hall. From his hiding spot, he overhears Notan describing him as a superhuman stranger and hears Than Kosis ordering a massive manhunt.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (hidden), Notan, Than Kosis, Palace Guards (off-screen/background).

**Participating Environments:** The Great Hall.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Macro shots of Carter's eyes/face within the ornament.
- Low-angle or wide shots of the Great Hall to show scale and chaos.
- Over-the-shoulder shots of the royals as they discuss the manhunt.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Sound design (muffled voices vs. clear palace noise).

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the cultural conflict and the insurmountable obstacle to their union.

**Scene Summary:** Dejah explains the Barsoomian custom regarding marriage and formal proposals. She reveals that despite his heroism, Carter failed to follow protocol, and she cannot wed him without causing the death of her husband, Sab Than.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** Palace corridor/Private area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Hope $\rightarrow$ Despair/Frustration.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Tight close-ups to emphasize the intimacy and tragedy; Low-angle shots to show the weight of tradition.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Character positioning as they move through the conversation.

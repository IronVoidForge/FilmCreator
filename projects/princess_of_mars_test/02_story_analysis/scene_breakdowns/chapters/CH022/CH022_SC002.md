# SC002

## Scene Purpose
The public renunciation creates the emotional catalyst for John Carter's subsequent actions.

## Scene Summary
Dejah Thoris publicly renounces her previous promise to John Carter in favor of Sab Than, Prince of Zodanga. The announcement is made before witnesses, creating maximum dramatic impact on John Carter.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (heartbroken reaction)
- Dejah Thoris (public declaration)
- Sab Than (mentioned/possibly present)
- Palace witnesses/guards

## Participating Environments
- Palace courtyard or public gathering area
- Announcement platform/stage

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Anticipation → Heartbreak/shock from renunciation

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Close-up of Dejah Thoris's declaration
- Reaction shots of John Carter's emotional response
- Wide shots showing audience reaction

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Audience positioning and reactions
- Sab Than's presence/absence consistency
- Time elapsed between arrival and announcement

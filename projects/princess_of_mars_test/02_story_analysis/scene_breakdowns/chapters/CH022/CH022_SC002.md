# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Demonstrate Carter's physical prowess and escalate the tension through violence.

**Scene Summary:** Driven by desperation, Carter abandons his post and pursues Dejah Thoris. He is intercepted in the palace corridors by four of her guards and kills them all in a swift, violent skirmish.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Four Guards.

**Participating Environments:** Palace corridors.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Violent aggression/Adrenaline.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots for choreography; Handheld/shaky cam for the combat; Close-ups on weaponry and impact.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Blood splatter patterns; Weapon positions post-combat; Damage to Carter's armor or clothing.

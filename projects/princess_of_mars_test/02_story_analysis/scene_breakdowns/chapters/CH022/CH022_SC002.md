# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To demonstrate Carter's martial prowess and escalate the tension through violence.

**Scene Summary:** Distraught by Dejah's declaration, Carter abandons his post to find her. He is intercepted in an antechamber by four Zodangan guardsmen. A brutal twenty-minute combat ensues, resulting in the death of all four guards.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Four Guardsmen.

**Participating Environments:** Palace corridors/Antechambers.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Rage/Combat Intensity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots to establish the geography of the fight.
- Handheld/Dynamic shots for the combat choreography.
- Close-ups on weapons and physical struggle.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Blood splatter, weapon positions, and the number of fallen guards in the frame.

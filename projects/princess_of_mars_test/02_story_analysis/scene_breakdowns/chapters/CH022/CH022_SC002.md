# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To demonstrate Carter's combat prowess and escalate the tension from emotional to physical danger.

**Scene Summary:** Driven by distress, Carter abandons his post and is intercepted in an antechamber by four Zodangan guardsmen. A violent twenty-minute battle ensues, resulting in the death of all four guards.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Four Guardsmen.

**Participating Environments:** Palace antechamber/corridors.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Violent Intensity $\rightarrow$ Exhaustion/Adrenaline.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots (choreography and spatial movement)
- Handheld/Dynamic tracking (combat intensity)
- Close-ups (impacts and weapon work)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Blood splatter patterns; weapon positions; the number of dead guards in the frame.

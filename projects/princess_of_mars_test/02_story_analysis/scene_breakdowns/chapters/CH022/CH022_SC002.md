## Scene Purpose
John Carter leaves his post to confront Dejah Thoris. He is stopped by guardsmen in the antechamber and engages them in combat, killing three quickly and the fourth within ten seconds.

## Scene Summary
Carter navigates winding corridors behind tapestries to find Dejah's antechamber. Four guardsmen stop him demanding an order or password. Carter refuses, tapping his long-sword as order. Guards draw weapons; a fight ensues. Carter kills three guardsmen quickly, then the fourth within ten seconds. Dejah Thoris stands at the door of her apartment watching throughout the conflict with Sola at her back. She does not recognize him initially.

## Participating Characters
John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Four Guardsmen.

## Participating Environments
Antechamber/Corridors behind tapestries.

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Tense/Hostile -> Action/Chaos.

## Likely Visual Coverage Families

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities

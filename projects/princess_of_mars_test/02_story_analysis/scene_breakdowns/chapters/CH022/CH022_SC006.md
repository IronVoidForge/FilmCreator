# SC006

**Scene Purpose:** Execute the protagonist's escape and demonstrate his superhuman nature.

**Scene Summary:** Realizing patrols are closing in, Carter waits for nightfall. He utilizes his extraordinary strength to leap from a high palace balcony, successfully escaping the grounds.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** Palace balcony; Exterior palace grounds (night).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Calculated patience $\rightarrow$ Exhilaration/Escape.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots to capture the scale of the leap; Tracking shots following his descent; High-angle shots looking down from the balcony.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Time of day (night); Lighting transition from interior palace to exterior darkness.

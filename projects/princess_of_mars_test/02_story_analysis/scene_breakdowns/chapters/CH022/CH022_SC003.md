# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To provide emotional closure and explain the cultural conflict driving the plot.

**Scene Summary:** Carter finds Dejah Thoris. She recognizes him but explains that Barsoomian custom makes her promise to Sab Than binding. She clarifies that her previous actions were a misunderstanding of marriage customs, leaving them both trapped by duty.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** Palace interior (near the antechamber/corridors).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confrontation $\rightarrow$ Sorrowful Resignation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Two-shots to capture the intimacy and distance between them.
- Tight close-ups on faces to emphasize emotional pain.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's physical state (sweat/blood from the previous fight) vs. Dejah's regal appearance.

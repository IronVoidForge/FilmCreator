# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the immediate conflict of identity and reveal the true motivation behind Dejah's actions.

**Scene Summary:** Carter reaches Dejah Thoris. After an initial moment of non-recognition due to his armor, she identifies him and breaks down in grief, explaining her political sacrifice was made under the belief that he was dead.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** Palace corridor/Antechamber area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Recognition $\rightarrow$ Intense Grief/Relief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Two-shots (medium and close); Extreme close-ups on eyes/facial expressions; Over-the-shoulder shots during the reveal.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's armor state (blood/scuffs from SC002); Dejah's physical reaction to seeing him.

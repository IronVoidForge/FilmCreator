# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To provide the emotional climax and establish the tragic social barriers preventing the protagonists' union.

**Scene Summary:** Carter finds Dejah Thoris. Though she recognizes him and loves him, she explains that Barsoomian custom makes her vow to Sab Than binding. She informs him that he cannot marry her while Sab lives, nor can he wed a man who kills her husband.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** Palace corridors/private area near the antechamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Aggression/Urgency $\rightarrow$ Despair/Tragic Resignation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Two-shots (emotional connection)
- Tight Close-ups (facial expressions of grief and longing)
- Medium shots (body language/distance between them)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Dejah Thoris's emotional state (tears/makeup); Carter's physical condition (sweat/blood from the fight).

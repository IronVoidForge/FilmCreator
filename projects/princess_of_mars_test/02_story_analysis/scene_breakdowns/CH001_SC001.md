# Scene SC001 - Introduction

## Purpose
Establish narrator's voice and thematic concerns about mortality while setting Arizona location.

## Summary
Carter, a soldier of fortune, reflects on his age and the strange events during ten years in an Arizona cave. He begins recounting the story to establish credibility and emotional stakes.

## Participating Characters
- Carter (narrator)

## Participating Environments
- Arizona hills/valley setting
- Cave entrance area

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Contemplative → Storytelling mode activation

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shots of Carter speaking
- Wide establishing shots of Arizona landscape
- Close-ups on Carter's face showing age and weariness

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Consistent lighting (clear Arizona morning)
- Carter's physical appearance (age indicators)
- Location markers for cave entrance

# Scene 005: The Prisoner

**Scene Purpose:**
Focus on the emotional resolution of the chapter through the interaction between Carter and the captured Martian figure.

**Scene Summary:**
Slender, girlish figure similar to earthly women; oval and beautiful face; finely chiseled features; large/lustrous eyes; coal black waving hair caught loosely into strange coiffure; light reddish copper skin; crimson glow of cheeks; ruby lips. Destitute of clothes, entirely naked save for highly wrought ornaments. Dragged roughly into nearby building by green Martian females. Eyes meet Carter's; she makes a little sign with free hand (ununderstood); expression shifts from hope/renewed courage to utter dejection/loathing/contempt because he did not answer her signal.

**Participating Characters:**
*   Carter (Observer)
*   Martian Female (Dragger)
*   Prisoner (Subject of interaction)

**Participating Environments:**
*   Nearby Building Exterior (Dragging point)
*   Building Interior (Interaction point)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:**
Hope -> Dejection/Contempt

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**

*   Medium Shots: Interaction between Carter and the prisoner.
*   Over-the-Shoulder: From Carter looking at the prisoner.

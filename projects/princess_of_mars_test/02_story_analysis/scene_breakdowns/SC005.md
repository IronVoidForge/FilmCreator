# Scene SC005 - Discovery of Captive Figure

## Scene Purpose
Introduce the central character discovery with emotional impact on narrator.

## Scene Summary
While returning to the plaza, the narrator spots a slender, girlish figure being dragged into a building by green Martian females. The captive looks at narrator with hope then loathing.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (primary observer)
- Sola (appears at end of scene)
- Woola (hound following close)
- Captive (slender, girlish figure - light reddish copper skin, coal black hair, oval face, large lustrous eyes, finely chiseled features, destitute of clothes except ornaments)

## Participating Environments
- Building interior (portal/depths)
- Plaza (crowded with Martians)
- Building exterior (drag scene)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Recovery satisfaction → Discovery shock → Hope mixed with dejection

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots showing plaza from distance
- Medium shots of narrator spotting figure
- Close-ups on captive's face and expression
- POV shots tracking dragging action into building
- Interior depth shots showing portal/depths

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Captive appearance consistency (light reddish copper skin, coal black hair)
- Facial expression changes (hope to loathing)
- Narrator's emotional reaction timing
- Sola's entrance coordination
- Building interior portal positioning

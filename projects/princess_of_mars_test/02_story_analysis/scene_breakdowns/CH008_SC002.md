**Purpose:** Depict aerial conflict and resourcefulness in salvaging supplies from a building window perspective.

**Summary:** From a window, narrator observes twenty gray airships approaching the valley. Martians fire volley from building windows. Ships return fire but suffer visible damage. One ship remains unmanned; Martians board it, loot it (arms, food, water), and burn it as funeral pyre before towing it away.

**Characters:** Green Martian warriors (active participants), Narrator (window observer).

**Environments:** Building windows overlooking the valley floor, Drifting warship in air space.

**Emotional Shift:** Tension of battle -> Triumph of salvage/solemnity of burning.

**Visual Coverage Families:** POV shots from window, Wide shots of airships, Medium shots of Martians firing/boarding, Close-ups on loot items (arms, food, water).

**Continuity Sensitivities:** Number of ships (20), specific loot items, state of the burning ship.

**Staging Facts:**
- Window POV establishes narrator's vantage point throughout scene
- Twenty airships create scale for wide coverage shots
- Martians positioned at building windows for volley fire sequences
- Damage to ships visible in wide/medium coverage
- Unmanned ship becomes focal point after damage sequence
- Loot items require extreme close-up coverage for detail
- Burning ship requires wide-to-medium transition for pyre effect
- Tow away shot provides scene resolution

**Beat Structure:** Eight reusable beats (BT001-BT008) organized by visual moment and continuity requirement.

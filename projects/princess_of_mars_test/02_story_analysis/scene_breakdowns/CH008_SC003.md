# SC003 - Disabled Ship Recovery & Looting/Burning
## Scene Purpose
Show the aftermath of battle and recovery of a specific vessel.
## Scene Summary
One ship is unmanned and drifting. Warriors rush to intercept it before it hits buildings south of their position. They board using spears and grappling hooks. Martians search the vessel, removing arms, silks, jewels, and food (including water). Afterward, they empty carboys over dead sailors and set the ship ablaze with a missile. The burning ship drifts southeast as a funeral pyre.
## Participating Characters
Green Warriors, Dead Sailors, Martians.
## Participating Environments
Drifting ship, South of position buildings.
## Dominant Emotional Shift
Urgency -> Regret (realizing failure to help).
## Likely Visual Coverage Families
Tracking shot of drifting ship, close-ups of looting, wide shot of burning ship.
## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
Ship movement from drifting to burning, specific items removed.

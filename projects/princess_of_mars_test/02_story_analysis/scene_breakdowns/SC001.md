# Scene SC001 - The Retreat After Ceremony

## Scene Purpose
Establish the aftermath of the incubator ceremony and the sudden retreat order, setting the stage for the approaching threat.

## Scene Summary
The procession returns to a deserted city immediately after an incubator ceremony due to sudden orders. The atmosphere shifts from ceremonial to tense as the city empties.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (primary observer)
- Sola (appears at end of scene)
- Woola (hound following close)

## Participating Environments
- Upper floor window (view of valley/hills)
- Building interior (portal/depths)
- Plaza (crowded with Martians - distant)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Peaceful ceremony → Tense retreat → Anticipation of threat

- Medium tracking shots through corridors

# Scene 1: The Return and Fleet Approach

**Location:** City Buildings (Upper Floor Window)
**Action:** John Carter and Sola return from incubator ceremony. Martians retreat into buildings. Carter investigates cause of retreat.
**Visuals:** Gray-painted airships approach over valley and hills. Banners and odd devices on prows visible in sunlight. Figures crowding decks.
**Uncertainty:** Narrator unsure if fleet discovered them or just looking at deserted city.

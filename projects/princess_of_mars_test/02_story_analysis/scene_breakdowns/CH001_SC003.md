# Scene SC003 - Rising Action

## Purpose
Build tension through observation and tracking; shift from static to active pursuit.

## Summary
Carter sees Powell's party vanish into shadows. He follows tracks, discovering they are unshod ponies galloping away. The mystery deepens as he pursues the disappearance.

## Participating Characters
- Carter

## Participating Environments
- Arizona valley trails
- Shadowy areas near claim

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Curiosity → Urgent pursuit mode

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Tracking shots following footprints/tracks
- Medium shots of Carter moving through terrain
- Wide shots showing party disappearing into shadows

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Track consistency (unshod ponies)
- Shadow/lighting transitions
- Trail path markers

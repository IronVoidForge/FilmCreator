**Participating Characters:** Fleet crew, Green Martian defenders (window shooters), Carter (observer)

**Participating Environments:** Deserted city buildings with upper floors and rooftops, Valley floor, Hills beyond, Open ground/plaza

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation → Tension → Shock at damage

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide establishing shots of fleet arrival, Medium shots of window defenders firing, Close-ups on ship damage indicators, Aerial perspective of engagement

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Ship count (twenty initially), Banner positions, Damage progression across shots, Character positioning in windows

## SC001 - The Retreat After Ceremony

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the aftermath of the incubator ceremony and the sudden retreat order, setting the stage for the approaching threat.

**Scene Summary:** The procession returns to a deserted city immediately after an incubator ceremony due to sudden orders. The atmosphere shifts from ceremonial to tense as the city empties.

**Participating Characters:** Narrator, Sola (appears at end), Woola (hound)

**Participating Environments:** Upper floor window (view of valley/hills), Building interior (portal/depths)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Peaceful ceremony → Tense retreat → Anticipation of threat

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide establishing shots, medium tracking shots through corridors, close-ups on narrator's anxious expression, POV shots from windows looking out at empty city

**Participating Characters:** Narrator, Sola (appears at end), Woola (hound)

**Participating Environments:** Building roofs (observation point), Upper floor window (view of valley/hills), Airship decks (distant)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation → Alarm → Defensive action

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide aerial shots of airships approaching, medium shots from building windows showing firing, close-ups on narrator watching ships, POV shots from rooftops tracking ship movement

## SC005 - Discovery of Captive Figure

**Scene Summary:** While returning to the plaza, the narrator spots a slender, girlish figure being dragged into a building by green Martian females. The captive looks at narrator with hope then loathing.

**Participating Characters:** Narrator, Sola (appears at end), Woola (hound), Captive (slender, girlish figure)

**Participating Environments:** Building interior (portal/depths), Plaza (crowded with Martians), Building exterior (drag scene)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Recovery satisfaction → Discovery shock → Hope mixed with dejection

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots showing plaza from distance, medium shots of narrator spotting figure, close-ups on captive's face and expression, POV shots tracking dragging action into building

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Captive appearance consistency (light reddish copper skin, coal black hair), facial expression changes (hope to loathing), narrator's emotional reaction timing, Sola's entrance coordination

# Scene 3: The Captive's Appeal

**Location:** Plaza / Building Entrance
**Action:** Carter enters plaza. Sees prisoner dragged into building by green females.
**Visuals:** Slender, girlish figure. Naked save for highly wrought ornaments. Coal black hair in strange coiffure. Light reddish copper skin, crimson cheeks, ruby lips.
**Interaction:** She signals for help with free hand. Carter fails to respond (ignorance of customs). Hope fades to dejection/contempt. She is taken into depths of edifice.

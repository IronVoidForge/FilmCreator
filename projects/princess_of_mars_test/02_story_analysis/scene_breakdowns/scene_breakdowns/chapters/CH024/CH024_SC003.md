# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Execute the political coup and establish the new power structure.

**Scene Summary:** In the Thark council, John Carter publicly challenges Tal Hajus's fitness to rule by highlighting his cowardice. A duel is decreed between Tars Tarkas and Tal Hajus. Tars Tarkas wins the duel, becoming the new Jeddak and elevating John Carter to chieftain status.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Tal Hajus, Lorquas Ptomel, Thark Council Members.

**Participating Environments:** Thark (Council Chamber).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Defiance $\rightarrow$ High Tension $\rightarrow$ Victory/Authority.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the council assembly to show scale and audience reaction.
- Medium shots of the duel between Tars Tarkas and Tal Hajus.
- Close-ups on Carter's speech and the proclamation of new ranks.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Weaponry used in the duel, wounds sustained by Tal Hajus, seating arrangements of the council.

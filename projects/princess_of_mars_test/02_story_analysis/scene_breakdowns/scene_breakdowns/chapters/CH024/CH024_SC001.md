# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish John Carter's combat prowess and forge a critical alliance through heroic intervention.

**Scene Summary:** During a massive battle between Tharks and Warhoons, John Carter's aircraft is shot down. He enters the fray and saves Tars Tarkas from being overwhelmed by three Warhoon warriors. After the skirmish, he reunites with Woola.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Warhoon Warriors, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** The Battlefield (Plains near an ancient dead city).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Chaos/Peril $\rightarrow$ Heroic Triumph $\rightarrow$ Relief/Bonding.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots of the massive scale of the Thark/Warhoon battle.
- Handheld/Shaky cam for the aircraft crash and combat.
- Close-ups on Carter's intervention and Tars Tarkas's reaction.
- Medium shots of the reunion with Woola.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Aircraft debris location, blood/dirt levels on characters after battle, Woola's position relative to Carter.

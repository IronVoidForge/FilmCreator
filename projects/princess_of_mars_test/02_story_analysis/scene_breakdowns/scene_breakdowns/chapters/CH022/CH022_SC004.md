# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To raise the stakes by revealing the scale of the hunt for Carter and his status as a superhuman threat.

**Scene Summary:** As the palace erupts in chaos over the dead guards, Carter hides inside a large hanging ornament in a great hall. He overhears Notan, the Royal Psychologist, reporting to Than Kosis that a single, superhuman fighter is responsible for the massacre.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter (hidden), Notan, Than Kosis (off-screen/distant).

**Participating Environments:** Great Hall; interior of a large hanging ornament.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Panic $\rightarrow$ Suspense/Dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- POV shots (from inside the ornament looking out)
- Low-angle shots (of the approaching officials)
- Close-ups (Carter's breath/eyes in the dark)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels within the hall vs. the darkness of the hiding spot; sound design (muffled voices).

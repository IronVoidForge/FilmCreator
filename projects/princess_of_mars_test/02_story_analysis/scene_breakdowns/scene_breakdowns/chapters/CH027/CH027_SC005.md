# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** The climax of the chapter; resolving the central conflict through the protagonist's unique ability.

**Scene Summary:** John arrives at the Atmosphere Plant to find a graveyard of workers. Using his mental breakthrough, he navigates the locks and opens the three great doors. He orders a survivor to restart the pumps before collapsing from exhaustion and hypoxia.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Surviving Worker.

**Participating Environments:** The Atmosphere Plant.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Intense Struggle $\rightarrow$ Triumphant Exhaustion/Collapse.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-ups of the mechanical locks and John's mental application.
- Wide shots of the massive doors opening.
- A final, slow fade to black or a heavy descent as John collapses.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The sequence of unlocking the three specific doors; the physical state of the surviving worker.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Create a high-stakes race against time.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter commandeers an air-scout machine. He strips it of all unnecessary weight to maximize speed and flies through the increasingly thin, turbulent atmosphere toward the plant.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter.

**Participating Environments:** The sky over Barsoom, Air-scout Machine.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Adrenaline/Focus.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- High-speed tracking shots of the machine.
- POV shots from the cockpit looking at the thinning horizon.
- Shaky cam to emphasize the turbulence and speed.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical state of the machine (stripped parts) and John's increasing struggle to breathe while flying.

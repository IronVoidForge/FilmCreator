# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Illustrate the scale of the disaster and the physical toll on the characters.

**Scene Summary:** Over three days, the atmosphere thins. The population suffers as oxygen levels drop. In the Helium palace, the royal family gathers in a sunken garden, physically weakened by asphyxiation, facing their impending deaths.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tardos Mors, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** Sunken Garden, Helium Palace.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Despair/Lethargy $\rightarrow$ Fatalism.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Slow, heavy movements to simulate oxygen deprivation.
- Low-angle shots of the dying characters.
- Wide, desolate shots of the thinning atmosphere/sky.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The progressive physical deterioration (pallor, labored breathing) of Dejah Thoris and the others over the three-day period.

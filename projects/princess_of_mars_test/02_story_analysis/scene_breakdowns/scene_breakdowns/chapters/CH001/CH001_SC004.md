# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the tragic emotional beat and escalate the stakes of the escape.

**Scene Summary:** Amidst the skirmish, Carter finds Powell's body, killed by arrows. He retrieves the body to protect it from mutilation and begins a desperate flight under heavy fire.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Captain James K. Powell (deceased), Apache Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** The plateau/skirmish zone.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From combat adrenaline to grief and desperation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups of Carter's reaction to finding Powell; medium shots of the struggle to move the body while under fire; wide shots of the pursuit.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The condition/position of Powell's body; the direction of incoming arrow fire.

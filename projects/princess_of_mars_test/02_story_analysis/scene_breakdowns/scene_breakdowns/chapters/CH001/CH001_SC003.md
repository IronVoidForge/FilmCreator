# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Provide the high-action climax of the chapter and demonstrate Carter's combat prowess.

**Scene Summary:** Carter discovers a massive Apache camp on a plateau. Instead of retreating, he launches a violent, sudden charge with his revolvers, causing mass confusion among the five hundred warriors.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Apache Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** A high plateau filled with Apache tepees.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From suspense to explosive violence and chaos.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Handheld/shaky cam for the charge; wide shots of the scale of the camp; rapid-fire close-ups of revolvers discharging.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Number of warriors visible; ammunition count (revolvers); dust clouds kicked up by horses.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the protagonist's disorientation and the physical reality of Martian gravity.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist wakes up naked in a yellowish, mossy basin on Mars. He discovers that the low gravity makes even basic movements result in massive, uncontrolled leaps.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist.

**Participating Environments:** A circular basin on Mars; yellowish moss-like vegetation; quartz-bearing rock outcroppings.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Disorientation $\rightarrow$ Physical shock/Surprise.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- POV shots of the alien landscape.
- Wide shots showing the scale of his leaps.
- Close-ups of physical exertion and confusion.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The height and distance of the protagonist's jumps; his state of undress.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the alien biology and the presence of the "Incubator."

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist explores a nearby glass-roofed enclosure. Inside, he observes hundreds of large white eggs, some of which have hatched into grotesque, six-limbed creatures with antennae and independent eyes.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist.

**Participating Environments:** The Incubator (low, walled glass enclosure).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Horror/Unease.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Macro shots of the eggs and hatching creatures.
- Medium shots of the protagonist peering through the glass.
- Tracking shots following his movement toward the structure.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The state of the eggs (hatched vs. unhatched); the movement patterns of the six-limbed creatures.

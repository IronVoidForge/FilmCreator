# SC006

**Scene Purpose:** Resolution and elevation of Carter's social status.

**Scene Summary:** Carter returns to Zodanga with the rescued scout. During a massive military ceremony in the plaza, Jeddak Than Kosis publicly honors Carter for his bravery, appointing him a *padwar* of the Guards and granting him quarters in the royal palace.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Than Kosis (Jeddak), Sab Than, Zodangan Military/Crowd.

**Participating Environments:** The Great Plaza of Zodanga; Royal Palace grounds.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Grandeur/Social Elevation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide sweeping shots of the ceremony; Low-angle "hero" shots of Carter; Reaction shots from the crowd and royalty.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Uniform/Armor changes (if any); Crowd density and placement.

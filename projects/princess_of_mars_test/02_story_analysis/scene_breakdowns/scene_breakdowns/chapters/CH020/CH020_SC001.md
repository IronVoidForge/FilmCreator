# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the physical toll of the journey and introduce the scale of the Atmosphere Factory.

**Scene Summary:** After two weeks of survival in the harsh Martian wilderness, battling beasts with Woola's help, John Carter discovers a massive, imposing Atmosphere Factory.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian Wilderness (harsh landscape, strange vegetation).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion/Survival $\rightarrow$ Awe/Intrigue.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the desolate landscape; Close-ups of Carter's fatigue and Woola's alertness; Establishing shots of the massive concrete factory.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's level of physical grime/exhaustion; Woola's condition after combat.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the antagonist and the central mystery of the Ninth Ray.

**Scene Summary:** Carter enters the factory and is hosted by an elderly man. While the host appears hospitable, Carter uses his telepathy to realize the man intends to kill him to protect the secrets of the atmosphere-generating machinery.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, The Old Man.

**Participating Environments:** Interior of the Atmosphere Factory (radium pumps, massive machinery).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Hospitality $\rightarrow$ Suspicion/Dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of the interaction; Close-ups on the Old Man's face to capture subtle shifts in expression; Macro shots of the Ninth Ray stone/diadem.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The presence and placement of the diadem; Lighting changes within the industrial interior.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To depict the final destruction of the Zodangan land forces.

**Scene Summary:** The Thark and Heliumite forces unload their thoats near Helium and launch a multi-pronged assault on the remaining Zodangan army on the plains. Despite being outnumbered, the combined tactical strength of the allies crushes the Zodangan resistance in a decisive land battle.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Thark Warriors, Heliumite Soldiers, Zodangan Army.

**Participating Environments:** The Plains near Helium.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Aggression $\rightarrow$ Decisive Triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide sweeping shots of the charge; Handheld/shaky cam for ground combat; Close-ups of thoat riders and infantry engagement.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Movement of the multi-pronged assault lines; The density of the Zodangan ranks as they are depleted.

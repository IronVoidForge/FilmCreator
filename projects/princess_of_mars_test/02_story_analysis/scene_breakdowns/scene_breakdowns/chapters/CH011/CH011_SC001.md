# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish Carter's dominance and protective role over Dejah Thoris while neutralizing Sarkoja's hostility.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter intervenes to protect Dejah Thoris from Sarkoja's mistreatment. He assigns Sola as a guard for the Princess and issues a lethal threat to Sarkoja, asserting his authority through strength and intimidation.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Sarkoja.

**Participating Environments:** The immediate vicinity of the captive quarters/prison area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension/hostility to decisive control/security.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-ups on Carter's eyes and mouth (threat delivery).
- Medium shots of Sarkoja's reaction (intimidation).
- Wide shot showing the power dynamic between the group.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's physical positioning relative to Dejah Thoris; Sola's movement from prisoner/captive status to guard status.

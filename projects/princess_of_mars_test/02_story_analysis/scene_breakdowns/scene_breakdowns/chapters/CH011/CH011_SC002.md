# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Character development and world-building through the revelation of Carter's origin.

**Scene Summary:** Carter moves Dejah Thoris and Sola into opulent, ancient apartments overlooking the plaza. In a moment of intimacy and vulnerability, Carter reveals he is from Earth (Virginia). Dejah Thoris explains how Barsoomians observe Earth via telescopes but failed to recognize him due to his lack of typical Earth clothing.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** The New Quarters (Ancient, opulent building with marble ceilings and frescoes).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From relief/comfort to profound revelation and curiosity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Establishing shots of the ancient architecture and frescoes.
- Two-shot mediums for the intimate conversation.
- Close-ups on Dejah Thoris as she explains Martian technology.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting changes from the harsh exterior to the softer, more luxurious interior; placement of characters within the new quarters.

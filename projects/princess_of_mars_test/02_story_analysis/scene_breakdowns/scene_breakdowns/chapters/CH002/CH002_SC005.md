# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** The transition from Earthly setting to the cosmic inciting incident.

**Scene Summary:** Fleeing the cave into the Arizona night, the protagonist gazes at Mars. He feels an irresistible magnetic pull toward the red planet and is suddenly whisked away through space into darkness.

**Participating Characters:**
- The Protagonist

**Participating Environments:**
- Arizona Mountain Landscape (Night)
- Deep Space/Void

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Fear to Fascination/Surrender to Transcendence.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide vistas of the desert under starlight
- Extreme Long Shots (Protagonist vs. Mars in sky)
- VFX-heavy transition (The "pull" and the jump into darkness)

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The position of Mars in the sky; the visual texture of the "transportation" effect.

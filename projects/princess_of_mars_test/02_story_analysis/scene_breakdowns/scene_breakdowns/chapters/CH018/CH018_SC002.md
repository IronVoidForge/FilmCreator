# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To execute a major narrative pivot through a violent power shift.

**Scene Summary:** Dak Kova openly defies Bar Comas. The two leaders engage in a primal, savage hand-to-hand duel. Dak Kova kills Bar Comas with a tusk thrust and claims the title of Jeddak.

**Participating Characters:** Dak Kova, Bar Comas, Warhoon Warriors (spectators).

**Participating Environments:** Open Martian plains near the Warhoon column.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Chaos/Violence $\rightarrow$ Shock/Awe.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of the combatants to emphasize scale; handheld, kinetic camera work during the duel; reaction shots of the surrounding horde.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Blood splatter patterns; positioning of the fallen Bar Comas; Dak Kova's physical state post-combat.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To demonstrate the protagonist's tactical intelligence over brute strength.

**Scene Summary:** Enraged by its mate's death, the second bull ape charges. The protagonist uses Earthly combat tactics—combining projectile use of the cudgel with precise hand-to-hand strikes—to overcome and kill the massive creature.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Bull Ape 2.

**Participating Environments:** Interior chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** High Tension $\rightarrow$ Exhausted Victory.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots tracking the protagonist's movement; wide shots to show the scale difference; close-ups of tactical strikes.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The trajectory and reuse of the cudgel; the physical exhaustion/injury level of the protagonist.

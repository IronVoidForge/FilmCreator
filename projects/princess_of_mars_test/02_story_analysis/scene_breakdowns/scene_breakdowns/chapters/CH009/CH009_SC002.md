# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Demonstrate the protagonist's physical progression and martial skill development.

**Scene Summary:** The protagonist engages in intensive combat training in the plaza against a Martian youth, utilizing his knowledge of Earthly fighting techniques to bridge the gap in raw strength.

**Participating Characters:** The Protagonist, Martian Youth.

**Participating Environments:** The Plaza.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Physical exertion and growing confidence/competence.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the plaza training.
- Dynamic medium shots of combat exchanges.
- Close-ups of footwork and weapon contact.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Weapon positions; sweat/dirt levels on the protagonist; time of day (lighting).

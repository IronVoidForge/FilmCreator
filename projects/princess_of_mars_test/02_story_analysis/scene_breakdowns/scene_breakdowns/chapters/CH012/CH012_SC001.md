# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the political tension and the precarious status of John Carter within Tharkian society.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter is summoned to an audience with Lorquas Ptomel. Ptomel confronts him regarding his ambiguous position, accuses him of plotting escape with a red prisoner, and issues a stern warning: while the Tharks are just, Carter may be killed in self-defense or during an escape attempt. He demands the delivery of Dejah Thoris to Tal Hajus.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Lorquas Ptomel.

**Participating Environments:** The Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From wary respect to mounting political pressure and threat.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Mid-shots of the two leaders facing off.
* Close-ups on Ptomel's stern expressions/accusations.
* Reaction shots of Carter processing the threats.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Carter's physical posture (defensive vs. proud); any weapons or ceremonial items present in the chamber.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the stakes and the ticking clock for the escape.

**Scene Summary:** Carter declares his devotion to Dejah Thoris and begins planning an escape. Sola breaks the news that Sarkoja has manipulated Tal Hajus into sentencing Dejah Thoris to death in the upcoming games, forcing a midnight escape plan via the northern waterway.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sola.

**Participating Environments:** Dejah Thoris's quarters (Plaza building).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Romance/Determination $\rightarrow$ Dread/Urgency.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Tight close-ups for the emotional revelation; low-key lighting to suggest secrecy; over-the-shoulder shots during the planning.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting levels (transitioning toward night); character positioning around maps or architectural layouts.

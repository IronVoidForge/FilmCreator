# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the narrator's status through a display of physical prowess and aggression.

**Scene Summary:** After an accidental physical altercation with a mocking warrior, the narrator is met with unexpected respect rather than execution. To solidify his standing, he performs a massive "sak" (leap), stunning the crowd.

**Participating Characters:**
* The Narrator
* Tars Tarkas
* The Chieftain
* Mocking Martian Warrior
* Martian Crowd

**Participating Environments:**
* The Audience Chamber / Grand Hall

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Peril $\rightarrow$ Shock $\rightarrow$ Triumph/Respect

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Handheld/shaky cam for the sudden physical strike.
* Wide, sweeping shots to capture the 150-foot leap.
* Reaction shots of the crowd (laughter turning to awe).

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
* The trajectory and landing point of the jump.
* The movement of the crowd during the commotion.

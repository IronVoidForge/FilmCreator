# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce a moment of tactical conflict and environmental tension.

**Scene Summary:** The party discovers a Warhoon incubator containing green Martian eggs. In an act of sabotage or defense, the incubator is destroyed, highlighting the high stakes of the Martian biological cycle.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Sarkoja, Dejah Thoris.

**Participating Environments:** An incubator site on the Martian plains.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Alertness $\rightarrow$ Destruction/Shock.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Macro shots of the eggs; wide shots of the destruction; reaction shots from the group.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** State of the incubator (intact vs. destroyed); debris patterns.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the emotional distance between Carter and Dejah Thoris and set the tone for the journey.

**Scene Summary:** As the group marches toward Thark, John Carter reflects on his complex feelings for Dejah Thoris and the cultural barriers between them. Dejah remains cold and distant due to a previous social transgression. Tars Tarkas manages the tension by taking custody of the keys to Dejah's chains to ensure her comfort.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Tars Tarkas, Sarkoja.

**Participating Environments:** The Martian plains/path toward Thark.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Melancholy and social tension $\rightarrow$ Resignation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups on Carter's contemplative expressions; wide shots of the marching group; medium shots of Dejah's cold demeanor; Tars Tarkas's authoritative presence.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Position of Dejah's chains; distance between characters in the march; lighting/time of day during the trek.

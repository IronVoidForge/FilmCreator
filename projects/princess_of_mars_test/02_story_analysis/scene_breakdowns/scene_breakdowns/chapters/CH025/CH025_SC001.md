# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale of the assault and the tension of the infiltration.

**Scene Summary:** John Carter leads fifty Tharks, under the command of Tars Tarkas, through the gates of Zodanga and into the palace gardens to disrupt the wedding ceremony.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Thark Warriors.

**Participating Environments:** Gates of Zodanga, Palace Gardens.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Aggression/Momentum.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the cavalry charge; medium tracking shots of Carter and Tars Tarkas moving through gardens; close-ups of determined faces.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Weapon positions, Thark armor/gear consistency during movement, lighting transitions from city streets to garden shadows.

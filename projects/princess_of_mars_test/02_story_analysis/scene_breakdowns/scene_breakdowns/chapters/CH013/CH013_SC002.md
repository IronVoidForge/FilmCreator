# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To mark the transition of the community and reward Carter for his contributions.

**Scene Summary:** After seven days, the community prepares to march toward Thark. Lorquas Ptomel rewards Carter with a gold anklet in recognition of his success with the mounts.

**Participating Characters:** John Carter, Lorquas Ptomel, Green Martian Horde.

**Participating Environments:** The city streets/assembly area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From industrious preparation to ceremonial honor.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Group shots of the marching horde.
- Medium close-up of the presentation of the gold anklet.
- Reaction shots from the warriors.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The presence/placement of the gold anklet on Carter; the level of readiness of the troops.

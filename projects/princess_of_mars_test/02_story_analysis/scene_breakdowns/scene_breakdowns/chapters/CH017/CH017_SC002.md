# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the grueling nature of the journey and the mounting desperation of the fugitives.

**Scene Summary:** The group travels northeast across a trackless mossy waste on thoats. They face starvation, dehydration, and exhaustion. During this trek, Woola joins the party, providing much-needed companionship and tracking ability.

**Participating Characters:** The Narrator, Dejah Thoris, Sola, Woola.

**Participating Environments:** The Mossy Waste (vast, trackless terrain).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Desperation/Exhaustion.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide establishing shots of the vast, desolate landscape.
- Medium tracking shots of the thoats moving through the moss.
- Close-ups on the weary faces and parched lips of the characters.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical condition/exhaustion level of the thoats; the progression of the group's supplies (or lack thereof).

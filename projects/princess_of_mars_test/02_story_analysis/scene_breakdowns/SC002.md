# SC002: Battle

## Scene Purpose
Depict first volley exchange between Earth defenders and Martian fleet, establishing combat effectiveness

## Scene Summary
Green warriors fire a terrific volley from building windows facing the valley. Air fleet returns fire with guns; ships move parallel to firing line then turn back. Banners dissolve in spurts of flame; green figures drop at bullet explosions; fire from vessels is ineffectual due to suddenness of first volley. Fleet limps away as one ship receives brunt of fire, appears unmanned and helpless.

## Participating Characters
- **Narrator** (Earth human) - Observer from building windows
- **Green Warriors** (Martian) - Defenders firing from windows
- **Air Fleet** (Martian) - Returning fleet personnel

## Participating Environments
- Building windows facing valley
- Air fleet formation above hill
- Combat field between positions

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Tension → Shock at sudden effectiveness of first volley

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of battle field showing both sides
- Medium shots from window perspective showing warriors firing
- Close-ups on banners dissolving in flame
- Medium shots of green figures dropping at bullet explosions
- Wide shot of fleet turning back with damaged ship

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Bullet trajectory consistency across shots
- Banner flame effects timing must match audio
- Ship movement patterns must be consistent
- Number of dropped figures must match narration

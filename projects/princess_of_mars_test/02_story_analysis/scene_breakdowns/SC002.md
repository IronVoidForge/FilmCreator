# Scene 002 - The Battle & Capture

## Scene Purpose
Depict the battle and capture of disabled airship while Carter observes from window.

## Scene Summary
Green Martians fire first, ships return fire. One ship disabled, captured, looted (arms, jewels, water), burned from within, then towed away. Carter watches entire sequence from building window.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter** (Observer from window)
- **Sola** (Observer from window)
- **Green Martians** (Collective - warriors firing, boarding ship)
- **Ship Crew** (Dead/missing figures on disabled vessel)

## Participating Environments
- **City Buildings** (Windows, roofs, balconies)
- **Valley** (Open terrain where ships land)
- **Hills** (Distant views of fleet movement)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Tense → Awe-inspiring → Depressed

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots: Battle sequence with volley fire and ship destruction
- Medium shots: Carter watching from window (reaction shots)
- Close-ups: Ship burning, flames consuming vessel
- Over-the-shoulder: Window view of disabled ship drifting down
- Detail shots: Martians looting ship, removing prisoner

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Ship positions: Disabled ship must remain consistent throughout sequence
- Fire effects: Burning ship must match described progression
- Martians' actions: Systematic rifling and burning must be accurate
- Time progression: Several hours for looting operation

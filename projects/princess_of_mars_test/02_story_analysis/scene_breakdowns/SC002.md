# Scene 002: The Battle

**Scene Purpose:**
Depict the combat dynamics and the specific targeting strategies employed by Carter's warriors against the Martian fleet.

Specific points are targeted: wireless finding/sighting apparatus, smaller guns, gunners, officers, crew members, upper works, steering gear/propellers.

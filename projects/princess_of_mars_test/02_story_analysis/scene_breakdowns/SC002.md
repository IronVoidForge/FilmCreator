# Scene SC002 - Airship Arrival Over Hills

## Scene Purpose
Introduce the approaching threat with visual spectacle of gray airships and defensive fire from city buildings.

## Scene Summary
Twenty gray-painted airships swing over the hills toward the city. They are met with a volley of fire from building windows. Initial peaceful advance turns deadly.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (primary observer)
- Sola (appears at end of scene)
- Woola (hound following close)

## Participating Environments
- Building roofs (observation point)
- Upper floor window (view of valley/hills)
- Airship decks (distant - bodies strewn about)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Anticipation → Alarm → Defensive action

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide aerial shots of airships approaching hills
- Medium shots from building windows showing firing
- Close-ups on narrator watching ships approach
- POV shots from rooftops tracking ship movement
- Distant shots showing bodies on airship decks

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Airship count and positioning consistency
- Fire patterns from windows
- Narrator's reaction timing to ship approach
- Bodies on decks visibility and placement

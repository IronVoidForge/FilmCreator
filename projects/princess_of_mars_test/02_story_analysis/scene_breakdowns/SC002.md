# Scene 002: Battle Begins with Volley Exchange

## Scene Purpose
Transition from observation to action. Show the dramatic shift when Green Martians fire volley and airships respond ineffectually.

## Scene Summary
Green Martian Warriors fire a volley from buildings, initiating battle. Airships return fire but results are ineffectual due to suddenness and damage. The scene captures the chaos of initial combat engagement.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (observer)
- Green Martian Warriors (active combatants)
- Airship Crews (distant combat figures)

## Participating Environments
- Building interiors (Martian firing positions)
- Rooftops (visible from window)
- Plaza/Street level (battle zone)
- Window of upper floor (Carter's observation point)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Anticipation → Shock → Concern as battle escalates

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shots of Carter watching battle unfold
- Wide shots showing volley exchange from buildings
- Action sequences with smoke and flame effects
- POV shots showing ineffectual airship fire results
- Close-ups on Carter's reactions to combat

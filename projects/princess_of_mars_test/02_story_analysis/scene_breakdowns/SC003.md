# Scene 003 - The Plaza Encounter

## Scene Purpose
Show the prisoner capture and Carter's failed response due to ignorance of local customs.

## Scene Summary
Carter returns to plaza, sees prisoner being dragged into building by Green Martian females. Prisoner makes eye contact with Carter, signals for help, then is taken away before he can respond.

## Participating Characters
- **John Carter** (Human protagonist, witness)
- **Sola** (Martian companion)
- **Prisoner Figure** (Slender girlish figure, light reddish copper skin, coal black hair, no clothes except ornaments)
- **Green Martians** (Females dragging prisoner)

## Participating Environments
- **City Plaza** (Open gathering space, central location)
- **Building Entrance** (Portal where prisoner is dragged into)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Hopeful → Dejected/Regretful

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Medium shots: Carter and Sola entering plaza
- Close-ups: Prisoner's face (oval, beautiful, large lustrous eyes, coal black hair)
- Over-the-shoulder: Carter's view of prisoner being dragged
- Detail shots: Prisoner's ornaments, skin color, expression
- Wide shots: Plaza throng of Martians

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Prisoner appearance: Must match detailed visual continuity facts (skin color, hair, ornaments)
- Character expressions: Carter's hope/fear/exultation/depression must be accurate
- Eye contact moment: Critical for emotional impact
- Time progression: Less than a week before cavalcade recommenced

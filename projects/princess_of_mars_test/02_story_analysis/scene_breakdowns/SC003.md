# SC003: Disabled Ship & Loot

## Scene Purpose
Show pursuit, boarding, and systematic rifling of disabled Martian vessel for intelligence gathering

## Scene Summary
Warriors chase disabled ship; board with spears and grappling hooks. Ship hauled to ground; systematic rifling of vessel including arms, ammunition, silks, furs, jewels, stone vessels, food/water. Ship towed out, emptied, set on fire by missile from last warrior. Drifts away as floating funeral pyre.

## Participating Characters
- **Green Warriors** (Martian) - Pursuers and boarders
- **Disabled Ship crew** (Martian) - Unmanned vessel personnel

## Participating Environments
- Ground level near disabled ship
- Air fleet formation above
- Combat field for pursuit

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Determination → Satisfaction at successful capture and intelligence gain

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of pursuit across ground
- Medium shots of boarding action with spears and grappling hooks
- Close-ups on loot items (arms, ammunition, silks, furs, jewels)
- Wide shot of ship being hauled to ground
- Medium shots of systematic rifling process
- Wide shot of burning ship drifting away

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Loot item consistency across close-up shots
- Ship fire progression must match audio timing
- Grappling hook positions must be consistent
- Number of warriors involved in boarding must remain clear

# Scene SC003 - Naval Engagement with Green Warriors

## Scene Purpose
Escalate the conflict with green Martian warriors returning accurate fire on airships.

## Scene Summary
Green Martian warriors return fire with high accuracy, targeting crew points and upper works. Several ships limp away; one is disabled and drifting. Deadly combat ensues.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (primary observer)
- Sola (appears at end of scene)
- Woola (hound following close)

## Participating Environments
- Building roofs (observation point)
- Upper floor window (view of valley/hills)
- Airship decks (distant with bodies strewn about)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Defensive action → Active combat → Casualty awareness

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots showing multiple ships under fire
- Medium shots from windows showing warriors firing
- Close-ups on narrator's horrified reaction
- POV shots tracking disabled ship drifting away
- Shots showing bodies strewn about decks

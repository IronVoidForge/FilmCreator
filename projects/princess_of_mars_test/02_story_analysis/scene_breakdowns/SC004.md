# SC004: Return & Prisoner

## Scene Purpose
Reveal captured Earth woman and narrator's emotional response to her plight, establishing personal stakes

## Scene Summary
Warriors return to plaza; danger of air craft passed for a week. Narrator sees prisoner dragged into building by green Martian females. Slender girlish figure with oval face, coal black hair, light reddish copper skin, naked except ornaments. She looks at narrator, makes sign (appeal for help), then is dragged away. Narrator feels hope and dejection.

## Participating Characters
- **Narrator** (Earth human) - Observer from building
- **Green Warriors** (Martian) - Returnees
- **Green Martian females** - Dragging prisoner
- **Captured Earth woman** - Prisoner with distinctive appearance

## Participating Environments
- Plaza area
- Building entrance
- Interior observation point

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Hope → Dejection at inability to help

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots of plaza showing warriors returning
- Medium shots of prisoner being dragged by females
- Close-ups on prisoner's face and ornaments (oval face, coal black hair, light reddish copper skin)
- Over-the-shoulder from narrator perspective watching prisoner
- Medium shot of prisoner making sign/appeal gesture

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Prisoner appearance must be consistent across all shots
- Ornament details must remain identifiable
- Narrator reaction timing must match emotional beat
- Sign/appeal gesture must be clearly visible and timed correctly

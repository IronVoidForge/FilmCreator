# Scene SC004 - Recovery Operation on Disabled Ship

## Scene Purpose
Show the aftermath of naval engagement with warriors boarding disabled ship for recovery.

## Scene Summary
Warriors board the disabled ship to loot arms, ammunition, jewels, and water. They burn the vessel from within before towing it away. Complete destruction of enemy asset.

## Participating Characters
- Narrator (primary observer)
- Sola (appears at end of scene)
- Woola (hound following close)

## Participating Environments
- Building roofs (observation point)
- Upper floor window (view of valley/hills)
- Disabled ship interior/exterior

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Combat aftermath → Looting/recovery → Destruction satisfaction

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Wide shots showing warriors boarding ship
- Medium shots from windows observing looting activity
- Close-ups on narrator watching destruction
- POV shots tracking burning vessel being towed away
- Interior/exterior shots of disabled ship

## Likely Continuity Sensitivities
- Warrior movement patterns during boarding
- Loot items visibility and consistency
- Fire progression on ship
- Narrator's emotional response to destruction
- Disabled ship positioning throughout scene

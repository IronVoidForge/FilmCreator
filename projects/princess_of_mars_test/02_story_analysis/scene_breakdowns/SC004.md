# Scene 004: The Burning

**Scene Purpose:**
Depict the climax of destruction where the disabled ship is set ablaze and launched into the air.

**Scene Summary:**
Warriors tow craft far out into valley; empty contents of carboys onto dead sailors/decks. Last warrior throws missile back onto vessel; faint spurt of flame rises. Guy ropes released; great warship soars majestically into air, decks/upper works a mass of roaring flames. Drifts to southeast, rising higher as flames eat wooden parts.

**Participating Environments:**

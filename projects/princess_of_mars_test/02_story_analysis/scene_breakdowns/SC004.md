# Scene 004: Burning Vessel and Emotional Resolution

## Scene Purpose
Show aftermath ritual (burning vessel) and emotional resolution - Carter's yearning for defeated people and woman prisoner moment with hope/dejection.

## Scene Summary
Crew empties carboys over dead sailors and burns the vessel before it drifts away. Carter feels a strange yearning for the "kindred people" defeated. He sees a human woman prisoner being dragged into a building; she looks at him with hope then dejection as he fails to signal help due to cultural ignorance.

## Participating Characters
- John Carter (primary emotional focus)
- Green Martian Warriors (boarders/looters)
- Airship Crews (distant figures on retreating fleet)
- Human Woman Prisoner (emotional focal point)
- Dead sailors (on drifting ship)

## Participating Environments
- Building interiors (woman dragged into building)
- Rooftops (visible from window)
- Plaza/Street level (burning vessel location)
- Window of upper floor (Carter's observation point)

## Dominant Emotional Shift
Disappointment → Yearning → Dejection as Carter fails to help woman prisoner

## Likely Visual Coverage Families
- Close-ups on Carter's facial expressions showing yearning
- Medium shots of burning vessel with smoke and flame effects
- Two-shot or three-shot including woman prisoner being dragged
- POV shots from Carter watching woman prisoner
- Wide shots showing deserted city context
- Close-ups on woman's face showing hope then dejection

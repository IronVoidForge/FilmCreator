# Scene Index for CH014

## SC001 - Setup & Tars Tarkas Intervention

**Purpose:** Establish the journey preparation, emotional stakes between John Carter and Dejah Thoris, and introduce Tars Tarkas's protective role.

**Summary:** John Carter reflects on his love for Dejah as they prepare to travel from Korad to Thark. Dejah is chained by Sarkoja's order to ensure security during the journey. Tars Tarkas intervenes, taking custody of the key and promising to protect her from Sarkoja's harassment.

**Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sarkoja, Tars Tarkas

**Environments:** Korad outskirts, preparation area for journey

**Emotional Shift:** Romantic reflection → Protective intervention

## SC002 - The Duel with Zad

**Purpose:** Main action sequence during the march, escalating conflict and introducing Dejah's combat intervention.

**Summary:** During the march, John Carter engages in a duel with Zad, a warrior who struck his animal. The fight is interrupted when Dejah Thoris throws a mirror that blinds Zad, allowing him to strike John in the shoulder. Sarkoja attempts to stab Dejah Thoris, but Sola intercepts.

**Characters:** John Carter, Zad, Dejah Thoris, Sarkoja, Sola

**Environments:** March route between Korad and Thark

**Emotional Shift:** Action escalation → Combat intervention → Escalation of threat

## SC003 - Climax & Cliffhanger

**Purpose:** Final confrontation leading to chapter's cliffhanger ending.

**Summary:** The chapter culminates in a fierce sword fight where John Carter is stabbed in the chest and collapses, ending on a cliffhanger of his potential death or unconsciousness.

**Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Sarkoja, Sola

# Scene Index

## SC001 - Recovery and Wound Care
John Carter recovers consciousness after being stabbed by Zad, removes the blade, and tends to his wounds using Martian healing agents provided by Dejah Thoris's women.

## SC002 - Confrontation with Dejah
Carter visits Sola and Dejah, learns Dejah is weeping in grief due to her prideful nature believing him dead, and promises to respect her feelings until she speaks.

## SC003 - Journey and Sola's Story
While traveling with Tars Tarkas in the massive silent caravan across the mossy sea bottom, Carter meets Sola at night who requests he listen to her story about her parents, revealing her father is Tars Tarkas.

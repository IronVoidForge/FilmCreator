# Chapter VII Scene Index

## SC001 - The Incubator Ceremony Setup
The narrator is escorted by Sola to the incubator where young Martians are hatched after five years of incubation. This scene establishes the ceremonial atmosphere and introduces the community's child-raising ritual.

## SC002 - The Gauntlet Release
The gauntlet ceremony releases the newly hatched Martians into the community, with women and children capturing them for communal upbringing. This scene reveals the harsh Martian social system regarding offspring selection and survival.

## SC003 - Training with Sola
After the ceremonial event concludes, the narrator trains with Sola, learning the simple Martian language and developing one-way telepathic communication that provides an advantage over local inhabitants. This scene shifts to training mode and introduces the narrator's unique abilities.

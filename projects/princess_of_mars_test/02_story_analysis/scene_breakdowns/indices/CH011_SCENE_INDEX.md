# Scene Index for CH011

## SC001 - Arranging Quarters and Establishing Rank with Sola
John Carter secures new living quarters for Dejah Thoris and Sola in Korad. He demonstrates his prowess to Sola, establishing himself as eleventh in the Thark community.

## SC002 - Deep Conversation About Origins with Dejah Thoris
Dejah Thoris questions John Carter's origins due to his lack of ornaments and clothing. She initially doubts he is from Earth but eventually believes him after hearing his story about Virginia and Earth, driven by her "earthly, feminine logic."

## SC003 - Discussion of Martian History
John Carter and Dejah Thoris discuss Martian history, including the extinct fair-haired race and the alliance of red, dark, and yellow Martians against the green men.

## SC004 - Receiving Summons from Lorquas Ptomel
The chapter concludes with John Carter receiving a summons from Lorquas Ptomel to appear in the audience chamber.

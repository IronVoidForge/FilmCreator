# Chapter IV Scene Index

## SC001 - Arrival at City
Carter and Tars Tarkas enter the ruined city, observe Martians camped in plaza and buildings. Establishes setting and initial tension.

## SC002 - Audience Chamber & Martian Culture
Martian life exposition (expectancy, death causes, pilgrimage). Carter brought before chieftain for greeting ceremony. Name exchange between Tars Tarkas and Principal Chieftain.

## SC003 - Physical Struggles & Sak Demonstration
Carter attempts to walk but fails, skipping like grasshopper and bruising himself. Fights Martian guard after forced standing, wins applause. Successfully performs the jump ("sak"), clearing 150 feet.

## SC004 - Sustenance & Quarters
Hungry and thirsty, Carter signals for food/water instead of further commands. Taken by young female Sola to spacious chamber with murals/mosaics. Ten-legged creature appears as obedient puppy.

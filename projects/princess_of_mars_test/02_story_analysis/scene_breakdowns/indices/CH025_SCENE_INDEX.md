# SC001 - Infiltration and Ceremony Disruption

John Carter leads fifty Tharks into the impregnable palace of Zodanga to interrupt a state ceremony where Prince Sab Than is to marry Princess Dejah Thoris. The scene establishes the stakes and Carter's determination as he approaches the throne room.

**Purpose:** Setup the infiltration mission and establish emotional stakes
**Summary:** Carter enters Zodanga palace with Thark warriors, approaching the wedding ceremony
**Characters:** John Carter, fifty Tharks, Prince Sab Than (off-screen initially)
**Environments:** Palace exterior, Throne Room approach
**Emotional Shift:** Determination → Urgency
**Visual Coverage Families:** Wide establishing shots, medium group shots, close-up on Carter's face
**Continuity Sensitivities:** Thark warrior count consistency, palace architecture details

# SC002 - The Attack and Killing

Inside the throne room, Carter shatters the window and attacks the assembly, killing Than Kosis and then Sab Than while defending Dejah Thoris from the guards. This is the action climax of the chapter.

**Purpose:** Execute the violent disruption and establish Carter's combat prowess
**Summary:** Window breaks, Carter attacks wedding party, kills Than Kosis and Sab Than
**Characters:** John Carter, Than Kosis, Prince Sab Than, Princess Dejah Thoris, guards
**Environments:** Throne Room interior
**Emotional Shift:** Urgency → Rage → Protective instinct
**Visual Coverage Families:** Dynamic action sequences, close-quarters combat, window shatter effect
**Continuity Sensitivities:** Character death confirmation, weapon consistency, guard count

With Tars Tarkas's help, they clear the hall. Carter secures Kantos Kan from the dungeons and finds him in a dark recess. After searching for keys to release Kantos Kan, they regroup with Dejah Thoris. This scene covers the escape and rescue operations.

**Purpose:** Execute the escape sequence and rescue Kantos Kan
**Summary:** Hall cleared with Tars Tarkas, Kantos Kan rescued from dungeons, key search
**Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Kantos Kan, Dejah Thoris
**Environments:** Throne Room, Dungeons, Dark recess
**Emotional Shift:** Combat adrenaline → Rescue urgency → Relief
**Visual Coverage Families:** Wide escape shots, dungeon exploration, close-up on keys

# SC004 - The Promise and Aftermath

In the throne room, amidst the chaos of war outside, Dejah Thoris promises herself to John Carter in marriage. This scene provides the emotional payoff and resolution of the chapter.

**Purpose:** Deliver the emotional climax and establish new relationship status
**Summary:** Throne room gathering, Dejah Thoris promises herself to Carter
**Characters:** John Carter, Dejah Thoris, Kantos Kan (off-screen), Tars Tarkas (off-screen)
**Environments:** Throne Room interior, palace exterior visible through windows
**Emotional Shift:** Relief → Romantic commitment → Hope
**Visual Coverage Families:** Medium two-shots, close-up on faces, wide establishing with war outside
**Continuity Sensitivities:** War chaos consistency, character positioning, emotional beats

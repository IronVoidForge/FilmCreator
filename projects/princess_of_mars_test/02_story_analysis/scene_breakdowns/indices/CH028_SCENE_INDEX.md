# Scene Index - CH028

## SC001 - Awakening and Initial Discovery
Narrator awakens in Arizona cave after ten years on Mars. Strange garments disintegrate upon rising. Discovers artifacts including matches and green powder inside the cave.

## SC002 - Mummified Woman and Skeletons Revealed
Further exploration of cave interior reveals mummified woman and hanging skeletons. Deepens mystery of narrator's situation.

## SC003 - Exit from Cave and Earth Realization
Narrator exits cave to realize he is back on Earth, not Mars as expected. Location change triggers major realization.

## SC004 - Reflection on Wealth and Longing
Narrator reflects on wealth from old mine but expresses deep longing for Dejah Thoris and fate of Helium. Emotional mode shifts from discovery to contemplation.

## SC005 - Vision of Reunion with Dejah Thoris
Chapter concludes with vision of her waiting in garden on Mars, suggesting reunion. Dramatic payoff scene with emotional climax.

# Scene Index - Chapter VIII / A Fair Captive From The Sky

## SC001 - Enemy Fleet Arrival & Battle Setup
**Purpose:** Establish the escalation of conflict as the enemy air fleet approaches the Green Martian city.
**Summary:** Narrator observes from window as massive gray fleet emerges from hills, triggering immediate retreat orders and opening fire from both sides.
**Characters:** Narrator, Woola (implied presence)
**Environments:** Green Martian City buildings, upper floors, windows, valley/hills approach route
**Emotional Shift:** Tension building, anticipation of conflict

## SC002 - Battle Sequence & Enemy Retreat
**Purpose:** Depict the intense combat and demonstrate Green Martian tactical superiority.
**Summary:** Green Martians fire deadly accurate volleys targeting enemy vessels; fleet responds but is caught off guard; after 20 minutes they retreat damaged.
**Characters:** Narrator, Woola (implied presence)
**Environments:** Martian Plains battle zone, Green Martian City buildings, hills/valley approach
**Emotional Shift:** High action intensity, tactical satisfaction, enemy defeat

## SC003 - Ship Capture & Looting Aftermath
**Purpose:** Show the immediate aftermath of battle and introduce the captured vessel.
**Summary:** One disabled ship is towed to city; warriors loot arms, food, valuables; set vessel ablaze before towing away.
**Characters:** Narrator, Woola (implied presence)
**Environments:** Green Martian City plaza, captured vessel interior/exterior
**Emotional Shift:** Victory satisfaction, resource acquisition, transition to quieter aftermath

## SC004 - Earth Girl Reveal & Emotional Connection
**Purpose:** Establish the emotional core through visual contact between Narrator and captive.
**Summary:** Prisoner dragged into building by Green Martian females; revealed as slender Earth girl with coal-black hair and reddish-copper skin; eye contact signals hope then dejection when Narrator fails to respond.
**Characters:** Narrator, Woola (implied presence), Earth Girl prisoner, Green Martian females
**Environments:** Building interior, window view, plaza entrance
**Emotional Shift:** Hope → Dejection, unspoken connection, narrative hook established

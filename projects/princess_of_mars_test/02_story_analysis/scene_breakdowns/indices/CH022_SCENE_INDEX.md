# Chapter XXII Scene Index

## SC001 - Setup & Witnessing Arrival
John Carter positioned to guard Jeddak Than Kosis during wartime. Witnesses Dejah Thoris's arrival at the palace.

## SC002 - Public Renunciation
Dejah Thoris publicly renounces her previous promise to John Carter in favor of Sab Than, Prince of Zodanga. Heartbroken reaction from John Carter.

## SC003 - Infiltration & Guard Elimination
John Carter infiltrates Dejah Thoris's apartments. Kills four guardsmen blocking his path through action sequence.

## SC004 - Final Confrontation
Dejah Thoris insists her promise is binding despite her love for him, citing Martian customs. Emotional exchange during confrontation.

## SC005 - Escape Through Glass Wall
John Carter realizes danger posed by dead guardsmen. Escapes palace through glass wall into the night.

## SC006 - Aftermath & Search Order
Jeddak's psychologist confirms encounter was single superhuman opponent. Than Kosis orders search for mysterious stranger.

# Scene Index: On the Arizona Hills (CH001)

  - *Focus*: Internal reflection on mortality and establishing the historical context of the 1865 gold expedition.

  - *Focus*: Escalation from guarding duty to violent conflict, leading to the discovery of Powell's death.

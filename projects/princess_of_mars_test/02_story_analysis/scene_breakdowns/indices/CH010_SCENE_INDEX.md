# Scene Index for CH010 - Champion and Chief

## SC001 - Exploration Outside City Limits
**Purpose:** Establish John Carter's relationship with Woola and the Mars cultural misunderstanding.  
**Summary:** Early morning exploration outside city limits where Carter tests Woola's loyalty, experiences a moment of laughter that signifies torture on Mars.  
**Characters:** John Carter, Woola  
**Environments:** Outside city limits, Martian landscape  
**Emotional Shift:** Affection → Realization of mistake  

## SC002 - Return to Plaza and Observation
**Purpose:** Transition from exploration back to the main plaza setting.  
**Summary:** Carter returns to the plaza area where he observes Dejah Thoris before the interrogation begins.  
**Characters:** John Carter, Woola, Dejah Thoris (observed)  
**Environments:** Plaza entrance, observation point  
**Emotional Shift:** Anticipation → Tension  

## SC003 - Interrogation Scene
**Purpose:** Main confrontation with Thark leadership and Sarkoja's cruelty.  
**Summary:** Lorquas Ptomel and Thark chieftains interrogate Dejah Thoris; she explains her peaceful scientific mission from Helium and pleads for amity. Sarkoja displays cruelty toward the prisoner.  
**Characters:** John Carter, Woola, Dejah Thoris, Lorquas Ptomel, Thark chieftains, Sarkoja  
**Environments:** Audience chamber/interrogation room  
**Emotional Shift:** Tension → Cruelty  

## SC004 - Action Climax
**Purpose:** John Carter's intervention and killing of the young warrior.  
**Summary:** A young warrior strikes Dejah Thoris across the face; John Carter intervenes in his rage, killing the brute. He gains status as a Tharkian chieftain through this act.  
**Characters:** John Carter, Woola, Young warrior (deceased)  
**Environments:** Audience chamber/interrogation room  
**Emotional Shift:** Rage → Consequence  

## SC005 - Aftermath and Resolution
**Purpose:** Status elevation and departure with Dejah Thoris.  
**Summary:** John Carter explains himself to Dejah Thoris, revealing his name and origin (Virginia, Earth). Tars Tarkas informs him of his new status as conqueror and warns of responsibilities. Chapter concludes with John Carter and Dejah Thoris leaving together accompanied by Woola.  
**Characters:** John Carter, Woola, Dejah Thoris, Tars Tarkas  
**Environments:** Audience chamber/exit path  
**Emotional Shift:** Resolution → Bond established

# Chapter 18 Scene Index

## SC001 - Awakening and Revelation
**Location:** Small room with sleeping silks and furs  
**Purpose:** Narrator regains consciousness, Dak Kova reveals his plans to use narrator as sport for the great games  
**Characters:** Narrator, Dak Kova, Ancient Female Warrior  
**Emotional Shift:** Confusion → Horror → Realization of captivity  

## SC002 - The Pursuit
**Location:** Wild thoat chase toward Warhoon camp  
**Purpose:** Narrator is strapped to wild thoat and pursued after the main column  
**Characters:** Narrator, Dak Kova (implied), Ancient Female Warrior  
**Emotional Shift:** Desperation → Fear → Determination  

## SC003 - Confrontation at Camp
**Location:** Warhoon camp where main column makes night stop  
**Purpose:** Narrator taken before Bar Comas, Dak Kova displays insubordination leading to brutal combat  
**Characters:** Narrator, Bar Comas, Dak Kova  
**Emotional Shift:** Tension → Violence → Shock  

## SC004 - Power Transfer and Imprisonment
**Location:** Warhoon camp transitioning to dark dungeon  
**Purpose:** Bar Comas killed by Dak Kova, expedition abandoned, narrator imprisoned in dark dungeon  
**Characters:** Narrator, Dak Kova (now Jeddak), Bar Comas (dead)  
**Emotional Shift:** Horror → Powerlessness → Darkness  

## SC005 - Escape from Dungeon
**Location:** Dark dungeon in city of Warhoon  
**Purpose:** Narrator kills jailer with chain, retrieves keys, gains freedom  
**Characters:** Narrator, Jailer (dead), Six pairs of eyes (watchers)  
**Emotional Shift:** Desperation → Cunning triumph → Freedom

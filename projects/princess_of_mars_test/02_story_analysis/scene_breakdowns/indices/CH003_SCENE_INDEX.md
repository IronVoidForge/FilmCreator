SC001 - The Narrator Wakes and Discovers Low Gravity
- Purpose: Establish narrator's disorientation and Mars environment
- Summary: Narrator awakens in circular basin, stands up and jumps three yards due to low gravity. Attempts to walk but hops and sprawls from unfamiliar muscle dynamics.
- Characters: Narrator (first-person)
- Environments: Circular glass incubation basin on Mars surface
- Emotional Shift: Confusion → Physical realization of alien environment
- Visual Coverage: Wide establishing shots, medium action shots of jumping/hopping
- Continuity Sensitivities: Gravity effects must be consistent; narrator's physical reactions to low gravity

SC002 - Discovery of Glass Incubator with Hatching Eggs
- Purpose: Introduce Martians and their reproductive cycle
- Summary: Narrator discovers glass incubator containing giant eggs. Observes grotesque young Martians hatching from snowy white eggs (2.5 feet diameter).
- Characters: Narrator, Young Martians (hatching)
- Environments: Glass incubation chamber within Mars basin
- Emotional Shift: Curiosity → Awe at alien life forms
- Visual Coverage: Close-up egg shots, medium shots of hatching process
- Continuity Sensitivities: Egg appearance and hatching process must be consistent; young Martian physical traits (15 feet tall, dark slate skin, yellow feet, cup-shaped antennae)

SC003 - Adult Martians Approach and Nearly Strike Narrator
- Purpose: Create conflict and tension with Martians
- Summary: Group of adult Martians approaches. Leader's spear nearly strikes narrator but sound alerts the leader. Martians are 15 feet tall with dark slate skin, blood red iris, white teeth with upward-curving tusks.
- Characters: Narrator, Adult Martians (group), Martian Leader
- Environments: Mars basin surface near incubation chamber
- Emotional Shift: Tension → Immediate threat awareness
- Visual Coverage: Low angle shots of approaching Martians, medium shots of leader's spear movement
- Continuity Sensitivities: Martian physical traits must be consistent; weapon appearance (40-foot metal-tipped spears)

SC004 - Narrator Leaps Away and Martians React with Surprise
- Purpose: Action consequence showing narrator's agility
- Summary: Narrator leaps thirty feet into air to escape, landing safely on opposite side. Martians surprised by this unexpected agility. Leader dismounts unarmed.
- Characters: Narrator, Adult Martians (group), Martian Leader
- Environments: Mars basin surface
- Emotional Shift: Escape success → Martian surprise at narrator's capabilities
- Visual Coverage: Wide action shots of leap, medium reaction shots of Martians
- Continuity Sensitivities: Leap distance and landing must be consistent; Martians' reactions to narrator's agility

SC005 - Peace Token Offered and Cavalcade Departs
- Purpose: Resolution showing Martian peace gesture
- Summary: Leader offers metal armlet as peace token. Narrator accepts it. They walk together before leader mounts his animal (ten feet high at shoulder, four legs, broad flat tail). Entire cavalcade departs towards hills.
- Characters: Narrator, Martian Leader, Martians on mounts
- Environments: Mars basin surface near departure point
- Emotional Shift: Acceptance → Departure with new relationship established
- Visual Coverage: Medium shots of armlet exchange, wide shots of cavalcade departing
- Continuity Sensitivities: Mount appearance (ten feet high at shoulder, four legs, broad flat tail, no hair, smooth glossy) must be consistent; armlet design and narrator's acceptance gesture

# Scene Index - Chapter XXIV

## SC001 - Crash Landing and Rescue of Tars Tarkas
**Location:** Ancient Mars battlefield (Tharks vs Warhoons)  
**Purpose:** John Carter crashes his craft into fierce battle, saves Tars Tarkas from death by Warhoons  
**Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Woola, Thark warriors, Warhoon warriors  
**Emotional Shift:** Survival → Respect earned

## SC002 - Council Meeting and Confrontation with Tal Hajus
**Location:** Thark council chamber  
**Purpose:** Tars Tarkas brings John Carter before Jeddak Tal Hajus; council meeting where John exposes Tal as coward  
**Characters:** John Carter, Tars Tarkas, Jeddak Tal Hajus, Thark council members  
**Emotional Shift:** Respect → Confrontation → Truth revealed

## SC003 - Combat Challenge and Victory
**Location:** Thark arena/battlefield  
**Purpose:** Thark council votes to challenge Tal Hajus to combat with Tars Tarkas; Tars Tarkas wins the fight  
**Characters:** Tars Tarkas, Jeddak Tal Hajus, Thark warriors  
**Emotional Shift:** Challenge → Victory → Transformation

## SC004 - New Jeddak and Alliance Proposal
**Location:** Thark council chamber  
**Purpose:** Tars Tarkas becomes new Jeddak of Thark; made chieftain and proposes alliance with Helium to rescue Dejah Thoris  
**Characters:** Tars Tarkas (new Jeddak), John Carter, Thark council members  
**Emotional Shift:** Transformation → Purpose → Agreement

## SC005 - March on Zodanga
**Location:** Journey from Mars to Zodanga  
**Purpose:** Tharks gather 150,000 warriors; march on Zodanga and enter city through gate constructed by John Carter  
**Characters:** Tars Tarkas (Jeddak), John Carter, Woola, 150,000 Thark warriors  
**Emotional Shift:** Agreement → Action → Victory

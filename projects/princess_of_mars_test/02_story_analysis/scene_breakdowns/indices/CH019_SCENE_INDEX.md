# Scene Index

## SC001 - Key Discovery & Reveal
John Carter discovers keys are gone, learns Dejah Thoris and Sola have been taken by Tharks for consumption.

## SC002 - Meeting Kantos Kan
Carter meets fellow prisoner Kantos Kan (Helium Navy Lieutenant), who shares details about failed expedition and ongoing search.

## SC003 - Amphitheater Drag & Arena Setup
Both prisoners dragged to Thark amphitheater, arena games begin with Carter fighting through beasts and warriors.

## SC004 - Action Escalation
Carter continues fighting through various beasts and warriors in the arena, building toward final confrontation.

## SC005 - Final Match & Escape Collusion
Carter faces Kantos Kan in final match; they collude to escape with Carter pretending to die.

## SC006 - Aftermath & Hill Escape
Carter escapes into hills east of city while Kantos Kan leaves him there, concluding the chapter.

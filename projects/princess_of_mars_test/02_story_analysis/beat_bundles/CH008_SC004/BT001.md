# BT001 - Missile Launch Sequence

## Beat Purpose
Warriors prepare and launch final missiles at vessel to conclude engagement.

## Start State
- Warriors positioned on 3 building roof levels
- Missiles loaded and ready for deployment
- Vessel visible in valley floor distance
- Smoke beginning to rise from previous impacts

## End State
- Missiles in flight toward target
- Warriors watching trajectory with binoculars or direct sight
- Launch sequence complete, warriors holding position

## Character Placement and Movement Logic
- Green Warriors distributed across roof levels (approximately 4 per level)
- Carter positioned below on plaza edge observing entire sequence
- Warriors maintain static positions during launch for stability
- No character movement until post-launch observation phase

## Geography, Axis, or Eyeline Facts
- Vertical axis from roofs down to valley floor
- Eyelines directed downward at vessel position
- Line of sight unobstructed by terrain features
- Camera positioned at plaza level looking up and across

## Prop, Vehicle, Crowd, and Environmental State
- Missiles: 2 per warrior, visible in hands or on shoulders
- Vessel: Burning with visible damage from previous hits
- Smoke: Rising steadily from valley floor
- Loot: Some warriors carrying small items from vessel
- Roof surfaces: Intact, no debris present

## Likely Coverage Families
- Medium shot of warrior preparing missile (close-up)
- Wide shot of multiple warriors on roofs (establishing)
- Extreme wide shot of missiles in flight toward vessel (action)
- Over-the-shoulder shot from Carter's perspective (observer)

# BT001 - Ship Extraction
## Beat Purpose
Establish the prisoner's removal from ship and introduce her physical state to audience.
## Start State
- Prisoner figure inside ship interior, small and vulnerable
- Green Warriors approaching with intent
- Ship lighting dim, industrial atmosphere
## End State
- Prisoner being dragged outside ship
- Exterior ship visible, portal open
- Warriors maintaining grip on prisoner
## Character Placement and Movement Logic
- Warriors move from ship interior toward exit
- Prisoner dragged backward against will
- Camera follows movement trajectory
## Geography, Axis, or Eyeline Facts
- Ship interior to exterior transition
- Vertical axis: prisoner low, warriors standing
- Eyeline: prisoner faces forward, warriors look down
## Prop, Vehicle, Crowd, and Environmental State
- Ship hull visible as environmental boundary
- No props in immediate vicinity
- Crowd: Warriors only (2-3 figures)
- Environmental state: dim lighting, industrial texture
## Likely Coverage Families
- Close-up of prisoner figure (slender girlish form)
- Wide ship exterior showing portal
- Medium shot warriors dragging action

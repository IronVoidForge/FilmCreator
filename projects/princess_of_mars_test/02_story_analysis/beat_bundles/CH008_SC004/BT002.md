# BT002 - Vessel Burning and Drifting

## Beat Purpose
Show aftermath of missile impact and vessel's retreat from engagement zone.

## Start State
- Vessel struck by final missile
- Flames erupting from hull damage
- Smoke plume expanding rapidly
- Vessel beginning to drift away from valley center

## End State
- Vessel drifting toward valley edge
- Roaring flames continuing but diminishing
- Smoke trail visible behind vessel
- Warriors watching from distance with concern

## Character Placement and Movement Logic
- Green Warriors on roofs maintaining observation positions
- Carter below observing from plaza edge, slight forward lean
- No character movement during this beat (observation phase)
- Warriors may shift weight to get better view of drifting vessel

## Geography, Axis, or Eyeline Facts
- Horizontal axis following vessel's drift path across valley
- Eyelines directed at vessel's position and smoke trail
- Camera positioned to capture full drift trajectory
- Vessel moves from center toward right side of frame

## Prop, Vehicle, Crowd, and Environmental State
- Vessel: Hull lightened by loot removal visible
- Flames: Roaring with orange/yellow coloration
- Smoke: Dark gray plume rising above vessel
- Loot: Small items visible on some warrior shoulders
- Valley floor: No debris from previous impacts

## Likely Coverage Families

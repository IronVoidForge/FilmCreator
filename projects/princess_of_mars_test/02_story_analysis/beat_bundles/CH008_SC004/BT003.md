# BT003 - Warriors Return to Plaza

## Beat Purpose
Warriors regroup after battle conclusion and prepare for next phase.

## Start State
- Warriors scattered across roofs and valley floor
- Some warriors carrying loot from vessel
- Smoke beginning to clear from immediate area
- Flames diminishing but still visible in distance

## End State
- Warriors gathered at plaza entrance
- Loot consolidated or being carried toward plaza
- Smoke clearing significantly
- Battle aftermath acknowledged by group

## Character Placement and Movement Logic
- Warriors moving from multiple points toward plaza center
- Converging paths from roofs, valley floor, and building edges
- Carter remains stationary as observer throughout beat
- Warriors may carry loot in hands or on shoulders during movement

## Geography, Axis, or Eyeline Facts
- Converging paths from various locations to plaza center
- Vertical axis from roofs down to plaza level
- Horizontal axis following warrior movement toward gathering point
- Camera positioned at plaza entrance looking outward

## Prop, Vehicle, Crowd, and Environmental State
- Loot: Visible on some warriors (small items, containers)
- Smoke: Clearing but still present in valley air
- Flames: Diminished to background elements
- Plaza surface: Clean, no debris from battle
- Warriors: Some carrying weapons, others with empty hands

## Likely Coverage Families
- Medium shot of warriors walking toward plaza (movement)
- Wide shot of group gathering at plaza entrance (establishing)
- Close-up of warrior carrying loot (detail)
- Over-the-shoulder shot from Carter's perspective (observer)

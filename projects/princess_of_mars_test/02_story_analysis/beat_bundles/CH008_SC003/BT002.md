# BT002 - Boarding & Looting Operations

## Beat Purpose
Document systematic search and removal of valuable items from vessel interior.

## Start State
Warriors aboard ship, initial sweep complete, Martians entering main deck.

## End State
All valuables removed (arms, silks, jewels, food/water), carboys emptied over dead sailors.

## Character Placement & Movement Logic
Martians move through ship compartments systematically. Warriors secure perimeter while looting occurs. Dead sailors remain stationary in designated areas.

## Geography, Axis, or Eyeline Facts
Ship interior layout: main deck with cargo holds. Martians work from aft to forward sections. Carboy dumping point: near dead sailor clusters.

## Prop, Vehicle, Crowd, Environmental State
Specific items tracked for continuity: arms (swords/shields), silks (robes/fabrics), jewels (necklaces/ornaments), food containers, water carboys. Ship interior shows signs of battle damage.

## Likely Coverage Families
Over-the-shoulder shots of looting actions, close-ups of item removal, wide shots showing multiple Martians working simultaneously.

# BT001 - Ship Drifting & Warriors Intercepting

## Beat Purpose
Establish the disabled vessel's trajectory and warrior response to prevent collision with buildings.

## Start State
Ship drifting southeast, unmanned, visible from distance south of position.

## End State
Warriors reach ship perimeter, grappling hooks deployed, boarding initiated.

## Character Placement & Movement Logic
Green Warriors move from north/south positions toward ship's drift path. Martians observe from elevated positions before engaging.

## Geography, Axis, or Eyeline Facts
Ship axis: southeast drift vector. Building collision point: south of position. Warrior eyelines track ship movement across horizon.

## Prop, Vehicle, Crowd, Environmental State
Drifting ship hull intact but disabled. No crew visible. Wind direction affects smoke/ash later. Buildings in background provide scale reference.

## Likely Coverage Families
Wide tracking shot following ship drift, medium shots of warrior approach, close-ups of grappling hook deployment.

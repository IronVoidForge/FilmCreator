**BT003 - Cultural Response Failure**

Beat Purpose: Show Narrator's failure to respond due to ignorance of customs.

Start State: Captive signals for help with hand gestures, Martians acknowledge but continue dragging.

End State: Martians drag captive into building, Narrator remains silent and unmoving.

Character Placement and Movement Logic: Martians maintain forward motion toward building interior. Captive follows their lead without resistance. Narrator fixed in position at plaza edge, no movement toward action or verbal response. All character movement is away from camera except for Narrator's static observation.

Geography, Axis, or Eyeline Facts: POV shots from Narrator's perspective showing lack of response. Camera positioned to capture both Narrator's reaction and building entrance simultaneously. Axis maintains 45-degree angle established in previous beats for continuity.

Prop, Vehicle, Crowd, and Environmental State: Captive's disappearance into shadowed building interior marks beat conclusion. No props present. Crowd consists of 2-3 Green Martian females actively moving captive away. Environmental state: Building entrance shadow deepens as captive enters, creating visual closure for sequence.

Likely Coverage Families: Over-the-shoulder shots from Narrator's perspective showing lack of participation, reaction close-ups on Narrator's face, wide shots capturing group departure into building interior.

BT001 - Fleet Arrival
---
**Beat Purpose:** Establish scale and arrival of fleet to city perimeter. Create anticipation for combat engagement.

**Start State:** Valley floor empty, hills visible beyond, twenty gray airships descending from hills in formation. Banners fully unfurled on each ship hull.

**End State:** Fleet positioned at city perimeter, valley floor now showing ships approaching buildings. Formation intact but ready to engage.

**Character Placement and Movement Logic:** Fleet crew inside ships maintaining formation. Ships descend diagonally from hills toward valley floor. Movement synchronized across all twenty vessels.

**Geography, Axis, or Eyeline Facts:** Valley floor serves as primary eyeline reference point. Hills beyond provide depth axis. Ships maintain horizontal alignment across frame. Camera positioned at valley level looking up toward approaching fleet.

**Prop, Vehicle, Crowd, and Environmental State:** Twenty gray airships (primary vehicle). Strange banners on each hull. Deserted city buildings in background with upper floors visible. Valley floor empty except for ship shadows. No crowd present yet.

**Likely Coverage Families:** Wide establishing shots of fleet arrival, Medium shots showing individual ships in formation, Aerial perspective from above valley floor, Low angle shots from valley looking up at descending fleet.

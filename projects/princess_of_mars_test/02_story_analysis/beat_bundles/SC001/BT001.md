# Beat 001 - Return Procession to City Plaza

## Beat Purpose
Establish the ceremonial return from incubation ceremony and set up the window observation point for fleet discovery.

## Start State
- Carter and Sola walking in procession through city plaza
- Green Martians visible melting into buildings along route
- Valley below visible from plaza level
- No enemy fleet yet detected

## End State
- Carter and Sola arrive at upper floor window of stone building
- Both pause to look out toward valley and hills
- Fleet still not visible, calm atmosphere maintained

## Character Placement and Movement Logic
- Carter walks slightly ahead of Sola (human lead position)
- Green Martians scatter into buildings as they approach plaza
- Movement flows from ceremony site → city plaza → upper window
- No sudden stops until reaching observation point

## Geography, Axis, or Eyeline Facts
- Plaza is open stone ground with 360° visibility
- Upper window faces valley and hills (primary eyeline direction)
- Building has two levels: street level and observation level
- Window frame creates natural framing for fleet shots

## Prop, Vehicle, Crowd, and Environmental State
- Procession includes ceremonial banners (visible in wide shots)
- Green Martians' melting effect affects crowd continuity
- Stone buildings provide texture for establishing shots
- Valley below remains clear of enemy presence at start

## Likely Coverage Families
- Wide: Full procession through plaza with valley background
- Medium: Carter and Sola walking together, banners visible
- Over-the-shoulder: Window view toward valley (pre-fleet)
- Close-up: Carter's calm expression before discovery
- Establishing: City buildings with Green Martians melting in

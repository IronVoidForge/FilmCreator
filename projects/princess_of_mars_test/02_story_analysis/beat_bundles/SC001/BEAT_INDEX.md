# Beat Index for SC001

## BT001 - Window Observation
**Purpose**: Establish narrator's position and initial observation of fleet retreat from safe distance
**Start State**: Narrator at window, ships just visible on horizon
**End State**: Full view of twenty ships over hill crests established
**Character Placement**: Narrator static at upper floor, Green Warriors in distant fleet formation
**Geography/Axis/Eyeline**: Window frame establishes interior boundary; eyeline follows ship movement across horizontal axis
**Continuity Elements**: Ship count (20), window framing, interior lighting level
**Coverage Families**: Medium shot from window, wide establishing exterior

## BT002 - Fleet Movement
**Purpose**: Show scale of retreat and synchronized fleet movement across terrain
**Start State**: Ships positioned at base of first hill crest
**End State**: Fleet distributed across multiple hill crests in formation
**Character Placement**: Narrator observing from fixed position, Green Warriors moving in coordinated pattern
**Geography/Axis/Eyeline**: Hill crests create depth axis; ships move along horizontal plane with slight elevation changes
**Continuity Elements**: Ship positions relative to crests, banner visibility, movement timing
**Coverage Families**: Wide exterior shots, over-the-shoulder from narrator

## BT003 - Detail Focus
**Purpose**: Emphasize mysterious elements on ship prows and banner designs
**Start State**: Fleet in wide formation visible
**End State**: Close examination of banners and devices on individual ships
**Character Placement**: Narrator's eyeline shifts to specific ships, Green Warriors static or minimal movement
**Geography/Axis/Eyeline**: Eyeline zooms from wide to close; focus axis moves from fleet-wide to individual ship prows
**Continuity Elements**: Banner designs, device shapes, ship prow details, lighting consistency
**Coverage Families**: Close-up shots, detail inserts, over-the-shoulder with focus pull

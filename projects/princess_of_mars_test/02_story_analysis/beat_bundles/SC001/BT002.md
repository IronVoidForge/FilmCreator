# Beat BT002 - Interior Corridor Transition

## Beat Purpose
Show the transition from exterior ceremony space to interior building corridors.

## Start State
Narrator at building entrance, doorway visible, interior depth beginning to reveal itself.

## End State
Narrator fully inside corridor, portal/depths of building becoming apparent.

## Character Placement & Movement Logic
Narrator moves through doorway into corridor, Woola follows, movement slows as tension increases.

## Geography/Axis/Eyeline Facts
Horizontal axis along corridor length, eyeline toward interior depths, vertical transition from entrance to corridor level.

## Prop/Vehicle/Crowd/Environmental State
Corridor walls visible, no crowd in immediate area, lighting dimmer than exterior, portal visible at far end.

## Likely Coverage Families
Medium tracking shots through corridors, close-ups on narrator's expression, interior depth shots showing portal.

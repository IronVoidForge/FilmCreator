# Beat 002 - Window Discovery of Enemy Fleet

## Beat Purpose
Reveal the approaching enemy fleet and establish the emotional shift from calm to alert/tense.

## Start State
- Carter and Sola at upper window looking out
- Valley below visible, no enemy presence yet
- Calm atmosphere maintained
- Green Martians still melting into buildings nearby

## End State
- 20 airships clearly visible approaching over hills
- Carter's expression shifts to alert/tense
- Sola mirrors Carter's concern
- Fleet movement creates urgency in scene

## Character Placement and Movement Logic
- Both characters remain at window (no retreat yet)
- Carter steps closer to glass for better view
- Sola stands slightly behind, observing Carter's reaction
- No sudden movement until fleet becomes threatening

## Geography, Axis, or Eyeline Facts
- Window eyeline directed toward hills and valley
- Fleet appears on horizon line, moving downward toward city
- Hills provide depth for establishing shots of approaching ships
- Valley below shows scale of enemy force (20 airships)

## Prop, Vehicle, Crowd, and Environmental State
- 20 airships visible in wide shots (continuity requirement)
- Fleet movement creates sound/visual continuity markers
- Green Martians' melting behavior continues during discovery
- Stone window frame provides framing for fleet shots
- Valley below shows no defensive positions yet

## Likely Coverage Families
- Wide: Fleet approaching over hills with valley background
- Medium: Carter and Sola at window, fleet visible in distance
- Over-the-shoulder: Window view showing fleet movement
- Close-up: Carter's reaction to fleet discovery
- Establishing: Full fleet count (20 airships) from hillside

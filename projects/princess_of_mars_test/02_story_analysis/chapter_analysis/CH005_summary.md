**Chapter 5 Summary:**
**Title:** CHAPTER V - I ELUDE MY WATCH DOG
**Key Characters:** The Protagonist ("I"), Sola (Martian female guardian), Martian Watch Dog, Giant Ape-like Martian Man.
**Setting:** Captive room within a Martian city/valley overlooking the landscape; Martian streets and sky.

**Events:**
1.  **Observation of the Watch Dog:** The protagonist observes Sola leaving with the "brute" (Watch Dog). The beast guards the protagonist intently, surveying him before lying down across the threshold to guard the exit.
2.  **Room Examination:** While alone, the protagonist examines the room's mural paintings. They depict beautiful earthly-like scenes (mountains, rivers, gardens) but notably lack any representation of living animals or humans, suggesting these Martians are extinct denizens or distinct from Earth life.
3.  **Sola's Care:** Sola returns with food and drink. The food is a tasteless cheese-like substance; the liquid is milk derived from a large plant (not an animal) that distills moisture from soil and air. A single plant yields eight to ten quarts of milk daily.
4.  **Martian Environment:** The protagonist notes the extreme cold of Martian nights, sudden temperature changes, and the illumination provided by Mars' two moons (one completing a revolution in ~7.5 hours). He observes the other occupants are five females sleeping on silks and furs.
5.  **Testing the Dog:** Feeling adventurous, the protagonist decides to test the Watch Dog's loyalty. He leaves the room briefly. The dog follows him but allows him to pass until they reach the city edge.
6.  **The Escape Attempt:** At the city edge, the dog springs and barks. The protagonist jumps over the dog and runs back toward the city. The dog pursues with incredible speed (described as the fleetest animal on Mars).
7.  **Capture:** The protagonist attempts to jump into a window of an overlooking building but is dragged back inside by a huge hand before he can secure himself.
8.  **The Giant Martian:** Upon being thrown onto his back, the protagonist sees a colossal ape-like creature standing over him. It is white and hairless except for bristly hair on its head.

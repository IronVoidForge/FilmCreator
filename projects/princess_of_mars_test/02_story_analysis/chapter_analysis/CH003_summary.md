The protagonist awakens on a Martian landscape characterized by yellowish moss, quartz rocks, and low hills. Realizing they are on Mars without doubt, they test their physical capabilities in the reduced gravity, resulting in hopping movements that differ from Earth norms. They discover an egg incubator containing large, strange creatures with six limbs, distinctive antennae-like ears, and formidable tusks. A party of full-grown Martians approaches; the protagonist narrowly escapes a spear thrust using agility. Impressed by this feat, the Martian leader captures the protagonist to exhibit them among their fellows. The chapter concludes with the protagonist being taken on a mount toward the hills after exchanging an armlet as a token of peace.

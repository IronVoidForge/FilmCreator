In this chapter, John Carter visits Kantos Kan at the Zodanga palace to discuss the political crisis regarding Dejah Thoris's engagement to Sab Than. Kantos Kan pledges his support to free Dejah Thoris from this union. Carter climbs the barracks roof using a strap hook and neutralizes a sentry to retrieve their air machines. They take off together toward Helium but are pursued by Zodanga air scouts and a cruiser. Carter is shot, causing his machine to fall and damaging his compass and speedometer. Despite flying for nearly six hours without guidance, he fails to locate Helium, which is distinguished by its twin circular cities and two immense colored towers (scarlet and yellow). He continues searching in the wrong direction.

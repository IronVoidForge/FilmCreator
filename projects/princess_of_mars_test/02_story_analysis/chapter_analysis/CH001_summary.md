## Story Summary
After an incubator ceremony, the procession returns to the city but retreats indoors upon seeing a fleet of gray airships approaching. Green Martian warriors fire a volley at the ships, which return fire with high accuracy targeting crew points and equipment. Whether the fleet had discovered them or simply observed the deserted city remains uncertain. One ship is disabled and drifts toward the city. Warriors board the vessel, loot it, empty carboys onto the dead sailors, and set it ablaze before towing it away. Carter feels a strange yearning for the defeated fleet. Upon returning to the plaza, he sees a human-like female prisoner being dragged into a building by green Martian females. She looks at him with hope then dejection when he fails to signal aid.

## Visual Continuity Facts
- **Narrator:** John Carter (referred to as "I").
- **Companion:** Sola, rushes up to the narrator upon return.
- **Animal:** Woola, a hound following at the heel.
- **Martian Warriors:** Green-skinned, mounted on chariots or mastodons, wearing ornaments.
- **Airships:** Long, low, gray-painted vessels with strange banners and odd devices on prows.
- **Prisoner:** Slender, girlish figure, oval face, coal black waving hair, light reddish copper skin, naked except for highly wrought ornaments.
- **Setting:** Deserted city buildings, open ground/plaza, valley, hills beyond.

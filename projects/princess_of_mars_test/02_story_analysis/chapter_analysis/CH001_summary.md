## Broad Story Summary

The narrator ("I") and Sola retreat toward home after an incubator ceremony but are ordered to return immediately due to the arrival of enemy airships. A fleet of twenty gray vessels attacks the city, engaging in a brief exchange of fire where Martians demonstrate deadly accuracy against the ships' crews and equipment. One vessel is disabled and drifting low over the ground. Green Martian warriors board the ship, loot its contents (arms, jewels, water), and burn it to prevent further use. Among the dead crew, they discover a human female prisoner who is dragged into a building by females. The narrator observes her from a window as she is taken away, feeling regret for not responding to her silent appeal for help.

## Visual Continuity Facts

- **Martian Movement:** Green Martians melt like mist into spacious doorways of nearby buildings within three minutes.
- **Airships:** Twenty long, low, gray-painted vessels swing slowly over the crest of hills, carrying strange banners and odd devices on their prows.
- **Battle Effects:** Banners and upper works dissolve in spurts of flame; bullets drop figures at explosions; fire rises from points where missiles strike.
- **Captive Appearance:** The prisoner is a slender, girlish figure with an oval face, coal black waving hair caught loosely into a coiffure, light reddish copper skin, and ruby lips. She is destitute of clothes save for highly wrought ornaments.
- **Companion:** Woola the hound follows close at the narrator's heel in his accustomed place.

The narrator is a captive in a room within a Martian city, guarded by two distinct entities: Sola, a female Martian exhibiting sympathy and kindness, and a "watch dog" brute that acts as a loyal protector. The environment features cold nights illuminated by Mars' two moons, a thin atmosphere, and rooms adorned with silks, furs, and mural paintings depicting landscapes but no living animals. Food consists of tasteless solid substance and milk derived from a large plant rather than an animal. The narrator possesses adventurous traits and physical agility, specifically noted as a skilled jumper.

This project follows John Carter, an Earthman transported to Barsoom (Mars), as he integrates into Thark society while protecting Dejah Thoris from hostile factions like the Sarkoja. The narrative explores cultural clashes between Earth customs and Martian traditions, the political hierarchy of the Tharks (chieftains, Tars Tarkas, Lorquas Ptomel), and the deepening bond between John and Dejah. Key themes include identity, loyalty, and the mystery of John's origin relative to Barsoomian viewing technology. The story is set against a backdrop of ancient Martian history, including the three great races (fair-haired, red, dark/black) and the drying of the seas forcing alliances.

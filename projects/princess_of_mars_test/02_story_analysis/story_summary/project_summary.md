# Project Title
A Princess of Mars (Chapter VIII)

# Setting
Barsoom (Mars), specifically a green Martian city characterized by chariots, mastodons, and mounted warriors. The sky is dominated by large gray airships/craft with banners and upper works.

# Key Characters
- **Narrator:** A human from Earth (implied by references to "earthly women of my past life"), currently living among green Martians.
- **Sola:** A Martian warrior/companion who accompanies the narrator.
- **Woola:** The hound, accustomed to following the narrator.
- **Green Martians:** Warriors inhabiting the city, utilizing buildings for defense and firing positions.
- **Air Fleet Crews:** Personnel on the attacking vessels, described as strange creatures or humans in conflict with green Martians.

# Core Plot
The narrative follows the narrator's life within a Martian city under threat of aerial bombardment. The central event involves the destruction of an enemy air fleet by green Martian warriors and the subsequent capture of a prisoner who turns out to be a human woman. The story emphasizes themes of war, strange creatures, and the awe-inspiring scale of Martian technology and warfare.

# Visual Continuity
- **Green Martians:** Trained for evolution, melting like mist into doorways upon retreat orders.
- **Airships:** Long, low, gray-painted vessels swinging slowly over hills with banners and odd devices on prows.
- **Battle:** Green warriors firing from building windows; airships returning fire with guns.
- **Disabled Ship:** Drifting southeast, limping, unmanned, eventually captured and burned.
- **Prisoner:** Slender, girlish figure, naked save for ornaments, coal black waving hair, light reddish copper skin.

# Tone
Warlike, awe-inspiring, strange, ferocious, and emotionally charged (depression over defeat of kindred people).

This project follows John Carter's captivity among the Warhoon hordes of Barsoom. The narrative centers on the arena games where prisoners are pitted against beasts and each other for survival. Key factions include the Tharks (Warhoons), Helium, Zodanga, and various Martian communities. Dejah Thoris remains a central figure in the backstory, with her safety being a driving concern for Carter and his allies like Kantos Kan. The setting involves ancient ruins, underground amphitheaters, and the harsh environment of Barsoom.

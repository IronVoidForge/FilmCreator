The story follows John Carter, a man from Earth (Virginia, USA) who has been transported to the planet Mars (Barsoom). Due to his unique physiology, he possesses superhuman strength and agility on Mars. He finds himself caught in the complex social and political webs of the various Martian races, including the green Tharks and the red Heliumites. Carter navigates these cultures by leveraging his combat prowess and a growing understanding of Martian customs, often acting as a protector for Dejah Thoris, the Princess of Helium. He is accompanied by Woola, a great white ape-like creature, and operates under the loose supervision of Sola, a kind Martian woman who represents a rare atavism of emotion in her race.

# Project Summary: Barsoom Adaptation

## Narrative Scope
This adaptation follows John Carter, a Martian adventurer from Helium, navigating the political and physical conflicts of Barsoom. The story centers on his rescue missions involving Dejah Thoris and Sola against local factions like the Tharks.

## Visual Continuity Context
- **Setting:** Distinct Barsoomian landscapes including mossy wastes, hills, and city courtyards (Thark).
- **Attire & Gear:** Characters wear distinct garments; Carter utilizes a Martian rifle, sword, and revolvers. Dejah Thoris wears specific dress indicative of her status.
- **Transport:** Thoats (winged beasts) are primary transport vehicles.
- **Tone:** High adventure mixed with romantic sacrifice and intense physical conflict.

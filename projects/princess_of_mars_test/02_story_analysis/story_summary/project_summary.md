# Project Context: The War of the Green Men
The narrative follows a human narrator stranded on Mars alongside the indigenous "green men." Communication relies heavily on telepathy, which is universal across Martian species and animals. The environment is harsh, arid, and dying, necessitating strict population control through communal child-rearing and selective breeding. Key locations include the Dead City (incubator site), subterranean vaults, and the network of Martian canals. Social structure is hierarchical with chieftains, warriors, and civilians, all bound by a culture that lacks traditional parental bonds but possesses advanced telepathic capabilities. The story explores themes of survival, cultural difference, and the narrator's gradual adaptation to Martian physiology and psychology.

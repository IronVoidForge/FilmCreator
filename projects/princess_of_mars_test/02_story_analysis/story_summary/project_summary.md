# Project Summary: Barsoom Escape Narrative

## Setting and World
- **Location:** Ancient cities of Mars (Barsoom), specifically Korad and Thark.
- **Environment:** Features vast waterways/canals, arid desert lands, high-walled plazas, and monumental architecture designed on a scale suitable for emperors.
- **Atmosphere:** Dominated by the red sun and wild Martian moons which light the landscape at night.

## Key Characters
- **John Carter (Dotar Sojat):** A fighting man from Earth seeking to protect Dejah Thoris and escape to Helium.
- **Dejah Thoris:** Princess of Helium, currently a captive in Thark under threat of arena games.
- **Sola:** A Thark woman who aids the escape plan and carries knowledge of local geography.
- **Tal Hajus:** Jeddak (leader) of Thark, ruling over the green men hordes.
- **Antagonists:** Sarkoja (seen at Tal Hajus's quarters), Red Martian troops, Green men warriors.

## Overarching Conflict
- The narrative focuses on a planned escape from Thark to Helium via Martian waterways.
- Tension exists between John Carter's desire for Dejah Thoris and the political/military constraints of Thark society.
- Threats include the yearly arena games where captives are thrown to wild calots, and the intervention of Sarkoja who has secured Dejah's safety with Tal Hajus at a cost.

## Visual Continuity Elements
- **Architecture:** Large buildings fronting plazas, courtyards with moss-like vegetation, second-story windows accessible via winding runways.
- **Transportation:** Thoats (large beasts) and zitidars used for travel; animals move restlessly in paddocks.
- **Mapping:** Maps drawn on marble floors using diamonds as tools to trace waterways and cities.
- **Lighting:** Night scenes illuminated by the upper rim of the red sun or wild Martian moons.

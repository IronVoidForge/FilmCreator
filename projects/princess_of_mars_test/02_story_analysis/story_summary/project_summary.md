This narrative follows John Carter, a Helium warrior transported to Barsoom (Mars), navigating life among the Tharks and other Martian peoples. The story centers on themes of warfare, survival, and romance against the backdrop of Martian customs and technology. Key characters include Dejah Thoris, Princess of Helium; Tars Tarkas, a Thark warrior; and Lorquas Ptomel, the Chieftain of the Tharks. The plot explores the contrast between Earthly values (kindness, mercy) and Martian norms (cruelty, death), particularly regarding beast training and combat tactics. Visual continuity relies on the dual-moon lighting, the green landscape of Mars, and the specific attire of the characters (silks, armor, thoats). The overarching arc involves Carter's integration into Thark society while maintaining his connection to Dejah Thoris, culminating in significant romantic developments and military advancements that challenge existing social hierarchies.

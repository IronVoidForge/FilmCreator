# Project Summary

**Setting:** Barsoom (Mars), specifically a city inhabited by Green Martians.
**Key Characters:** John Carter (narrator), Sola, Woola (hound).
**Technology:** Chariots, mastodons, large airships/crafts, firearms, grappling hooks.
**Context:** Post-incubation ceremony; ongoing conflict between Green Martians and an external fleet.
**Tone:** Action-oriented war narrative with underlying melancholy regarding inter-species relations.
**Continuity Notes:** Carter frequently observes from building windows or rooftops. Woola remains close to Carter's heel. Sola acts as a companion/protector figure.

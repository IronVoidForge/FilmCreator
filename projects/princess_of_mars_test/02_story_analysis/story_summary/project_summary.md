John Carter serves as a protector and rising figure within the Thark community on Barsoom (Mars). He is currently guarding Dejah Thoris, a captive princess of the red race, while managing tensions with Sarkoja, a hostile female warrior. The narrative takes place in the city of Korad, where Carter has established quarters for himself, Sola, and Dejah Thoris. His status among the Tharks is elevated to that of an eleventh Chieftain, approaching the rank of Tars Tarkas. The story explores themes of identity, as Carter attempts to convince Dejah Thoris of his Earth origins despite her skepticism regarding his lack of Barsoomian markings and knowledge of places like Virginia.

# Project Summary: Barsoom (Mars) - Thark Arc

## Setting & World
The story takes place on Mars (Barsoom), specifically within the domain of Thark. Martian society is distinct from Earthly norms, characterized by green-skinned inhabitants and a harsh environment. Females hold significant societal power, serving as artisans who manufacture all goods, weapons, and ammunition, and acting as the primary defense force and reserves in warfare. Males are trained in high-level strategy, troop maneuvering, and law-making. There are no lawyers on Mars; justice is administered by peers or custom without legal precedent.

## Key Characters
*   **John Carter ("I"):** A human from Earth, currently residing in Thark. He is learning the Martian tongue and adapting to local customs.
*   **Sola:** A female Martian guardian and mentor to Carter. She is protective, kind, and possesses a strong aversion to cruelty and barbarity. She acts as Carter's primary contact for training and social integration.
*   **Woola:** A hound accompanying Carter.
*   **Lorquas Ptomel:** The leader of Thark (mentioned).
*   **Tars Tarkas:** A high-ranking Martian leader mentioned by Sola.
*   **Sarkoja:** An older female Martian who shares the sleeping quarters and acts as a guard for the prisoner.

## Themes & Dynamics
The narrative explores gender roles on Mars where females are the producers and warriors, while males handle strategy and law. The story focuses on Carter's integration into this society, his training in combat and language, and his growing desire to escape with a female Earthling prisoner. The relationship between Carter and Sola is central, built on mutual trust and Sola's protective nature.

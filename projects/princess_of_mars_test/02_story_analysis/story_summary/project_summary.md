A sweeping interplanetary romance and adventure set on the dying planet of Mars (Barsoom). The narrative follows John Carter, a human from Earth, as he navigates the complex political landscapes and dangerous environments of various Martian races. Central to the plot is his relationship with Dejah Thoris, the Princess of Helium, and their struggle against the various warring factions—including the green-skinned Tharks—that threaten their survival and the stability of the Helium empire.

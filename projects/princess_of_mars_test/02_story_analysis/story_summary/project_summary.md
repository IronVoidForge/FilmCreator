An Earthman, stranded on the planet Mars, navigates the complex and brutal social structures of the "green men." Living among a community of telepathic Martians, he must learn their language, customs, and the harsh realities of a dying world where survival is dictated by physical prowess and pitiless selection. As he integrates into their society, he discovers unique abilities—including an impenetrable mind—that may provide him with a distinct advantage in a landscape defined by constant warfare and the struggle for existence.

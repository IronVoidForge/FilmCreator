# Project Summary: A Princess of Mars

**Narrative Context:**
The story follows John Carter, a human from Earth who has been transported to Mars (Barsoom). He lives among the indigenous Martians, specifically the "green warriors," and navigates their complex social and military structures. The narrative is driven by inter-factional warfare involving ground forces using chariots and mastodons, as well as advanced aerial technology in the form of gray-painted airships.

**Key Characters:**
*   **John Carter:** The narrator and protagonist, an outsider observing Martian customs and conflicts. He possesses a unique perspective on the "green warriors" and their technology.
*   **Sola:** A companion to John Carter, often seen accompanying him during movements between city locations.
*   **Woola:** A hound that follows Carter closely.
*   **Lorquas Ptomel:** An astute old warrior who leads the Martians in strategic decisions regarding safety and movement.
*   **The Captive (Dejah Thoris):** A female figure captured from a destroyed airship, described as having human-like features but Martian coloring. She becomes a focal point of emotional tension for Carter.

**Setting:**
The action takes place within a fortified Martian city featuring spacious doorways, upper floors with windows, and an open plaza. The surrounding environment includes valleys, hills, and the sky where airships operate. The city serves as a safe haven from aerial attacks but is subject to the strategic movements of the green warriors.

**Themes:**
*   **Warfare:** Depicts naval-style engagements on land using airships, focusing on accuracy, targeting specific ship components (sighting apparatus, steering gear), and the aftermath of destruction.
*   **Discovery:** The introduction of new Martian technologies (airships) and biological entities (the captive).
*   **Emotional Resonance:** Carter's internal conflict regarding the fate of "kindred people" versus his duty to the green warriors, and his growing connection to the female captive.

**Visual Continuity:**
*   **Green Martians:** The primary ground forces, wearing green attire, utilizing spears, grappling hooks, and chariots. They operate from buildings and rooftops.
*   **Gray Airships:** Large, low, gray-painted vessels with banners and odd devices on the prow. They possess guns and upper works that are vulnerable to fire.
*   **City Architecture:** Multi-story buildings with windows for firing positions, plazas for gathering, and open ground for movement.
*   **Captive Appearance:** Distinctive physical traits including light reddish copper skin, coal black hair, and a slender girlish figure, often depicted as naked except for ornaments.

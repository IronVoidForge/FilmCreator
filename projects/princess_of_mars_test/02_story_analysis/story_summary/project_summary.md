**Setting:** A Martian valley city featuring spacious doorways, open ground for processions, and buildings with upper floors and rooftops suitable for observation and defense. Surrounding hills frame the valley.
**Factions:** Green Martians (warriors, chariots, mounted) and Gray Airship Fleet (naval force, unmanned vessels).
**Characters:** John Carter (human observer), Sola (Martian companion), Woola (hound).
**Tone:** High-stakes aerial warfare mixed with exploration and discovery. The narrative perspective is first-person ("I"), emphasizing observation of distant events and sudden shifts in fortune.
**Core Conflict:** Defense against aerial bombardment, followed by the recovery of a disabled vessel and the discovery of a human captive within it.

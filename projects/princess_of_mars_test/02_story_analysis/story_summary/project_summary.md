This project chronicles the adventures of John Carter, formerly Captain Jack Carter of Virginia. Following the American Civil War, Carter possesses Confederate wealth and a cavalry commission but finds himself masterless in a vanished state. Seeking to retrieve his fortunes through gold prospecting in Arizona, he partners with mining engineer Captain James K. Powell. After locating a rich quartz vein, Powell returns to civilization for machinery while Carter guards the claim. Upon Powell's departure, Carter discovers Powell has been ambushed and killed by Apaches. Pursuing the attackers, Carter enters a remote cave where he finds Powell's body. The narrative explores themes of mortality, duty, and strange phenomena gained upon Mars (as hinted in the text), setting the stage for his ten-year survival story within an Arizona cave that defies natural laws.

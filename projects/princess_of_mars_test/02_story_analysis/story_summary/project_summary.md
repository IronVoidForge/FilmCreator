The narrator resides in a study overlooking the Hudson River, having returned to Earth after a decade-long absence from Mars. His life is defined by a profound longing for Dejah Thoris and the fate of the people on the distant planet. While financially successful due to an untouched old mine, he remains emotionally tethered to Mars, viewing wealth as secondary to his desire to be reunited with his lost love. The narrative spans twenty years since his first awakening on Mars, maintaining a consistent perspective of Earth-bound observation versus Martian memory.

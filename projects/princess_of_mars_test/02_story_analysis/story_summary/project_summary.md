# Project Summary: Princess of Mars Test

## Genre & Setting
Science fiction/horror narrative set in Arizona wilderness, featuring a western protagonist who experiences supernatural transformation and interdimensional travel. The story blends frontier danger with cosmic horror elements.

## Main Character Profile
Protagonist is a fighting man accustomed to combat, wearing revolvers and carbine, initially clothed then mysteriously naked after paralysis event. Possesses strong willpower and physical resilience despite extraordinary circumstances.

## Key Story Elements
- Paralysis phenomenon affecting protagonist's muscles while mental faculties remain intact
- Apache warriors who display awe/fear rather than aggression toward protagonist
- Mysterious moaning sounds from cave depths that cause panic among warriors
- Protagonist's mysterious metamorphosis (clothed to naked, paralyzed to mobile)
- Red star attraction leading to space transition
- Setting includes Arizona cave, mountain ledge, rocky gorge vista

## Visual Continuity Facts
- Cave opening faces daylight trail with cliff turn
- Horse stands before cave entrance with dragging rein
- Dead body of friend placed on ledge within protagonist's vision range
- Moonlight floods cave during transformation sequence
- Starry Arizona night sky visible from cave opening
- Red star positioned near distant horizon in landscape vista

## Narrative Themes
Danger vs. mystery, paralysis as supernatural phenomenon, warrior culture reactions to unknown entities, cosmic attraction overriding mortal concerns, physical transformation without explanation.

# Project Summary

**Project Title:** Princess of Mars (Test)
**Slug:** princess_of_mars_test
**Protagonist:** John Carter (Captain Jack Carter), Civil War veteran.
**Setting:** Arizona Territory, late 1860s; chronicle spans ten years in an Arizona cave.
**Premise:** A chronicle by an immortal soldier recounting his life and death. He seeks gold in the Southwest after the Civil War.
**Key Themes:** Mortality, duty, frontier survival, mystery of Mars.
**Narrative Voice:** First-person retrospective; narrator claims to be ageless but fears death.

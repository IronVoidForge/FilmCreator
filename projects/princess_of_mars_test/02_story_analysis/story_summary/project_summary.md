**Project Title:** Princess of Mars Test
**Narrative Perspective:** First-person account of a human stranded on the planet Mars.
**Core Themes:** Exploration, isolation, biological discovery, and cultural encounter with indigenous Martian inhabitants.
**Setting:** The surface of Mars, specifically focusing on a valley region where the protagonist is held captive by a nomadic Martian group.
**Protagonist Status:** A visitor or explorer who has been captured but maintains agency through observation and physical capability (jumping).
**Key Interactions:** The protagonist interacts with specific Martian individuals, including a female guardian named Sola, various "watch dogs," and larger humanoid Martians.
**Documentary Style:** The text functions as a log or journal entry, detailing environmental conditions (Martian nights, moons, temperature), biological facts (Martian flora producing milk, fauna speed and loyalty), and social dynamics (Martian nomadic lifestyle, lack of artificial lighting).

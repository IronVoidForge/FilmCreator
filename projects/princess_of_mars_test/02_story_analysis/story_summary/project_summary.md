# Project: A Princess of Mars

John Carter is a human from Earth residing on Mars with his companion Sola and hound Woola. The local population consists of Green Martians who utilize chariots, mastodons, and mounted warriors for travel and combat. Society features fortified cities with multi-story buildings overlooking vast valleys and hills. Conflict involves firearms and spears against enemy forces utilizing gray-painted airships equipped with heavy guns and wireless sighting apparatus.

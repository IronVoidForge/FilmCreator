## Production Intent

Demonstrate John Carter's specialized skill of mastering Martian beasts through empathy and authority rather than the brute force used by Tharks. The sequence must visually transition from aggressive, wild animal behavior to calm, docile cooperation to validate his unique methodology.

## Scene Short Description

A human man uses gentle authority to tame large, aggressive Martian beasts within a city complex.

## Scene Primary Scale Story Point

Human scale vs massive beast scale; individual empathy vs collective brute force.

## Purpose

To demonstrate John Carter's unique ability to master Martian beasts through empathy rather than force, establishing his specialized skill set.

## Summary

Following the airship battle, Carter spends several days training with the "thoats." While Tharks use brute strength, Carter uses a mix of authority and kindness. His success is so profound that he teaches Tars Tarkas and other warriors his methods, transforming the beasts into docile mounts.

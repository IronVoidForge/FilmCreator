# Open Ground Valley

## Location Description

The deserted Martian valley and hills visible from windows, where the airships approach and retreat.

## Visual Continuity Facts

- **Setting:** Daytime with sunlight gleaming on devices
- **Terrain:** Open ground with valleys and hills visible in distance
- **Atmosphere:** Desolate, abandoned city environment

## Narrative Context

This environment serves as the primary stage for the airship fleet's approach and retreat. The valley provides a clear line of sight between the buildings and the approaching vessels.

## Key Elements

- Airships (long, low, gray-painted)
- Martian warriors positioned in buildings
- Open ground where ships land and are looted
- Distant hills and valleys visible from windows

## Continuity Notes

- The fleet approaches from one direction and retreats the same way
- Warriors fire from building windows into the valley
- The damaged vessel drifts southeast through this environment
- Daytime lighting with sunlight gleaming on devices

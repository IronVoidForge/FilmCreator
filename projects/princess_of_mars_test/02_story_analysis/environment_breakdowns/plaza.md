# Plaza

## Location Description

The central gathering area of the city where the cavalcade returns to at the end of the chapter.

## Visual Continuity Facts

- **Setting:** Central open area of the city
- **Function:** Gathering point for Martians and their children
- **Atmosphere:** Throngs of Martians present

## Narrative Context

This environment serves as the return destination for John Carter and Sola after witnessing the airship battle. The cavalcade returns to the plaza, and the homeward march is given up for that day.

## Key Elements

- Central gathering area
- Throngs of Martians present
- Accessible from city buildings
- Return point for chariots and mounted warriors

## Continuity Notes

- Sola and John Carter enter the plaza after witnessing the battle
- A sight meets their eyes as they near the throng of Martians
- The prisoner from the battle craft is being dragged into a nearby building
- Lorquas Ptomel remains too astute to be caught on open plains with caravan
- They remain at the deserted city until danger seems passed

# City Buildings

## Location Description

Buildings with spacious doorways and upper floors overlooking valleys and hills, where John Carter and Sola are positioned.

## Visual Continuity Facts

- **Architecture:** Buildings with spacious doorways and upper floors
- **View:** Windows overlooking valleys and hills beyond
- **Interior:** Rooms accessible from street level

## Narrative Context

This environment serves as the primary observation point for John Carter. He enters a building on the front of the city, mounts to an upper floor, and peers from the window out over the valley.

## Key Elements

- Upper floors with windows
- Spacious doorways at street level
- Interior rooms accessible from plaza
- Observation points overlooking open ground

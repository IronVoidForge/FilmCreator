A young girl named Dorothy is transported from Kansas to the magical Land of Oz via a cyclone. After accidentally destroying the Wicked Witch of the East, she becomes a celebrated figure among the Munchkins. Her primary goal is to find the Great Oz in the Emerald City to request passage back home to Kansas. Along her journey, she travels the Yellow Brick Road and begins to assemble a group of companions, each seeking something from the Great Oz.

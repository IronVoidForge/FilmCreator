# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Transition from action to a state of suspended, eerie calm; establishes the new status quo.

**Scene Summary:** The violent shaking subsides into a rhythmic swaying. Exhausted and overwhelmed, Dorothy and Toto fall asleep as the house continues its long, silent journey through the sky.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Toto

**Participating Environments:** Interior of the floating farmhouse; high altitude/sky.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Chaos $\rightarrow$ Exhaustion/Eerie tranquility.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Slow, sweeping pans.
- Soft, rhythmic lighting (simulating passing clouds).
- Close-ups on Dorothy and Toto sleeping.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The "sway" rhythm; light patterns moving across the floor as the house drifts through different cloud layers.

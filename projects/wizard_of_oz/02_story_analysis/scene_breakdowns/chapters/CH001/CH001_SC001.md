# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the setting, the constant threat of nature, and the tension of the approaching cyclone.

**Scene Summary:** In the colorless Kansas prairie, the family lives under the shadow of potential cyclones. As a massive storm approaches from two directions, Uncle Henry prepares for the worst while Aunt Em directs Dorothy toward safety.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Aunt Em
- Uncle Henry
- Toto

**Participating Environments:** One-room farmhouse; vast, gray Kansas prairie.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Mundane vigilance $\rightarrow$ Escalating dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the desolate landscape and approaching storm clouds.
- Mid/Close shots of characters' weathered faces (Aunt Em, Uncle Henry).
- Low angle shots of the house against the darkening sky.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting shifts from bright/gray to dark/stormy; wind speed/direction affecting hair and clothing.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** High-stakes action sequence within the confined space of the moving house.

**Scene Summary:** Inside the airborne house, chaos ensues. Dorothy struggles to maintain her footing and must act quickly to rescue Toto when he nearly falls through the open trap door during a violent lurch.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Toto

**Participating Environments:** Interior of the floating farmhouse.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Desperate protective instinct.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tight, claustrophobic medium shots.
- Tracking shots following Dorothy's movement through the tilting rooms.
- Close-ups on Toto and the trap door.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The angle of the floor/walls (tilting); loose objects sliding in a consistent direction relative to the house's motion.

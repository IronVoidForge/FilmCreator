# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** The inciting incident of the chapter—the cyclone strikes and physically displaces the protagonist.

**Scene Summary:** Just as Dorothy attempts to reach the cellar, a powerful cyclone hits. The house is ripped from its foundations and lifted into the air by intense wind pressure.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Toto (indirectly)

**Participating Environments:** Inside/Exterior of the farmhouse; the eye of the storm.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Panic $\rightarrow$ Disorientation/Terror.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Handheld/Shaky cam to simulate impact.
- Extreme close-ups of Dorothy's reaction.
- POV shots looking out windows as the ground drops away.
- VFX heavy sequences of the house lifting.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Interior objects flying; sudden change in light levels (spinning through clouds); dust and debris movement.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the journey's progress and the escalating magical hostility of the Wicked Witch.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, and Lion travel through the rugged West. The Witch attempts several assassination attempts: a pack of wolves (defeated by Tin Woodman), crows (defeated by Scarecrow), and black bees (neutralized by Scarecrow's straw).

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Wicked Witch (off-screen/magical influence).

**Participating Environments:** Rugged, untilled lands of the West.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Growing Dread $\rightarrow$ Relief/Exhaustion.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the landscape; medium tracking shots of the group walking; close-ups of character reactions to attacks; action coverage for the animal assaults.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Dorothy's clothing (white); Scarecrow's straw density; Tin Woodman's physical integrity after combat.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To separate the protagonists and establish the new status quo of captivity.

**Scene Summary:** The Monkeys capture the group: Tin Woodman is dropped on rocks, Scarecrow is stripped of straw, and the Lion is bound. Dorothy is spared due to the mark on her forehead but taken to the castle.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Winged Monkeys.

**Participating Environments:** The West landscape $\rightarrow$ Witch's Castle (Kitchen/Courtyard).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Chaos $\rightarrow$ Defeat $\rightarrow$ Isolation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** High-angle shots of the group being scattered; medium shots of Dorothy entering the kitchen; lonely wide shots of the Lion in the courtyard.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Scarecrow's lack of straw; Dorothy's transition from traveler to servant; the visual presence of the mark on her forehead.

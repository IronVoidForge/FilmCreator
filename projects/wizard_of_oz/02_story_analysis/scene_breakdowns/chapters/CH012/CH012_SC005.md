# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Climax and resolution of the primary antagonist's arc.

**Scene Summary:** Enraged by the theft, the Witch confronts Dorothy. In a moment of reflex/defense, Dorothy throws a bucket of water at her. The Witch melts away into a shapeless mass.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Wicked Witch.

**Participating Environments:** The Witch's Kitchen.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Outrage $\rightarrow$ Disbelief $\rightarrow$ Triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Tight two-shot of the confrontation; slow-motion or high-frame-rate coverage of the water impact; VFX-heavy shots of the melting process.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Water levels in the bucket; Dorothy's physical state (wet/dry).

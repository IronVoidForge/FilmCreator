# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To create tension and provide the catalyst for the climax.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy performs domestic labor in the kitchen. The Witch, using invisible magic, trips Dorothy to steal one of her Silver Shoes.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Wicked Witch (invisible/presence).

**Participating Environments:** The Witch's Kitchen.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Monotony $\rightarrow$ Confusion $\rightarrow$ Shock.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups of kitchen tasks; POV shots of Dorothy feeling the "trip"; extreme close-up on the Silver Shoe being snatched.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The presence/absence of one silver shoe; cleanliness/state of the kitchen.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Escalation of the conflict through a major magical event.

**Scene Summary:** In desperation, the Wicked Witch uses the final charge of the Golden Cap to summon the Winged Monkeys. The monkeys descend upon the group.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Winged Monkeys, Wicked Witch (presence/influence).

**Participating Environments:** The rugged West landscape.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Terror.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of the monkeys descending; handheld/shaky cam for the chaos of the attack; wide shots showing the scale of the monkey swarm.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting shifts as the monkeys cloud the sun; positioning of characters during the scramble.

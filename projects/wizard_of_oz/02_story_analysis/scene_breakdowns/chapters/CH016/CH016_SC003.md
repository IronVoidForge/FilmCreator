# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To fulfill the Lion's quest and establish the Wizard's use of psychological placebo.

**Scene Summary:** The Cowardly Lion seeks courage. Oz provides a green liquid in a dish, claiming it must be swallowed to work. The Lion drinks it and immediately feels transformed by the perceived power of the potion.

**Participating Characters:** The Cowardly Lion, Oz (The Wizard).

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room of Oz.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Fear/Anxiety $\rightarrow$ Hesitation $\rightarrow$ Sudden Bravado.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-up of the green liquid in the carved dish.
- Medium shot of the Lion's trembling as he drinks.
- Low-angle shots of the Lion to emphasize his new "courageous" stature.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The level of liquid in the dish; the Lion's posture change from slumped to upright.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To reveal the Wizard's true nature and create tension regarding Dorothy's future.

**Scene Summary:** After the group departs, Oz is left alone. He drops the persona of the Great Wizard, admitting to himself that he is merely a humbug using sleight of hand. He expresses profound doubt about his ability to solve Dorothy's problem of returning home.

**Participating Characters:** Oz (The Wizard).

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room of Oz (now quiet/empty).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Performance/Confidence $\rightarrow$ Isolation $\rightarrow$ Anxiety/Dread.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots showing the emptiness of the large throne room to emphasize isolation.
- Tight close-ups on Oz's face to capture his internal conflict and fear.
- Lighting shift from "grand" to more intimate/shadowy.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The lighting state of the room compared to previous scenes.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To fulfill the Tin Woodman's quest and demonstrate the Wizard's mechanical "magic."

**Scene Summary:** The Tin Woodman requests a heart. Oz uses shears to cut into the Woodman's tin chest, inserts a silk-and-sawdust heart, and solders the metal shut. The Woodman experiences an immediate emotional awakening.

**Participating Characters:** The Tin Woodman, Oz (The Wizard).

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room of Oz.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Physical Vulnerability $\rightarrow$ Joy/Emotional Overflow.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Macro shots of the shears cutting tin.
- Close-ups of the soldering process (sparks/heat).
- Medium shot of the Woodman's reaction to his new "feeling."

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The seam where the tin was cut and subsequently soldered; the appearance of the silk heart.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To fulfill the Scarecrow's quest and establish the Wizard's method of "magic" through physical manipulation.

**Scene Summary:** The Scarecrow enters the throne room to receive his brains. Oz performs a crude but effective procedure, replacing the straw in the Scarecrow's head with bran, pins, and needles. The Scarecrow emerges convinced of his newfound intellect.

**Participating Characters:** The Scarecrow, Oz (The Wizard).

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room of Oz.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Confusion/Shock $\rightarrow$ Intellectual Satisfaction.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-ups of the "brain" mixture (bran/pins).
- Medium shots of the physical manipulation of the Scarecrow's head.
- Reaction shots of the Scarecrow's face as he "wakes up."

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The amount of straw vs. bran in the Scarecrow's head; the placement of pins/needles.

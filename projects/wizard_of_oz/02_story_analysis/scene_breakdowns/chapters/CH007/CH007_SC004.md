# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Transition to a new environment and provide an emotional beat of longing/hope.

**Scene Summary:** The group reaches a swift river, seeing a beautiful meadow on the far side. Exhausted, they camp by the bank while the Tin Woodman begins building a raft. Dorothy falls asleep, dreaming of the Emerald City.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** Riverbank (overlooking a beautiful meadow).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion $\rightarrow$ Hope/Dreaminess.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide sweeping shots of the river and the distant meadow.
- Medium shots of the Woodman working on the raft.
- Soft, ethereal lighting/focus for Dorothy's dream sequence.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The progress of the raft construction; the lighting transition from dusk to night.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish group dynamics and provide a moment of respite before the upcoming obstacles.

**Scene Summary:** The travelers set up camp in a dense forest. Individual tasks are performed: Tin Woodman gathers wood, Scarecrow collects nuts for Dorothy, and the Lion hunts alone.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** Dense Forest (under a large tree).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Calm/Routine $\rightarrow$ Anticipation of travel.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the forest camp setting.
- Medium tracking shots of characters performing chores.
- Close-ups of food/supplies being gathered.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Amount of wood/nuts collected; position of the campfire/camp setup.

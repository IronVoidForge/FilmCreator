# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the first physical obstacle and showcase the Lion's unexpected utility.

**Scene Summary:** The group encounters a wide, deep chasm. The Cowardly Lion overcomes his fear to leap across the gap, acting as a ferry by carrying each companion on his back one by one.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** Deep Forest Chasm/Ditch.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anxiety $\rightarrow$ Relief/Awe.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-angle shots of the deep ditch to emphasize scale.
- Dynamic tracking shots following the Lion's leaps.
- Reaction shots from the characters waiting at the edge.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The depth and width of the chasm; the order in which characters are carried across.

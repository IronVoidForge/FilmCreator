# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Escalation of tension through a life-threatening encounter and a high-stakes decision.

**Scene Summary:** The group reaches a second ditch too wide for the Lion to jump. The Woodman chops a tree to create a bridge. As they cross, Kalidahs attack. To save the group, the Scarecrow directs the Woodman to chop the bridge, dropping the monsters into the abyss.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Kalidahs.

**Participating Environments:** The Second Ditch/Bridge Site.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Terror $\rightarrow$ Triumph/Exhaustion.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Handheld/shaky cam for the Kalidah attack.
- POV shots from the bridge looking down at the monsters.
- Close-ups of the Woodman's axe work and the Scarecrow's commands.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The position of the tree/bridge; the timing of the chop relative to the characters crossing.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To execute the climax (the return) and provide the final resolution/aftermath.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy clicks her heels three times and commands herself home. She is instantly transported to the Kansas prairie. In the transition, the Silver Shoes are lost in the desert. Dorothy arrives safely at her farmhouse to find Uncle Henry and Toto.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Uncle Henry, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** The Desert (transitional), Kansas Prairie/Farmhouse.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Magical Chaos $\rightarrow$ Sudden Arrival $\rightarrow$ Grounded Relief/Melancholy (loss of shoes).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Fast-paced/blurred POV or montage for the flight through space.
- Wide establishing shot of the Kansas landscape.
- Medium shots of Dorothy's reunion with Uncle Henry and Toto.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The sudden change in lighting/color palette from magical to sepia/natural; the visual disappearance of the shoes during the transition.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To resolve the subplots of the companions through magical intervention.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy surrenders the Golden Cap to Glinda. Glinda uses the cap's magic to command the Winged Monkeys to transport the Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, and Lion to their respective destinations.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Lion, Glinda the Good Witch, Winged Monkeys (implied/action).

**Participating Environments:** Glinda's Throne Room.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Magical Resolution/Release.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low angle shots of Glinda performing the magic.
- Reaction shots of the companions as they are whisked away.
- Tracking shots following the monkeys' ascent/departure.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The movement of the Golden Cap from Dorothy to Glinda; the timing of the three distinct commands.

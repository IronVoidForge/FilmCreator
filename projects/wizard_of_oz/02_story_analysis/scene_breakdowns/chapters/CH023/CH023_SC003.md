# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To provide the "how-to" for the climax and deliver the emotional payoff of the journey.

**Scene Summary:** Glinda reveals the secret power of the Silver Shoes. The companions share a final, tearful goodbye, realizing they have gained the wisdom, heart, and courage they sought. Dorothy prepares to leave.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Glinda the Good Witch (and departing companions in farewell).

**Participating Environments:** Glinda's Throne Room.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Revelation $\rightarrow$ Bittersweet Nostalgia.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme close-ups on the Silver Shoes.
- Tight emotional close-ups on Dorothy and her friends during the farewell.
- Soft lighting to emphasize the sentimental mood.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The visual state of the Silver Shoes; Dorothy's physical stance as she prepares to click her heels.

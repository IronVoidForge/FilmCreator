# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the final objective and negotiate the terms of Dorothy's return.

**Scene Summary:** The companions gather in Glinda's throne room to express their individual desires for returning to their respective homes/kingdoms. Dorothy presents her plea to return to Kansas, leading to Glinda's condition regarding the Golden Cap.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Lion, Glinda the Good Witch.

**Participating Environments:** Glinda's Throne Room (Ruby Throne).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Negotiation/Solemnity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots of the throne room and the group's arrangement.
- Medium close-ups of each companion expressing their desire.
- Close-ups on Glinda to establish her authority.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The presence/positioning of the Golden Cap; the physical proximity of the companions to the throne.

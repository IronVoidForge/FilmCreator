# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the Lion as a physical threat and establish the group's vulnerability.

**Scene Summary:** While traveling through the dark, obscured forest, the group is suddenly ambushed by a large Lion. The beast attacks the Scarecrow and the Tin Woodman, creating immediate chaos.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** Thick, dark forest; Yellow Brick Road (partially obscured).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Calm/Travel $\rightarrow$ Terror/Chaos.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots of the dense forest environment.
- Handheld/shaky cam for the ambush.
- Close-ups of character reactions (fear/surprise).
- Low-angle shots of the Lion to emphasize size and threat.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Position of the group relative to the obscured road; direction of the Lion's charge.

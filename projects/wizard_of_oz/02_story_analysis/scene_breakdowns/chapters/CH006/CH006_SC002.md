# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Subvert the threat and introduce the Lion's internal conflict.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy intervenes by slapping the Lion on the nose and calling him a coward. This breaks the tension, revealing that the "King of Beasts" is actually paralyzed by fear. After hearing about their journey to Oz, the Lion asks to join them to seek courage.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** The forest clearing/ambush site.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Aggression $\rightarrow$ Vulnerability/Empathy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of the confrontation (Dorothy vs. Lion).
- Close-ups on the Lion's face to capture his sadness/shame.
- Two-shots between Dorothy and the Lion during the negotiation.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The Lion's physical proximity to Dorothy after the slap; Toto's position.

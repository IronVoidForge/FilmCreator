# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the Tin Woodman's moral character and create a physical complication.

**Scene Summary:** As the group continues their journey, the Tin Woodman accidentally steps on a beetle. Overcome with grief for the tiny creature, he begins to cry, which causes his jaw to rust shut.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** The forest path.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Routine Travel $\rightarrow$ Guilt/Distress.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme close-up of the beetle under the foot.
- Close-up on the Tin Woodman's face as he realizes what happened.
- Macro shots of the rust spreading across the metal jaw.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific location of the beetle; the visual progression of the rust.

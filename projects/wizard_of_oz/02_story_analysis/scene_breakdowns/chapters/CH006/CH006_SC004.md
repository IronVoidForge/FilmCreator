# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the immediate physical crisis and reinforce character themes.

**Scene Summary:** The Scarecrow uses an oil-can to lubricate the Tin Woodman's jaw, restoring his ability to speak. The Woodman expresses a renewed vow to walk with extreme care to protect all living things.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** The forest path.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Panic/Frustration $\rightarrow$ Relief/Resolution.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-up of the oil-can being applied to the metal joints.
- Medium shot of the group gathered around the Woodman.
- Profile shots of the Woodman as he makes his vow.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The amount of oil used; the state of the Tin Woodman's jaw (moving vs. stuck).

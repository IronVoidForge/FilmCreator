# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce a narrative obstacle and reveal the limitations of the group's resources.

**Scene Summary:** The Golden Cap is used to summon the Monkey King and his band. However, the Monkey King refuses to fly Dorothy across the desert, explaining that Winged Monkeys are bound to their own territory.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Monkey King, Winged Monkeys.

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room (now occupied by monkeys).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Hope $\rightarrow$ Frustration/Disappointment.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low angle shots of the Monkey King to establish power; reaction shots of Dorothy and Scarecrow; wide shot showing the scale of the monkey troop.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Winged Monkeys' flight patterns and positioning in the room.

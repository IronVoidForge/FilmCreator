**Scene Summary:** A soldier with green whiskers is summoned. He suggests seeking Glinda, the Good Witch of the South, but warns of the dangers of the desert road. The group accepts the challenge and resolves to leave the next morning.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Soldier (with green whiskers).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Uncertainty $\rightarrow$ Resolute Courage.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shot of the Soldier delivering the warning; group reaction shots (nodding in agreement); final wide shot of the group planning their departure.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting shifts if moving toward "evening" as they plan for the next morning.

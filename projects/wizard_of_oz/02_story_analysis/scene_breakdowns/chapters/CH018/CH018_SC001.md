# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the emotional aftermath of the Wizard's departure and transition the group's status.

**Scene Summary:** In the wake of the balloon's departure, the group experiences a moment of grief. Dorothy provides comfort to the Tin Woodman to ensure he does not rust from tears.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room, Emerald City.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Despair/Melancholy $\rightarrow$ Quiet Resignation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups on faces (grief), medium shots of the group huddling, detail shot of Tin Woodman's joints/tears.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Tear tracks on Tin Woodman; positioning of characters relative to the empty throne.

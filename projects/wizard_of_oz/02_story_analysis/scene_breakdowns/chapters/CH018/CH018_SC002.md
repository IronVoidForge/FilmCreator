# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Transition from mourning to active problem-solving; establish the Scarecrow's new authority.

**Scene Summary:** The Scarecrow, now acting as ruler, convenes a meeting with the travelers. Dorothy states her goal of returning to Kansas, leading the Scarecrow to suggest using the Golden Cap to summon the Winged Monkeys for flight.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room (formalized setting).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Melancholy $\rightarrow$ Hopeful Determination.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shot of the throne room meeting; medium shots of Scarecrow addressing the group; close-up on the Golden Cap.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The presence/handling of the Golden Cap.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To complete the group's reunion through a moment of physical restoration.

**Scene Summary:** The search party finds the Scarecrow's discarded clothes in a tall tree. Using his new axe, the Tin Woodman chops down the tree. They retrieve the clothes, restuff them with fresh straw, and restore the Scarecrow to life.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Cowardly Lion, Tin Woodman, Scarecrow, Winkies.

**Participating Environments:** A rocky plain featuring a tall, smooth-trunked tree.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From loss/despair to triumph and joy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low angle shots of the tall tree.
- Action shots of the Tin Woodman using the axe.
- Close-ups of the straw being stuffed into the clothes.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The condition of the Scarecrow's clothes (empty vs. stuffed); the appearance of the new gold-handled axe.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To provide closure to the West arc and set the stage for the final journey.

**Scene Summary:** The group prepares to leave for the Emerald City. The Winkies present them with parting gifts (jewelry, gold-headed stick, silver oil-can). During food gathering, Dorothy finds the Golden Cap in the Witch's cupboard and decides to wear it as they depart.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Cowardly Lion, Tin Woodman, Scarecrow, Toto, Winkies.

**Participating Environments:** Yellow Castle; The Witch's Cupboard/Storage area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From communal warmth to adventurous anticipation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of the gift exchange (focus on the items).
- Close-ups of Dorothy discovering and donning the Golden Cap.
- Wide shot of the group walking away toward the horizon.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific jewelry worn by characters (Toto's collar, Dorothy's bracelet, etc.); the presence of the Golden Cap on Dorothy's head for the final exit.

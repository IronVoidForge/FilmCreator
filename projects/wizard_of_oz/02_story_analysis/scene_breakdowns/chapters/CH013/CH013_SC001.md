# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the immediate aftermath of the Witch's death and the shift in political power.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy frees the Cowardly Lion from his cage and announces to the Winkies that they are no longer slaves. The Winkies celebrate their liberation with a feast at the Yellow Castle.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Cowardly Lion, Winkies.

**Participating Environments:** The Yellow Castle (Interior/Courtyard).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension/captivity to jubilation and relief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the castle courtyard during the feast.
- Close-ups of Dorothy's declaration.
- Medium shots of the Lion being released from his cage.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The state of the Lion's cage; the presence/absence of the Witch's remains (implied).

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To reunite the core group and demonstrate the communal effort of the Winkies.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy and the Lion lead a search party across a rocky plain, discovering the rusted Tin Woodman. They bring him back to the castle where Winkie tinsmiths spend three days repairing his body with patches.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Cowardly Lion, Tin Woodman, Winkies (Tinsmiths).

**Participating Environments:** Rocky Plain; Yellow Castle (Workshop area).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From desperation/worry to relief and gratitude.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the desolate rocky plain.
- Close-ups of the rusted, battered Tin Woodman.
- Montage/Time-lapse style coverage of the tinsmiths working on the Woodman.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The level of rust on the Woodman; the specific placement and appearance of the patches on his body post-repair.

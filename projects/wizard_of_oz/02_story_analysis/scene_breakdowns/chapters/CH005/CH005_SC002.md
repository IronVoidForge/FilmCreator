# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the secondary companion and create a moment of tension/mystery.

**Scene Summary:** While searching, the group hears strange groaning sounds. They discover the Tin Woodman, who is completely immobilized by rust.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman (immobile).

**Participating Environments:** Forest clearing near a cottage.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From curiosity to alarm/pity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** POV shots of the group looking for the source of the sound; low-angle reveal of the rusted Tin Woodman; close-ups on the mechanical details of his rust.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific "frozen" pose of the Woodman.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the immediate post-waking reality and the physical struggle of the journey.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy and Toto, accompanied by the Scarecrow, navigate the forest in search of basic necessities like food and water.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow.

**Participating Environments:** Forest near a small cottage/spring.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From disorientation to weary determination.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the forest environment; medium tracking shots of the trio walking; close-ups on Dorothy's fatigue and searching eyes.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Level of dirt/exhaustion on characters; position of Toto relative to Dorothy.

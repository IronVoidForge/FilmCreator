# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the internal group dynamic and thematic conflict.

**Scene Summary:** A debate breaks out between the Scarecrow (who wants brains) and the Woodman (who wants a heart). Dorothy remains caught in the middle, distracted by the practical worry of their dwindling food supplies.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman.

**Participating Environments:** The Yellow Brick Road.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From philosophical debate to grounded anxiety.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Over-the-shoulder shots during the debate; close-ups on the conflicting expressions of the characters; medium shot of Dorothy looking isolated by her practical concerns.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical distance between the three travelers as they walk and talk.

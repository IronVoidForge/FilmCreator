# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Provide character backstory and raise the stakes of the journey's emotional weight.

**Scene Summary:** As they travel along the Yellow Brick Road, the Woodman uses his axe to clear the path. He recounts how he was once a human wood-chopper who was transformed into tin by the Wicked Witch of the East.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman.

**Participating Environments:** The Yellow Brick Road (thick with branches).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From rhythmic travel to melancholy/tragedy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the road being cleared; medium shots of the Woodman working; stylized or flashback-adjacent lighting during his monologue.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The amount of brush cleared from the path as they move.

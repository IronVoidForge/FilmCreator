# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the immediate physical conflict (the rust) and expand the party.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy finds an oil-can in the Woodman's cottage. She lubricates his joints, restoring his movement. The Woodman awakens and expresses his desire to seek Oz for a heart.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman.

**Participating Environments:** The Tin Woodman's cottage/immediate vicinity.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension to relief and newfound companionship.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups of the oil being applied; medium shots of the mechanical movement returning (limbs jerking into life); reaction shots of Dorothy and Scarecrow.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The "wetness" or sheen of the oil on the tin surfaces.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** The climax of the chapter and the tragic reversal of fortune.

**Scene Summary:** As the balloon begins to lift, Dorothy realizes Toto is missing and rushes to find him. Just as she reaches the basket, the ropes snap prematurely. The balloon ascends rapidly into the sky with only Oz inside, leaving Dorothy and her friends stranded on the ground.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Oz (The Wizard)
- Scarecrow
- Tin Woodman
- Lion
- Toto (off-screen/searching)

**Participating Environments:**
- Launch site, Emerald City

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From frantic hope to sudden shock and abandonment.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tracking shots: Dorothy running toward the balloon.
- High-angle/POV shots: Looking up at the ascending balloon.
- Close-ups: The snapping ropes; Dorothy's face of realization/despair.
- Wide shot: The balloon shrinking into the sky, leaving the group small on the ground.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The timing of the rope snap relative to Dorothy's position; the height and speed of the balloon's ascent.

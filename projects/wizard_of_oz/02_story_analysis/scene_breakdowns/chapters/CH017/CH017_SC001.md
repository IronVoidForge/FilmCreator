# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the new objective and reveal the Wizard's true motivation.

**Scene Summary:** After three days of silence, Oz summons Dorothy. He proposes a journey via balloon to cross the desert but reveals his secret identity as a former circus performer. He expresses a desperate need to flee before his fraud is discovered.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Oz (The Wizard)

**Participating Environments:**
- The Throne Room, Emerald City Palace

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From suspenseful waiting to revelation and shared desperation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Two-shot: Dorothy and Oz discussing the plan.
- Close-ups: Oz's face during his confession/revelation.
- Medium shots: The scale of the Throne Room to emphasize isolation.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Oz's physical demeanor (transitioning from "Wizard" persona to "Humbug" performer).

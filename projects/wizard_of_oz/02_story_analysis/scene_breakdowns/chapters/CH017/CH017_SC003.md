# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the political transition and public stakes.

**Scene Summary:** Oz makes a formal announcement to the citizens of the Emerald City. He claims he is traveling to visit a "brother Wizard" in the clouds. To maintain order, he appoints the Scarecrow as the temporary ruler of the city.

**Participating Characters:**
- Oz (The Wizard)
- The Scarecrow
- Citizens of the Emerald City

**Participating Environments:**
- Palace Exterior/Public Square, Emerald City

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From public spectacle to a sense of formal transition.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots: Oz addressing the crowd.
- Reaction shots: The citizens' curiosity; Scarecrow's reaction to his new role.
- Medium shot: The official appointment of the Scarecrow.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Crowd density and placement; Scarecrow's regal/official posture.

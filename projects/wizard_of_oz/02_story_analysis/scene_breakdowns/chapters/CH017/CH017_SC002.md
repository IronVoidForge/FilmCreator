# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Montage of preparation and technical setup for the flight.

**Scene Summary:** A three-day period where Dorothy and Oz work together to construct a large green silk balloon. They apply glue to make it airtight, attach a basket, and prepare a heating mechanism using wood provided by the Tin Woodman.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Oz (The Wizard)
- Tin Woodman (providing materials)

**Participating Environments:**
- Exterior/Interior Palace grounds, Emerald City

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From heavy labor to focused anticipation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Montage/Insert shots: Glue being applied, silk being stretched, wood being gathered.
- Wide shots: The scale of the balloon growing over time.
- Medium shots: Dorothy and Oz working in tandem.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The size and state of the green silk balloon; weather conditions over the three days.

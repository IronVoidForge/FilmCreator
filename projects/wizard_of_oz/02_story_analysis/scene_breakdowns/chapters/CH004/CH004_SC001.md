# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the physical struggle and the character dynamic of the journey.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy, Toto, and the Scarecrow navigate a deteriorating stretch of the Yellow Brick Road. The Scarecrow's lack of intellect is highlighted through his repeated stumbles into holes in the broken road.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** A desolate, uneven stretch of the Yellow Brick Road transitioning from farmland to wasteland.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Frustration/Exhaustion.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the landscape; medium tracking shots of the trio walking; close-ups of feet/bricks and Scarecrow's clumsy falls.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific pattern of broken bricks; the physical state of the Scarecrow (straw displacement after falls).

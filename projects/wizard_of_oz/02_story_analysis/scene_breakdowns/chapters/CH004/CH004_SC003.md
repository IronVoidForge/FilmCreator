# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Backstory exposition and establishing the Scarecrow's motivation.

**Scene Summary:** The Scarecrow recounts his creation by a farmer and a Munchkin. He describes his first moments of consciousness on a pole and the pivotal moment when a crow informed him that he lacked brains.

**Participating Characters:** Scarecrow (as narrator), Farmer (flashback/implied), Munchkin (flashback/implied), Crow (flashback/implied).

**Participating Environments:** The brookside (present); A cornfield (memory/flashback).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Calm $\rightarrow$ Existential longing/Inadequacy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-up on Scarecrow's face; stylized or dream-like coverage for the flashback sequence.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Transition between present day and flashback lighting/color grading.

# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Resolution of the chapter's journey and establishment of a temporary safe haven.

**Scene Summary:** The group finds a small log cottage for shelter. Dorothy and Toto settle down on a bed of dried leaves to sleep, while the Scarecrow remains standing watch through the night.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** A small cottage made of logs and branches inside the forest.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Fear $\rightarrow$ Exhausted relief/Vigilance.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of the interior; close-ups of Dorothy sleeping; static wide shot of Scarecrow standing guard in the shadows.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting from any small fire or moonlight; placement of the leaf bed.

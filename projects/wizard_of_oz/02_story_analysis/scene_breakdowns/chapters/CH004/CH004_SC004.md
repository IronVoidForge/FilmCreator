# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Escalation of tension and environmental shift.

**Scene Summary:** As evening falls, the group enters a dense, dark forest where the canopy blocks out the light. The atmosphere shifts from open road to claustrophobic gloom, with Dorothy relying heavily on Toto's sight and Scarecrow's guidance.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** A thick, dark forest with a heavy canopy.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Comfort $\rightarrow$ Apprehension/Fear.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-light cinematography; POV shots through the brush; wide shots showing the scale of the trees vs. the small travelers.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Light levels (transition from dusk to deep forest dark); foliage density.

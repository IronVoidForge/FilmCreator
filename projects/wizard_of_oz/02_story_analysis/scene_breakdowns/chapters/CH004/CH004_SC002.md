# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Character development and thematic exposition regarding "home."

**Scene Summary:** During a midday break by a brook, Dorothy and the Scarecrow engage in a philosophical discussion. Dorothy expresses her longing for Kansas, while the Scarecrow struggles to comprehend why anyone would prefer a gray landscape over Oz.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow.

**Participating Environments:** A peaceful brook beside the Yellow Brick Road.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Physical rest $\rightarrow$ Melancholy/Philosophical curiosity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Two-shots of the characters; close-ups on Dorothy's face during her monologue; reaction shots from Scarecrow.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Water flow/ripples; position of lunch items/props.

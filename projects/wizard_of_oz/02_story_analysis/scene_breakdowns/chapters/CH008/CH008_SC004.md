# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Execute the primary conflict/climax of the chapter; demonstrate the stakes of the environment.

**Scene Summary:** The deadly scent takes effect. Dorothy and Toto fall into a deep sleep. The Cowardly Lion, unable to be easily moved by the others, also succumbs to the poppies and falls asleep.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** Deep within the poppy field.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Peace $\rightarrow$ Terror/Urgency (for the non-sleeping characters).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-ups of Dorothy's eyes closing.
- Slow-motion shots of Toto falling asleep.
- Wide shot showing the Lion collapsing heavily into the flowers.
- Medium shots of Scarecrow and Tin Woodman realizing they are unaffected but their friends are in danger.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical positioning of the sleeping characters; the level of "sleepiness" expressed through movement.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the immediate crisis of the lost companion through a "deus ex machina" moment.

**Scene Summary:** After reaching the shore, the group realizes the Scarecrow is gone. A passing Stork arrives and offers assistance. The Stork flies out to the middle of the river, retrieves the Scarecrow from his pole, and delivers him safely back to the group on the bank.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Stork.

**Participating Environments:** Riverbank.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Despair $\rightarrow$ Relief/Wonder.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low angle shots of the group looking lost/defeated on the bank.
- Tracking shot following the Stork's flight path.
- Wide shot showing the scale of the Stork carrying the Scarecrow.
- Medium reaction shots of the group as the Scarecrow is returned.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The height/scale of the Stork; the physical handling of the Scarecrow during the mid-air retrieval.

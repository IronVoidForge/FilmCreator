# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the physical obstacle of the river and create the initial conflict/loss.

**Scene Summary:** The group attempts to navigate a wide, swift river on a makeshift raft. The current proves too strong, sweeping them downstream and resulting in the Scarecrow being separated from the group and stuck on his pole in the middle of the water.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** Wide river with swift current, makeshift raft.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Panic/Loss.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots of the river and the small raft to establish scale/danger.
- Medium shots of the group struggling against the current.
- Close-ups of Scarecrow's face as he realizes he is being swept away.
- POV shots from the raft looking at the receding Scarecrow.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Water spray/wetness on characters; position of the raft relative to the shore; the pole holding the Scarecrow.

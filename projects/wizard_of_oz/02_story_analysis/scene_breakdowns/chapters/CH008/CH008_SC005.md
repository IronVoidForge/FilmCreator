# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Provide the resolution to the immediate threat and an emotional aftermath.

**Scene Summary:** Realizing they are immune, the Scarecrow and Tin Woodman work together to form a "human chair" to carry Dorothy and Toto out of the field. They manage to reach safety, but they are forced to make the heartbreaking decision to leave the Cowardly Lion behind in the poppies.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion (sleeping).

**Participating Environments:** Poppy field (transitioning to safe ground).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Exhausted Relief/Melancholy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of the "human chair" mechanism and the physical struggle of carrying the weight.
- Tracking shots following the group's slow progress out of the red field.
- Wide shot showing the small group leaving the massive, sleeping Lion behind in the sea of red.
- Close-ups of the Scarecrow and Tin Woodman's expressions of sadness/duty.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical coordination of the "human chair"; the distance between the escaping group and the abandoned Lion.

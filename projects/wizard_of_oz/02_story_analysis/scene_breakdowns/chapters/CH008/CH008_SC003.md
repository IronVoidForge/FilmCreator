# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Transition from relief to a new, insidious threat; establish the atmosphere of the poppy field.

**Scene Summary:** The group continues their journey along the riverbank toward the Yellow Brick Road. They wander into a vast, beautiful meadow filled with scarlet poppies. The visual beauty masks the deadly scent of the flowers.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion.

**Participating Environments:** Grassy riverbank, massive meadow of scarlet poppies.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief $\rightarrow$ Tranquility/Lethargy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme wide shots (EWS) of the vast poppy field to establish beauty and scale.
- Close-ups (CU) of the poppies and the scent "wafting" through the air.
- Medium shots of the characters walking, becoming increasingly slow/heavy-lidded.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The density and color saturation of the poppies; the lighting transition from sunny riverbank to the hazy atmosphere of the field.

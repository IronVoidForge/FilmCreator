# SC001

**Scene Purpose**
To provide a cathartic resolution to Dorothy's journey and establish her return to reality.

**Scene Summary**
Dorothy arrives back at the farm in Kansas. She runs toward Aunt Em, who is shocked to see her. The two share an emotional embrace as Dorothy explains she has returned from the Land of Oz with Toto.

**Participating Characters**
- Dorothy
- Aunt Em
- Toto

**Participating Environments**
- The Farm (Kansas)

**Dominant Emotional Shift**
From exhaustion/anticipation to overwhelming relief and joy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families**
- Wide shots of the Kansas landscape/farmstead.
- Tracking shots following Dorothy's run toward the house.
- Medium close-ups of Aunt Em's reaction (shock).
- Tight close-ups/Two-shot for the embrace.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities**
- Dorothy's physical appearance (dirt/wear from travel vs. home attire).
- Toto's position and movement during the run and embrace.
- Lighting transition from the "dreamlike" Oz to the grounded, natural light of Kansas.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Provide the "magical item" and establish the primary quest/obstacle.

**Scene Summary:** The body of the Wicked Witch of the East dries up, leaving behind enchanted silver shoes which are given to Dorothy. When Dorothy asks to go home, she learns the land is impassable, but a magical slate reveals her path: the Emerald City to see Oz the Great.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- The Witch of the North
- Munchkins

**Participating Environments:**
- The clearing near the house

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Despair (at being trapped) $\rightarrow$ Purpose/Determination

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Insert shots: The silver shoes and the magical slate.
- Close-ups: Dorothy's reaction to the news that she cannot simply go home.
- Medium shots: The Witch of the North using the magic slate.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The transition of the Witch's body (drying up).
- The visual appearance of the silver shoes on Dorothy's feet.

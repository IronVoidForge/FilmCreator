# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the central conflict and the accidental consequence of Dorothy's arrival.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy discovers that her house has landed on top of a figure; upon investigation, she realizes she has killed the Wicked Witch of the East, liberating the Munchkins from their oppression.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Toto
- Munchkins

**Participating Environments:**
- The site of the house landing

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Shock $\rightarrow$ Realization of liberation/Triumph (Munchkins)

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low angle shots: Looking up at the house to show its impact.
- Medium shots: Interaction between Dorothy and the small Munchkins.
- Reveal shot: The discovery of the Witch under the house.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Position of the house relative to the characters.
- The scale difference between Dorothy and the Munchkins.

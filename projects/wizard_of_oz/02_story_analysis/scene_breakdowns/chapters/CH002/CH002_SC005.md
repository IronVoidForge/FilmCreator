# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Provide a magical blessing and set the protagonist on her journey.

**Scene Summary:** The Witch of the North kisses Dorothy's forehead for protection and points her toward the Yellow Brick Road before vanishing in a magical display.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- The Witch of the North

**Participating Environments:**
- The edge of the clearing / Start of the Yellow Brick Road

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Solemnity $\rightarrow$ Loneliness/Resolve

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme close-up: The kiss on the forehead.
- Special effects shot: The magical disappearance of the Witch.
- Wide shot: Dorothy standing alone at the start of the Yellow Brick Road.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Lighting/Atmosphere during the magical disappearance.
- Dorothy's direction of travel toward the road.

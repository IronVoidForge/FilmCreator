# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the transition from the gray Kansas reality to the vibrant, surreal world of Oz.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy wakes up following the cyclone to find herself not in a storm-ravaged Kansas, but in a lush, colorful landscape filled with flowers and fruit.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Toto

**Participating Environments:**
- The exterior/immediate vicinity of Dorothy's house (Oz landscape)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Wonder/Awe

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots: Establishing the scale and color of the new world.
- Close-ups: Dorothy’s facial reactions to the sensory overload.
- POV shots: Showing what Dorothy sees as she wakes up.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- Lighting transition (from dark/stormy to bright/saturated).
- Dorothy's physical state (disheveled from the cyclone).

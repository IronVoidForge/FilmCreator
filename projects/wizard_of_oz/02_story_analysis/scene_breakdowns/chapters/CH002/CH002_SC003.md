# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the magical guide and establish the political landscape of Oz.

**Scene Summary:** The Witch of the North arrives to greet Dorothy. She explains that while one witch is dead, another (the Wicked Witch of the West) remains a threat.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- The Witch of the North
- Munchkins

**Participating Environments:**
- The clearing near the house

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Intimidation/Confusion $\rightarrow$ Reassurance/Safety

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Two-shots: Establishing the relationship between Dorothy and the Witch.
- Over-the-shoulder shots: During the exposition regarding the West.
- Medium wide: Showing the group dynamic of the Munchkins surrounding them.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The appearance/costume of the Witch of the North (white, star-patterned).
- The presence of the Munchkins in the background.

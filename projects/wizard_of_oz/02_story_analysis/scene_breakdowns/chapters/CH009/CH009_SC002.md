# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Escalation and preparation for the rescue mission.

**Scene Summary:** The Scarecrow asks the Queen to help rescue the Cowardly Lion from the poppy field. The Tin Woodman begins constructing a makeshift wooden truck using tree limbs and pegs, while the mice begin arriving in massive numbers, each carrying string to serve as harness lines.

**Participating Characters:**
- Scarecrow
- Tin Woodman
- Queen of the Field Mice
- Thousands of Field Mice

**Participating Environments:** Grassy field near the poppy bed.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Industrious Hope.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Montage-style coverage of the Woodman building.
- Wide shots (WS) showing the scale of the approaching mouse army.
- Detail shots of the mice carrying individual pieces of string.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The assembly state of the wooden truck; the increasing density of the mouse crowd.

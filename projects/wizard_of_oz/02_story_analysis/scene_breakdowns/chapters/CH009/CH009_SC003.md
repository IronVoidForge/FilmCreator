# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Climax/Action sequence involving the rescue.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy wakes from her poppy-induced sleep to see the mice gathered. The massive force of mice, combined with the pushing of the Scarecrow and Woodman, pulls the heavy, unconscious Cowardly Lion out of the dangerous poppy field and into the safe, green grass.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Cowardly Lion (unconscious)
- Scarecrow
- Tin Woodman
- Queen of the Field Mice
- Thousands of Field Mice

**Participating Environments:** The edge of the poppy bed and the transition to the green field.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ High-Stakes Effort $\rightarrow$ Relief.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Tracking shots following the movement of the "truck" and the Lion.
- Low-angle shots looking up at the massive effort of the mice.
- Close-ups of Dorothy's waking realization.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical connection between the string, the truck, and the Lion; the direction of travel from poppies to grass.

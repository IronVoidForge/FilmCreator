# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish a debt of honor and introduce a new ally.

**Scene Summary:** While traveling near the river, the Tin Woodman kills a predatory yellow Wildcat that was attacking a small field mouse. The mouse reveals herself to be the Queen of all the Field Mice. After a brief tension where the Woodman protects the mice from Toto, the Queen pledges her people's service to him.

**Participating Characters:**
- Tin Woodman
- Queen of the Field Mice
- Scarecrow
- Dorothy
- Toto

**Participating Environments:** Grassy field near a river, adjacent to poppy beds.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Violence/Tension $\rightarrow$ Wonder/Gratitude.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low-angle shots for the Wildcat attack.
- Extreme close-ups (ECU) of the Queen Mouse to establish her character.
- Medium shots of the group reacting to the revelation.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The position of the dead Wildcat; the proximity of Toto to the mice during the confrontation.

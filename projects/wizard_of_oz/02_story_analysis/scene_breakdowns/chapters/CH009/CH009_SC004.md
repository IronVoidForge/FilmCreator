# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Resolution and emotional payoff.

**Scene Summary:** With the Lion safely out of the poppies, the Queen of the Mice promises future assistance before leading her subjects away. The group settles down in the grass, waiting anxiously for the Lion to wake up.

**Participating Characters:**
- Dorothy
- Scarecrow
- Tin Woodman
- Cowardly Lion (unconscious)
- Queen of the Field Mice
- Thousands of Field Mice

**Participating Environments:** Open green field.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Triumph $\rightarrow$ Quiet Anticipation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots showing the mice departing.
- Medium group shots of the exhausted but relieved travelers.
- Close-up on the Lion's face to build tension for his awakening.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The position of the Lion relative to the poppy bed; the lighting/time of day transition.

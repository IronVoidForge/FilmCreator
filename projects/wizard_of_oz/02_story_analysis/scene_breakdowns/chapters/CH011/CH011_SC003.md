# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the first manifestation of Oz and the personal stakes for Dorothy.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy enters her audience with the Wizard, who appears as a Giant Head. He tells her she can only return home if she kills the Wicked Witch.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, The Great Oz (Giant Head).

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room/Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Fear $\rightarrow$ Determination/Despair.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of the Giant Head to emphasize scale; extreme close-ups of Dorothy's face; wide shots showing the disparity in size between Dorothy and Oz.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The visual effects/scale of the Giant Head relative to Dorothy.

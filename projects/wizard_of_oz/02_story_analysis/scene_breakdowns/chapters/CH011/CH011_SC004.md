# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the second manifestation of Oz and the stakes for Scarecrow.

**Scene Summary:** The Scarecrow meets the Wizard, who appears as a Lovely Lady. She promises him brains on the condition that he kills the Witch.

**Participating Characters:** Scarecrow, The Great Oz (Lovely Lady).

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room/Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Hope $\rightarrow$ Moral Conflict.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of the interaction; close-ups on Scarecrow's expression as he processes the demand.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting shifts to match the "Lovely Lady" persona.

# SC006

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the fourth manifestation of Oz and the stakes for Cowardly Lion.

**Scene Summary:** The Cowardly Lion meets the Wizard, who appears as a Ball of Fire. He demands proof of the Witch's death before granting courage.

**Participating Characters:** Cowardly Lion, The Great Oz (Ball of Fire).

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room/Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Helplessness.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Close-ups on the Lion's trembling; lighting effects from the Ball of Fire illuminating the room and the character.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Light flicker/intensity from the fire source.

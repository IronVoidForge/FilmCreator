# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the third manifestation of Oz and the stakes for Tin Woodman.

**Scene Summary:** The Tin Woodman meets the Wizard, who appears as a Terrible Beast. He promises a heart if the Woodman helps Dorothy kill the Witch.

**Participating Characters:** Tin Woodman, The Great Oz (Terrible Beast).

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room/Audience Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Intimidation $\rightarrow$ Resolve.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** High-angle shots of the Woodman to show vulnerability; close-ups on the "Beast" features.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Sound design (the roar/growl of the beast).

# SC007

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the chapter by uniting the group's resolve for the upcoming quest.

**Scene Summary:** The group reunites and discusses their individual encounters. Realizing they have no other way to get what they want, they agree to face the Witch and prepare for their journey to the land of the Winkies the next morning.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** Palace Guest Quarters.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Uncertainty $\rightarrow$ Somber Resolve.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Group medium shots; close-ups of each character as they commit to the plan; final wide shot of the group preparing for travel.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Character fatigue/emotional state following their individual trials.

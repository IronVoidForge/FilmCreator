# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the central conflict and the Wizard's impossible demand.

**Scene Summary:** The group is lodged in the palace. They are informed that they may see the Wizard, but he will only grant their wishes if they first kill the Wicked Witch of the West.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Green Girl (attendant).

**Participating Environments:** Palace Guest Quarters/Hallway.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Dread/Confusion.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of the group discussing the news; reaction shots to the attendant's delivery of the message.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Props (lodging items, food); character positioning in the palace quarters.

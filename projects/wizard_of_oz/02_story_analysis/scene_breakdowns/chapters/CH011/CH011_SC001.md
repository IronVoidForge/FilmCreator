# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the awe and scale of the Emerald City setting.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, and Cowardly Lion arrive at the gates of the Emerald City. They are overwhelmed by the green marble streets and emerald-studded architecture before being led toward the Palace of Oz.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Guardian of the Gates, Palace Soldiers.

**Participating Environments:** The Streets of the Emerald City (Green Marble/Emerald).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity/Awe $\rightarrow$ Overwhelmed Wonder.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide establishing shots of the city; medium tracking shots of the group walking; close-ups of character reactions to the green splendor.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific shade and intensity of "green" lighting/filters; the group's formation while walking through crowds.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Transition the characters from one geographic region to another via aerial movement.

**Scene Summary:** The Winged Monkeys arrive and pick up the travelers. They fly over the hill, bypassing the frustrated Hammer-Heads, and transport the group across the landscape.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto, Winged Monkeys, Hammer-Heads (below).

**Participating Environments:** Sky/Aerial view of the rocky hills and transitioning landscapes.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Fear $\rightarrow$ Awe/Exhilaration.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Extreme wide shots (aerial) showing the scale of the journey.
- Tracking shots following the monkeys' flight path.
- POV shots from the travelers looking down at the receding Hammer-Heads.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The height and speed of the flight; character grip/posture while being carried.

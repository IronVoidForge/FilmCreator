# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the new setting (Quadling Country) and provide a moment of respite.

**Scene Summary:** The group lands in the lush, red-themed Country of the Quadlings. They meet a kind farmer's wife who provides them with food and rest, allowing them to recover from their journey.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto, Farmer's Wife.

**Participating Environments:** The lush, red-colored landscape of the Quadling Country; a farmhouse/homestead.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion $\rightarrow$ Relief/Comfort.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots establishing the red color palette of the environment.
- Medium shots of the meal and interaction with the farmer's wife.
- Warm, soft lighting to emphasize comfort.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The amount of food/drink consumed; the state of the characters' clothing (dirty from travel).

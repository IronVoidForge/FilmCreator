# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the magical solution to the physical conflict.

**Scene Summary:** Realizing they cannot win a physical fight against the Hammer-Heads, Dorothy decides to use the Golden Cap. She performs the ritual to summon the Winged Monkeys.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto, Hammer-Heads (background/threat).

**Participating Environments:** The rocky hill.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Desperation $\rightarrow$ Hope/Anticipation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-ups of Dorothy and the Golden Cap.
- Low angle shots looking up at the sky as the summons takes effect.
- Reaction shots of the travelers' faces.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The lighting/atmosphere change during the magical summoning; Dorothy's hand placement on the cap.

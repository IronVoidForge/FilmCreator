# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the physical obstacle and the threat of the Hammer-Heads.

**Scene Summary:** The travelers attempt to scale a rocky hill but are intercepted by the armless Hammer-Heads. The creatures use their projectile heads to knock the Scarecrow and the Lion down the slope, demonstrating their unique combat style.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto, Hammer-Heads.

**Participating Environments:** A rocky, steep hill.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Confusion $\rightarrow$ Terror/Defeat.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Wide shots of the terrain and the group's ascent.
- Medium shots of the Hammer-Heads emerging.
- Action close-ups/tracking shots of the heads being launched like projectiles.
- Low angle shots of the characters falling.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The positioning of the travelers before the attack; the trajectory and impact points of the projectile heads.

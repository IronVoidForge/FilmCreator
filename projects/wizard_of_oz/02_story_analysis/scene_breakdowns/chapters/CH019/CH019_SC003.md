# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce a physical obstacle and escalate tension through sudden action.

**Scene Summary:** The group enters a dense forest and is suddenly ambushed by "Fighting Trees." The trees use their branches like limbs to seize the travelers, specifically targeting the Scarecrow.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** Dense Forest.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Calm to Terror/Chaos.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of trees looking menacing; Handheld/shaky cam for the struggle; Close-ups of branches grabbing characters.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific way a branch grabs a character; Character positions during the scramble.

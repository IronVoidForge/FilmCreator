# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To provide closure to the Emerald City arc and establish the new status quo for the Scarecrow.

**Scene Summary:** The group says their goodbyes at the gates of the Emerald City. The Guardian returns the green spectacles to the group and formally acknowledges the Scarecrow's new role as a ruler.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto, Guardian of the Gate, Green-whiskered Soldier.

**Participating Environments:** Outskirts/Gates of the Emerald City.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Formal/Ceremonial to Liberated.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the city gates; Medium two-shots for the hand-off of spectacles; Close-ups on Scarecrow's reaction to his new status.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The green spectacles (must be seen being returned/worn); The group's physical proximity to the gate.

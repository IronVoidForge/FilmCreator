# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To provide character development and thematic reflection on their experiences with the Wizard.

**Scene Summary:** As the group travels toward the South, they engage in a philosophical discussion regarding the Wizard's true nature and the validity of the gifts (brain, heart, courage) they received.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** Green fields outside the Emerald City.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Contemplative/Melancholic to Determined.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Walking tracking shots; Group medium shots; Close-ups on individual faces during dialogue.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Walking direction (consistent movement toward the South); Character spacing in the walking line.

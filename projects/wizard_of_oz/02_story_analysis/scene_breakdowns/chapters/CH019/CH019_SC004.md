# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To demonstrate the utility of the characters' abilities and resolve the immediate conflict.

**Scene Summary:** The Tin Woodman uses his axe to hack through the aggressive branches, carving a path for the group. They realize the trees are only aggressive at the perimeter/first row.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** Dense Forest (clearing/pathway).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Panic to Relief/Triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Action shots of the axe striking wood; Wide shots showing the path being cleared; Medium shots of the group regrouping.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The state of the chopped branches; Tin Woodman's axe condition.

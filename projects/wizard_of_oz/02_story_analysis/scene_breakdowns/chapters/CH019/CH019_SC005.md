# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce a new physical barrier and set the objective for the next sequence.

**Scene Summary:** Emerging from the forest, the group is confronted by a massive, smooth wall made of white china. The Tin Woodman proposes building a ladder to overcome it.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** Edge of the forest / Base of the China Wall.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhaustion to Bewilderment/Problem-solving.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme wide shots (low angle) looking up at the massive wall; POV shots looking up the height of the wall; Medium group shot reacting to the sight.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The scale and texture of the white china wall relative to the characters.

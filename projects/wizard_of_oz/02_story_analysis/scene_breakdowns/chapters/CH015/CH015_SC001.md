# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the group's new status and their demand for justice/fulfillment of promises.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy and her companions arrive at the Emerald City gates, triumphant after defeating the Wicked Witch. They confront the authorities, demanding to see the Wizard to claim the rewards promised to them.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Guardian of the Gates.

**Participating Environments:** The Great Gate of the Emerald City.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Triumph $\rightarrow$ Impatience/Demanding.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the group approaching the gate; medium shots of the confrontation with the Guardian; close-ups on Dorothy's determination.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical state of the companions (dirt, wear) following their journey from the West.

# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Character backstory and resolution of the immediate conflict.

**Scene Summary:** In a smaller, more intimate chamber, Oz explains his true identity as a balloonist from Omaha. He admits he has no magic but promises to fulfill his obligations (brains, heart, courage) through non-magical means and pledges to help Dorothy find a way home.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Oz.

**Participating Environments:** The Small Chamber.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anger $\rightarrow$ Melancholy/Resignation/Cautious Hope.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Eye-level medium shots for the conversation; close-ups on Oz during his monologue to build empathy; group reaction shots.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Character proximity and grouping in the smaller space compared to the Throne Room.

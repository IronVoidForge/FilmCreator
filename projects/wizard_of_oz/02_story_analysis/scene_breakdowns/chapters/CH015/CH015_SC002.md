# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Build tension and establish the passage of time through waiting.

**Scene Summary:** The travelers are ushered into guest rooms within the palace. They wait in a state of growing agitation, discussing their expectations and the Wizard's perceived power.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Palace Staff (Green-whiskered Soldier/Girl).

**Participating Environments:** The Palace of Oz (Guest Rooms).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Irritation/Anxiety.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Interior medium shots of the group in the room; reaction shots as they wait; montage-style coverage of time passing.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting changes to indicate passage of time; character positioning within the guest quarters.

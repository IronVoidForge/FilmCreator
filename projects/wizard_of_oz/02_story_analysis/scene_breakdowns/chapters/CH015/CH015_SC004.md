# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** The central reveal and subversion of the Wizard's power.

**Scene Summary:** The illusions vanish or are bypassed, revealing a small, ordinary man behind a curtain or machine. The "Great Wizard" is exposed as a mere humbug using stagecraft.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Oz (The Man).

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room / Behind the scenes area.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Awe $\rightarrow$ Disillusionment/Anger.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Reveal shot (moving from the illusion to the man); medium shots of the characters' reactions; close-ups on the mechanical props and levers.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The transition from "magical" lighting to "mundane" workshop lighting.

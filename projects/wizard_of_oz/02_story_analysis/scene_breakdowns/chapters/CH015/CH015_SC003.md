# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** The climax of the Wizard's "Great and Terrible" persona.

**Scene Summary:** The travelers are brought into the Throne Room. They are met with terrifying, massive illusions (the Head, the Beast) designed to intimidate them. The Lion's roar inadvertently triggers a reveal or breaks the tension.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, The Wizard (as Illusions).

**Participating Environments:** The Throne Room.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Terror $\rightarrow$ Confusion/Shock.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots to emphasize the scale of the illusions; extreme close-ups of the characters' terrified faces; wide shots showing the vastness of the dome.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Scale of the illusions relative to the characters; lighting shifts during the "magical" displays.

# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** Resolve the chapter with a climax of accidental destruction and an exit from the setting.

**Scene Summary:** The group reaches the far wall to exit. As they climb, the Cowardly Lion's tail accidentally smashes a china church. They successfully scale the wall and leave the country, reflecting on the fragility of their journey.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** The far wall; the exit point of China Country.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Urgency $\rightarrow$ Climax (Accident) $\rightarrow$ Relief/Reflection.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Action shots of the climbing process.
- A "destruction" shot of the church being hit by the tail.
- Wide shot of the group looking back at the land from the outside.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The state of the church (broken) vs. the previous untouched landscape; the physical position of the characters as they exit.

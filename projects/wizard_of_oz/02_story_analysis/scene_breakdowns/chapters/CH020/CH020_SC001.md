# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the new setting's unique physics and the group's entry into a high-stakes, fragile environment.

**Scene Summary:** The travelers use a wooden ladder constructed by the Tin Woodman to scale a massive wall. Upon crossing over, they enter the Dainty China Country, immediately realizing that every movement must be calculated to avoid destruction.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** The high wall of China Country; the white, platter-like landscape of the Dainty China Country.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Determination $\rightarrow$ Awe $\rightarrow$ Extreme Caution/Tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Low angle shots of the ladder and the daunting wall.
- Wide establishing shots of the porcelain landscape.
- Close-ups on feet/steps to emphasize careful movement.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The height of the wall relative to the characters; the stability of the wooden ladder.

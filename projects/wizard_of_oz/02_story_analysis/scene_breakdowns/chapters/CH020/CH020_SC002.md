# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Demonstrate the physical danger and "lethality" of the environment through a mishap.

**Scene Summary:** While navigating the landscape, Dorothy startles a china cow. The resulting chaos causes the cow to kick over a China Milkmaid; the cow breaks its leg and the milkmaid is nicked on the elbow, highlighting the permanent consequences of breakage in this world.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto, China Milkmaid, China Cow.

**Participating Environments:** The Dainty China Country (open landscape).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Caution $\rightarrow$ Panic $\rightarrow$ Guilt/Shock.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Medium shots of the interaction between Dorothy and the cow.
- Quick cuts during the "accident" sequence.
- Close-ups on the cracks/breaks in the porcelain characters.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific location of the milkmaid's nick; the orientation of the broken cow leg.

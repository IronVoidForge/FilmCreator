# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Provide exposition regarding the nature and limitations of the China Country inhabitants.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy meets a beautiful China Princess. The Princess explains the tragedy of being mended (loss of beauty) and the physical stiffness that occurs if they are removed from their homeland.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, China Princess.

**Participating Environments:** A porcelain settlement/area within China Country.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Melancholy/Pathos.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Two-shots between Dorothy and the Princess.
- Close-ups on the Princess to emphasize her delicate beauty.
- Over-the-shoulder shots during the exposition.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The visual appearance of the Princess (ensuring she looks "perfect" but fragile).

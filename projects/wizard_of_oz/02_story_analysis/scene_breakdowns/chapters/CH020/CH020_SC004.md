# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Add character texture and a moment of levity/absurdity to the journey.

**Scene Summary:** The group encounters Mr. Joker, a cracked China Clown whose appearance is a result of his own foolishness (standing on his head too often).

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Mr. Joker.

**Participating Environments:** The Dainty China Country.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension $\rightarrow$ Bewilderment/Amusement.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Medium shots of the group reacting to the clown.
- Close-ups on Mr. Joker's cracks to show his "foolish" history.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The pattern of cracks on Mr. Joker.

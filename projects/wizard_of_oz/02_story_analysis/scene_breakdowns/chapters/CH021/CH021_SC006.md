# SC006

**Scene Purpose:** Provide emotional payoff and resolve the immediate conflict.

**Scene Summary:** The Lion returns to the clearing, triumphant. He is officially recognized as the King of Beasts. He promises to return to rule once Dorothy has safely reached Kansas.

**Participating Characters:** Lion, various forest beasts, Dorothy and companions.

**Participating Environments:** Forest Clearing.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Triumph and solemnity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle "hero" shots of the Lion; wide shots of animals bowing/celebrating; medium close-ups of Dorothy's reaction to the new King.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lion's physical state (scratches/dirt from battle); animal positioning in the clearing.

# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the difficult terrain and transition the group into a new, more dangerous environment.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy, Toto, and their companions struggle through disagreeable bogs and marshes before entering a vast, ancient forest characterized by massive trees and thick moss.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Lion.

**Participating Environments:** Bogs/Marshes, Ancient Forest.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From physical exhaustion/discomfort to a sense of awe and foreboding.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the landscape; medium tracking shots of the group struggling through mud; close-ups of feet in bogs and mossy textures.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Mud levels on clothing/limbs; lighting shifts from open marsh to dense forest canopy.

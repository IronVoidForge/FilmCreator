# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Build suspense and escalate the tension toward the climax.

**Scene Summary:** The Lion moves through the forest, tracking the monster. He eventually locates the creature sleeping in an oak grove.

**Participating Characters:** Lion, Great Spider Monster (sleeping).

**Participating Environments:** Oak Grove.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension and suspense.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Point-of-view shots from the Lion's perspective; slow tracking shots through trees; extreme close-ups of the sleeping monster's breathing/movements.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Lighting (dappled sunlight through oaks); the physical scale of the monster relative to the Lion.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the central conflict and the scale of the threat.

**Scene Summary:** The travelers stumble upon a clearing filled with a massive gathering of wild animals, all appearing terrified.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Toto, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Lion, various forest beasts (tigers, elephants, bears, wolves, foxes).

**Participating Environments:** Forest Clearing.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion to intense fear/tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme wide shots of the animal assembly; medium shots of diverse animals looking anxious; reaction shots of the travelers.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Animal placement within the clearing; lighting consistency in the forest opening.

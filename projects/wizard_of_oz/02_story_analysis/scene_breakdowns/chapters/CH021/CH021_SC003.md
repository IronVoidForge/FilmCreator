# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the Lion's motivation and the stakes of the quest.

**Scene Summary:** A tiger explains that a giant spider-like monster is terrorizing the forest. The Lion seizes the opportunity, striking a deal with the animals: if he kills the beast, they will crown him King of the Forest.

**Participating Characters:** Lion, Tiger, various forest beasts, Dorothy and companions (as observers).

**Participating Environments:** Forest Clearing.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Fear to ambitious determination (Lion).

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Over-the-shoulder shots during the dialogue; close-ups of the Tiger describing the monster; low-angle hero shots of the Lion.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Eye contact between animals; Lion's posture/expression change from cowardice to bravado.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Provide the solution to the group's predicament via a magical guide.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy uses her whistle to summon the Queen of the Mice. The Queen appears and informs them they are heading the wrong way, then reveals that the Golden Cap can be used to command the Winged Monkeys for transport.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto, Queen of the Mice.

**Participating Environments:** The Scarlet Fields.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Despair $\rightarrow$ Curiosity/Hope.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots for the Queen of the Mice; reaction shots from the companions; close-ups on Dorothy's interaction with the whistle and the Cap.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Scale difference between the mice and the travelers; placement of the Golden Cap.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Execute the magical ritual and provide a high-energy transition.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy performs the ritual of the Golden Cap, standing on different feet and reciting the magic words. The Winged Monkeys descend from the sky to obey her command to fly them to the Emerald City.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto, Winged Monkeys (including Monkey King).

**Participating Environments:** The Scarlet Fields.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Tension/Anticipation $\rightarrow$ Awe/Exhilaration.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle "hero" shots of Dorothy performing the ritual; sweeping aerial shots as the monkeys arrive; fast-paced movement shots as the group is lifted.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The specific sequence of Dorothy's movements (feet/words); the arrival pattern of the monkeys.

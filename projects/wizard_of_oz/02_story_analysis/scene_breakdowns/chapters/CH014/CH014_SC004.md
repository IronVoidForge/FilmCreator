# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Provide world-building and backstory through dialogue during transit.

**Scene Summary:** While flying high above the landscape, the Monkey King tells Dorothy the story of how his people were cursed by the sorceress Gayelette to obey the Golden Cap after a prank gone wrong.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Monkey King, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion (as listeners).

**Participating Environments:** The Sky (aerial view of the landscape).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Exhilaration $\rightarrow$ Melancholy/Understanding.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Medium shots of Dorothy and the Monkey King; wide "beauty shots" of the landscape passing below; perhaps stylized flashback imagery if used.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Wind effects on hair/clothing; the physical positioning of characters on the monkeys' backs.

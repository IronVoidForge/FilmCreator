# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the group's state of exhaustion and disorientation.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, and Toto wander aimlessly through a vast, overwhelming field of scarlet buttercups and yellow daisies. Without the Yellow Brick Road to guide them, they are lost and losing hope.

**Participating Characters:** Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, Cowardly Lion, Toto.

**Participating Environments:** The Scarlet Fields (vast flower fields).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Hopeful/Determined $\rightarrow$ Despair/Exhaustion.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the massive landscape to emphasize scale; medium tracking shots of the group's weary movement; close-ups on faces showing fatigue.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Direction of travel through the flowers; physical state of the Tin Woodman (rust/movement) and Lion (panting).

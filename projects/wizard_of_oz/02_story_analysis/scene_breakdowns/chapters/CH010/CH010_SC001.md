# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Reintegrate the Cowardly Lion into the group and transition the journey toward the Emerald City.

**Scene Summary:** After being rescued from a poppy field by mice, the Cowardly Lion reunites with Dorothy, Scarecrow, Tin Woodman, and Toto. The group continues their trek along the yellow brick road through a landscape characterized by green-painted houses and emerald attire.

**Participating Characters:**
* Dorothy
* Scarecrow
* Tin Woodman
* Cowardly Lion
* Toto

**Participating Environments:**
* The Yellow Brick Road / Green Countryside

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Relief (from the Lion's rescue) to steady determination.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Wide shots of the green landscape and yellow brick road.
* Medium tracking shots of the group walking.
* Close-ups of the Lion's reunion with the companions.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The Lion's physical state (recovering from poppy sleep); the visual consistency of the "green" aesthetic in the background.

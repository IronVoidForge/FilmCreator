# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the scale and overwhelming visual intensity of the Emerald City.

**Scene Summary:** The group arrives at the massive, emerald-studded gates of the Emerald City. They encounter the Guardian of the Gates, who presents the first obstacle to their entry.

**Participating Characters:**
* Dorothy
* Scarecrow
* Tin Woodman
* Cowardly Lion
* Toto
* Guardian of the Gates

**Participating Environments:**
* The Emerald City Gates (Exterior)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Wonder/Awe to apprehension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Extreme wide shots showing the scale of the gates and walls.
* Low-angle shots of the group looking up at the Guardian.
* Close-ups of the emerald embellishments on the gate.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The height/scale of the gate relative to the characters; the brightness/glare of the city in the background.

# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce a physical constraint (the spectacles) and transition the characters into the city proper.

**Scene Summary:** To protect them from being blinded by the city's brilliance, the Guardian forces Dorothy, her companions, and Toto to wear green-lensed spectacles. The Guardian locks the glasses onto their heads with a key he keeps, then leads them through the gates and into the streets of the Emerald City.

**Participating Characters:**
* Dorothy
* Scarecrow
* Tin Woodman
* Cowardly Lion
* Toto
* Guardian of the Gates

**Participating Environments:**
* The Emerald City Gates / Streets of the Emerald City

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Constraint/Frustration to sensory overload.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Close-ups of the spectacles being fitted and locked.
* POV shots through green-tinted lenses.
* Tracking shots following the group as they enter the city streets.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The fit and appearance of the green spectacles on each character; the specific "green" tint applied to the camera/POV.

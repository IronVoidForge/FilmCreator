# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Provide exposition regarding the Wizard's nature and reinforce the characters' individual motivations.

* Scarecrow
* Tin Woodman

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Curiosity/Awe regarding Oz to focused resolve.

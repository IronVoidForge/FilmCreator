# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Transition from the safety of Munchkin hospitality to the uncertainty of the road.

**Scene Summary:** After a night's rest, Dorothy continues her journey. Boq offers a warning about the dangers ahead. While resting near a cornfield, Dorothy encounters a Scarecrow perched on a pole who suddenly comes to life.

**Participating Characters:** 
- Dorothy
- Boq (briefly)
- The Scarecrow

**Participating Environments:** 
- Yellow Brick Road
- Edge of a large cornfield

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From cautious optimism to sudden shock/surprise.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Low angle: Looking up at the Scarecrow on his pole.
- Close-ups: The movement of the straw as the Scarecrow awakens.
- Wide shots: The isolation of the cornfield setting.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Dorothy's level of fatigue/dishevelment after traveling; the physical positioning of the Scarecrow on the pole.

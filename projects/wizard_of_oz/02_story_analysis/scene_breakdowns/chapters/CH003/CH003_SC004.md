# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the primary traveling duo and their shared goal.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy helps the Scarecrow down from his pole. The Scarecrow explains his desire for brains and asks Dorothy to take him to the Emerald City to see Oz. Dorothy agrees, and they set off together, with a skeptical Toto following.

**Participating Characters:** 
- Dorothy
- The Scarecrow
- Toto

**Participating Environments:** 
- Cornfield edge/Yellow Brick Road

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From confusion to companionship and shared purpose.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Two-shots: Dorothy and the Scarecrow walking together.
- Close-ups: The Scarecrow's expressive (if straw) face; Toto's skeptical expressions.
- Profile shots: The duo walking away into the distance.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical interaction of helping the Scarecrow down; the placement of Toto relative to the two main characters.

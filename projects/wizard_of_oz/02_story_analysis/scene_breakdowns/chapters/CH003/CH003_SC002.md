# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce Boq and provide world-building regarding how Dorothy is perceived.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy attends a celebration at the home of Boq, a wealthy Munchkin. Amidst the festivities, Boq mistakenly identifies her as a "friendly witch" because of her silver shoes and blue-and-white attire.

**Participating Characters:** 
- Dorothy
- Boq
- Munchkin party guests

**Participating Environments:** 
- Boq's wealthy Munchkin home/Celebration grounds

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From festive celebration to social confusion/tension.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Medium shots: Group interactions and dancing.
- Over-the-shoulder: Boq addressing Dorothy.
- Reaction shots: Dorothy's response to being called a witch.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Food/drink props at the celebration; lighting transitions from day to night if implied by "night's rest" following this scene.

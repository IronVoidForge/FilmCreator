# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish Dorothy's new status and the beginning of her journey.

**Scene Summary:** Dorothy changes into a clean gingham dress and discovers the silver shoes fit perfectly. She sets out on the Yellow Brick Road, passing prosperous Munchkin farms and receiving bows from locals.

**Participating Characters:** 
- Dorothy
- Toto
- Local Munchkins (background/extras)

**Participating Environments:** 
- Dorothy's temporary quarters/Munchkin Country outskirts
- The Yellow Brick Road

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From domestic preparation to a sense of wonder and curiosity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** 
- Close-ups: The silver shoes, the gingham fabric.
- Wide shots: The colorful Munchkin landscape, the long stretch of the Yellow Brick Road.
- Tracking shots: Dorothy walking along the road.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The fit and appearance of the silver shoes; the cleanliness of the gingham dress.

A surreal journey through a nonsensical Wonderland where a young girl named Alice undergoes frequent, unpredictable physical transformations in size and shape. The narrative follows her attempts to maintain her identity and navigate a world populated by eccentric, often irritable creatures that operate under illogical social and biological rules. Key themes include the instability of self, the frustration of communication, and the navigation of a landscape where physical scale is a primary mechanic of interaction.

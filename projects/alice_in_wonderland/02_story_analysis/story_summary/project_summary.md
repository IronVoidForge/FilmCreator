A surreal journey through a nonsensical Wonderland where logic is inverted and characters possess animalistic or distorted features. The narrative follows Alice, a young girl navigating a landscape of shifting scales, unpredictable physics, and eccentric inhabitants including a Mad Hatter, a March Hare, and a Cheshire Cat. Key themes include the instability of identity (transformation), the breakdown of social etiquette, and the pervasive presence of "madness" as a fundamental law of the environment.

The story follows Alice, a young girl who falls into the nonsensical and surreal world of Wonderland. Through various encounters with eccentric characters—including a Mad Hatter, a March Hare, and a volatile Queen of Hearts—Alice experiences rapid physical transformations in size and navigates a landscape governed by illogical rules and dream-like shifts in reality. The narrative explores themes of identity, the absurdity of authority, and the boundary between dreams and waking life.

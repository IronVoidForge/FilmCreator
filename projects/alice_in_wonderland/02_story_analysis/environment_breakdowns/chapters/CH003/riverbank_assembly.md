# Riverbank Assembly

**Role:** Primary setting

**Architecture or Geography:** 
A low-lying, muddy riverbank. The terrain is uneven, wet, and saturated with water. A circular course is marked out in the mud for the Caucus-race.

**Lighting and Atmosphere Cues:** 
Damp, heavy atmosphere; overcast or diffused light reflecting off wet surfaces; a sense of collective discomfort due to moisture; transition from chaotic movement (the race) to a quiet, somber ring for storytelling.

**Scale Cues and Recurring Environmental Anchors:** 
Low-angle perspective relative to the animals; patches of clinging mud; puddles; the circular track boundary; the small scale of the comfits and thimble against the muddy expanse.

**Descriptive Noun Phrases:** 
Saturated riverbank, muddy shoreline, draggled feathers and fur, damp earth, circular race course, wet silt, riverside assembly, sodden terrain.

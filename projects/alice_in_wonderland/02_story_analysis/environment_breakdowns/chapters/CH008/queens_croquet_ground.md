# Environment: Queen's Croquet-Ground

**Role:** Primary setting for the chapter's central conflict and chaotic game.

**Architecture & Geography:**
An outdoor garden landscape defined by uneven terrain consisting of ridges and furrows. The space is populated by large rose-trees, specifically one featuring white roses being painted red. The layout is dynamic and unstable due to the living nature of the game components.

**Lighting & Atmosphere Cues:**
Bright, daylight garden setting; atmosphere shifts between manicured royal order and surreal, chaotic absurdity. High tension during royal processions and sudden outbursts of aggression from the Queen.

**Scale & Recurring Environmental Anchors:**
- **The Rose-Tree:** A central visual anchor where gardeners work.
- **Living Arches:** Humanoid soldiers (Clubs) doubling over to form moving, walking goalposts.
- **The Croquet Set:** Live hedgehogs acting as balls and live flamingoes acting as mallets.
- **Large Flower-Pot:** A significant prop used for hiding characters.

**Descriptive Noun Phrases for Prompting:**
Ridged garden soil, furrowed earth, rose-tree with crimson-painted white roses, living hedgehog croquet balls, pink flamingo mallets, card-soldier arches, royal procession in a surrealist garden, chaotic croquet match, Cheshire Cat's floating grin.

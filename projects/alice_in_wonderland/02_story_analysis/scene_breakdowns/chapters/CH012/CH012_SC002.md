# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To present the "evidence" and escalate the tension through a logic-defying trial.

**Scene Summary:** The White Rabbit presents the verses found on the Knave. As the Rabbit reads the cryptic poem, the King attempts to interpret it as proof of the theft. Alice watches with growing skepticism as the absurdity of the legal proceedings mounts.

**Participating Characters:**
- The White Rabbit
- The King
s- The Knave
- The Queen (watching)
- Alice

**Participating Environments:**
- The Courtroom

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From curious investigation to mounting frustration/absurdity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-ups of the paper/verses being held by the White Rabbit.
- Reaction shots of the King's intense, bumbling interpretation.
- Alice's skeptical facial expressions.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The physical prop of the verse paper.
- The positioning of the Knave in his dock.

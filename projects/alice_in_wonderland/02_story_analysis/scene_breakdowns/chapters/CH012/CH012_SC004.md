# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To ground the story in reality and provide a thematic epilogue.

**Scene Summary:** Alice wakes up on the riverbank, back to her normal size. She tells her sister about the dream. As they sit together at sunset, the sister reflects on how the sounds of the real world echo the strange sounds of Wonderland.

**Participating Characters:**
- Alice (Normal)
- Alice's Sister

**Participating Environments:**
- The Riverbank (Real World/Natural Setting)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From the disorientation of waking to a peaceful, reflective calm.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the beautiful riverbank at sunset.
- Close-ups of Alice and her sister's faces.
- Static, tranquil shots of the natural environment.

**Likely Continuity Sensitities:**
- The lighting transition from bright daylight to golden hour/sunset.
- The contrast in scale between the dream world and this grounded reality.

# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To provide a climactic confrontation and the transition from dream to reality.

**Scene Summary:** Alice challenges the logic of the trial, prompting the Queen to order "Off with her head!" The cards fly at Alice in an overwhelming swarm. This chaotic assault triggers Alice's sudden awakening as she shrinks back to normal size.

**Participating Characters:**
- Alice (Giant)
- The Queen
- The Pack of Cards (The Jury/Soldiers)

**Participating Environments:**
- The Courtroom (surreal/dreamscape)

**Dominly Emotional Shift:** From high-stakes tension to a sudden, jarring release.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low angle shots of the Queen's outburst.
- Fast-paced, kinetic shots of cards flying toward the camera/Alice.
- A "match cut" or seamless transition from the swarm to Alice waking up.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The visual effect of the cards as a physical force.
- The sudden change in scale (Giant Alice to human-sized Alice).

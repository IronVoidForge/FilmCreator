# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the surreal scale of Alice and the chaotic start to the trial.

**Scene Summary:** As Alice grows to a massive size, she accidentally tips over the jury box, scattering the jurors like goldfish. The King attempts to maintain order by reading Rule 42, which dictates that anyone over a mile high must leave the court, sparking an argument between Alice and the King.

**Participating Characters:**
- Alice (Giant)
- The King
- The White Rabbit
- The Jury (including Bill the Lizard)
- The Knave (Prisoner)

**Participly Environments:**
- The Courtroom (surreal/dreamscape)

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From chaotic physical accident to a tense, bureaucratic confrontation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots showing Alice's massive scale relative to the tiny jury.
- Medium shots of the King reading from his notebook.
- Close-ups of the jurors falling/tumbling out of the box.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:**
- The physical size of Alice vs. the courtroom elements.
- The state of the jury box (tipped vs. upright).

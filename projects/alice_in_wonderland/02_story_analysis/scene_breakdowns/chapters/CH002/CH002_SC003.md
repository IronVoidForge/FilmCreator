# SC003

**scene purpose:** Execute a rapid physical transformation and an internal identity crisis.

**scene summary:** Alice uses the Rabbit's fan to cool herself, leading to a philosophical monologue about her identity and failed attempts at reciting lessons. She realizes she is shrinking rapidly. As she reaches two feet in height, she drops the fan and runs toward the garden door, only to fall into the pool of tears she created earlier.

**participating characters:** Alice

**participating environments:** The Hall (transitioning from giant scale to miniature scale)

**dominant emotional_shift:** Existential dread $\rightarrow$ Panic $\rightarrow$ Physical disorientation

**likely visual coverage families:** Time-lapse or rapid scale-change shots; close-ups of Alice's face during the monologue; POV shots of the floor rushing up.

**likely continuity sensitivities:** The shrinking rate of Alice; the size of the golden key relative to her new height.

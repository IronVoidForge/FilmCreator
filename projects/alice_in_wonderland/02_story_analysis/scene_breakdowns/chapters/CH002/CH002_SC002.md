# SC002

**scene purpose:** Introduce a secondary character and create a moment of frantic movement/interruption.

**scene summary:** The White Rabbit scurries into the hall, preoccupied with his errands for the Duchess. Alice attempts to engage him, but her massive size startles him so much that he drops his gloves and fan before fleeing into the darkness. Alice retrieves the dropped items.

**participating characters:** Alice, The White Rabbit

**participating environments:** The Hall (near the pool of tears)

**dominant emotional_shift:** Distraction $\rightarrow$ Startle/Panic

**likely visual coverage families:** Tracking shots of the Rabbit; medium shots of the interaction; close-ups of the dropped gloves and fan.

**likely continuity sensitivities:** Position of the White Rabbit relative to Alice's giant feet; placement of the gloves/fan on the floor.

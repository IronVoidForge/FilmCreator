# SC001

**scene purpose:** Establish Alice's massive scale and her initial frustration with the physical environment.

**scene summary:** Alice, now over nine feet tall, struggles with her size in the hall. Her head hits the ceiling, and she feels disconnected from her own feet. After picking up a golden key, she realizes she is too large to fit through the garden door and begins to cry, creating a pool of salt water.

**participating characters:** Alice

**participating environments:** The Hall (Large scale/High ceiling)

**dominant emotional shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Frustration $\rightarrow$ Despair

**likely visual coverage families:** Extreme low-angle shots (looking up at Alice), wide shots to show scale, close-ups of the golden key and Alice's face.

**likely continuity sensitivities:** The height of Alice relative to the door; the rising level of the salt water pool.

# SC004

**scene purpose:** Introduce a new character through a social/linguistic misunderverstanding.

**scene summary:** While swimming in the pool of tears, Alice encounters a Mouse. She attempts to communicate with it in English and then French to test its intelligence. The mention of "cats" offends the Mouse, leading to a tense but polite negotiation about their shared predicament.

**participating characters:** Alice, The Mouse

**participating environments:** The Pool of Tears (Water surface)

**dominant emotional_shift:** Curiosity $\rightarrow$ Social awkwardness $\rightarrow$ Tension

**likely visual coverage families:** Underwater/Surface level shots; close-ups of the Mouse's expressions; medium shots of Alice swimming.

**likely continuity sensitivities:** Water level relative to Alice's chin; the physical proximity of the Mouse to Alice.

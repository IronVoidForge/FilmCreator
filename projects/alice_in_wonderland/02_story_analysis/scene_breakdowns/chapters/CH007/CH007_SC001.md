# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce the chaotic social dynamic of the tea party and Alice's initial interaction with the hosts.

**Scene Summary:** Alice approaches a crowded tea table where the March Hare, Hatter, and Dormouse are squeezed together. Despite ample space, they insist there is "no room." Alice finds a seat in a large armchair, and the conversation turns to the Hatter's nonsensical riddle about ravens and writing-desks.

**Participating Characters:**
- Alice
- The March Hare
- The Hatter
- The Dormouse

**Participating Environments:** A large table set under a tree in front of a house.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From curiosity/confusion to social frustration.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide shots of the crowded table vs. empty space.
- Medium close-ups of the eccentric hosts' faces during the riddle.
- Alice's reaction shots (eye-level).

**Likely Continuity Sensenstitivities:** The arrangement of tea-things; the specific placement of the Dormouse as a cushion/object.

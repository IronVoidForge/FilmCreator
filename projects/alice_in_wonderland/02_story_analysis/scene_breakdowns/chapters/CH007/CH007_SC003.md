# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To escalate the absurdity through a surreal story and the physical movement of the characters.

**Scene Summary:** The Dormouse tells a dream-like story about three sisters in a treacle-well. As Alice questions the logic, the group performs a synchronized shift: they move one seat over to find clean dishes. Alice is splashed with milk during the transition as she takes the March Hare's spot.

**Participating Characters:**
- Alice
e- The March Hare
- The Hatter
- The Dormouse

**Participating Environments:** Under the tree at the tea table.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From whimsical storytelling to chaotic, physical movement.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium shots of the Dormouse telling the story.
- Tracking/panning shots as the characters move around the table.
- Close-up of milk splashing on Alice.

**Likely Continuity Sensenstitivities:** The flow of the "musical chairs" style movement; liquid physics (the milk splash).

# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To resolve the chapter with a successful transition into the new environment (the garden).

**Scene Summary:** Alice finds a door in a tree and enters a long hall. She uses her golden key on the glass table, nibbles on the mushroom she has in her pocket, shrinks down, and successfully enters the beautiful garden.

**Participating Characters:**
- Alice

**Participating Environments:** A long hall with a glass table; The Beautiful Garden.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension/shrinking to wonder/triumph.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Low angle shots of the glass table.
- Special effects/Scale shots (Alice shrinking).
- Wide, lush shots of the garden reveal.

**Likely Continuity Sensenstitivities:** The golden key; the mushroom piece in Alice's pocket; the scale shift between Alice and the environment.

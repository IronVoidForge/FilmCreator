# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To provide the emotional climax of Alice's frustration and her transition away from the tea party.

**Scene Summary:** Frustrated by the Hatter's rudeness, Alice decides to leave. She walks through the woods toward a long hall. As she leaves, she observes the hosts putting the Dormouse into a teapot.

**Participating Characters:**
- Alice
- The March Hare (briefly seen)
- The Hatter (briefly seen)
- The Dormouse (seen in distance/transition)

**Participating Environments:** The wooded path leading away from the table.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From irritation to a sense of lonely, purposeful departure.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium tracking shot of Alice walking.
- Wide shots showing the scale of the woods vs. Alice.
- A final look back at the tea party (distance shot).

**Likely Continuity Sensenstitivities:** Alice's costume/appearance as she moves from the table to the path.

# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce the Queen's terrifying authority and its arbitrary nature.

**Scene Summary:** The Queen of Hearts makes a sudden, startling appearance. She presents the Duchess with an ultimatum; the Duchess chooses to depart immediately to avoid execution.

**Participating Characters:** Alice, The Duchess, The Queen of Hearts.

**Participating Environments:** The path/garden entrance.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From calm walking to sudden terror and abrupt departure.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide: The arrival of the Queen.
- Medium: The interaction between the Queen and the Duchess.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Suddenness of movement, costume/prop consistency for the Queen.

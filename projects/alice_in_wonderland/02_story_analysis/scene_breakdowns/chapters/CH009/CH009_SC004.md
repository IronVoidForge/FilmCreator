# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** To transition Alice from the court to a more melancholic, surreal environment.

**Scene Summary:** After the Queen sends Alice off to find the Mock Turtle (explaining it is soup), Alice meets the Gryphon and they travel toward the sound of sobbing.

**Participating Characters:** Alice, The Queen of Hearts, The King of Hearts, The Gryphon.

**Participating Environments:** The Croquet-ground transitioning into a sunny area/pathway.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From the high energy of the court to a more whimsical, searching mood.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Medium: Interaction between Alice and the Queen/King.
- Wide: Alice walking with the Gryphon through the landscape.

**Likely Continuity Sensities:** The transition from the bright garden to the path where the Gryphon is found.

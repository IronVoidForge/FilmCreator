# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To showcase the chaos of the croquet game and the Queen's capricious rule.

**Scene Summary:** Alice follows the Queen to a croquet-ground where players (soldiers) are frequently ordered to be executed. The game is a cycle of arbitrary arrests and pardons.

**Participating Characters:** Alice, The Queen of Hearts, The King of Hearts, Soldiers/Players.

**Participting Environments:** The Croquet-ground.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From tension to chaotic absurdity.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Wide: The scale of the croquet ground and the players.
- Medium: The Queen issuing commands; Alice observing the carnage.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The movement of the game pieces/players, the visual distinction between soldiers and players.

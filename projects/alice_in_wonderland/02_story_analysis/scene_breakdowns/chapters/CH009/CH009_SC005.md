# SC005

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce a new emotional weight through the Mock Turtle's tragic backstory.

**Scene Summary:** Alice and the Gryphon find the weeping Mock Turtle on a ledge. The Mock Turtle shares his history of schooling in the sea, explaining the "lessons" that lessen over time.

**Participating Characters:** Alice, The Gryphon, The Mock Turtle.

**Participating Environments:** A rocky ledge/sunny area with the sea below.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From curiosity to deep, melancholic whimsy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Close-up: The Mock Turtle's weeping face and expressive movements.
- Medium: Alice listening intently; the Gryphon observing.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The physical position of the characters on the ledge; the visual distinction between the sea/sky background.

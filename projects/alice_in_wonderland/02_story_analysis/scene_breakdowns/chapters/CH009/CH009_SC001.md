# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To establish the eccentric, didactic nature of the Duchess and Alice's growing bewilderment.

**Scene Summary:** Alice walks with the Duchess, who provides a constant stream of illogical "morals" regarding life and temper, much to Alice's polite frustration.

**Participating Characters:** Alice, The Duchess.

**Participating Environments:** A path leading away from the kitchen.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From polite curiosity to mild bewilderment/irritation.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
- Two-shot: Walking side-by-side.
- Close-up: The Duchess's sharp features and Alice's reacting expressions.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** Walking pace, the physical distance between characters as they move through the environment.

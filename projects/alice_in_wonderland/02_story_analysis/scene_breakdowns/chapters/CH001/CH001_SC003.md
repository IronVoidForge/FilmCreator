# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the goal (the garden) and the immediate obstacle (the locked doors).

**Scene Summary:** Alice lands on a heap of sticks and leaves. She follows the Rabbit into a long, lamp-lit hall filled with many locked doors. She discovers a tiny golden key on a glass table and finds a small door hidden behind a curtain. The key works, revealing a glimpse of a lush garden, but she is too large to enter.

**Participating Characters:** Alice, The White Rabbit (fleeting).

**Participating Environments:** The Hallway (lamp-lit); The Small Door/Curtain; The Garden (viewed through the door).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Disorientation $\rightarrow$ Discovery $\rightarrow$ Frustration/Longing.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Low-angle shots of the hall; Close-ups of the golden key and the lock; Macro shots of the garden through the small opening.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The scale of Alice relative to the door; The lighting transition from the dark hall to the bright garden view.

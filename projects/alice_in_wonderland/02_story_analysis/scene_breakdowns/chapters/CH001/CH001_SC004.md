# SC004

**Scene Purpose:** Execute the primary physical transformation and the escalating conflict of scale.

**Scene Summary:** Alice drinks from a "DRINK ME" bottle, shrinking to ten inches tall. She realizes she has left the key on top of the high glass table and struggles to climb it. In desperation, she finds an "EAT ME" cake. After eating it, she expects another change in size, but her height remains unchanged, leaving her in a state of confusion.

**Participating Characters:** Alice.

**Participating Environments:** The Hallway (near the glass table).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Wonder $\rightarrow$ Panic/Despair $\rightarrow$ Uncertain Hope.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Extreme close-ups of the labels ("DRINK ME", "EAT ME"); Low-angle shots looking up at the table from Alice's new perspective; Wide shot showing her small stature in the large hall.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The exact height of Alice after shrinking; The visual distinction between the liquid and the cake; The physical sensation of the "flavor" described.

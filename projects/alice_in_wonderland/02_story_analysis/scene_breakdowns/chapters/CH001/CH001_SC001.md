# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** Establish the mundane reality and introduce the inciting incident (the Rabbit).

**Scene Summary:** Alice sits bored by the riverbank with her sister. The tranquility is broken when a White Rabbit in a waistcoat runs past, checking a pocket watch, prompting Alice to chase it into a rabbit-hole under a hedge.

**Participating Characters:** Alice, Alice's Sister (background), The White Rabbit.

**Participating Environments:** Sunny riverbank; Hedge/Rabbit-hole entrance.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Boredom $\rightarrow$ Intense Curiosity/Urgency.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Wide shots of the landscape; Medium shots of Alice and her sister; Close-ups of the White Rabbit's watch and waistcoat; Tracking shot of the chase.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The transition from the sunny bank to the shade of the hedge; Alice's physical movement into the hole.

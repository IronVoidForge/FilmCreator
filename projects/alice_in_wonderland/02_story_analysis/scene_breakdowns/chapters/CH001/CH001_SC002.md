# SC002

**Scene Purpose:** Introduce the surreal physics and scale of Wonderland through a descent.

**Scene Summary:** Alice falls slowly down a deep, vertical tunnel. The walls are lined with domestic objects like cupboards and bookshelves. She interacts with her environment by grabbing an "ORANGE MARMALADE" jar to save it. During the fall, she engages in internal monologue about geography and her cat, Dinah.

**Participating Characters:** Alice.

**Participating Environments:** The deep vertical well/tunnel (lined with shelves and cupboards).

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** Confusion $\rightarrow$ Philosophical Contemplation $\rightarrow$ Impact.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:** Vertical tracking shots; POV shots looking down the shaft; Close-ups of the marmalade jar and bookshelves; Extreme long shot of Alice falling.

**Likely Continuity Sensitivities:** The speed of the fall (slow/dreamlike vs. fast); The placement of objects on the shelves as she passes them.

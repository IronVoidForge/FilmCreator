# SC001

**Scene Purpose:** To introduce the whimsical, physical, and pun-heavy nature of the Mock Turtle and Gryphon through a dance.

**Scene Summary:** The Mock Turtle and the Gryphon perform the "Lobster Quadrille" around Alice, stepping on her toes as they explain the dance. This is interspersed with the Mock Turtle's melancholic singing about whiting and snails, leading to a linguistic debate over puns.

**Participating Characters:**
* Alice
* The Mock Turtle
* The Gryphon

**Participating Environments:** A seaside-adjacent landscape/environment.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From playful, rhythmic movement to whimsical confusion and melancholic whimsy.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Wide shots of the dance/movement around Alice.
* Medium close-ups of the Mock Turtle's weeping face.
* Close-ups of feet stepping on toes for physical comedy.

**Likely Continuity Sensences:** The specific pattern of the "first figure" of the dance; the interaction between the creatures' movements and Alice's stationary/reactive position.

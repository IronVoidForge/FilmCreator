# SC003

**Scene Purpose:** To provide a moment of beautiful, sad musicality before an abrupt, high-stakes transition.

**Scene Summary:** The Mock Turtle sings "Beautiful Soup" with deep sorrow. The song is suddenly interrupted by the distant cry that the trial is beginning. The Gryphon grabs Alice and leads her away in a frantic run, cutting the moment short.

**Participating Characters:**
* Alice
* The Mock le Turtle
* The Gryphon

**Participating Environments:** The seaside-adjacent environment.

**Dominant Emotional Shift:** From deep, soulful melancholy to sudden, urgent momentum.

**Likely Visual Coverage Families:**
* Close-up of the Mock Turtle singing (emotional focus).
* Handheld/dynamic shots as the Gryphon grabs Alice and they run away.
* A wide shot showing the distance between them and their destination.

**Likely Continuity Sensences:** The sudden change in pace from a slow song to a fast run; the physical grip of the Gryphon on Alice's hand.

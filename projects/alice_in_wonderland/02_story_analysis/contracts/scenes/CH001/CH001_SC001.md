Alice sits bored by a riverbank until a White Rabbit in a waistcoat runs past checking a watch. She chases him into a rabbit-hole, falling down a deep well lined with cupboards and shelves. After landing on a heap of sticks, she finds herself in a long hall of locked doors where she discovers a tiny golden key and a small door leading to a garden. After shrinking by drinking from a 'DRINK ME' bottle, she realizes she cannot reach the key, then eats an 'EAT ME' cake to alter her size further.
